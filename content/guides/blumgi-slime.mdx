## Mastering the Core Jump Mechanic and Scoring

### The Perfect Jump Technique
The heart of Blumgi Slime's gameplay revolves around precise jump control. Mastering this mechanic is essential for success in later levels.

**Controlling Jump Power Through Mouse Hold Duration**
The jump mechanic operates on a charge system: the longer you hold the mouse button, the more power builds up in your slime's jump. A quick tap produces a small hop, ideal for minor adjustments, while a full charge creates a massive leap capable of clearing large gaps. The key to precision is developing muscle memory for different charge durations. Practice on early levels to internalize the relationship between hold time and jump distance.

**Optimizing Jump Precision to Minimize Total Jumps**
Efficiency is crucial for achieving perfect scores. Each level can be completed with an optimal number of jumps, and minimizing unnecessary jumps improves your final rating. Study the level layout before your first jump and plan your route to avoid backtracking. Often, taking a slightly longer path with consistent jumps is better than risky long jumps that might require correction hops.

### Failure Conditions and Obstacle Avoidance
Understanding what causes failure is as important as mastering success.

**Avoiding Spikes and Environmental Hazards**
Spikes, pits, and moving obstacles are the primary threats in Blumgi Slime. The key to avoiding these hazards is careful observation before acting. Watch moving platform patterns for several cycles before attempting to cross. For spike-filled areas, approach slowly and look for safe pathways rather than rushing forward.

**The Screen Boundary Penalty System**
Falling below the visible screen area results in immediate level failure. This mechanic encourages forward progress but can be punishing in sections with significant vertical movement. Always ensure you have a clear upward path before descending, and be cautious when dropping down pits - some may be too deep to recover from.

### Conquering Difficult Puzzle Stages
Later levels introduce complex mechanics that test your understanding of the game's physics.

**Reverse Jump and Moving Obstacle Strategies**
Some levels feature reverse gravity or moving platforms that require precise timing. For reverse jump sections, reorient your thinking - up becomes down, and timing becomes crucial. With moving obstacles, study their patterns thoroughly before attempting to cross. Often, waiting for the optimal moment is better than rushing.

**Mastering Portal and Spring Mechanics**
Portals transport your slime between different areas, while springs provide significant bounce boosts. When dealing with portals, note both the entry and exit locations to understand the transportation effect. Springs require careful power management - too much jump power before hitting a spring can send you too high, while too little may not reach the target.
