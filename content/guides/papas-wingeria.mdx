## Core Gameplay Mechanics and Scoring Secrets

Before you can start stacking up perfect orders, it's essential to understand the fundamentals of how the game works. Scoring is directly linked to your precision in each of the stations: Frying, Saucing, Plating, and Customer Patience.

### Mastering the 100/100/100 Perfect Order

Achieving a perfect order in Papa's Wingeria isn't just about speed; it's about precision and balance in every step of the cooking process. The perfect order consists of three crucial components:

#### Fry Station Precision: Achieving Perfect Frying Accuracy and Timing

The Fry Station is where it all begins. The key to earning high scores here is to achieve a 100% accuracy in your frying. Pay attention to the color of the wings as they cook, and make sure you’re frying them to the ideal level (golden brown) without overcooking or undercooking.

- **Pro Tip**: Keep your eyes on the fryer timer and don’t let it expire. Perfect frying time varies depending on the meat type, so learn each one’s optimal frying time.

#### Sauce Application Mastery: Hitting the Green Zone on the Spin Meter

Applying sauce to your wings is just as important as frying them to perfection. To earn a “Great!” rating, you need to hit the sweet spot on the Spin Meter for each sauce application.

- **Pro Tip**: Focus on hitting the green zone, especially when applying a special sauce. Too little sauce or too much can drastically lower your score.

#### Plating Arrangement Value: How Even Placement and Proper Side Items Boost the Visual Score

Presentation is key! Ensure your wings are arranged evenly on the plate and that side items are placed carefully. A disorganized plate will result in a low score, even if the wings themselves are perfect.

- **Pro Tip**: Always make sure sides like fries or drinks are neatly placed. The better your plating, the higher your visual score will be.

#### The Patience Penalty: Strategies to Manage and Minimize the Customer Patience Meter Drop

One of the most crucial aspects of keeping your customers happy is managing the Customer Patience Meter. A full meter leads to a tip increase, while a drop results in lower tips. Be quick, but don’t rush—precise timing is key.

- **Pro Tip**: Keep an eye on customer patience, and try to avoid leaving orders on hold for too long. Quick service and efficient plating lead to the best tips.
