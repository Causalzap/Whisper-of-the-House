<h2 id="voidling-bound-quackies-mini-wiki">
  Voidling Bound Quackies mini-wiki and checklist
</h2>

**Quackies** are not combat upgrades. They are hidden collectibles spread across Voidling Bound missions, and each one has its own name and visual theme. That makes them better suited to a checklist-style guide than a long route walkthrough.

Instead of trying to memorize every turn, use three clues:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>
    <strong>Mission:</strong> the only place that Quacky appears.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Look:</strong> color or theme, such as Canary, Violet, Bronze, Mirage, or Oxblood.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Landmark:</strong> the object, ledge, wall, cave route, arena edge, plant cluster, or machinery area you should check.
  </li>
</ul>

For full completion routing, pair this page with the <Link href="/voidling-bound/all-collectibles">All Collectibles Guide</Link>. If you are still building power, use the <Link href="/voidling-bound/mutagen-research-points-farm">Mutagen and Research Points Farm Guide</Link> first.

<h2 id="faq">
  Voidling Bound Quackies FAQ
</h2>

### How many Quackies are in Voidling Bound?

There are 26 Quackies in this checklist: 10 in Vireo, 8 in Solum, 7 in Aulis, and 1 in The Abyss.

### What do Quackies do in Voidling Bound?

Quackies are hidden duck collectibles. After you collect them, they appear on your ship as part of your collection.

### Are Quackies power upgrades?

No. Quackies are completion collectibles. If your build is still weak, farm Mutagens and Research Points before doing full Quacky cleanup.

### Are Low, Medium, and High official Quacky difficulties?

No. The cleanup friction ratings in this guide are our own player-facing labels, not official in-game difficulty categories. They estimate how easy each Quacky is to miss based on landmark clarity, color contrast, route pressure, screenshot review, community notes, and editorial judgment.

### Does every mission have a Quacky?

Use each mission as if it has one hidden Quacky. The checklist above maps each mission to its Quacky name, look, and main landmark.

### What is the easiest Quacky to find first?

Canary Quacky in Faint Signal is the best first target because the mission-end platform is a clear landmark.

### Which Vireo Quackies are easiest to find?

Canary Quacky in Faint Signal, Violet Quacky in Save the Cauldron, Red Quacky in Centennial Bloom, and Blue Quacky in Secret Stronghold are good early targets because their landmarks are relatively clear.

### What is the easiest Solum Quacky to miss?

Subterranean Expedition's Beige Quacky is easy to miss because it blends into icy cave walls and pale terrain around the fire-crystal and ice-blockage route.

### What is the easiest Aulis Quacky to miss?

Navigate the Maze's Quicky Quacky is one of the easiest Aulis Quackies to miss because it sits away from the obvious path on a lower side platform under a rock overhang.

### Where is Mirage Quacky?

Mirage Quacky is in Stay on Track. Check the cliff-side platform near the right rock wall and the large blue-green leaf cluster.

### Where is Toxic Quacky?

Toxic Quacky is in Storm Breaker. Check the red pond side area and nearby ledges after the upper route opens.

### Where is Poltergeist Quacky?

Poltergeist Quacky is in Machine Non Grata. Search machinery-side ledges, route edges, and platform corners.

### Where is Fancy Quacky?

Fancy Quacky is in On the Brink of Ruin. Look for the small round stone pedestal near tall pale rock blocks and broken ruin structures.

### Where is The Dark Entity Quacky?

The Dark Entity Quacky is in End to the Unknown. Search the dark floor pocket between tall rock walls and pale root-like structures before leaving.

### Should I collect Quackies during timer routes?

Usually no. Timer routes reward speed, while Quacky cleanup requires slow side checks.
