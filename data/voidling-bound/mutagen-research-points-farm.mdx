<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
For **Voidling Bound** farming, open the **Evolution Chamber** first and check which Mutagen element you actually need. Use **Centennial Bloom** or **Reclaim Sanctuary** for early Organic/Pyro farming, **Secret Stronghold** or **Subterranean Expedition** for Cryo/Cyber-focused gaps, and **Navigate the Maze** when you need a mixed all-element catch-up route.

For **Research Points**, combine mission rewards with smart release decisions. Extra Voidlings can become Research Points, but inspect rare Natures, Golden Egg variants, and high-stat candidates before releasing them. Research Points can cap at **99,999**, so spend before long farming sessions.

<h2 id="early-organic-pyro-farm">
  Early Organic and Pyro farm: Centennial Bloom vs Reclaim Sanctuary
</h2>

Early players often overuse **Centennial Bloom** because it is one of the first obvious Mutagen cleanup routes. It is still good, especially when you want a clear route with fixed Organic and Pyro pickups.

But **Reclaim Sanctuary** deserves a spot in your farming plan. It can give **4 Organic + 4 Pyro** value and works well as an arena-wave route, which means the enemy waves can add extra reward chances while you farm.

<h2 id="faq">
  Voidling Bound Mutagen and Research Points FAQ
</h2>

### What is the best early Mutagen farm?

Centennial Bloom is a good early route for Organic and Pyro Mutagens, but Reclaim Sanctuary is also excellent because it gives Organic/Pyro value and wave-based enemy drop chances.

### Where should I farm Organic Mutagens?

Start with Centennial Bloom or Reclaim Sanctuary. If you need a broader mixed route later, use Navigate the Maze.

### Where should I farm Pyro Mutagens?

Use Centennial Bloom, Reclaim Sanctuary, or Storm Breaker. Navigate the Maze is the best backup if you need several elements at once.

### Where should I farm Cryo Mutagens?

Use Secret Stronghold, Subterranean Expedition, or Navigate the Maze. Check the Evolution Chamber first so you know Cryo is actually the blocked element.

### Where should I farm Cyber Mutagens?

Use Secret Stronghold, Pull the Plug, or Navigate the Maze. Look for machinery, stronghold, and platform-heavy routes.

### Where should I farm Plasma Mutagens?

Use Pull the Plug, End to the Unknown, or Navigate the Maze. Plasma is better treated as a later farming target.

### Is Navigate the Maze good for Mutagen farming?

Yes. Navigate the Maze is the best mixed-element catch-up route because it can cover all five Mutagen types, but it is better once your build is stable.

### How do I farm Research Points?

Farm mission rewards, clear optional objectives, release surplus Voidlings after inspection, and use stronger reward routes once your build can clear them safely.

### Do released Voidlings give Research Points?

Yes. Normal surplus Voidlings can give 150 Research Points, and rare Nature Voidlings can give more. Inspect candidates before releasing them.

### What is the Research Points cap?

Research Points can cap at **99,999**. Spend useful points before long farming sessions so rewards do not get wasted.

### Should I release Golden Egg Voidlings for Research Points?

Usually no, at least not immediately. Golden Egg variants can have rare Natures, unusual traits, unique looks, or later Splicing value.

### Should I farm Mutagens or Research Points first?

Farm the resource that fixes your current bottleneck. Mutagens unlock evolution nodes. Research Points improve species-wide upgrades.

### Should I farm Quackies while farming Mutagens?

Usually no. Quackies are completion collectibles. If your build still needs power, farm Mutagens and Research Points first.
