<h2 id="who-is-oread">
  Who is Oread in Realm of Ink?
</h2>

Oread is the 1.0 crossover form from BlazBlue Entropy Effect. She plays very differently from a simple weapon-focused character because her best routes revolve around curses, butterflies, lasers, arrays, and ability upgrades.

To unlock Oread, update Realm of Ink to version 1.0 and check the **Fox Inn / hub form unlocks**. She is part of the 1.0 crossover content, so if you do not see her yet, keep progressing the hub unlock system and make sure you are not playing an older version.

The most important thing to know is this: Oread does not become strong by picking random damage upgrades. She becomes strong when curse effects, ability damage, Ink Relic skill damage, light attacks, heavy attacks, and laser triggers work together.

If you are new to Realm of Ink, read the <Link href="/realm-of-ink/beginner-guide">Beginner Guide</Link> first. Oread is easier to understand once Ink Gems, Momo, Curios, Primordial Ink, and Elixirs make sense.

    Butterfly Roosting Call is a strong Oread starter because it turns light attack butterflies into more hits, and those hits scale with curses.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

Butterfly Roosting Call is one of the best early Oread picks because it gives your light attack route a real identity. When a large butterfly disappears, it creates smaller butterflies that converge toward the center. The important part is that the small butterfly damage improves when curses are stacked on the enemy.

That means your build wants two things:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>more ways to apply curses,</li>
  <li>and more ways to make butterfly hits matter.</li>
</ul>

This is why Oread likes multi-hit effects, curse duration, curse effect, ability damage, and upgrades that make butterflies track, spiral, or trigger extra effects.

Do not judge the ability only by the first few rooms. Oread often needs a few correct pieces before the build starts feeling unfair.

<h2 id="six-line-secret-art">
  Six-Line Secret Art: curse effect and ability damage
</h2>

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/realm-of-ink/realm-of-ink-oread-six-line-secret-art.webp"

    width={400}

    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    Six-Line Secret Art rewards rooms where enemies are already cursed, turning curse spread into more ability damage.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

Six-Line Secret Art is one of the most important Oread scaling pieces. It increases Curse Effects and gives more ability damage for each cursed target in the room.

This is strong because Oread can create a lot of hits. Butterflies, lasers, arrays, clones, and repeated attacks all give you more chances to apply or benefit from curses.

Take Six-Line Secret Art when:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>you already have curse application,</li>
  <li>you are using butterfly or laser abilities,</li>
  <li>you want ability damage to scale harder,</li>
  <li>and rooms have enough targets for the bonus to matter.</li>
</ul>

It is weaker if your build cannot curse enemies consistently. Oread does not want isolated pieces. She wants a chain reaction.

<h2 id="best-curios">
  Best Curios and stats for Oread
</h2>

Oread wants Curios and Elixirs that reward repeated hits, curses, ability damage, laser payoff, and safe positioning. At this point, do not just ask whether a Curio is rare. Ask whether it supports the Oread route you are actually playing.

Prioritize these stats and effects:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li><strong>Curse effect</strong> — makes your curse route stronger.</li>
  <li><strong>Curse duration</strong> — keeps enemies cursed long enough for butterflies, lasers, and ability effects to benefit.</li>
  <li><strong>Ability damage</strong> — improves Oread's form-based damage sources.</li>
  <li><strong>Ink Relic skill damage</strong> — helps if your active skills and Primordial effects are doing real work.</li>
  <li><strong>Light attack damage</strong> — useful when Butterfly Roosting Call and butterfly frequency are your main route.</li>
  <li><strong>Heavy attack damage</strong> — becomes more important after Power of Prayer, laser, or clone upgrades.</li>
  <li><strong>Cooldown or interval support</strong> — helps Oread trigger butterflies, lasers, and ability effects more often.</li>
  <li><strong>Status effect chance</strong> — poison, bleeding, burning, and similar triggers pair well with many-hit builds.</li>
  <li><strong>Crit rate and crit damage</strong> — strong once your hit count is high enough to make crits consistent.</li>
  <li><strong>Shield, HP, or damage reduction</strong> — important because Oread can feel fragile on higher difficulty.</li>
</ul>

Use this shortcut:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>If your run is built around butterflies, prioritize light attack speed, curse effect, and ability damage.</li>
  <li>If your run is built around Power of Prayer, prioritize heavy attack damage, lasers, Ink Relic skill damage, and cooldown.</li>
  <li>If your run is built around arrays, prioritize Sigil, zone triggers, defensive positioning, and effects that reward standing in arrays.</li>
</ul>

Do not tunnel on damage only. A dead Oread build does zero DPS.

<h2 id="how-to-play">
  How to play Oread in rooms and bosses
</h2>

Oread plays best when you keep moving while letting curses, butterflies, lasers, and arrays do work. She is not a stand-still DPS character. If you stop moving just to watch the screen fill with effects, late bosses and high difficulty enemies will punish you.

In normal rooms, your goal is to apply curses early and create enough repeated hits for your build to snowball. Send butterflies through enemy groups, use Ink skills before enemies spread too far, and only stand inside helpful arrays when the area is actually safe.

Against bosses, the goal changes. Bosses expose whether your damage is really scaling or just looking flashy. Curses are useful, but you still need ability damage, Ink Relic skill damage, heavy attack lasers, crit, status triggers, or another payoff that can hurt one target.

  </figcaption>
</figure>

Use these rules in boss fights:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Apply curses early so Butterfly Roosting Call, Six-Line Secret Art, and laser effects have something to scale from.</li>
  <li>Save your strongest bursts for safe windows instead of casting into dangerous patterns.</li>
  <li>Use Momo and Ink Pet skills when the boss is vulnerable, locked, or standing inside your damage zone.</li>
  <li>Weave heavy attacks once Power of Prayer, lasers, or clone upgrades become your real payoff.</li>
  <li>Do not stand still just because lasers are active.</li>
  <li>Add shield, HP, damage reduction, or healing food before late bosses if your damage is already good.</li>
</ul>

If your boss damage feels low, check whether you are missing ability damage, Ink Relic skill damage, heavy attack scaling, crit, status triggers, or enough curse payoff.

<h2 id="high-difficulty">
  Is Oread good for high difficulty?
</h2>

Yes, Oread can clear high difficulty, but she is not a free win. Her damage can look weak before the right pieces come together, especially against tankier bosses.

On higher difficulty, enemies can hit harder, survive longer, and gain more dangerous patterns. Oread needs:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>enough early damage to avoid slow rooms,</li>
  <li>curse application that stays consistent,</li>
  <li>ability or laser scaling for late bosses,</li>
  <li>defensive support from shield, HP, damage reduction, or food,</li>
  <li>and clean dodging during busy fights.</li>
</ul>

If you want to push Difficulty 7 or Rupture, do not rely on one pretty-looking skill. Build the whole run around Oread's best synergies.

<h2 id="faq">
  Realm of Ink Oread FAQ
</h2>

### How do I unlock Oread in Realm of Ink?

Oread is part of the 1.0 BlazBlue Entropy Effect crossover content. Update the game to version 1.0 and check the Fox Inn / hub form unlocks. If she is not available yet, keep progressing the hub unlock system.

### Is Oread good in Realm of Ink?

Yes. Oread is good when you build around curses, butterflies, lasers, arrays, and ability upgrades. She can feel weak if you do not add enough damage payoff behind the curse setup.

### What is the best Oread build?

The best Oread build is a curse butterfly / laser build. Use Butterfly Roosting Call, curse effects, ability damage, Ink Relic skill damage, laser upgrades, and enough defense to survive high-pressure bosses.

### Should I use Butterfly Roosting Call?

Yes, it is one of the cleanest early choices for Oread. It creates more butterfly hits, and those small butterflies gain damage when curses are stacked.

### Is Six-Line Secret Art good?

Yes, if you can curse enemies consistently. It increases Curse Effects and gives more ability damage based on cursed targets in the room.

### Should I use Demon Puppet Summon?

Use Demon Puppet Summon if you want a pet route. It can be strong, but it pushes the build toward Ink Pet support instead of the cleaner butterfly laser plan.

### Should I pick Spiraling Light Dance Speed or Sigil?

Pick Speed when your build wants faster light attacks, more butterfly frequency, and repeated curse application. Pick Sigil when your Curios reward arrays, zones, or standing in triggered effects.

### Should I take Primordial Ink on Oread?

Take Primordial Ink when your Oread build already has curse, ability, butterfly, laser, or array support. Do not take it randomly if your two-Ink setup is already carrying your run.

### Why does my Oread damage feel low?

Your damage usually feels low because you have curse setup but not enough payoff. Add ability damage, Ink Relic skill damage, heavy attack laser scaling, crit, status effects, or Curios that multiply your main damage source.

### Is Oread good for bosses?

Yes, but boss fights require real scaling. Use curses early, save bursts for safe windows, and do not ignore defense. Book Spirit and other late bosses can punish low-damage Oread builds.

### Can I replay Oread runs for achievements?

Yes. Oread can be used for repeat clears and form-related achievement goals, including Book Spirit clears if that achievement is available in your version or platform. For achievement runs, use a safer Oread build with real boss damage and at least one defensive layer instead of chasing the flashiest butterfly setup.

### Is Oread good on high difficulty?

Yes, but she needs more support on high difficulty. Bring damage scaling, defensive layers, food when hurt, and upgrades that keep her butterflies and lasers relevant.
