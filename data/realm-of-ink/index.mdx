<h2 id="beginner-tips">
  Beginner tips that matter most
</h2>

If you are new, focus on practical decisions first. Use **dash + held heavy attack** when you need a forward lunge-style punish, keep ranged enemies on screen, and do not spend every Ink skill the moment it comes off cooldown.

At Way Stations, keep the decision simple: heal if you are hurt, buy Curios when you need a build-defining effect, and upgrade Ink Gems only when you know the gem will stay in your run.

For the full first-run route, use the <Link href="/realm-of-ink/beginner-guide">Realm of Ink Beginner Guide</Link>.

<h2 id="ink-gems-curios-overview">
  Ink Gems and Curios overview
</h2>

Ink Gems are flexible. Curios are usually harder to undo. That means a strong named Curio can decide which Ink Gem you should take next, while a new Ink Gem can change your Momo form, active skill, and build direction.

For specific reward decisions, use the <Link href="/realm-of-ink/ink-gems-curios-guide">Ink Gems and Curios Guide</Link>. That page covers Burning Ink timing, Venom + Ivy DOT, Tiger Ink crit, Grace cooldown routes, Momo Ink elements, named Curios, cursed Curios, and Primordial Ink.

<h2 id="oread-overview">
  Oread guide overview
</h2>

Oread is the 1.0 BlazBlue Entropy Effect crossover form. Her best route is curse butterfly / laser, built around curse setup, Butterfly Roosting Call, Six-Line Secret Art, Power of Prayer, Primordial upgrades, and enough defense for bosses.

If you want to unlock or build her, use the <Link href="/realm-of-ink/oread-guide">Oread Guide</Link>. It covers unlocks, Speed vs Sigil, Power of Prayer lasers, Book Spirit, achievements, and high difficulty.

<h2 id="final-boss-ending-overview">
  Final boss and true ending overview
</h2>

Book Spirit is the major final story boss route, and the true ending route begins opening after your first Book Spirit clear. Ink God and talisman choices come later depending on your endgame progress.

For boss mechanics, true ending logic, talisman choices, and achievement planning, use the <Link href="/realm-of-ink/final-boss-true-ending">Final Boss and True Ending Guide</Link>.

<h2 id="endless-rupture-overview">
  Endless, Dragon Hair Brush, and Rupture overview
</h2>

Endless unlocks after the true ending and uses completed-run builds through Archive / Sanctum. Rupture is the modifier system with reward milestones, while Dragon Hair Brush is difficulty 7.

For unlock requirements, Rupture rewards, Rupture 50, boss checks, and prep checklists, use the <Link href="/realm-of-ink/endless-rupture-guide">Endless and Rupture Guide</Link>.

<h2 id="faq">
  Realm of Ink FAQ
</h2>

### What is Realm of Ink?

Realm of Ink is an action roguelite with fast combat, Ink Gems, Momo, Curios, Elixirs, Primordial Ink, multiple forms, high difficulty systems, and endgame routes like Book Spirit, Ink God, Endless, and Rupture.

### Is Realm of Ink good for new roguelite players?

Yes, but the reward system can feel confusing at first. Start with the Beginner Guide, learn Ink Gems and Curios, then use simple routes like Red Crit before forcing advanced builds.

### Which Realm of Ink guide should I read first?

Read the Beginner Guide first if you are new. Read Best Builds if your damage falls off. Read Ink Gems and Curios if reward choices confuse you. Read Oread Guide if you want the crossover form. Read Final Boss or Endless / Rupture when you reach endgame.

### Are Curios the same as perks?

Yes. In Realm of Ink 1.0, the current in-game term is Curios. Older guides, comments, and discussions may still call them perks.

### What are the best builds in Realm of Ink?

Strong routes include Red Crit, Venom + Ivy DOT, Violeta Primordial Status / Curse, Oread Curse Butterfly / Laser, Momo / Ink Pet, and Grace / cooldown builds. Use the Best Builds Guide for the full routes.

### How do I unlock Oread?

Oread is part of the 1.0 BlazBlue Entropy Effect crossover content. Update the game to 1.0 and check the Fox Inn / hub form unlocks. If she is not available yet, keep progressing the hub unlock system.

### How do I unlock the true ending?

Defeat Book Spirit once through the Story Relics route. After the first Book Spirit clear, the true ending route starts opening.

### How do I unlock Endless mode?

Endless mode unlocks after you reach the true ending. After that, use the Archive / Sanctum system with completed-run builds.
