<h2 id="how-to-unlock-huntress">
  How to unlock Huntress in Sol Cesto
</h2>

Huntress is one of the simplest character unlocks in the game.

You do **not** need to:

- carry a hidden item
- reroll the shop
- trigger a special biome event
- buy a separate progression node first

The unlock condition is:

### Beat the game once

That is the whole route.

Once you complete a full successful run, Huntress should unlock afterward in the main menu.

<GuideCallout title="Simple rule">
Huntress is a **completion unlock**, not a **hidden unlock**.
</GuideCallout>

---

<h2 id="what-counts-as-finishing-the-game">
  What counts as finishing the game?
</h2>

The safest interpretation is:

**a full successful clear, not just a deep run.**

That distinction matters because some players:

- reach late content
- get close to the end
- assume they were far enough
- then wonder why Huntress did not unlock

If Huntress is not appearing yet, the first thing to ask is:

**Did I actually finish the run?**

If the answer is no, the unlock condition was probably not met.

<h2 id="why-huntress-may-not-be-unlocking">
  Why Huntress may not be unlocking
</h2>

If Huntress is not showing up for you yet, the most likely reasons are:

- you reached late content but did **not** finish the game
- the run ended before true completion
- you assumed upgrades were part of the requirement
- you mixed Huntress up with a more hidden unlock like Lizard

This is one of those cases where the confusion usually comes from **expecting something more complicated** than the game is actually asking for.

<GuideCallout title="Most common mistake">
The most common mistake is thinking that “almost winning” or “getting very deep” is enough.

For Huntress, you should assume the run only counts if you clearly **beat the game**.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="what-huntress-does">
  What Huntress does in Sol Cesto
</h2>

Huntress is an **arrow-based character**.

The important early takeaway is that she is not just another cosmetic unlock. She has a more offensive identity than the basic starter characters.

Current player descriptions point to Huntress being able to fire **up to three arrows**, with the attack outcome rolling into different arrow counts.

That gives her a distinct feel:

- more aggressive
- more attack-focused
- more obviously different from simpler early characters

This page is about the unlock, so the goal here is not to force a half-finished build guide.

The important thing is just knowing that Huntress is a **real gameplay unlock**, not just an extra portrait on the select screen.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How do you unlock Huntress in Sol Cesto?

Beat the game once. That is the core unlock condition.

### Do you need upgrades to unlock Huntress?

No. The unlock is tied to finishing the game, not buying a separate node.

### Do you need a hidden item like the Egg?

No. Huntress is not an item-based unlock.

### Why is Huntress not showing up for me?

The most likely reason is that you did not actually complete a full winning run yet.

### Is Huntress a secret character?

Not in the same way as Lizard. Huntress is better understood as a progression unlock.

### What does Huntress do?

Huntress is an arrow-based character whose attack pattern can roll into multiple arrows, up to three.

### Is Huntress easier to unlock than Lizard?

Yes. Huntress is much simpler because it only requires clearing the game once, while Lizard needs a specific hidden-item run.
