<h2 id="gambit">
  Gambit unlock: why it fails so often
</h2>

Gambit is the perk most likely to confuse players because the wording around buying Epic items can be strict.

If you are trying to unlock Gambit, keep the attempt clean:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Only buy items that clearly satisfy the condition.</li>
  <li>Avoid lower-rarity items even if they look harmless.</li>
  <li>Be careful with potions if you do not know whether they count.</li>
  <li>Be careful with encounter rewards if they add items to your board.</li>
  <li>Do not mix the attempt with a normal “win however possible” run.</li>
</ul>

If Gambit fails, the most likely issue is not your final board. It is something you clicked earlier in the run.

<h2 id="essential-oils">
  Essential Oils unlock: keep the Essence goal simple
</h2>

Essential Oils is easier to understand if you focus on the unlock condition: use Essence to give items new tags.

Do not overcomplicate the run. You do not need to build the perfect Essence board. You need clean chances to use Essence on items and complete the condition.

Use Essence when:

- the item will stay on your board,
- the new tag helps your current plan,
- the run is stable enough to spend time on the unlock,
- the action clearly counts toward the condition.

Avoid Essence detours when:

- your board is already dying,
- the item is temporary,
- you are using Essence only because you want the unlock,
- the tag does not help the next fight.

Good signs:

- your carry already has duplicates,
- the 2-star items are useful by themselves,
- your board is winning or stable,
- the 3-star item will actually carry the run.

Bad signs:

- you are buying weak copies just for the condition,
- you have no real carry,
- you are losing hearts every fight,
- the upgrade target is only a support item.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How do perks work in Oaken Tower?

You select a pool of perks before the run, and one of those selected perks is chosen when the run starts. That means you should build a balanced perk pool, not just pick four greedy options.

### How do I unlock Gambit?

The known condition is to earn 10 trophies while only ever buying Epic items. Keep the run clean and be careful with potions or encounter rewards that might count against the condition.

### Why did Gambit not unlock?

Usually because the condition was stricter than expected. Check whether you bought a lower-rarity item, took a potion, accepted an encounter reward, or otherwise broke the requirement.

### How do I unlock Essential Oils?

Use an Essence to give an item a new tag 3 times. Keep the run stable and use Essence on items that actually stay on your board.

### How do I unlock Merge Perk?

The known condition is to earn the 10th Trophy with a 3-star item 3 times. Do this when a 3-star carry path appears naturally instead of forcing it from nothing.

### What perks should I unlock first?

Prioritize perks that help real runs: Toxic for Poison direction, Flash Sale or shop-value perks for economy, Healing Touch for survival, and Strike for multicast carry setups.

### Is Toxic worth unlocking?

Yes, Toxic is worth unlocking if you like Poison builds or want a clear beginner-friendly direction. It is strongest when your packs and shop can support Poison items.

### Is Essential Oils good?

Essential Oils is flexible support. It is good when the extra gold and Essence choices help your current board, but it should not replace a real carry item.

### Should I chase perk unlocks while trying to climb?

Usually no. Unlock runs and serious win runs have different goals. If the unlock condition forces awkward choices, do it separately from serious 10-win or ranked attempts.
