<h2 id="which-guide-should-you-read-first">
  Which guide should you read first
</h2>

### Read the Beginner Guide first if:

- you have not committed to a build yet
- you are still choosing class / skill direction
- you want the least confusing possible start

### Read the Fireball Build Guide first if:

- you already know you want a practical ranged starter
- you want the cleanest early leveling build
- you want a build page, not a general intro page

### Read the Crafting Guide first if:

- you do not understand item rarity, sockets, or skill upgrades
- you are scared of wasting materials
- you want alpha / omega affixes and brick risk explained

### Read the Inventory Guide first if:

- your bags are constantly full
- you do not know what to stash or sell
- loot pressure is slowing down your runs
