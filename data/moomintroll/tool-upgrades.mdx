<h2 id="where-is-too-ticky">
  Where is Too-ticky?
</h2>

Too-ticky is your main upgrade helper. She is usually found at or near the Bathing House on the ice, which you reach from the early outside route after leaving Moominhouse.

If a quest temporarily moves her or points you somewhere else, follow the current quest marker first. For upgrades, return to Too-ticky when you have an item that sounds useful but does not work on its own.

You should go back to Too-ticky when you find:

- sturdy yarn
- lamp oil
- whetstone
- scrap metal

<GuideCallout title="Too-ticky tip">
When in doubt, bring strange materials to Too-ticky. She is the closest thing Moomintroll Winter's Warmth has to an upgrade station.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="how-to-get-matches">
  How to get matches
</h2>

Matches are the first tool in the game. You find them in Moomintroll's bedroom during the opening, before leaving the room.

Use matches to:

- light lamps
- explore dark rooms
- light candles
- make cave routes safer
- complete some winter being objectives

Later, the lamp oil upgrade makes your matches last longer.

**Vase pieces this helps with:** #01, #02, #08, #09, #25.  
For the full collectible route, use the <Link href="/moomintroll/vase-piece-locations">All 25 Vase Piece Locations guide</Link>.

<h2 id="how-to-get-the-scarf">
  How to get the scarf
</h2>

Too-ticky gives you the scarf after the first storm sequence outside Moominhouse.

The scarf is a story progression item rather than an active tool. You do not equip it to solve puzzles. Instead, it marks the point where Moomintroll can safely continue outdoor exploration after the dangerous opening storm.

Before this point, the storm sequence limits where you can go. After receiving the scarf, the game opens the early outside route toward the Bathing House, crossroads, and later the bonfire site.

**Vase pieces this helps with:** It does not directly unlock a vase piece, but it opens the outdoor route toward #03 and #04.

<h2 id="how-to-get-mittens">
  How to get the mittens
</h2>

You get the mittens inside the Bathing House.

To get them:

1. follow Too-ticky into the Bathing House
2. search the room for the mittens
3. chase the ancestor after it runs away with them
4. pick up the mittens when the ancestor drops them
5. return to Too-ticky
6. practice throwing snowballs at icicles

Mittens unlock snowballs, which are used constantly for the rest of the game.

You can use snowballs to:

- knock down icicles
- create snow bridges
- hit birds carrying items
- fight Little My
- solve small route puzzles

**Vase pieces this unlocks:** #03, #04, #06, #16.  
For bird-held vase pieces, use the <Link href="/moomintroll/vase-piece-locations">vase piece locations guide</Link>.

<h2 id="how-to-get-reinforced-mittens">
  How to get reinforced mittens
</h2>

Reinforced mittens are the first major tool upgrade.

The Hemulen gives you sturdy yarn after the Moominhouse guest chain progresses. Bring the yarn to Too-ticky, and she will reinforce your mittens.

Reinforced mittens let you roll large snowballs. These are used for:

- large snowball bridges
- snow platforms
- pond route puzzles
- snow fort / friend quest progress
- some late cleanup routes

If you see a big snow pile that looks like it should become a huge snowball, you probably need reinforced mittens first.

**Vase pieces this unlocks:** #24.

<GuideCallout title="Why can't I roll this snowball?">
If Moomintroll can throw normal snowballs but cannot push a large one, you probably need reinforced mittens. Get sturdy yarn from the Hemulen and bring it to Too-ticky.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="how-to-get-dash">
  How to get dash
</h2>

You learn dash from the Joxter during the camp meal route.

The Joxter asks for help around his camp, including food, bark, snow cleanup, and a cooking pot. During this route, he teaches Moomintroll how to move through snow clumps.

Dash lets you pass through certain snow-blocked routes that cannot be cleared with the shovel.

Use dash for:

- Joxter camp side paths
- snow clump shortcuts
- route cleanup after the camp sequence

**Vase pieces this unlocks:** #10, #11.

<h2 id="where-to-find-the-whetstone">
  Where to find the whetstone
</h2>

The whetstone comes from Fillyjonk's children before the herb cave route.

After Sorry-oo gets sick, the group points you toward a cave where herbs can grow during winter. Before you leave, Fillyjonk's children give you a whetstone and suggest sharpening the axe.

Bring the whetstone to Too-ticky. She can sharpen Moominpappa's axe.

**Vase pieces this unlocks after upgrading:** #21, #22, #23.

<h2 id="how-to-get-the-upgraded-shovel">
  How to get the upgraded shovel
</h2>

The upgraded shovel comes from scrap metal.

This is a late cleanup upgrade, so do not worry about it during the opening or early Fillyjonk route. Continue the story until you have broader access to blocked paths, then look for scrap metal during cleanup and bring it to Too-ticky.

The upgraded shovel is less central than the axe or sharp axe, but it helps with late snow cleanup before the Great Winter Bonfire.

Use it when you are:

- clearing remaining snow piles
- searching for leftover firewood
- checking optional paths
- finishing completion cleanup

**Vase pieces this helps with:** late cleanup pieces that involve snow piles, especially if you are checking remaining forest or side routes.

<GuideCallout title="Scrap metal note">
If you have not found scrap metal yet, you are probably not far enough into late cleanup. Focus on axe, sharp axe, and Great Winter Bonfire progress first.
</GuideCallout>

</h2>

  caption="Moomintroll Winter's Warmth obstacles and the tools that solve them."

/>

When you are stuck, identify the obstacle instead of wandering the whole map.

- ice hanging above a gap usually means snowball
- deep snow means shovel
- logs and dead trees mean axe
- stronger ice means sharp axe
- snow clumps mean dash
- huge snowball puzzles mean reinforced mittens

<h2 id="why-tool-not-working">
  Why is my tool not working?
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Common tool problems and how to fix them."
  columns={troubleshootingColumns}
  rows={troubleshootingRows}
/>

Most tool problems happen because two obstacles look similar but require different solutions.

The common example is ice. Some ice-related obstacles are solved with snowballs, while stronger ice blocks require the sharp axe. If the normal axe does nothing, that is usually not a bug. It means you need the whetstone upgrade.

<h2 id="cleanup-after-upgrades">
  Best cleanup route after each upgrade
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Where to return after each major tool unlock."
  columns={cleanupColumns}
  rows={cleanupRows}
/>

Every new tool should trigger a short cleanup loop.

The best pattern is:

1. unlock the tool
2. continue the current story objective
3. return to nearby blocked routes
4. collect firewood, vase pieces, notes, or side quest rewards
5. then move back to the Great Winter Bonfire objective

This is especially important after the axe and sharp axe, because many earlier blocked paths become useful again.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many tools are in Moomintroll Winter's Warmth?

The main progression tools and abilities are matches, scarf, mittens, shovel, reinforced mittens, dash, axe, longer-lasting matches, sharp axe, and upgraded shovel.

### Where is Too-ticky?

Too-ticky is usually at or near the Bathing House on the ice, reached from the early outside route after leaving Moominhouse. Return to her when you find upgrade items such as sturdy yarn, lamp oil, whetstone, or scrap metal.

### How do I get the shovel?

Beat Little My in the first snowball fight near Moominhouse. After you win, you get Moominmamma's shovel.

### How do I get the axe?

You get the axe during the musician / lunchbox route. After a tree blocks the path, the musician gives you Moominpappa's axe.

### Why can't my axe break ice?

You probably need the sharp axe. Get the whetstone from Fillyjonk's children and bring it to Too-ticky.

### Where is the lamp oil?

Lamp oil is found in the cave. Bring it to Too-ticky to upgrade your matches.

### What does lamp oil do?

Lamp oil lets Too-ticky make your matches last longer.

### Where is the whetstone?

The whetstone is given by Fillyjonk's children before the herb cave route.

### How do I sharpen the axe?

Bring the whetstone to Too-ticky. She will sharpen Moominpappa's axe into the sharp axe.

### How do I roll large snowballs?

You need reinforced mittens. Get sturdy yarn from the Hemulen and bring it to Too-ticky.

### What should I do after getting a new tool?

Return to earlier blocked routes. Many vase pieces, firewood piles, notes, winter being quests, and side paths are locked behind tools.
