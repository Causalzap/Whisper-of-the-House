<h2 id="how-to-collect-firewood">
  How to collect 125 firewood
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="How to collect 125 firewood for the Great Winter Bonfire."
  columns={firewoodPlanColumns}
  rows={firewoodPlanRows}
/>

Firewood comes from many small sources, but the important thing is tool timing. You are not expected to reach 125 firewood the moment the bonfire quest appears.

If you are short, check your newest tool first:

- shovel opens snow pile cleanup
- axe opens the biggest dead tree cleanup routes
- sharp axe opens late cave and stronger ice routes
- side quests can provide the final top-up

<h2 id="how-to-find-the-musician">
  How to find the musician
</h2>

The musician route begins after the Moominhouse guest chain brings music into the story.

The musician can help with the Great Winter Bonfire, but he needs provisions first. Follow his lunchbox quest: the bird takes the lunchbox toward the Bathing House / beach route, and you need to recover it before the musician can continue.

<h2 id="how-to-get-little-my-to-help">
  How to get Little My to help
</h2>

Little My appears early, but her first snowball fight is mainly about the shovel.

For the bonfire, the important part comes later around the pond / slide route. After the Joxter camp and reinforced mittens progress, you find Little My again. She challenges you to more snowball fights before agreeing to help.

For detailed fight strategy, use the <Link href="/moomintroll/little-my-snowball-fight">Little My Snowball Fight Guide</Link>.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What does the Great Winter Bonfire require?

The Great Winter Bonfire requires 125 firewood, five guests, one musician, and three winter being tasks.

### Where does the Great Winter Bonfire quest start?

It starts at the old bonfire site after Too-ticky asks you to meet her east of Moominhouse.

### How do I get enough firewood?

Follow the 125 firewood table above: collect early loose wood first, then use the shovel, axe, sharp axe, and side quests for the remaining cleanup.

### Why can I not light the Great Winter Bonfire yet?

You are probably missing one tracked requirement: firewood, guests, musician progress, or winter being progress. Use the troubleshooting table above to find the blocker.

### How do I invite guests?

Finish the character's problem, then return for the follow-up conversation. Some guest routes do not count immediately after the first interaction.

### Who is the musician?

The musician is recruited through the music / lunchbox route. Follow the bird toward the Bathing House / beach route and recover the lunchbox.

### Do I need Little My for the bonfire?

Yes. The first Little My fight gives the shovel, but the later pond fights move her bonfire involvement forward.

### How many winter beings do I need to help?

The main bonfire objective asks you to help three winter beings.

### What do I do with Sorry-oo?

Follow the forest and rescue route, help him through the wind section, then continue until you can get the whetstone, sharpen the axe, enter the herb cave, and gather herbs.

### Should I collect all vase pieces before lighting the bonfire?

Yes, that is the safest route if you care about completion.

### Does lighting the Great Winter Bonfire start the ending?

It starts the celebration and pushes the story toward the ending stretch, so finish important cleanup first.
