</h2>

You usually do not need to route these separately. If one is missing, continue the story or check whether the related character route is unfinished.

<h2 id="collectible-achievements">
  Collectible achievements
</h2>

The main collectible achievements are:

- **Moominmamma's Favorite** — fix the vase
- **You've Got Mail!** — read or find every letter
- **Friend of Winter** — help every winter being

The vase achievement is the biggest collectible check because there are 25 vase pieces, and several are not simple ground pickups.

<h2 id="letters-achievement">

</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Letters and notes to check for You've Got Mail."
  columns={letterColumns}
  rows={letterRows}

**You've Got Mail!** is one of the most likely achievements to block 100% because the letter chain sends you between areas instead of keeping everything in one route.

Use the table above as your route-order checklist. Start at the mailbox, follow each clue as it points to the next area, and return to the mailbox when the chain loops back.

<GuideCallout title="Letter cleanup tip">
If you are missing You've Got Mail!, do not search randomly. Start at the mailbox, then follow the clue chain through water, mountain, icy pond, forest, cave depths, and back to the mailbox.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="tool-upgrade-achievement">
  Upgrades: improve every tool
</h2>

**Upgrades!** requires you to improve all tools.

The upgrades to check include:

- reinforced mittens from sturdy yarn
- longer-lasting matches from lamp oil
- sharp axe from whetstone
- upgraded shovel from scrap metal

If this achievement is missing near the end, return to Too-ticky and check whether you still have an upgrade material in your inventory.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are in Moomintroll Winter's Warmth?

There are 23 achievements in Moomintroll Winter's Warmth.

### Are any achievements missable?

Most achievements are safe if you do completion cleanup before the ending stretch, but Great Shot, Friend of Winter, You've Got Mail!, Spelunker, All Fired Up, Upgrades!, and Moominmamma's Favorite are the easiest to overlook.

### What is the hardest achievement?

The hardest achievement is likely **Snufkin Would Be Proud**, because it requires every other achievement. The most annoying individual check is usually **Great Shot**, because you need to snowball every valid character or character group.

### How do I unlock Moominmamma's Favorite?

Collect all 25 vase pieces, return to the vase objective, and fix Moominmamma's vase.

### How do I unlock Friend of Winter?

Help every winter being. The Great Winter Bonfire only needs three winter being tasks, but this achievement requires full winter being cleanup.

### How do I unlock You've Got Mail?

Follow the full letter chain: mailbox, water / bottle route, mountain note, icy pond note, forest note, cave depths note, return mailbox, and late letter follow-ups.

### How do I unlock Upgrades?

Improve every tool by bringing upgrade materials to Too-ticky, including sturdy yarn, lamp oil, whetstone, and scrap metal.

### How do I unlock Spelunker?

Light every candle in the cave and underground routes. Recheck side rooms, bridge exits, ice-block paths, and cave exits if it does not unlock.

### How do I unlock All Fired Up?

Light every bonfire, including smaller winter being bonfires and fire spots, not only the Great Winter Bonfire.

### How do I unlock Great Shot?

Throw a snowball at every valid character or character group. Use the snowball target checklist above and do a final sweep during the Great Winter Bonfire celebration.

### What does Snufkin Would Be Proud require?

Snufkin Would Be Proud requires completing all other achievements.

### What should I check if I am missing one final achievement?

Check snowball targets, letters, winter beings, cave candles, small bonfires, tool upgrades, and vase piece side quest rewards first.
