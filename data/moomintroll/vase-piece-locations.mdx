<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**There are 25 vase pieces in Moomintroll Winter's Warmth. The easiest way to collect them all is to follow the main story, unlock the key tools, then do final cleanup before lighting the Great Winter Bonfire.**

The most common pieces to miss are:

- bird-held vase pieces
- winter being reward pieces
- sharp axe cave pieces
- mountain pieces during the Little My chase
- late side quest rewards such as the snow fort and diary quest

Use the checklist if you need a specific number. Use the tool index if you are asking, **"I just got the axe, sharp axe, dash, or reinforced mittens — which pieces can I collect now?"**

<h2 id="bird-vase-pieces">
  Bird-held vase pieces
</h2>

Bird-held vase pieces are easy to miss because they do not behave like normal collectibles. Instead of simply picking up a sparkling item, you need to find the bird and hit it with a snowball.

The clearest bird-held vase piece in this guide is:

- **Vase Piece #16** in the forest route

To handle bird pieces:

1. equip the mittens
2. aim a snowball at the bird
3. hit it once to make the item drop
4. follow the bird if it moves to another perch
5. pick up the dropped vase piece before leaving the area

<GuideCallout title="Bird piece tip">
If a bird flies away, keep following the nearby route instead of assuming the piece is gone. Birds usually move between nearby perches, and the item can still be recovered by hitting the bird with a snowball.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="side-quest-vase-pieces">
  Side quest reward vase pieces
</h2>

The most important non-obvious vase pieces are the reward pieces.

Watch especially for:

- **Vase Piece #12** from a winter being quest
- **Vase Piece #24** from the snow fort / friend quest
- **Vase Piece #25** from the winter being diary quest

These are easy to miss because they do not always appear as normal map pickups. You may need to finish the objective, then return to the winter being or quest-giver to receive the piece.

If your counter is stuck at 24/25, check side quest rewards before rechecking every snow pile.

<h2 id="what-happens-when-you-collect-all-vase-pieces">
  What happens when you collect all 25 vase pieces?
</h2>

After collecting all 25 vase pieces, return to the broken vase objective in Moominhouse and complete the side quest before lighting the Great Winter Bonfire.

This works as a natural completion checkpoint. If you have all 25 pieces, you have likely cleared the major side routes, caves, winter being rewards, bird drops, and tool-gated cleanup paths.

At this point, you should also check:

- tool upgrades
- winter being quests
- firewood count
- side quest rewards
- any remaining Steam achievements

<GuideCallout title="Best cleanup point">
The best time to finish the vase is after getting the sharp axe and reinforced mittens, but before lighting the Great Winter Bonfire.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many vase pieces are in Moomintroll Winter's Warmth?

There are 25 vase pieces in Moomintroll Winter's Warmth.

### Where does the broken vase quest start?

The broken vase quest starts in Moominhouse early in the story after Moominmamma's vase is knocked over.

### Are all vase pieces found on the ground?

No. Some are ground pickups, but others are bird drops, cave pickups, tool-gated pieces, or side quest rewards.

### Which vase pieces are easiest to miss?

The easiest pieces to miss are bird-held pieces, winter being reward pieces, snow fort rewards, and sharp axe cave pieces.

### What should I check if I am missing one vase piece?

Check side quest rewards first, especially the diary quest, snow fort / friend quest, and winter being rewards. Then recheck bird drops, the forest route, and the sharp axe cave route.

### Do birds with vase pieces come back?

If a bird moves, follow the nearby route and look for its next perch. Bird-held items are recovered by hitting the bird with a snowball.

### Do I need the axe for every vase piece?

You do not need the axe for every piece, but several mid-to-late cleanup pieces require the axe or sharp axe.

### Do I need the sharp axe?

Yes. The sharp axe is needed for the herb cave and late ice cleanup pieces.

### Can I collect vase pieces after lighting the Great Winter Bonfire?

The safest route is to collect all 25 before lighting the Great Winter Bonfire. Treat the bonfire as your final cleanup checkpoint.

### Is there an achievement for collecting all vase pieces?

This guide treats the vase as a completion objective. Check your achievement list after turning in all 25 pieces, then use the achievements guide for any remaining cleanup.
