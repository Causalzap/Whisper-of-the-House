  All side quests checklist
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Important side quests and quest chains in Moomintroll Winter's Warmth."
  columns={sideQuestColumns}
  rows={sideQuestRows}
/>

Moomintroll Winter's Warmth does not always separate side quests from main progress cleanly. Some objectives behave like optional tasks, but still help with guests, winter beings, firewood, tools, or collectibles.

Use the **Required / Optional** column to decide what matters for your current run. If you only want to finish the main story, prioritize bonfire-required and guest-progress routes. If you want completion, also finish reward quests and optional winter being cleanup.

<GuideCallout title="Side quest rule">
Always check for a follow-up after solving a side quest. Several rewards and objective updates happen after the final conversation, not at the moment you solve the puzzle.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="diary-quest">
  Diary quest
</h2>

The diary quest begins when a winter being says a bird took its bad book or secret book.

The route is:

1. talk to the winter being
2. learn that a bird took the diary
3. follow the bird route
4. recover the mysterious diary
5. return to the winter being
6. finish the diary follow-up
7. collect the reward

<h2 id="snow-fort-friend-quest">
  Snow fort / friend quest
</h2>

The snow fort / friend quest happens later, after you have reinforced mittens and can roll large snowballs.

The route is:

1. find the winter beings near the snow fort
2. listen to their friendship problem
3. roll the missing large snowballs into place
4. light the fort or final interaction if needed
5. talk to them again
6. collect the reward

<h2 id="fillyjonk-children-quest">

<h2 id="misabel-briefcase-and-lights">
  Misabel briefcase and lights
</h2>

Misabel's route starts outside Moominhouse after Fillyjonk and her children come inside.

The route is:

1. hear crying outside Moominhouse
2. talk to Misabel
3. search the nearby snow for her briefcase
4. return the briefcase and bring her inside
5. relight the lamps after she turns the house dark
6. listen for crying after the lights are restored
7. check the drawing room
8. find Misabel hiding in the closet
9. talk to her about the Great Winter Bonfire
10. continue into the silver tray / Little My route

The easiest place to get stuck is after relighting the house. Once the lights are back on, do not keep searching random rooms. Follow the crying sound to the drawing room and check the closet.

This quest chain matters because it pushes the Moominhouse guest route forward and leads into later objectives.

<GuideCallout title="Misabel tip">
If Misabel seems missing after the lights are restored, check the drawing room closet. The crying sound is the clue.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="little-my-side-quest-chain">
  Little My side quest chain
</h2>

Little My has two important quest moments.

The first is the snowball fight for Moominmamma's shovel. This opens the route to Fillyjonk and several snow cleanup areas.

The later part is the pond / slide route. These rematches help move Little My's bonfire involvement forward.

You should complete Little My's route if:

- you need the shovel
- deep snow blocks your progress
- the bonfire still needs guest or character progress
- you are cleaning up shovel-gated vase pieces or firewood

For fight strategy, use the <Link href="/moomintroll/little-my-snowball-fight">Little My Snowball Fight Guide</Link>.

---

<h2 id="sorry-oo-quest-chain">
  Sorry-oo quest chain
</h2>

Sorry-oo's route appears later in the family / forest section and becomes important for late-game progression.

The route is:

1. progress the Fillyjonk family and children route
2. find Sorry-oo during the forest / rescue chain
3. guide or carry Sorry-oo through the dangerous wind route
4. continue when Sorry-oo becomes sick
5. receive the herb cave objective
6. get the whetstone from Fillyjonk's children
7. sharpen the axe with Too-ticky
8. enter the herb cave and gather herbs

</h2>

<GuideTable

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### Are side quests required in Moomintroll Winter's Warmth?

Some are required for main progress, especially guest routes, winter being tasks, Little My's route, the musician route, and Sorry-oo's route. Others are optional but useful for vase pieces, firewood, and completion.

### Which side quests can I skip?

For a story-focused route, you can skip most completion-only reward quests. Do not skip bonfire-required winter being tasks, guest routes, the musician lunchbox route, Little My's later route, or Sorry-oo's late route.

### How many winter beings do I need to help?

The Great Winter Bonfire objective asks for three winter being tasks, but helping extra winter beings can still be useful for rewards and cleanup.

### Which side quests reward vase pieces?

The diary quest, snow fort / friend quest, and some winter being reward quests can give vase pieces. If you are missing a vase piece, check quest rewards before rechecking every map route.

### Why did my side quest not complete?

Check the side quest troubleshooting section above. Most blockers are missing tools, unfinished follow-ups, or longer quest chains.

### How do I finish the diary quest?

Recover the diary from the bird route, return it to the winter being, then finish the final diary interaction.

### How do I complete the snow fort quest?

You need reinforced mittens to roll large snowballs. Place the missing snowballs, finish the light interaction if needed, then collect the reward.

### Where is Misabel hiding?

After you return her briefcase and relight the house, follow the crying sound to the drawing room and check the closet.

### Is Little My's first fight a side quest?

It works like a progression quest. The first fight gives you the shovel, while the later pond rematches help move her bonfire involvement forward.

### What do I do with Sorry-oo?

Use the Sorry-oo quest chain section above. The route connects forest rescue, the wind section, whetstone, sharp axe, herb cave, and herbs.

### Should I finish side quests before lighting the Great Winter Bonfire?

Yes, especially if you care about completion. Finish vase piece rewards, winter being tasks, tool-gated routes, and guest-chain follow-ups first.

### What should I check if I am stuck at the end?

Use the quick diagnosis table above. Missing endgame progress is usually tied to reward quests, winter beings, firewood cleanup, guest follow-ups, Little My, or Sorry-oo.
