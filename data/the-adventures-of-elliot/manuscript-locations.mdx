<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
There are **31 manuscripts** in **The Adventures of Elliot: The Millennium Tales**. Collecting all of them unlocks **History's Tapestry**.

Manuscripts are tracked on the **bookshelf on the second floor of the orphanage in the Kingdom of Huther**. The numbers in this guide follow the same **left-to-right, top-to-bottom bookshelf order**, so if slot 9 is empty, check **No. 9** in the table below.

Manuscripts are mainly for lore, worldbuilding, and completion. They do **not** give a direct gameplay upgrade, so skip them unless you want the story background or the History's Tapestry achievement / trophy. They are not permanently missable, but **3 manuscripts only appear after the True Ending final boss**. 
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="manuscript-locations-guide">
  The Adventures of Elliot manuscript locations guide
</h2>

This **The Adventures of Elliot manuscript locations guide** is built for cleanup, not just a raw title list. Use it to match your orphanage bookshelf, find the exact manuscript you are missing, and avoid wasting time before the final epilogue manuscripts are available.

Unlike cats and Shards of Life, manuscripts are easier to lose track of because they do not all sit in one obvious reward chain. They appear as notes, books, sparkly spots, shelves, desks, dungeon pickups, and late story objects.

<h2 id="historys-tapestry-not-unlocking">
  History's Tapestry not unlocking
</h2>

If **History's Tapestry** is not unlocking, first check your count. The most common situation is being stuck at **28 / 31** before the epilogue.

That does not automatically mean you missed something. Three manuscripts only appear after the True Ending final boss:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li><strong>Gogyo's Diary</strong> — Kaifried's Manse, second floor.</li>
  <li><strong>Marnie's Diary</strong> — Castle Huther, Euygene's study.</li>
  <li><strong>Diona's Diary</strong> — Castle Huther, Princess Heuria's room.</li>
</ul>

If you already finished the True Ending and still do not have the achievement, compare your orphanage bookshelf order against the full list below.

<h2 id="epilogue-manuscripts">
  Epilogue manuscripts: Gogyo's Diary, Marnie's Diary, and Diona's Diary
</h2>

The final three manuscripts are not normal mid-game collectibles. They appear after the **True Ending final boss** when you load the clear save.

Do not waste time searching for these before the epilogue:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li><strong>Gogyo's Diary</strong> — desk on the second floor of Kaifried's Manse.</li>
  <li><strong>Marnie's Diary</strong> — floor near the southwest bookshelf in Euygene's study.</li>
  <li><strong>Diona's Diary</strong> — southwest corner of Princess Heuria's room.</li>
</ul>

If your count is **28 / 31** before the final sequence, the missing three may simply be these epilogue manuscripts.

<h2 id="northern-tower-manuscripts">
  Northern Tower manuscripts: do not mix up the tower checks
</h2>

Northern Tower is a common source of missing-manuscript confusion because it has multiple checks across different ages and route states.

The key tower manuscripts are:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li><strong>Report on Newly Discovered Epigraph</strong> — Age of Safekeeping, Northern Tower Sublevel Three.</li>
  <li><strong>Kaifried's Journal</strong> — Age of Reconstruction, Northern Tower Sublevel Two.</li>
  <li><strong>Log of a Former Forthewor Researcher</strong> — Age of Safekeeping, Northern Tower Sublevel One, True Ending path.</li>
</ul>

If one tower manuscript is missing, confirm the **age**, **sublevel**, and **route state** before searching the same room again.

<h2 id="mount-phoenix-manuscripts">
  Mount Phoenix manuscripts: check the correct age and level
</h2>

Mount Phoenix also has several manuscripts, and they are not all in the same age.

Check these separately:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li><strong>History of the Myu, Vol. I</strong> — Age of Reconstruction, Mount Phoenix after the Salathmadra boss.</li>
  <li><strong>History of the Myu, Vol. II</strong> — Age of Budding, Mount Phoenix Sublevel One.</li>
  <li><strong>The Tale of the Grey Hero</strong> — Age of Safekeeping, Mount Phoenix Third Level.</li>
  <li><strong>A Traveler's Notes on the Fire</strong> — Age of Magic, Mount Phoenix First Level.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="faq">
  The Adventures of Elliot manuscript locations FAQ
</h2>

### How many manuscripts are in The Adventures of Elliot?

There are **31 manuscripts** in The Adventures of Elliot: The Millennium Tales.

### What do manuscripts unlock?

Collecting every manuscript unlocks the **History's Tapestry** achievement or trophy.

### Do manuscripts give gameplay upgrades?

No. Manuscripts mainly provide lore, worldbuilding, character background, and completion progress. They do not give direct stat boosts, combat upgrades, or exploration abilities.

### Where can I check my collected manuscripts?

Check the bookshelf on the second floor of the orphanage in the Kingdom of Huther.

### Does this guide follow the orphanage bookshelf order?

Yes. The numbers in the full table follow the same left-to-right, top-to-bottom order as the orphanage bookshelf. Match an empty bookshelf slot to the same number in this guide.

### Are manuscripts missable?

Manuscripts are not permanently missable, but three only appear after the True Ending final boss. Before the epilogue, you should expect to stop at 28 / 31 if everything else is collected.

### Why am I stuck at 28 / 31 manuscripts?

You are probably missing the three epilogue manuscripts: Gogyo's Diary, Marnie's Diary, and Diona's Diary. Finish the True Ending, load the clear save, then collect them.

### Why is History's Tapestry not unlocking?

Compare your orphanage bookshelf order against the full list. The usual causes are missing an epilogue diary, a Northern Tower manuscript, a Mount Phoenix manuscript, or an ability-gated dungeon manuscript.

### Which manuscripts are after the True Ending final boss?

The epilogue manuscripts are **Gogyo's Diary**, **Marnie's Diary**, and **Diona's Diary**.

### Which manuscript should I check first if one is missing?

Start with the epilogue diaries, Northern Tower manuscripts, Mount Phoenix manuscripts, A Traveler's Notes on the Ice, A Traveler's Notes on the Sand, and Memorandum on Permilleshrooms.
