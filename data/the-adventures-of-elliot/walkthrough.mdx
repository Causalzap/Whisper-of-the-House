<h2 id="how-to-use-this-route">
  How to use this route planner
</h2>

The safest way to play for 100% is not to full-clear every area the first time you see it. The game repeatedly shows rewards before you have the right tool, spell, route, shortcut, trail, or age state.

Use this rhythm:

<ol className="my-6 list-decimal space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Follow the current story objective until the next major route opens.</li>
  <li>Open the map and check obvious unopened chest or Shrine markers.</li>
  <li>If a marker is blocked, do not force it. Match it to the requirement later.</li>
  <li>Before ending branches or final-route progress, stop and clean up.</li>
  <li>After the True Ending, load the clear save for epilogue-only cleanup.</li>
</ol>

This keeps the walkthrough useful without turning it into a giant duplicate of the cat, Shard, and manuscript pages.

### Where should I save for endings?

Keep a clean save before the Northern Tower bottom route tied to The Sorcerer / curse branch, and another clean save before the final True Ending route.

### When should I do 100% cleanup?

Do light tracking from the start, but do major cleanup after the route opens more tools, spells, shortcuts, Monster Trails, and ages. Do final cleanup before the True Ending route, then epilogue-only cleanup after the clear save.

### Should I 100% every area the first time I visit?

No. Many chests, Shrines, and routes require later access. Open reachable rewards, mark blocked ones, and return later.

### What should I use the map for?

Use the map to find unopened chest and Shrine markers. If a marker looks unreachable, use the relevant guide to solve the route requirement.

### Are Shards of Life missable?

No. Shards of Life are not permanently missable. Every 4 Shards gives one max HP increase, so collect reachable Shards early and clean up blocked ones later.

### When should I start cat cleanup?

Start tracking cats after cat collecting is introduced, but save full 50-cat cleanup for later when more ages, routes, and Cat Needle access are available.

### When should I finish manuscripts?

Clean up most manuscripts before the final route. If you have 28 / 31 before the True Ending final boss, the last three are epilogue diaries after the clear save.

### What is the Moon Mirror route?

Moon Mirror is a main-story route after the Golden Bell / time travel loop. It pushes the story toward the curse route and the Three Sages / Mystic Seal progression.

### What is the True Ending route?

The True Ending route continues after the curse route into Mew cooperation, Cradle the Phoenix, Mount Phoenix, and Demise.

Because its job is different. It gives 100% timing, ending-save planning, blocked-route decisions, and final cleanup order instead of duplicating every collectible page.
