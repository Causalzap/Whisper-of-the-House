<h2 id="how-shards-of-life-work">
  How Shards of Life work
</h2>

Shards of Life are HP fragments. Collect **4 Shards of Life** to gain one max HP increase. A complete 60-Shard cleanup gives **15 max HP upgrades**, which makes late dungeons, Shrine routes, hazards, and bosses much safer.

Shards are **not permanently missable**, so you do not need to panic if a route is blocked. Mark the chest or Shrine on the map, continue until you get the needed route access, then return later.

<h2 id="shards-shrines-and-secret-routes">
  Shards of Life vs Shrines of Life vs Secret Shortcuts
</h2>

Do not mix these systems together.

A **Shard of Life** is the HP fragment. A **Shrine of Life** is a challenge location that can reward a Shard. A **Secret Shortcut** or **Monster Trail** is a route that can lead you to a chest, Shrine, or isolated map section.

The map can show the chest or Shrine, but it may not make the access route obvious. That is where Secret Shortcut numbers, Monster Trail exits, cave entrances, and sublevel directions matter.

<h2 id="faq">
  The Adventures of Elliot Shards of Life FAQ
</h2>

### How many Shards of Life are in The Adventures of Elliot?

There are **60 Shards of Life** in The Adventures of Elliot: The Millennium Tales.

### Are Shards of Life missable?

No. Shards of Life are not permanently missable. If a route is blocked, continue progressing, then return later with the required ability, spell, shortcut, trail access, or age state.

### Does the map show Shards of Life?

The map can show unopened chests and Shrine of Life locations. Use the map first, then use this guide when the marker is visible but the route is unclear.

### How many Shards of Life are in each age?

There are **16** in the Age of Safekeeping, **18** in the Age of Reconstruction, **13** in the Age of Magic, and **13** in the Age of Budding.

### How many Shards of Life do I need for one HP upgrade?

You need **4 Shards of Life** for one max HP increase.

### How many HP upgrades do all Shards give?

All 60 Shards give **15 max HP upgrades**.

### Are Shards of Life and Shrines of Life the same thing?

No. Shards of Life are HP fragments. Shrines of Life are challenge locations that can reward Shards.

### Do 4 Shards of Life unlock a Shrine of Life?

No. Four Shards of Life give a max HP increase. A Shrine of Life is a separate map location, not something unlocked by collecting 4 Shards.

### Why does my map say Hucarea instead of Fields of Flon?

Eastern / Western Fields of Flon may appear as Eastern / Western Hucarea before the area renaming. Use the east / west position to match the route.

### What should I check first if I am missing one Shard?

Check map markers first, then Shrines of Life, Secret Shortcuts, Monster Trails, dive routes, Water Ruins, Dragonpillar, Mount Phoenix, and ruins sublevels.

### Why can I see a Shard chest but not reach it?

The chest may require bombs, dive, swimming, Heavy Hammer, spear, Vacuum, Warp, Ignite, a key, a Secret Shortcut, a Monster Trail, or a different age.

### Which age has the most Shards of Life?

The Age of Reconstruction has the most Shards of Life, with **18** total.

### Are Shards of Life worth collecting early?

Yes. Since every 4 Shards increase max HP, collecting reachable Shards early makes combat, hazards, Shrines, and bosses easier.

### Is this checklist in story order?

No. It is organized by age for cleanup. Use it to verify which Shards you collected in each age, then check requirements for any missing route.
