<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**Shards of Life** are health-upgrade fragments in **The Adventures of Elliot: The Millennium Tales**. Collect **4 Shards of Life** to gain a max HP increase, and track each Shard by its route clue instead of only by the final reward popup.

Do not confuse Shards of Life with **Shrines of Life**. A Shrine of Life is a separate challenge-style map location with combat, puzzles, and rewards. Also watch for **Secret Treasure Routes**: cracked walls can be opened with bombs and may lead into hidden treasure areas. 
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="shards-of-life-locations">
  The Adventures of Elliot Shards of Life locations
</h2>

Shards of Life are some of the most valuable collectibles in **The Adventures of Elliot: The Millennium Tales**. They are not just completion filler. Four Shards of Life give Elliot a max HP increase, which makes caves, hazard rooms, Shrines, trials, and boss fights more forgiving.

This page focuses on confirmed Shard routes and route clues. It avoids repeating the same reward popup for every entry, because the route clue is what helps you actually find the Shard again later.

<h3 id="bomb-rock-cracked-wall-and-secret-treasure-routes">
  Bomb rocks, cracked walls, and Secret Treasure Routes
</h3>

Bomb rocks and cracked walls are not the same route clue.

A **bomb rock** usually blocks a visible path or reward route. A **cracked wall** can be opened with bombs and may lead into a **Secret Treasure Route**, which is a small hidden dungeon-style treasure area.

  <li><strong>Secret Treasure Route:</strong> clear the hidden route and track the entrance wall as the landmark.</li>

<h3 id="shrine-of-life-notes">
  Shrine of Life notes
</h3>

A **Shrine of Life** should be tracked as a separate map location, not as something unlocked by collecting Shards. Shrines are challenge-style routes with combat, puzzles, and rewards.

If a Shrine contains a Shard of Life, record both the Shrine location and the Shard pickup in your tracking notes. This keeps Shrine cleanup and Shard cleanup from blending together.

<h2 id="faq">
  The Adventures of Elliot Shards of Life FAQ
</h2>

### What are Shards of Life?

Shards of Life are health-upgrade fragments. Collecting 4 Shards of Life gives Elliot a max HP increase.

### Are Shards of Life and Shrines of Life the same thing?

No. Shards of Life are collectible fragments, while Shrines of Life are separate challenge-style locations with combat, puzzles, and rewards.

### Do 4 Shards of Life unlock a Shrine of Life?

No. Four Shards of Life give a max HP increase. A Shrine of Life is a separate map location, not something unlocked by collecting 4 Shards.

### Can Shrines of Life contain Shards of Life?

Yes, a Shrine of Life can contain a Shard reward, so track the Shrine location and the Shard pickup separately.

### What are Secret Treasure Routes?

Secret Treasure Routes are hidden treasure areas opened by bombing cracked walls. They are different from ordinary bomb-rock paths.

### Should I collect Shards immediately?

Collect reachable Shards immediately. If a Shard route is blocked by a tool, story objective, Shrine challenge, cracked wall, Secret Treasure Route, or another age, mark it by route clue and return later.

### Why do several Shard pickups look the same?

Many Shards end with similar chest or key-item screens. That is why this guide tracks Shards by route clues such as room names, cave entrances, grassy paths, bomb rocks, cracked walls, Secret Treasure Routes, mirror puzzles, and cracked floors.

### Why can I see a chest but not reach it?

That usually means the chest needs another entrance, tool, route, story step, Shrine challenge, Secret Treasure Route, or age. Do not waste too much time forcing it early.

### Do cracked walls hide Shards?

Cracked walls can open Secret Treasure Routes, which may contain Shards or other strong rewards. Return with bombs and track the wall location.

### Are cracked-floor rooms worth checking?

Yes. Cracked-floor rooms can hide rewards after a movement or hazard check.

### What should I write down for Shard cleanup?

Track area, age, room name, nearby landmark, route type, required tool or progress, and whether the Shard is already collected.

### Is this a complete Shard of Life guide?

Not yet. This is a confirmed-route version and will be expanded with more exact area names and late-game Shards as more routes are verified.
