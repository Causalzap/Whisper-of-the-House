<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
There are **44 trophies** in **The Adventures of Elliot: The Millennium Tales**. The main completion tracks are **three named endings**, weapon and Bomb kills, cats, Shards of Life, Shrine of Life, vitality level, manuscripts, Magicite, bonus drops, pots and barrels, grass cutting, Faie attack kills, trials, and special enemies.

The best early trophy route is to **rotate all 7 weapon types**, track cats and Shards as you find them, break obvious pots and barrels, cut grass naturally, and keep separate notes for manuscripts and Magicite. The three ending trophy names are known, but the exact ending triggers should stay marked as unconfirmed until route data is verified.

<h2 id="weapon-and-bomb-kill-trophies">
  Weapon and Bomb kill trophies
</h2>

Weapon cleanup is one of the easiest parts of the trophy route to make painful if you ignore it early. The key detail is that **Bombs are different from the weapon kill trophies**: Sword, Spear, Hammer, Chain-and-Sickle, Bow, and Boomerang each need **200 kills**, while **Bombs need 100 kills**.

<GuideTable
caption="Weapon and Bomb kill targets."

The best route is to rotate weapons in safe rooms, use your strongest or most comfortable weapon in dangerous rooms, and let Bomb kills build naturally when rocks, tight enemy packs, or cave routes make bombs useful.

<h2 id="smooth-fighting-bonus-drops">
  Smooth Fighting and bonus drops
</h2>

**Smooth Fighting** requires chaining **30 bonus drops**. This is why the “avoid unnecessary hits” advice matters: it is not only about survival, it also connects to a trophy-specific resource chain.

Treat bonus drops as a clean-combat habit. Use dash, shield, block, parry, and safer spacing so weak enemies do not break your momentum. If you are trophy planning, do not wait until the end to learn this system.

<h2 id="shards-shrine-and-vitality">
  Shards of Life, Shrine of Life, and vitality level 10
</h2>

Shards of Life matter because they feed into Shrine of Life upgrades. Every **4 Shards of Life** unlock **1 Shrine of Life** upgrade, and **Great Radiance of Life** requires reaching **vitality level 10**.

<GuideTable
caption="Vitality trophy planning."

When you collect a Shard, note the area, age, room name, and nearest landmark. Later cleanup is much easier if you remember whether a Shard came from a grassy side path, cave entrance, mirror room, cracked-floor room, bomb-rock route, or another route clue.

For route details, use the <Link href="/the-adventures-of-elliot/shards-of-life-locations">Shards of Life Locations guide</Link>.

<h2 id="faq">
  The Adventures of Elliot achievements FAQ
</h2>

### How many trophies are in The Adventures of Elliot?

There are 44 trophies.

### What are the three ending trophy names?

The three named ending trophies are **At the Thousand-Year Journey's End**, **The Peace in Which We Walk**, and **As Long as Any Hope Remains**. Exact triggers are not confirmed here yet.

### How many kills do weapon trophies need?

Sword, Spear, Hammer, Chain-and-Sickle, Bow, and Boomerang each need 200 kills. Bombs need 100 kills.

### What is Smooth Fighting?

Smooth Fighting requires chaining 30 bonus drops. Treat it as a clean-combat trophy and build good habits early.

### How many pots or barrels do I need to break?

Good for Throwing and for Breaking requires breaking 200 pots or barrels.

### How many times do I need to cut grass?

Master Mower requires cutting grass 300 times.

### What is History's Tapestry?

History's Tapestry is the manuscript collection trophy. Track manuscripts separately from ordinary chests.

### What vitality level do I need?

Great Radiance of Life requires vitality level 10.

### How do Shards of Life and Shrine of Life work?

Every 4 Shards of Life unlocks 1 Shrine of Life upgrade. Since vitality level 10 is required, Shard tracking matters for completion.

### How many kills does Fighting Fairy need?

Fighting Fairy requires 30 kills with Faie attacks.

### Are cats tied to trophies or completion?

Yes. Cats are a major completion track with reward thresholds at 5, 10, 20, 35, and 50 cats.

### What are the cat rewards?

The confirmed cat rewards are Cat Needle at 5 cats, Bomb Pouch at 10 cats, Quiver at 20 cats, Golden Egg at 35 cats, and Medal of the Katzenmeister at 50 cats.

### Should I farm all weapon kills at the end?

No. Rotate weapons during normal exploration so Sword, Spear, Hammer, Chain-and-Sickle, Bow, Boomerang, and Bomb progress build naturally.

### Should I make a final Magicite build early?

No. Track Magicite names, costs, and effects, but do not force a final build before you understand more weapons and enemy types.
