<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
There are **50 cats** in **The Adventures of Elliot: The Millennium Tales**: **2 cats** from the early **Capricious Neighbors** route, **45 cats** spread across the four ages, and **3 cats** tied to the late **True Ending** route.

Talk to the **Ailurophilic Traveler** near the **Kingdom of Huther fountain** to turn cats in. Rewards unlock at **5 / 10 / 20 / 35 / 50 cats**: **Cat Needle**, **Bomb Pouch**, **Quiver**, **Golden Egg**, and **Medal of the Katzenmeister**. Get **Cat Needle** early, then use this guide as a cleanup route for dungeon cats, ability-gated cats, and True Ending cats.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="how-to-start-cat-quest">
  How to start the cat quest
</h2>

Cat collection does not fully open from the first minute of the game. If the cat quest is not starting, progress the story until the Moonmirror route is complete, then continue the related town dialogue and look for the **Ailurophilic Traveler**.

<h2 id="capricious-neighbors-cat-quest-not-starting">
  Capricious Neighbors cat quest not starting
</h2>

<h2 id="faq">
  The Adventures of Elliot cat locations FAQ
</h2>

### Can I miss cats permanently?

Most missing-cat problems come from checking the wrong age, missing a dungeon route, or searching before a story-gated cat appears. Before assuming a cat is gone forever, check the correct age, Cat Needle result, dungeon entrance, and True Ending route state.

### Do I need all 50 cats for the True Ending?

The full 50-cat set is for the final cat reward, **Medal of the Katzenmeister**. However, three cats are tied to the True Ending route, so cat collection and late story progress overlap.

### Why is Cat Needle not showing the last cat?

The cat may be in another age, behind a dungeon route, on a different height level, or locked behind True Ending progress. Use the missing-cat checklist above before rechecking every open field.

### Is Medal of the Katzenmeister useful?

It is mainly a decorative completion reward. The more practical cat rewards are Cat Needle, Bomb Pouch, and Quiver.

### What should I do if the cat marker looks close but I cannot reach it?

Look for another entrance, ladder, bridge, shortcut, cave, or sublevel. Cat positions are not always reached from the closest visible screen.

### What is the fastest final cleanup order?

Check **Safekeeping → Reconstruction → Magic → Budding**, and in each age prioritize dungeons, Monster Trails, shortcut routes, sublevels, and True Ending cats before open fields.
