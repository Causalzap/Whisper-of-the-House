<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
There are **50 cats** in **The Adventures of Elliot: The Millennium Tales**. Bring found cats to the **cat-loving traveler beside the fountain in the Kingdom of Huther**. Cats are spread across the four ages of Philabieldia, and a **meow** usually means one is nearby.

Cat rewards unlock at **5, 10, 20, 35, and 50 cats**: **Cat Needle**, **Bomb Pouch**, **Quiver**, **Golden Egg**, and **Medal of the Katzenmeister**. Cat Needle is the key cleanup tool because it helps show remaining cats in the current area and points toward their map position.

<h2 id="how-cat-collecting-starts">
  How cat collecting starts
</h2>

The first major cat moment happens during the village route, when a cat becomes tied to the Golden Bell event. This introduces cats as part of the game's route and completion layer.

Later, the cat-loving traveler asks Elliot to bring back any cats he finds. After you catch the nearby cats and return them, the traveler says they want to make the place a cat paradise and asks you to bring any more cats you find during the adventure.

  Cat meow clue: stop when you hear a cat
</h2>

The most basic cat-finding clue is sound. If you hear a cat meow while exploring, slow down before leaving the area.

Check:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>corners,</li>
  <li>rooftops,</li>
  <li>ledges,</li>
  <li>side paths,</li>

The meow clue is important because it helps before Cat Needle does the work for you. Many cats are easier to notice by sound before you see them.

<h2 id="faq">
  The Adventures of Elliot cat locations FAQ
</h2>

### How many cats are in The Adventures of Elliot?

There are 50 cats.

### Where do I turn in cats?

Bring found cats to the cat-loving traveler beside the fountain in the Kingdom of Huther.

### How do I start the cat quest?

Talk to the cat-loving traveler, catch the nearby cats, and bring them back. After the first turn-in, the traveler asks you to bring any more cats you find.

### How do I know a cat is nearby?

Listen for a cat meow while exploring. If you hear a meow, slow down and check nearby corners, rooftops, ledges, side paths, large trees, and hidden spaces.

### What does Cat Needle do?

Cat Needle unlocks at 5 cats. It helps with cleanup by showing how many cats remain in the current area and pointing toward their map position.

### Can I turn Cat Needle off?

You can leave Cat Needle guidance off if you want the search to feel more exploratory, then use it later for cleanup.

### What are the cat rewards?

The cat rewards are Cat Needle at 5 cats, Bomb Pouch at 10 cats, Quiver at 20 cats, Golden Egg at 35 cats, and Medal of the Katzenmeister at 50 cats.

### Are cats spread across all ages?

Yes. Cats are spread across the four ages of Philabieldia, so track each cat by age, area, and nearest landmark.

### Can I feed or play with cats?

Yes. Collected cats can be part of the cat paradise side activity, including feeding, interaction, and toys when those options are available.

### Should I wait for Cat Needle before collecting cats?

No. Collect cats naturally when you hear or see them. Cat Needle is best used when an area still has missing cats and you need cleanup help.

### Is this the full 50-cat list?

This page is currently an updating 50-cat guide with confirmed route notes, reward data, Cat Needle mechanics, and a by-age framework. The full checklist should be expanded as exact cat locations are verified.
