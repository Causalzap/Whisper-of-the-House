<h2 id="how-side-jobs-start">
  How side jobs start
</h2>

Most side jobs are accepted from NPCs at the **P.I. Office**.

That makes the office both a story hub and a **side-job checkpoint**.

The clean habit is:

1. finish a main mission
2. return to the **P.I. Office**
3. check for new side jobs
4. accept them immediately
5. clear them before pushing too far forward

There are also **2 exceptions** during **To Catch a Mockingmouse**, so do not assume every side job waits safely at the office.

<h2 id="when-side-job-rewards-become-permanently-missable">
  When side job rewards become permanently missable
</h2>

The side-job cleanup window effectively ends when you start **Jack Squat**.

Once you cross that line, you can no longer treat the **P.I. Office** like an open checklist board.

So the practical rule is simple:

**if a side job still depends on the P.I. Office loop, finish it before Jack Squat.**

<GuideCallout title="Point of no return">
For side-job cleanup, the real lockout is **starting Jack Squat**, not the final cutscene.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="which-side-job-rewards-matter-most-for-100-percent">
  Which side job rewards matter most for 100% completion?
</h2>

If you are prioritizing, the most important rewards are:

### 1) Unique Baseball Cards

These are the highest-risk side-job rewards.

Confirmed examples include:

- **Oh Yes... They Float** → **Jeremiah Curd Baseball Card**
- **Friends in Deep Places** → **Jacob “Cheesehead” McQueen Baseball Card**

These should **not** be treated like ordinary shop cleanup.

### 2) Prize Tokens

Known side-job token rewards include:

- **They Call it “Azurro Burgerro”** → **2 Prize Tokens**
- **Fishy Business** → **1 Prize Token**
- **Perfect Bog Fondue** → **2 Prize Tokens**
- **Slugs in the Shell** → **1 Prize Token**

That gives you **at least 6 confirmed Prize Tokens** from side jobs.

### 3) Schematics

Known schematic rewards include:

- **Blueprints, Please** → **2 Schematics**
- **Poster Girl** → **3 Schematics**

That gives you **at least 5 confirmed Schematics** from side jobs.

### 4) Completion progress

Even low-value coin jobs can still matter because the full side-job set contributes to the all-side-jobs completion line.

<h2 id="what-you-should-finish-before-jack-squat">
  What you should finish before Jack Squat
</h2>

Before starting **Jack Squat**, your checklist should be:

- accept and clear every office-based side job you currently have
- finish any outstanding **Baseball Card reward** side jobs first
- cash in **Prize Token** jobs if you still need Prize D-Spenser progress
- finish **Schematic** jobs if your upgrades still feel behind
- make a fresh **manual save** before crossing the lockout

Priority order:

1. **Baseball Card side jobs**
2. **unfinished side jobs needed for Real Deal Gumshoe**
3. **Prize Token side jobs**
4. **Schematic side jobs**
5. **coin-only side jobs**

<GuideCallout title="Best late-game rule">
If you only keep one protection save for side-job cleanup, keep one **before Jack Squat**.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### Are side-job rewards missable in MOUSE: P.I. For Hire?

Yes. Side-job rewards can be missed if you fail to accept or finish the side job in time, especially once the P.I. Office cleanup loop ends.

### When do side jobs become dangerous to leave unfinished?

The biggest risk point is **starting Jack Squat**, because that is the effective lockout for the office-based side-job cleanup structure.

### Are all side jobs accepted at the P.I. Office?

No. Most are, but current route coverage still points to **2 exceptions during To Catch a Mockingmouse**.

### Which side-job rewards matter most for 100% completion?

The highest-risk rewards are the **unique Baseball Card rewards**.

### Which known side jobs reward Baseball Cards?

Current route coverage confirms **Oh Yes... They Float** rewards the **Jeremiah Curd Baseball Card**, and **Friends in Deep Places** rewards the **Jacob “Cheesehead” McQueen Baseball Card**.

### How many Prize Tokens come from side jobs?

Known side-job rewards currently account for **at least 6 Prize Tokens**.

### Do side jobs reward only coins?

No. Launch routing already confirms rewards including **Baseball Cards**, **Prize Tokens**, **Schematics**, and **Coins**.

### Is Behind the Nuts fixed yet?

Treat **Behind the Nuts** as **still under patch watch** unless you have personally confirmed the latest version.
