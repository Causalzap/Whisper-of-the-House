<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
To join the **Old Camp** in **Gothic 1 Remake**, build Shadow support, complete **Diego's Test of Faith** in the Old Mine, reach **Level 5**, then go through **Thorus → Raven → Gomez**.

The safest support route is **Whistler, Sly, Fingers, and Scatty**, with **Dexter** and **Fisk** as extra support options. During Gomez's dialogue, never say you serve **Y'Berion** and never threaten Gomez.

</h2>

The Old Camp is the first major faction route most players meet, but you cannot simply walk up to Gomez and join. You need support in the outer ring, Diego's Test of Faith, enough level progress, and the right final conversation.

The clean route is:

<h2 id="requirements">
  Old Camp joining requirements
</h2>

Old Camp has three big requirements: reputation, Diego's Test of Faith, and Level 5. Thorus, Raven, and Gomez come after that.

<GuideTable
caption="Old Camp joining requirements."
columns={requirementColumns}
rows={requirementRows}
/>

The 1000 ore bribe to Thorus is not the same as joining the Old Camp. It can get you through the gate for specific castle business, such as reaching the Fire Mages, but it does not give you a proper Gomez audience or count as admission. If your goal is becoming a Shadow, follow Diego's route.

<h2 id="talk-to-thorus">
  Step 1: Talk to Thorus at the castle gate
</h2>

When you reach the Old Camp, go to the inner castle gate and speak to Thorus. He makes it clear that the inner castle is not open to random newcomers.

You can ask about joining Gomez's people, but Thorus will not simply test you himself. You need someone trusted to vouch for you. That sends you back toward Diego and the outer-ring reputation path.

<h2 id="talk-to-diego">
  Step 2: Ask Diego how to earn trust
</h2>

After Thorus sends you away, talk to Diego. He explains that key figures in the outer ring can help your reputation. Once enough of them respect you, Diego gives you his own test.

This is where the Old Camp route really starts. You are not doing random errands. You are building a case that you are useful enough for Gomez's people.

/>

You do not need every support route, but knowing the extra options matters. If Whistler, Sly, Fingers, or Scatty becomes inconvenient, Dexter and Fisk can help fill the gap.

<h2 id="graham-map">
  Graham's map: 20 ore or free by threat
</h2>

Before you travel too far for Diego's Test of Faith, consider getting the map from Graham near the Old Camp north gate.

<GuideTable
caption="How to get Graham's map."

rows={mapRows}
/>

The important detail is that threatening Graham for the map does not block your Old Camp admission progress. If you are short on ore and need navigation help, this is a useful option.

<h2 id="whistler">
  Step 3: Get Whistler's support
</h2>

Whistler wants an ornamented sword from Fisk. He gives you **100 ore** and asks you to buy it without telling Fisk who sent you.

Fisk asks for **110 ore**. Do not mention Whistler during the purchase. If you want the clean route, get the extra 10 ore, buy the sword, and return it to Whistler.

That earns Whistler's support and keeps your Old Camp reputation clean.

<h2 id="sly">
  Step 4: Get Sly's support
</h2>

Sly's task is about Nek, the missing guard. You need to leave through the southern side of the Old Camp and look for the cave area where Nek's body can be found.

Take the amulet from Nek and return it to Sly.

Expect danger near the cave. This is still early Chapter 1, so save before heading out and do not charge into enemies as if you are already trained.

<h2 id="fingers">
  Step 5: Get Fingers' support
</h2>

Fingers tests whether you can handle a lock. His task points you toward a chest tied to Scatty's hut and a ring inside.

This is also your first serious reminder that lockpicking is not just a side activity. It can directly affect faction progress.

Save before trying the chest. If you want to train lockpicking first, Fingers can help, but that costs Learning Points and ore. For the Old Camp join route, the main goal is simple: get the ring and bring it back.

<h2 id="scatty">
  Step 6: Get Scatty's support
</h2>

Scatty runs the arena. To impress him, challenge fighters and show that you have the nerve to step into the ring.

The useful part is that you do not need to win every fight. Challenging a strong opponent can still prove courage, even if you get knocked down. Winning gives better rewards, but losing does not always mean the route is dead.

For a weak early character, this makes Scatty one of the better support options because the task is about proving yourself, not becoming the best fighter in the camp immediately.

<h2 id="dexter">
  Optional support: Dexter and The Cult's Recipe
</h2>

Dexter is another Shadow support source. His task, **The Cult's Recipe**, sends you toward the Swamp Camp / Cor Kalom side of the world to get the Dreamjuice Recipe.

This is not the fastest first support task, but it is valuable if you are already visiting Swamp Camp before choosing a faction. Bring the recipe back to Dexter to add another support option to your Old Camp route.

If you are still early, do Whistler, Sly, Fingers, and Scatty first. Use Dexter when you want extra XP, extra support, or a broader Chapter 1 route.

<h2 id="fisk">
  Optional support: Fisk and A New Fence for Fisk
</h2>

Fisk can also become part of your admission route through **A New Fence for Fisk**.

This connects to the Mordrag problem. After Mordrag leaves the Old Camp through **Out of Sight**, Fisk needs a new fence. Go to the New Camp and recruit **Sharky** as Fisk's replacement contact.

This is another reason not to kill Mordrag too early. Removing him cleanly opens more route value than treating Thorus's problem as a simple fight.

<h2 id="mordrag">
  Step 7: Handle Mordrag without killing him
</h2>

Thorus wants Mordrag gone from the Old Camp. The easy mistake is thinking this means you should kill him.

Do not do that if you want a flexible Chapter 1 route. Talk to Mordrag and convince him to leave. He can guide you to the New Camp, help you survive the road, and give you his ring for the Lares route.

This still removes him from the Old Camp, which helps with Thorus, but it also keeps your New Camp path alive and can support Fisk's fence route later.

<h2 id="diegos-test">
  Step 8: Complete Diego's Test of Faith
</h2>

After you have enough support, Diego gives you his own test: get the supply list from Ian in the Old Mine.

Diego gives you the countersign for Ian:

<strong>The crawlers look mighty hungry.</strong>

Use that phrase when Ian asks. Without it, you may reach the right person and still not know what to say.

This is a major Old Camp requirement, but it also connects to New Camp planning. If you are trying to maximize Chapter 1 rewards, make a manual save before handing the list directly to Diego.

The short version is:

<ol className="my-6 list-decimal space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Ask Diego about his Test of Faith.</li>
  <li>Get Graham's map if you need the route help.</li>
  <li>Travel to the Old Mine northwest of the Old Camp.</li>
  <li>Find Ian.</li>
  <li>Use Diego's countersign: “The crawlers look mighty hungry.”</li>
  <li>Take the supply list.</li>
  <li>Decide whether to involve Lares before returning it to Diego.</li>
</ol>

For the full route and the list choice, read the <Link href="/gothic-1-remake/old-mine-diegos-list">Old Mine / Diego's List guide</Link>.

<h2 id="level-5">
  Step 9: Reach Level 5
</h2>

Even if you have support and Diego's list, you still need Level 5 before the Old Camp formally accepts you.

If you are short on XP, do not grind randomly into dangerous areas. Use safer sources:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>finish remaining Old Camp support tasks,</li>
  <li>use Mordrag's New Camp route for travel XP and loot,</li>
  <li>visit Swamp Camp with Baal Parvez,</li>
  <li>complete safe errands in camps,</li>
  <li>fight only enemies you can isolate and survive.</li>
</ul>

This is also a good moment to check your build. If you are not sure Old Camp is still the right choice, read the <Link href="/gothic-1-remake/best-faction">Best Faction Guide</Link> before talking to Gomez.

<h2 id="raven-gomez">
  Step 10: Talk to Thorus, Raven, and Gomez
</h2>

Once you have enough support, Diego's test, and Level 5, talk to Diego again. If he says you are ready, go to Thorus and tell him Diego vouches for you.

Once inside, speak to **Raven** at the podium near the entrance. He will escort you to Gomez.

This is the most dangerous dialogue in the Old Camp admission route. Gomez is not testing your curiosity. He is testing whether you are useful, connected, and loyal enough to accept.

<GuideTable
caption="How to survive the Gomez conversation."
columns={gomezColumns}
rows={gomezRows}
/>

Two answers are especially dangerous:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Do not say you serve <strong>Y'Berion</strong> or answer <strong>“To Y'Berion.”</strong></li>
  <li>Do not threaten Gomez with violence.</li>
</ul>

If Gomez asks who you know in other camps, name people you actually met. Good examples include Lares, Mordrag, or Lester if your route already introduced you to them. Do not pretend you know powerful leaders you never met.

<h2 id="rewards">
  What do you get for joining Old Camp?
</h2>

After Gomez accepts you, you become a **Shadow**. This changes your standing inside the Old Camp and starts the next stage of the route.

The main rewards are:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>formal Old Camp membership,</li>
  <li>Shadow status,</li>
  <li>access to Old Camp armor options,</li>
  <li>better standing with Gomez's people,</li>
  <li>Chapter 2 progress,</li>
  <li>a clearer path toward Guard or Fire Mage direction later.</li>
</ul>

If your main goal is Fire Mage, Old Camp is the cleanest early direction. If your main goal is freedom, mercenary identity, or Brotherhood style, make sure you are not choosing Old Camp only because you reached it first.

<h2 id="faq">
  Gothic 1 Remake Old Camp FAQ
</h2>

### How do I join the Old Camp in Gothic 1 Remake?

Get enough Old Camp support, complete Diego's Test of Faith in the Old Mine, reach Level 5, then talk to Diego, Thorus, Raven, and Gomez.

### Who are the easiest Old Camp supporters?

Whistler, Sly, Fingers, and Scatty are the easiest early support route. Dexter and Fisk are useful extra routes if you want more options.

### What are the fatal Gomez dialogue choices?

Do not say you serve Y'Berion or answer “To Y'Berion,” and do not threaten Gomez with violence. Either answer can get you killed immediately.

### Where is Raven?

After Thorus lets you into the castle, speak to Raven near the entrance podium. He escorts you to Gomez.

### What is Diego's countersign for Ian?

Tell Ian: “The crawlers look mighty hungry.” Diego gives you this phrase for the Old Mine supply list.

### How do I complete Whistler's sword quest?

Take Whistler's 100 ore, buy the ornamented sword from Fisk for 110 ore, do not mention Whistler, and return the sword to Whistler.

### How do I get Dexter's support?

Complete The Cult's Recipe by getting the Dreamjuice Recipe from the Swamp Camp / Cor Kalom route and bringing it back to Dexter.

### How do I get Fisk's support?

After Mordrag leaves through Out of Sight, go to the New Camp and recruit Sharky as Fisk's new fence.

### Should I kill Mordrag for Thorus?

No, not if you want a flexible route. Convince Mordrag to leave and let him guide you to the New Camp instead.

### Can I bribe Thorus to join the Old Camp?

No. The 1000 ore bribe can get you through the castle gate for other business, but it does not count as Old Camp admission or get you a proper Gomez audience.

### Where do I get Diego's list?

Go to the Old Mine and speak with Ian. Use Diego's countersign to get the supply list.

### Should I give Diego's list to Diego immediately?

Not always. Save first and consider showing it to Lares before returning it to Diego if you want extra New Camp value.
