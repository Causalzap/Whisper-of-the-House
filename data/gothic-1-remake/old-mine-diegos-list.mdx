To get **Diego's List** in **Gothic 1 Remake**, earn enough Old Camp reputation for Diego's **Test of Faith**, go to the **Old Mine**, find **Ian**, and use the countersign: **“The crawlers look mighty hungry.”**

Do **not** hand the list to Diego immediately if you want the best reward. Directly giving it to Diego gives **750 XP**. Showing it to **Lares first**, then returning the falsified list to Diego, gives **1,750 XP total** and opens the New Camp plan.

<h2 id="diegos-list-guide">
  Gothic 1 Remake Diego's List guide
</h2>

Diego's List is one of the most important early quest items in Gothic 1 Remake. It looks like a simple Old Camp errand, but it can also give you a much better New Camp reward path if you use it in the right order.

The short version is:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li><strong>Diego only:</strong> 750 XP.</li>
  <li><strong>Lares first, then Diego:</strong> 1,750 XP total.</li>
</ul>

That difference is why this item deserves its own guide. If you hand the list to Diego immediately, you finish the Old Camp test but give up the better flexible route.

<h2 id="start-diegos-test">
  How to start Diego's Test of Faith
</h2>

Diego does not send you to the Old Mine the moment you enter the Old Camp. You need to prove yourself first.

A clean route is to get support from Old Camp figures such as Whistler, Sly, Fingers, and Scatty. Once Diego thinks your reputation is moving in the right direction, ask him if you are ready for his test.

Diego then tells you to go to the Old Mine and find Ian. He also gives you the phrase you need:

<strong>The crawlers look mighty hungry.</strong>

That phrase is the countersign. Remember it.

<h2 id="get-old-mine-map">
  Get Graham's Old Mine map if you need it
</h2>

When Diego gives you the test, he points you toward Graham for a map. Graham's shack is near the Old Camp's north gate, and he can sell you the map that helps you reach the Old Mine.

If you already know the route, you can move without it. But for a first run, the map is worth considering because the wrong road can cost more time, health, and supplies than the map itself.

<h2 id="reach-old-mine">
  How to reach the Old Mine
</h2>

The Old Mine is northwest of the Old Camp. The important thing is not to turn the trip into a full wilderness expedition. Your goal is Ian's supply list, not clearing every side path on the way.

Before leaving:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>save manually,</li>
  <li>bring food or healing,</li>
  <li>carry a torch or light source,</li>
  <li>avoid unnecessary fights,</li>
  <li>use the map if you bought one,</li>
  <li>do not travel at night unless you know the road.</li>
</ul>

If you are too weak for the trip, step back and improve your gear first with the <Link href="/gothic-1-remake/early-ore-weapons-armor">Early Gear Guide</Link>.

<h2 id="what-the-list-does">
  What is Diego's List for?
</h2>

The list is a supply requirement sheet for the Old Mine. Diego wants it because Old Camp needs to know what the mine requires for the next convoy.

But the list is also valuable because Lares can use it. If you show it to Lares before Diego, he tampers with the list and turns Diego's errand into part of the New Camp plan.

<h2 id="give-list-to-diego">
  When should you give the list to Diego?
</h2>

Give the list to Diego when you are ready to continue the Old Camp route.

If you already showed it to Lares, bring the falsified list back to Diego afterward. Diego may notice something is off, but he still accepts it, letting you complete the Old Camp requirement while also keeping the New Camp payoff.

The safest order is:

<ol className="my-6 list-decimal space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Get the list from Ian.</li>
  <li>Make a manual save.</li>
  <li>Show it to Lares if you want the 1,750 XP route and New Camp progress.</li>
  <li>Return the falsified list to Diego afterward.</li>
  <li>Check your Old Camp readiness with Diego.</li>
  <li>Move to Thorus and Gomez only when you are sure.</li>
</ol>

If you are already sure you want Old Camp and do not care about the extra New Camp route, you can hand it directly to Diego for 750 XP.

<h2 id="faq">
  Gothic 1 Remake Diego's List FAQ
</h2>

### Should I give Diego's List to Diego or Lares?

Give it to Lares first if you want the best reward. Directly giving it to Diego gives 750 XP. Showing it to Lares first, then returning the falsified list to Diego, gives 1,750 XP total.

### What is Diego's countersign for Ian?

Tell Ian: “The crawlers look mighty hungry.” Diego gives you this phrase when he sends you to the Old Mine.

### Where is Ian in the Old Mine?

Go deeper through the main mine route. After Ulbert, keep going until you see Ian talking with Alberto. Ian gives you the supply list after you use Diego's countersign.

### What is Something the Matter?

Something the Matter? is the New Camp route that starts when Lares tampers with Ian's supply list. You then bring the falsified list to Diego, which helps set up the later New Camp convoy ambush.

### Does Diego accept the falsified list?

Yes. Diego may notice something is wrong, but he still accepts the list and your Old Camp progress continues.

### Can I still join Old Camp if I show the list to Lares first?

Yes. Showing the list to Lares first does not stop you from returning to Diego and continuing the Old Camp route.

### Is the Old Mine dangerous?

Yes, especially if you wander into deeper or crawler-heavy areas. Stay focused on Ian and the list if you are still weak.

### Do I need Graham's Old Mine map?

No, but it helps if you do not know the route. For a first run, the map can save time, healing, and wrong turns.

### Does giving the list to Diego lock me into Old Camp?

Not by itself. The bigger lock-in is the final faction join step, but you should still save before major turn-ins.
