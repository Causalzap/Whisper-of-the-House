<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
To join the **Swamp Camp** in **Gothic 1 Remake**, reach **Level 5**, earn **four Guru approvals**, then pass **Cor Kalom's** final check. The five key Guru routes are **Baal Namib**, **Baal Tyon**, **Baal Orun**, **Baal Tondral**, and **Baal Cadar**.

Before the final Cor Kalom conversation, make a manual save. Choose the answer that supports faith in the **Sleeper**. Questioning or insulting the belief can turn Cor Kalom hostile and block the route.

<h2 id="baal-parvez-escort">
  Step 1: Reach Swamp Camp with Baal Parvez
</h2>

Baal Parvez is near the southern exit of the Old Camp, close to the Fisk area. Ask him to guide you to Swamp Camp.

This is the safest first route because he knows the road and can help you survive enemies along the way. You also get a better introduction to the Brotherhood than if you wander there alone.

  Step 2: Talk to Joru for Guru advice
</h2>

After you get Fortuno's daily swampweed ration, find **Joru** in the Swamp Camp's northwest / work area. Give him the swampweed and talk through his advice.

<h2 id="guru-approvals">
  Guru approvals: the five routes
</h2>

You need four Guru approvals before Cor Kalom accepts you. The five main approval routes are Namib, Tyon, Orun, Tondral, and Cadar.

<GuideTable
caption="Guru approval routes for joining Swamp Camp."
columns={guruColumns}
rows={guruRows}
/>

The clean route is usually to secure Namib, Tyon, Orun, and Cadar. If one route is inconvenient, Baal Tondral's Dusty / Melvin route gives another real approval path.

<h2 id="baal-namib-lester">
  Step 3: Get Baal Namib's approval with Lester
</h2>

When you first enter, you will meet Lester near Baal Namib. Do not walk up to Namib and start talking like a normal outsider.

Instead:

<ol className="my-6 list-decimal space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Talk to Lester near the entrance / Namib area.</li>
  <li>Ask him to show you around.</li>
  <li>Move him away from Namib.</li>
  <li>Ask how to join his master.</li>
  <li>Play along with the conversion story.</li>
  <li>Return with Lester and let the scene impress Baal Namib.</li>
</ol>

This is one of the fastest approvals if you handle it correctly.

<h2 id="baal-tyon-dreamcaller">
  Step 4: Get Baal Tyon's approval
</h2>

Baal Tyon's fast route depends on the reward you chose from Baal Kagan's cigarette task near New Camp.

If you picked the option that helps you join the Brotherhood, you receive the item needed for Tyon's approval. Bring it to Baal Tyon in the temple area and hand it over.

This is why you should save before choosing Kagan's reward. If you were using Kagan for New Camp, you may have taken scrolls for Roscoe instead. If you are aiming for Swamp Camp, choose the Brotherhood-help reward.

<h2 id="cor-kalom-first-check">
  Step 5: Talk to Cor Kalom early
</h2>

Before finishing the Orun / Cadar chain, go to Cor Kalom's laboratory and ask about joining.

Lester can guide you to the lab if you ask him to take you there. Cor Kalom is up near the circular stair / laboratory area.

He explains what kind of proof the Brotherhood expects. This also points you toward the work chain with Ghorim, Harl, and Baal Orun.

<h2 id="ghorim-harl">
  Step 6: Help Ghorim by forcing Harl back to work
</h2>

Go to the swampweed pressers and speak with **Ghorim**. He explains that **Harl** is slacking instead of taking his shift.

Find Harl near the camp vendor / tree area and force him back to work. This may require fighting him.

After Harl returns, go back to Ghorim and complete the step. This draws Baal Orun's attention and leads into Swampweed Harvest.

<h2 id="swampweed-harvest">
  Step 7: Complete Swampweed Harvest for Baal Orun
</h2>

After helping Ghorim, Baal Orun sends you into the swamp to collect the day's harvest.

This is more specific than “go deeper into the swamp.” You need to collect from **Balor** and **Viran**, deliver the harvest to **Cor Kalom**, then return to **Baal Orun**.

<h2 id="faq">
  Gothic 1 Remake Swamp Camp FAQ
</h2>

### How do I join Swamp Camp in Gothic 1 Remake?

Reach Swamp Camp safely, earn four Guru approvals, reach Level 5, then pass Cor Kalom's final joining check and become a Novice.

### How many Guru approvals do I need?

You need four Guru approvals. The main Guru routes are Baal Namib, Baal Tyon, Baal Orun, Baal Tondral, and Baal Cadar.

### What level do I need to join Swamp Camp?

You need Level 5 before the final Cor Kalom joining step.

### Why does Cor Kalom say I am not experienced enough?

Usually because you are not Level 5 yet. If you already have the approvals, gain more XP and return.

### What Cor Kalom answer should I choose?

Choose the answer that supports faith in the Sleeper. Questioning or insulting the belief can turn Cor Kalom hostile and block the route. A vague answer may give you one more chance.

### Who is Joru and why should I talk to him?

Joru is an important advice NPC in Swamp Camp. Give him Fortuno's daily swampweed ration and he explains how to impress the Gurus.

### How do I get Baal Namib's approval?

Use Lester. Move him away from Namib, ask how to join his master, then return and play along with the conversion scene.

### How do I get Baal Tyon's approval?

Use the Dreamcaller / Brotherhood-help reward from Baal Kagan's route, then bring it to Baal Tyon.

### How do I complete Swampweed Harvest?

After helping Ghorim and Harl, collect the harvest from Balor and Viran, deliver it to Cor Kalom, then report back to Baal Orun.

### How do I get Baal Cadar's approval?

Complete Baal Orun's route first, keep the Sleep scroll reward, then use Sleep on a student during Cadar's lesson.

### Is Dusty required for Swamp Camp?

Dusty is not the only possible approval path, but he is part of Baal Tondral's New Souls for the Brotherhood route and can count as a real Guru approval.

### What rewards do I get for joining Swamp Camp?

The full admission route can total around 3850 XP. Final acceptance as a Novice can give 750 XP and Novice Armor, along with Brotherhood faction access.

### Is Swamp Camp good for magic?

Swamp Camp has strong Brotherhood magic flavor and Novice / Templar identity, but it is not the strongest pure mage route compared with Fire Mage or Water Mage direction.
