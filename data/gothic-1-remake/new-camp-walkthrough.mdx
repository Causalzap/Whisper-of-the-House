<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
To join the **New Camp** in **Gothic 1 Remake**, reach **Level 5** and complete **at least 3 major Lares tasks**. The clean route is usually **Mordrag's Ring**, **Diego's List**, and **Herb Merchant**, with **Rice Lord / Lefty** and **The Raid** as additional task options.

Do not kill **Mordrag**. Let him escort you to New Camp, keep his ring, use **Charm** on **Roscoe** if needed, sell **Baal Isidro's swampweed** for **400 ore**, and return the full money to **Lares**. 
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="new-camp-walkthrough">
  Gothic 1 Remake New Camp walkthrough
</h2>

The New Camp route is not a single fixed checklist where every task is mandatory. It works more like a proof pool: complete enough important tasks for Lares, reach Level 5, then ask to join.

The practical requirement is:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li><strong>Reach Level 5.</strong></li>
  <li><strong>Complete at least 3 major Lares tasks.</strong></li>
  <li><strong>Return to Lares before committing to the faction.</strong></li>
</ul>

<GuideTable
caption="New Camp joining requirements in Gothic 1 Remake."
columns={requirementColumns}
rows={requirementRows}
/>

If you are still choosing between camps, read the <Link href="/gothic-1-remake/best-faction">Best Faction Guide</Link>. If you have not reached New Camp yet, use the <Link href="/gothic-1-remake/perfect-start">Perfect Start Route</Link> first.

Use this route if you want the strongest low-confusion path:

The key idea is simple: New Camp rewards preparation. Keep the list, keep the ring, keep your save files, and do not spend quest money before Lares gets it.

<h2 id="mordrag-ring">
  Step 1: Get Mordrag's Ring
</h2>

Thorus wants Mordrag removed from Old Camp. Do not kill him.

Instead, talk to Mordrag and tell him Thorus wants him gone. Mordrag can guide you to New Camp, clearing or surviving the route much better than you can alone.

At the New Camp entrance, he gives you **Mordrag's Ring**. Keep it. You need it for Lares.

<h2 id="bridge-toll">
  Watch for the 10 ore bridge toll
</h2>

After Mordrag brings you to New Camp, you may return toward Old Camp to continue Diego's List, Old Camp tasks, or other errands.

On the way back, two guards at the bridge can stop you and demand **10 ore**. Do not get surprised by this encounter.

<GuideTable
caption="What to do when the bridge guards demand 10 ore."
columns={bridgeColumns}
rows={bridgeRows}
/>

For a weak early build, paying is often the safest option. If you want to fight or run, save before crossing back.

<h2 id="mordrag-old-room">
  Mordrag's old room is useful after he leaves
</h2>

Once Mordrag leaves Old Camp, his old room becomes useful as an early safe spot. You can use it for sleeping and storing items while you are still moving between camps.

This is a small but valuable reward for handling Mordrag cleanly. Killing him solves Thorus's problem in the worst possible way; sending him away opens New Camp, gives the ring, and leaves you with a practical Old Camp room.

<h2 id="meet-lares">
  Step 2: Meet Lares with the ring and list
</h2>

Once you reach New Camp, use Mordrag's Ring to get past the first gatekeeping layer and reach Lares.

Lares wants proof that you are worth taking seriously. Mordrag's Ring and Diego's List are the two biggest early pieces.

Give Lares the ring when the route calls for it. If you also have Diego's List, this is where New Camp starts to feel like a real faction route instead of a sightseeing trip.

<h2 id="get-diegos-list-first">
  Step 3: Get Diego's List, but do not hand it in
</h2>

Before pushing New Camp too far, get **Diego's List** from Ian in the Old Mine. This is the same supply list from Diego's Test of Faith.

For New Camp, the important part is the turn-in order. Do not give the list straight to Diego if you want the better route. Bring it to Lares first. Lares can tamper with it, and you can still return the falsified list to Diego later.

The short rule:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li><strong>Diego only:</strong> simpler Old Camp progress.</li>
  <li><strong>Lares first, then Diego:</strong> better flexible Chapter 1 route.</li>
</ul>

For the full list route, read the <Link href="/gothic-1-remake/old-mine-diegos-list">Diego's List Guide</Link>.

<h2 id="charm-roscoe">
  Step 4: Charm Roscoe
</h2>

Roscoe can block clean access to Lares. The smooth route uses a **Charm** scroll.

To get it, complete Baal Kagan's cigarette task near the New Camp lake cave area. When Kagan lets you pick a reward, choose the scrolls and make sure one of them is Charm.

Then:

<ol className="my-6 list-decimal space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Equip the Charm scroll.</li>
  <li>Go toward Lares's shack.</li>
  <li>When Roscoe addresses you, exit the conversation instead of arguing.</li>
  <li>Cast Charm on Roscoe.</li>
  <li>Enter and talk to Lares.</li>
</ol>

Make a manual save before choosing Kagan's reward. His rewards can help different faction routes, so do not lock yourself out of a Swamp Camp option by accident.

<h2 id="herb-merchant">
  Step 5: Complete Herb Merchant for 400 ore

The two details players often miss are:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">

  <li><strong>You do not need one single buyer:</strong> sell the swampweed through buyers such as Sharky, Cronos, Malon, Silas, mercenaries, and bandits until you reach the 400 ore target.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="rewards">

New Camp is the route for players who want more independence from Gomez's Old Camp structure.

The main rewards are:

  <li>formal New Camp membership,</li>
  <li>Bandit / Rogue-style status under Lares's side of the camp,</li>

The tradeoff is that the early path takes more setup. If you want the simplest first playthrough, Old Camp is easier. If you want more freedom and better long-term route flavor, New Camp is worth it.

<h2 id="faq">
  Gothic 1 Remake New Camp FAQ
</h2>

### How do I join the New Camp in Gothic 1 Remake?

Reach Level 5 and complete at least three major Lares tasks. The clean route is usually Mordrag's Ring, Diego's List, and Herb Merchant, with Rice Lord / Lefty and The Raid as extra task options.

### Do I need to complete every New Camp task?

No. You need enough proof for Lares, commonly at least three major joining tasks plus Level 5. You do not need to finish every optional route before checking with him.

### Should I kill Mordrag for Thorus?

No. If you want New Camp, Mordrag is far more valuable alive. He escorts you to New Camp, gives you the ring Lares needs, and leaves his old Old Camp room usable.

### What does Mordrag's Ring do?

Mordrag's Ring proves he sent you and helps you reach Lares. It is one of the key New Camp joining items.

### What happens on the way back to Old Camp?

Two guards at the bridge can stop you and ask for 10 ore. You can pay, run, or fight, but save first so the encounter does not surprise you.

### Should I give Diego's List to Lares?

Yes, if you want the stronger flexible route. Show it to Lares before Diego so Lares can use it for New Camp progress.

### How do I Charm Roscoe?

Complete Baal Kagan's cigarette task, choose scrolls as the reward, make sure you have a Charm scroll, then cast it on Roscoe before entering Lares's shack.

### How do I complete Herb Merchant?

Accept the quest from Lares, buy rice schnapps from Silas if Baal Isidro needs convincing, get Isidro's swampweed shipment, sell it through New Camp buyers until you have 400 ore, then return the money to Lares.

### Who buys the Herb Merchant swampweed?

Possible buyers include Sharky, Cronos, Malon, Silas, and individual mercenaries or bandits. You can split sales as long as you return 400 ore to Lares.

### What is The Raid?

The Raid is a Lares task that sends you to Wedge and the raiders on the road between Old Mine and Old Camp. You help distract the guards and trigger the ambush.

### What level do I need to join New Camp?

Reach Level 5 before the final New Camp joining step.
