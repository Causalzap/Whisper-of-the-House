<h2 id="moonlight-peaks-misty-shores-mines-copper-guide">
  Moonlight Peaks Misty Shores, mines, and copper guide
</h2>

**Misty Shores** is one of the first places where Moonlight Peaks can make you feel blocked even when you are following the game normally.

The problem is that three different systems meet here:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>
    <strong>Story progress:</strong> the bridge is cursed, so the area is not open until A Bridge Too Far is handled.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Fishing:</strong> Noel's competition gives the early fishing route.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Mining:</strong> Cave of Echoes becomes the practical answer when Copper Ore or Copper Bars become the bottleneck.
  </li>
</ul>

My rule is:

<strong>open the route first, then farm the resource.</strong>

If I need copper but the bridge is still blocked, mining harder will not help. If I want the fishing rod but have not followed Noel, shop-checking will not help. If I see a huge fish shadow and the beginner rod fails, forcing the same catch will not help.

<h2 id="a-bridge-too-far">
  A Bridge Too Far: how to open the Misty Shores route
</h2>

**A Bridge Too Far** becomes the key route after Orlock's early wine problem.

The short version is that Orlock cannot return home because the bridge to his side has been cursed. The route then sends you through a character chain rather than a tool check.

The practical flow is:

<ol className="my-6 list-decimal space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>finish Orlock's early wine route,</li>
  <li>learn that the bridge is cursed,</li>
  <li>speak with Orlock at The Broken Lamp,</li>
  <li>deal with the 299G tab step,</li>
  <li>follow the Mina return step,</li>
  <li>go to Fiona,</li>
  <li>learn that Fiona cannot fix the bridge alone,</li>
  <li>recruit Sabrina, Luna, and Noel,</li>
  <li>then return to the bridge.</li>
</ol>

This is why I do not treat Misty Shores as a hidden shortcut. If one of those character steps is missing, the bridge remains the blocker.

<h2 id="when-to-leave-mines">
  When to leave the mines
</h2>

A good mine run is not the one where I break every rock. It is the one where I leave with the resource I came for.

I leave when:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>I have enough Copper Ore for the next bar target,</li>
  <li>my inventory is nearly full,</li>

</ul>

I come back later when the pickaxe is stronger, the bag is bigger, or the next quest specifically asks for more ore.

<h2 id="faq">
  Moonlight Peaks Misty Shores, mines, and copper FAQ
</h2>

### How do I unlock Misty Shores in Moonlight Peaks?

Follow Orlock's Wine Scheme into A Bridge Too Far. The cursed bridge route then sends you through Orlock, Fiona, Sabrina, Luna, Noel, and the bridge cutscene.

### What is A Bridge Too Far?

A Bridge Too Far is the quest that resolves the cursed bridge leading toward Misty Shores and the Ambrosia side.

### Why is the Misty Shores bridge still blocked?

You are probably missing a character step in A Bridge Too Far. Check Orlock, Fiona, Sabrina, Luna, Noel, and your quest log.

### Where do I get the fishing rod?

The beginner fishing rod comes from Noel's fishing competition route.

### How many fish do I need for Noel's challenge?

Noel's early competition asks for 3 fish.

### Is Noel's fishing competition a 250 gold bet?

Some routes frame Noel's challenge as a 250 gold wager, so I do not start it without time and inventory space.

### Why did my rod break on a big fish?

The beginner rod is not strong enough for giant fish shadows. Leave those fish for a later rod upgrade.

### Where is Copper Ore in Moonlight Peaks?

Copper Ore is found in Cave of Echoes on the lower-left side of Misty Shores.

### Where is Cave of Echoes?

Cave of Echoes is on the lower-left side of Misty Shores. If you cannot reach it yet, finish the bridge route first.

### How do I make Copper Bars?

Use the Furnace with 4 Copper Ore and 1 Charcoal.

### Should I sell Copper Ore?

Not early. Copper Ore becomes Copper Bars, and bars are needed for quests, crafting, and tool upgrades.

### What ore nodes should I mine first?

Mine large ore clusters first. They can drop 6 ore, while regular nodes usually drop 1 ore.

### Should I buy the inventory upgrade before mining?

Buy it if inventory space is ending mine runs early. The first upgrade costs 2,000 gold and adds 10 slots.
