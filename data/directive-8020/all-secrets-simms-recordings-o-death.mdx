### How many Secrets are in Directive 8020?

There are 50 Secrets.

### How many Simms Recordings are in Directive 8020?

There are 10 Simms Recordings.

### How many O Death collectibles are in Directive 8020?

There are 5 O Death collectibles.

### Are collectibles missable?

Yes. Treat every collectible as run-missable once you leave its section, but recoverable through episode replay / Turning Points cleanup.

### Does Episode 4 have collectibles?

No. This route lists no collectibles in Episode 4.

### What is Secret #35?

Secret #35 is **Love Lost** in Episode 5 — Radio Silence.

### What is the most important Secret for the mimic storyline?

Secret #45 — Duplication Analysis is the most important collectible for the mimic / duplicate storyline.

### What does Williams 2.0 connect to?

Williams 2.0 connects to late-game conspiracy, replacement horror, and the duplicate / identity themes.

### Are Simms Recordings separate from Secrets?
