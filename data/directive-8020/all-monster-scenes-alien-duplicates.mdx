<h2 id="two-eiseles">
  Which Eisele is fake?
</h2>

In the two Eisele scene:

**The left Eisele is fake.**  
**The newcomer is real.**  
**Choose Test instead of Shoot.**

This is the most important duplicate choice in the game. The scene is designed to make you panic, but the safe route is to verify identity instead of reacting emotionally.

The game also tracks this as a real consequence state, not just a throwaway cutscene. That matters because duplicate mistakes can affect later route cleanup.

<h2 id="scanner-verification">
  How to identify a mimic
</h2>

The scanner is the safest way to identify a mimic.

Once the crew realizes the alien can copy voices, appearance, and behavior, emotional recognition stops being reliable. A familiar face can be wrong. A familiar voice can be bait.

Use this rule:

**If the game gives you Test, choose Test. If the game gives you scanner verification, use it.**

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### Which Eisele is fake in Directive 8020?

The left Eisele is fake. The newcomer is real.

### Should I shoot or test in the two Eisele scene?

Choose Test. Shooting immediately is the panic route.

### How do I identify a mimic?

Use scanner verification or Test whenever the game gives you that option. Do not rely only on voice, appearance, or urgency.

### Should I authorize the weapon?

For the safer survival route, refuse the weapon. Weapon branches are better for cleanup routes.

### What does Eisele Double mean?

Eisele Double is a consequence state tied to the duplicate encounter. If you see this state, check the two Eisele scene and make sure you used Test / scanner logic instead of reacting with Shoot.

### Why does Episode 3 matter?

Episode 3 creates the search-party split, power-cell route, sample pressure, and isolation setup that later mimic and survival scenes build on.

### Can you stop Williams from taking the sample?

Not cleanly. Cooper can push back, but Williams still drives the group toward taking the sample.

### Why does Eisele die later?

Check the immediate scene first, then work backward to earlier Stafford / identity pressure and Episode 3 route state.

### Why does Cernan die later?

Check the Cernan / Williams route and earlier pressure states before replaying only the late death scene.

### What does Save Your Souls mean?

It is an outcome / rewind prompt. Use it to go back before a major consequence becomes locked into your route.

### What does Docked / Not Alone mean?

It means the route reaches a docked-style outcome, but an alien, intruder, or duplicate condition is still unresolved.

### What collectible explains the mimic system best?

Secret #45 — Duplication Analysis is the strongest collectible connection to the duplicate storyline.
