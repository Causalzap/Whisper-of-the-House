Starting the train is only the opening step. Later failures usually happen
because the team reaches a location underfueled, injured, separated, or too far
from the locomotive to recover.

The final route adds a second resource problem: each mounted-cannon shot uses
**1 Coal and 2 Rock**. Coal is therefore both train fuel and part of the cannon
cost. Wasting shots during invulnerable phases can leave the train unable to
move when the Warden releases poison.
