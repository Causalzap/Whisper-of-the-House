<h2 id="missable">
  Are any achievements missable?
</h2>

I treat none of the 15 achievements as permanently missable.

After the ending, the completed save remains available for:

- adding missing people and locations;
- entering unused joke or invalid codes;
- marking a Timeline death;
- replaying optional scenes;
- and filling the remaining archive gaps.

I still collect achievements in a sensible order because it reduces cleanup.

### My one-save order

1. Earn Seance and Persistence during the opening setup.
2. Let the three generator achievements unlock through the story.
3. Add every room as it is mentioned.
4. Mark one confirmed death on the Timeline.
5. Solve Person 12 and Person K before switching fully to Admin Access.
6. Use 32-LI-70 after the modern IDs are available.
7. Finish every valid scene.
8. View Scene 00.
9. Use 01-HE-L-P after the ending if it is still missing.

This order keeps the puzzle natural while avoiding a long search through old
save states.

<h2 id="seance-persistence">
  Seance and Persistence
</h2>

### Seance

The first valid reconstruction unlocks Seance.

The opening code is:

```text
01-QU-1-11
```

I earn this naturally while learning the machine.

### Persistence

Enter the same invalid code twice.

The cleanest early method is:

```text
01-QU-1
```

That attempt is missing Person 11, so it cannot be the valid Quail Lane scene.
Enter it, let the machine reject it, and enter the exact same combination a
second time.

I do this before correcting the code to 01-QU-1-11.

The launch rate of **72.9%** shows that many players earned Persistence without
trying because repeating a nearly correct code is one of the most common early
mistakes.

<h2 id="power-achievements">
  Machine Supervisor, Researcher, and Technical Engineer
</h2>

These three achievements are story progression checks:

- **Machine Supervisor** — restore power after the first outage;
- **Researcher** — restore power after the second outage;
- **Technical Engineer** — restore power after the third outage.

I do not try to force an outage.

When the machine loses power:

1. leave the code interface;
2. turn toward the generator beside the machine;
3. interact with it to restart the system;
4. return to the machine only after the antennas and interface come back.

<h2 id="house-tour">
  House Tour
</h2>

House Tour requires at least one viewed scene in every required room.

This is different from merely adding the room under Locations.

I check the Timeline for scenes in:

- Living Room;
- Entrance;
- Study;
- Billiard Room;
- Dining Room;
- Kitchen;
- Eddie's Room;
- Attic;
- Helen's Room;
- Victoria's Room;
- Tony's Room;
- Oswald's Room;
- Martha's Room;
- and the later Chapel route.

If House Tour has not unlocked near the end, I do not replay every scene. I
check the rooms with the fewest obvious main-path memories first:

1. Helen's Room;
2. Martha's Room;
3. Oswald's Room;
4. Tony's Room;
5. Attic;
6. Chapel.

The launch rate was **35.6%**, higher than Full House, which makes sense:
viewing one scene in a room can happen before every location name has been
manually entered.

<h2 id="spectronoeticist">
  Spectronoeticist: how to find the last missing scene
</h2>

Spectronoeticist requires every valid scene in both the historical and modern
timelines.

I do not solve this by randomly testing IDs.

### My cleanup order

1. Confirm Inciting Incident and Not Over Yet are unlocked.
2. Confirm every one of the 16 locations is available.
3. Fill every visible Timeline connection and undiscovered-scene indicator.
4. Recheck the bedrooms, Attic, Chapel, and Wintercote.
5. Complete all modern Admin Access scenes.
6. Enter the final Scene 00 code last.

The modern scenes most likely to be missed are the short single-person or
side-room reconstructions, especially:

```text
28-ST-64
29-BI-73
31-KI-66
32-CH-64
```

I also verify the main modern connections:

```text
27-EN-67-70-74
28-BI-65-66
29-LI-70-74
30-ST-67-74
31-LI-67-70
```

This is not intended as a second complete scene-code list. These are the
modern-day checks I use when every story achievement is complete but
Spectronoeticist still has not appeared.

<h2 id="cleanup-route">
  Fast completed-save cleanup route
</h2>

When the credits have already played, I use this order:

1. Open the achievement list and identify what is actually missing.
2. Use 32-LI-70 for Still Alive.
3. Add L and P, then use 01-HE-L-P.
4. Add any missing location from the 16-code list.
5. Place a skull reminder on Time 01.
6. Recheck 01-ST-12 and 25-WI-K.
7. Fill modern optional scenes.
8. Use 00-LI-3-5-6-7-8-10-12 last.

This avoids damaging the Timeline with unnecessary temporary profiles before
the real Person 12 and Person K deductions are complete.

<h2 id="common-problems">
  Achievement problems and exact fixes
</h2>

### Persistence does not unlock

The second entry must be exactly the same invalid code. Changing one person or
room creates a different failed attempt.

### House Tour is missing but Full House is unlocked

A room can be listed without having a viewed scene. Check the bedrooms, Attic,
and Chapel.

### Full House is missing

Compare the Locations panel with all 16 codes. Wintercote is the usual final
entry.

### Death Note is missing

Viewing a death is not enough. Open Reminders and place the skull marker on the
Timeline.

### Still Alive is missing

Use Admin Access and enter 32-LI-70. Do not use a historical dead profile.

### TYPE HE-L-P is missing

Create literal profiles L and P. Renaming existing story profiles can damage
your clean roster and is unnecessary.

### Hallucination is missing

Use the exact Scene 00 group:

```text
00-LI-3-5-6-7-8-10-12
```

Do not include Person 1.

### Spectronoeticist is missing after Hallucination

An earlier optional scene remains undiscovered. Return to the Timeline and
check modern single-person scenes and rarely used rooms before replaying the
main ending.

<h2 id="faq">
  The Incident at Galley House Achievements FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are there?

There are 15 Steam achievements.

### Can I get every achievement in one save?

Yes. The completed save remains available for scene and machine-code cleanup.

### What is the Still Alive code?

Use **32-LI-70** after Admin Access becomes available.

### What is the TYPE HE-L-P code?

Create profiles L and P, then use **01-HE-L-P**.

### What is the Person 12 code?

Use **01-ST-12** for Rupert Galley.

### What is the Person K code?

Use **25-WI-K** for Katherine Beaumont.

### What is the final Hallucination code?

Use **00-LI-3-5-6-7-8-10-12**.

### Why did Hallucination unlock but not Spectronoeticist?

Scene 00 is complete, but at least one earlier optional scene is still missing.

### Do I need to view a scene in every location for Full House?

No. Full House checks whether every location is unlocked. House Tour checks
viewed rooms.

### Which achievement had the lowest launch completion rate?

TYPE HE-L-P was lowest in the July 16 snapshot at 12.5%, followed by Still
Alive at 14.4% and Spectronoeticist at 14.5%.
