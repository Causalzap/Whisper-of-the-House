<h2 id="common-sticking-points">
  Common sticking points
</h2>

### A scene contains the correct speaker but still fails

Count everyone physically present, not only the speaker.

### A room code looks correct but is unavailable

Open Locations and add the room before trying the reconstruction again.

### A body was found in one room, but that code fails

The discovery location and the remembered death location may be different.

### The Timeline appears complete, but one achievement is missing

Check the two hidden victims, single-person scenes, rarely used rooms, modern
side scenes, and achievement-only inputs.

### Person 12 seems to replace someone in the ordinary roster

It does not. Rupert is an additional earlier profile.

### Person K seems like another numbered guest

She is not inside the house. Katherine uses a letter profile and Wintercote.

### The Time 31 code works, but the modern story still feels unclear

Use All Scene Codes for the remaining inputs and Ending Explained for the
memory-chain logic.

<h2 id="faq">
  The Incident at Galley House Guide FAQ
</h2>

### Which guide should I use if I want as few spoilers as possible?

Start with the **Hints and Scene Solutions Guide**. It uses progressive
reveals, so I can open a gentle hint first and reveal the exact reconstruction
only when I am ready.

### Should I use the Hints Guide or the All Scene Codes page?

Use **Hints and Scene Solutions** when I want to understand why a reconstruction
fails. Use **All Scene Codes and Walkthrough** when I want the complete answer
sheet without further deduction.

### Is there a complete walkthrough with every scene code?

Yes. **All Scene Codes and Walkthrough** contains all 106 valid
reconstructions, including Times 01–26, Person 12, Person K, the modern
investigation, and Scene 00.

### Where can I find the Person 1–11 names and portrait matches?

The **All Scene Codes and Walkthrough** page includes a compact Person 1–11
reference with each character's name, animal portrait, and strongest identifying
clue.

### Are Person 12 and Person K required?

They are not part of the ordinary Person 1–11 roster, and the story can move
forward before both are solved. A complete original investigation and their two
related achievements require `01-ST-12` and `25-WI-K`.

### Which guide should I use for missing achievements?

Use the **Achievements Guide** after completing the story. It separates normal
story unlocks, hidden-person achievements, intentionally invalid codes, Scene
00, and missing-scene cleanup.

### Can I return after the ending to finish missing content?

Yes. The completed save remains available for hidden profiles, missed
