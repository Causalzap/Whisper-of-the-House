<h2 id="first-hour-roadmap">
  First hour roadmap
</h2>

Your first hour in Fatekeeper has a clean route:

```txt
Explore Haven
→ finish portal training
→ craft at the alchemy table
→ enter Ancient Hallways
````

That order matters because each step gives you something practical. Haven lets you check your weapons and skill tree. Portal training teaches block, kick, stamina, and telekinesis. The alchemy table gives you healing or mana support. Ancient Hallways is the first route where keys, side paths, enemies, and early pickups start to matter.

<h2 id="npc-rescue">
  Rescue NPCs and send them back to Haven
</h2>

Do not skip side rooms just because they are not on the main path. Fatekeeper uses Haven as a hub, and rescued NPCs can return there to unlock useful services.

The important beginner loop is:

```txt
check side rooms and optional routes
→ rescue NPCs
→ send them back to Haven
→ unlock new gear shop options or skill point refund support
```

This matters because exploration is not only about loot. A side path can lead to materials, rings, keys, rescued NPCs, new Haven services, or better shop options.

If you are wondering how to unlock more gear choices or how skill refunds work, do not only push the main route. Recheck optional rooms and rescue NPCs when the game gives you the chance.

For most first-time players, use this simple build rule:

```txt
2 health / survival nodes
→ 1 stamina node
→ 3 points into your main weapon or spell route
```

This is not a permanent meta build. It is a safe first-hour structure.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What is the best first-hour route?

Use this route: Haven, portal training, alchemy table, Ancient Hallways. In Ancient Hallways, look for the Ornate Copper Key, Ring of Life, side paths, and NPC rescue opportunities.

### Should I use Blade of the Sentinel or Axe of the Sentinel?

Use Blade of the Sentinel if you want higher listed starting damage. It has 5 attack costs, 5 dash costs, and 30 slash damage. Axe of the Sentinel has the same listed costs but 20 slash damage, making it a simpler one-handed option if that rhythm feels better.

### How much stamina should I keep?

Keep at least 1/3 of your stamina bar before an enemy swing connects. If you cannot dodge or block after attacking, your string was too long.

### When is telekinesis better than attacking?

Use telekinesis when a group is in a corridor, when a dangerous enemy is just outside melee range, when you need time to heal, or when an object is highlighted for search mode.

### How does telekinesis search mode work?

Switch to telekinesis, hold search mode, hover over a valid target, then release to interact. Use it on highlighted objects instead of trying to hit everything with your weapon.

### How do I break enemy guard?

Use kick when an enemy guards. If kick does not create an opening on a specific enemy, stop forcing it and reposition.

### Is there an FOV setting?

If you cannot find an FOV option in your current build, check the display and graphics menus first, then use lower motion settings or more comfortable display settings where available. If you are sensitive to first-person camera movement, take breaks and watch for Early Access updates.

### Should I craft before Ancient Hallways?

Yes. Craft healing first, then mana support if you cast often. Poison, vials, and weapon coatings become more useful once you understand the route.

### Where is the Ornate Copper Key?

The Ornate Copper Key is in the early Ancient Hallways route after you first see the locked copper-key door. Push through the nearby lower / side route, pick up the key, then return to open the locked door.

### Where is Ring of Life?

Ring of Life appears early in Ancient Hallways around the first left-side corridor / ledge section after entering the area. It gives +5 health.

### How do I unlock more gear shop options?

Explore side rooms and rescue NPCs when the game gives you the chance. Rescued NPCs can return to Haven and unlock useful services, including gear shop options or skill point refund support.

### Can I respec or refund skill points?

Early respecs are free at the Sanctuary of First Flame. Later respecs cost a rare resource found through exploration. Treat early points carefully until you know where the sanctuary is in your current build.

### What should I spend my first skill points on?

For most first-time players, use 2 health or survival nodes, 1 stamina node, then 3 points into your main weapon or spell route if those nodes are available.

### What is the biggest beginner mistake?

The biggest mistake is entering Ancient Hallways without preparation. Check weapons, learn portal training tools, craft before leaving Haven, and keep enough stamina to escape after attacking.
