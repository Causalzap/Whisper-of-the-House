<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
To get the **Isle Map** in Mina the Hollower, reach **Ossex**, enter the **Hollower's Guild** through the side route, follow Muriel's lead south to rescue **Rhene** from the **Rebel Barracks** in the **Southern Outskirts**, then return to the Guild.

When you get back, **Drillhardt does not simply appear as a normal vendor**. Look for the moving green patch on the left side of the Guild room, burrow underneath it, and dig him out. After that, buy the **Isle Map** from **Drillhardt** for **500 Bones**.

The short route is: 

**Ossex → Hollower's Guild (enter from the side route) → Southern Outskirts → Rebel Barracks → rescue Rhene → return to Guild → dig out Drillhardt → buy Isle Map**.

</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="buy-map">
  Dig out Drillhardt and buy the Isle Map
</h2>

After Rhene is rescued and you return to the Hollower's Guild, do not expect Drillhardt to be standing at a normal counter. Check the left side of the Guild room for the moving green spiral / patch near the wall. If vines or debris are blocking the spot, clear them first, then burrow underneath the moving patch to dig Drillhardt out.

Once Drillhardt is revealed, he becomes the Guild vendor. Buy the **Isle Map** from him for **500 Bones**.

<h2 id="how-map-works">
  What the Isle Map actually does
</h2>

The Isle Map is helpful, but it is not a full modern map system. Do not expect it to mark every exact room, treasure chest or shortcut.

<GuideTable
caption="What the Isle Map does and does not do."
columns={mapUseColumns}
rows={mapUseRows}
/>

The map is best used as a direction tool. It tells you where the important generator routes are, but you still need to read signs, check the newspaper, remember landmarks and explore side paths.

If you want collectible tracking, look for the **Enhanced Map** upgrade from **Drillhardt** later. The base Isle Map does not track every item or show everything you missed.

This is why the map feels different from a typical dungeon map. It does not remove exploration. It just keeps you from feeling completely blind.

<h2 id="when-to-buy-map">
  Should you buy the map before other upgrades?
</h2>

Yes, in most cases. The **Isle Map** should be one of your first 500-Bone purchases because it solves a bigger problem than raw stats: direction.

Early Bones are tempting to spend on weapons, outfits or other upgrades, but the map changes how the first half of the island feels. Buy it before you commit too much money elsewhere.

Use this priority:

<ol className="my-6 list-decimal space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Buy the Isle Map from Drillhardt.</li>
  <li>Check the Tenebrous Times for your next soft direction hint.</li>
  <li>Put early Bone Ups into Attack and Defense.</li>
  <li>Buy survival upgrades that help you stay alive.</li>
  <li>Use the Isle Map to aim toward Eastern Heath and Queensbury Crypt if you want the smoothest first major route.</li>
  <li>Only buy a new weapon if your starting weapon feels bad.</li>
  <li>Start thinking about train funding after your basics are stable.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### Where is the map in Mina the Hollower?

The Isle Map is sold by Drillhardt in the Hollower's Guild after you rescue Rhene from the Rebel Barracks and dig Drillhardt out inside the Guild.

### How much does the Isle Map cost?

The Isle Map costs 500 Bones. It is one of the best early purchases because it helps you understand where the generator routes are.

### How do I unlock Drillhardt?

Enter the Hollower's Guild, follow the Guild lead south for Rhene, rescue her from the Rebel Barracks, then return to the Guild. Look for the moving green patch on the left side of the Guild room, burrow underneath it, and dig Drillhardt out.

### Where is Rhene?

Rhene is held in the Rebel Barracks in the Southern Outskirts. Leave Ossex through the southern gate, follow the route left and upward, then work through the Kear route, spike switch and Plasma Vial door.

### Why can't I enter the Hollower's Guild?

The front entrance is blocked at first. Go around the side of the building, break through the crates and enter from the side route.

### Why can't I find Drillhardt after rescuing Rhene?

He is hidden under a moving green patch inside the Hollower's Guild. Burrow underneath the patch and dig him out before trying to buy the map.

### Does the Isle Map show every room?

No. The Isle Map gives broad direction for the island and generator routes. It does not show every exact room, treasure chest or secret path.

### What is the Enhanced Map?

The Enhanced Map is a later Drillhardt upgrade that helps with collectible tracking. The base Isle Map is mainly for route direction and does not track everything you missed.

### Where do I check the Isle Map?

You check the Isle Map from your Underlab. Treat Underlab stops as planning points before choosing your next route.

### Should I buy the map before upgrading?

For most players, yes. Buy the Isle Map early, then start putting Bones into Attack, Defense and survival upgrades.

### What should I do after buying the map?

Check the Tenebrous Times, spend or protect loose Bones, then use the Isle Map to aim toward Eastern Heath and Queensbury Crypt if you want the smoothest first major route.

### Can I miss the map?

You can delay buying it, but it is not a one-time pickup that disappears. If you skipped it, return to the Hollower's Guild after unlocking Drillhardt and buy it for 500 Bones.
