<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
To unlock train fast travel in **Mina the Hollower**, donate Bones at any train station until the shared funding goal reaches **10,000 Bones**. This is a **shared train-network goal**, not a separate 10,000-Bone payment at every station.

The first station most players should learn is **Ossex: Bowery Station**. Once the train is running, you can travel between the five stations and reach destinations such as **Coltrane Peak**.

Mirrors are a separate fast travel and progression system. They lead toward **Astral Orrery**, and several mirrors require specific room triggers or boss progress. Do not just “remember mirrors” — track where each one is and activate it when you find it. 
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="how-to-unlock-fast-travel">
  How to unlock fast travel
</h2>

Fast travel in Mina the Hollower has three separate pieces: the **train**, the **mirrors**, and the **Poppit Shop holes**. The train is the main region-to-region travel system. Mirrors are tied to **Astral Orrery** progress. Poppit holes are a smaller shortcut system around the areas bordering Ossex.

<h2 id="station-locations">
  All train station locations
</h2>

There are five train stations to know. If you are trying to unlock fast travel, do not just look for “major regions” in general — use the station names and region clues below.

<GuideTable
caption="All Mina the Hollower train station locations."

The most important early station is **Ossex: Bowery Station**. It teaches you how the donation system works and gives you a clear place to start funding the replacement train.

The easiest station to miss is **Bone Beach Train Station**. It is tied to the Sandy Station route in Sandfalls. If you are searching from **Sifted Sands**, head toward the bottom-right path and use the **TNT** to open the way forward.

<h2 id="how-much-cost">

<h2 id="astral-orrery">
  How mirrors unlock Astral Orrery
</h2>

The mirror system eventually points toward **Astral Orrery**. This area is tied to mirror progress rather than normal map exploration, so do not expect it to behave like a regular region marker.

The practical rule is:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Find mirrors in major regions.</li>
  <li>Enter them and activate the switches inside their Astral Orrery routes.</li>
  <li>Use the large Ossex mirror as the final return / access point when the path is ready.</li>
  <li>"If Astral Orrery is not accessible yet, check the mirror table above and confirm which regions you have not yet activated.</li>
</ul>

This is why mirrors matter. They are not just optional scenery. They connect regions, switches and hidden progress into one larger route.

<h2 id="when-to-fund">

</h2>

Train funding is useful, but it competes with everything else you want to buy. If you put all your Bones into the train too early, you may be underpowered. If you ignore it too long, travel becomes tedious.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How do you unlock fast travel in Mina the Hollower?

Fund the replacement train by donating Bones at train station donation boxes. Once the shared 10,000-Bone goal is reached, train travel opens across the network.

### Is the 10,000-Bone train cost shared?

Yes. The 10,000-Bone train fund is shared across all stations. You do not need to donate 10,000 Bones at every station.

### Where is the first train station?

The easiest early station to learn is Ossex: Bowery Station. It is north of Bowery, near the Western Hills exit.

### What are all train station locations?

The five stations are Ossex: Bowery, Backwaters: Shanty Swamp, Kindlewood: Farm Crossing, Sandfalls: Sandy Station, and Coltrane Peak: Frigid Station.

### How do I get to Coltrane Peak?

Once the train is funded, Coltrane Peak becomes a train destination through Frigid Station. You can also find the station after scaling the mountain north of Eastern Heath.

### What happens after I select a train ticket?

Board quickly. If you select a ticket and then linger too long, the train can leave without you.

### Is the train required for progress?

Yes, eventually. The train is not just a convenience unlock; it becomes important for reaching and revisiting key areas.

### Where are the mirror locations?

Important mirrors include Radiant Manor: Grand Hall, Queensbury Crypt: Statue Head Hall, Nox's Bayou: Moonlit Path, Septemburg: Farm House, Bone Beach: Worm's Back, and Coltrane Peak: Icebound Cavern.

### What do mirrors do?

Mirrors connect to Astral Orrery progress. Enter them, activate their routes or switches, and track which regions you have completed.

### How do I unlock Astral Orrery?

Astral Orrery is tied to mirror progress. Find mirrors across major regions, enter them, activate their routes, then use the large Ossex mirror once the path is ready.

### Should I fund the train early?

Not before your essentials. Buy the Isle Map and upgrade Attack, Defense and survival first. After that, start donating spare or protected Bones to the train.

### Should I use Bone Stones for train funding?

Yes, Bone Stones are useful for saving toward the train because they protect value better than loose Bones while you move through risky routes.

### What is Poppit Shop fast travel?

After finding all three Poppit Shops, Grinnsly's lair gives you holes that send Mina to Western Wilds: Molten Foundry, Southern Outskirts: Commons, and Eastern Heath: Grassland.

### How do I avoid losing Bones while saving for the train?

Use Bone Stones, spend before dangerous routes and avoid carrying more loose Bones than you are willing to lose.

### How do I reach Bone Beach Train Station?

Go to the Sandfalls region near Sifted Sands, head toward the bottom-right route, and use the TNT to open the station path.
