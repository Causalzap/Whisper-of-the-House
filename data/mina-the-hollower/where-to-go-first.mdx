<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
After reaching **Ossex** in Mina the Hollower, your first goal should be to set up the **Hollower's Guild**, rescue **Rhene**, dig out **Drillhardt**, and buy the **Isle Map**. After that, the best first generator route for most players is **Queensbury Crypt** through **Eastern Heath**.

The recommended generator order is:

**Ossex setup → Queensbury Crypt → Nox's Bayou → Kindlewood / Septemburg → Sandfalls → Bone Beach → Astral Orrery**.

The first four generator routes are flexible, but Queensbury Crypt is recommended first because it has a smoother early difficulty curve and leads to **Proto Spark**, one of the best early survival tools.

<h2 id="where-to-go-first">
  Where to go first after Ossex
</h2>

Once Mina reaches **Ossex**, the game opens up enough that it is easy to feel lost. The most important thing to understand is that “where to go first” has two answers:

First, handle the **Ossex setup**: Hollower's Guild, Rhene, Drillhardt and the Isle Map.

Second, start your first generator route with **Queensbury Crypt** through **Eastern Heath**.

<h2 id="head-east">
  Go east first: Eastern Heath and Queensbury Crypt
</h2>

The first four generators can be done in any order, but **Queensbury Crypt** is the best first recommendation for most players. It is easier to route from Ossex, the difficulty curve is more forgiving, and it gives you a chance to get **Proto Spark**.

Leave **Ossex** through the eastern exit, then follow the route into **Eastern Heath** until the path starts pointing you toward **Queensbury Crypt**.

<h2 id="proto-spark">
  Do not miss Proto Spark in Queensbury Crypt
</h2>

One reason Queensbury Crypt is such a strong first route is **Proto Spark**. It can save Mina from death once, which makes early bosses and long rooms much less punishing.

You get Proto Spark through the Duke side route in Queensbury Crypt. The important quick reminder is: in the red-carpet room before the Ancestral Chamber, take the left path, spend the required vials on the side route, find the Duke and finish the escort after the boss.

<h2 id="after-queensbury-crypt">
  Where to go after Queensbury Crypt
</h2>

After Queensbury Crypt, return to Ossex instead of immediately throwing yourself into the next far region. Spend or protect your Bones, check the Tenebrous Times, then decide your next route from the Isle Map.

For most players, the next broad direction is **Nox's Bayou** through the western Backwaters route. From there, you can keep following the recommended generator order toward **Kindlewood / Septemburg**, **Sandfalls**, **Bone Beach**, and finally **Astral Orrery**.

<h2 id="what-if-wrong-way">
  What if you went the wrong way?
</h2>

You probably did not ruin your save. Mina the Hollower expects you to poke at routes, back out, spend Bones and come back stronger.

caption="What to do if you went the wrong way."

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### Where should I go first after Ossex?

First finish the Ossex setup: enter the Hollower's Guild, rescue Rhene, dig out Drillhardt and buy the Isle Map. After that, head east through Eastern Heath toward Queensbury Crypt.

### What is the recommended dungeon order in Mina the Hollower?

The recommended order is Ossex setup, Queensbury Crypt, Nox's Bayou, Kindlewood / Septemburg, Sandfalls, Bone Beach and Astral Orrery.

### Can I do the generators in any order?

The first four generator routes are flexible, but Queensbury Crypt is the safest first recommendation for most players because it is easier to route from Ossex and leads to Proto Spark.

### Why should I do Queensbury Crypt first?

Queensbury Crypt has a smoother early difficulty curve and gives access to Proto Spark through the Duke side route, which makes later deaths less punishing.

### Where do I go after Queensbury Crypt?

Return to Ossex first, spend or protect your Bones, check the Tenebrous Times, then move toward Nox's Bayou through the western Backwaters route.

### Should I buy the Isle Map before the first generator?

Yes. The Isle Map only costs 500 Bones and gives enough direction to make your first generator route much easier to understand.

### What if I went the wrong way?

Back out, return to Ossex, spend or protect your Bones, upgrade if needed and choose another route from the Isle Map. Going the wrong way does not ruin your save.

### Is Bone Beach an early route?

No. Bone Beach is better treated as a later route because its access is more specific and tied to later travel / route progression.

### How do I unlock Astral Orrery?

Astral Orrery is tied to mirror progress. Find and activate mirror routes across major regions, then use the large Ossex mirror when the path is ready.

### Should I get Proto Spark before leaving Queensbury Crypt?

Yes, if you can. Proto Spark is one of the best early safety tools because it saves Mina from death once.

### Do I need fast travel for the full route order?

Eventually, yes. Train stations and mirror progress become more important as the island opens up, especially for later areas such as Bone Beach, Coltrane Peak and Astral Orrery.
