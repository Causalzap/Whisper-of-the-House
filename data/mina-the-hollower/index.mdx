<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
Start **Mina the Hollower** by reaching **Ossex**, entering the **Hollower's Guild** from the side route, rescuing **Rhene**, digging out **Drillhardt**, and buying the **Isle Map** for 500 Bones.

After that, the best first generator route for most players is **Queensbury Crypt** through **Eastern Heath**. The first few routes are flexible, but Queensbury Crypt is the smoothest early recommendation because it leads toward **Proto Spark**, one of the best early safety tools.

For most new players, the early plan is:

**Isle Map → Attack / Defense → survival → Queensbury Crypt → Proto Spark → broader route planning**.

<h2 id="where-to-go-first">
  Go east first for Queensbury Crypt
</h2>

After the Isle Map, head out of **Ossex** through the eastern route into **Eastern Heath**, then continue toward **Queensbury Crypt**. The first four generator routes are flexible, but this is the cleanest first recommendation for most players.

Queensbury Crypt is also where you can work toward **Proto Spark**, a trinket that saves Mina from death once.

Need the full dungeon order after Ossex? Read the <Link href="/mina-the-hollower/where-to-go-first">Where to Go First and Dungeon Order guide</Link>. Need the full Proto Spark route? Read the <Link href="/mina-the-hollower/best-early-trinkets">Best Early Trinkets guide</Link>.

<h2 id="starting-weapon">
  Pick a starting weapon that matches how you play
</h2>

For most beginners, **Nightstar** is the safest starting weapon because its reach makes four-direction combat easier. **Whisper and Vesper** are better if you like fast close-range pressure, while **Blaststrike Maul** rewards charge timing and roll control.

You can try the starting weapons before committing, and you can buy other weapons later, so do not restart just because the first weapon feels awkward.

Need the full comparison? Read the <Link href="/mina-the-hollower/best-starting-weapon">Best Starting Weapon guide</Link>.

<h2 id="early-trinkets">
  Grab early trinkets that solve real problems
</h2>

Do not chase every trinket immediately. Pick the one that fixes your current problem: dying, running out of healing, losing Bones, bad terrain or weak damage.

For most players, the first major target is **Proto Spark** from Queensbury Crypt. Other strong early picks include **Primed Vial Pouch**, **Steady Soles**, **Bell of Grace**, **Chain Capacitor** and **Plasma Funnel**.

Need routes and setup advice? Read the <Link href="/mina-the-hollower/best-early-trinkets">Best Early Trinkets guide</Link>. If you want every Trinket, build recommendation and 100% cleanup checklist, use the <Link href="/mina-the-hollower/all-trinkets-guide">All Trinkets Guide</Link>.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What should I do first in Mina the Hollower?

Reach Ossex, enter the Hollower's Guild from the side route, rescue Rhene, dig out Drillhardt and buy the Isle Map. After that, head east toward Eastern Heath and Queensbury Crypt.

### What is the best first generator route?

Queensbury Crypt is the best first generator route for most players. It is easier to route from Ossex and leads toward Proto Spark.

### Do I need the Isle Map early?

Yes. The Isle Map only costs 500 Bones and makes the early routes much easier to understand.

### Where do I get the Isle Map?

Buy it from Drillhardt in the Hollower's Guild after rescuing Rhene from the Rebel Barracks and digging Drillhardt out inside the Guild.

### What should I spend Bones on first?

Buy the Isle Map first, then prioritize Attack, Defense and survival upgrades. Save weapons, sidearms and train funding for later unless you have a specific reason.

### What is the best starting weapon?

Nightstar is the safest starting weapon for most beginners because its reach makes early combat more forgiving. Read the starting weapon guide if you want a full comparison.

### What early trinket should I get first?

Proto Spark is the strongest early safety trinket because it saves Mina from death once. It is tied to the Duke route in Queensbury Crypt.

### Where can I find all Trinkets in Mina the Hollower?

Use the <Link href="/mina-the-hollower/all-trinkets-guide">All Trinkets Guide</Link> for all 60 Trinkets, best builds, easy-to-miss rewards, region cleanup, acquisition types and a complete checklist.

### How do I farm Bones early?

Start with safe Bone Stones and short starter loops. For serious farming later, use Elizabeth's Deboning Wand, Stolenoid and Mourner's Mile.

### When should I unlock fast travel?

Do not rush train funding before the map and core upgrades. Once your basics are stable, start saving toward the shared 10,000-Bone train fund.

### What if I went the wrong way?

Back out, spend or protect your Bones, check the Tenebrous Times and use the Isle Map to choose the next route. Going the wrong way does not ruin your save.
