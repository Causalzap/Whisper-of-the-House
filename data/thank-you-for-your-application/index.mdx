</h2>

This **Thank You For Your Application guide** is the hub for the full guide set: beginner survival, resume rules, Thanks Letters, endings, achievements, final evaluation, and job-hopping answers.

The game looks simple at first because you drag resumes into a green pass folder or red reject folder. The real difficulty comes from changing workday rules, forged documents, exact Thanks Letter reasons, rent and bills, C89's breakdown index, A02 apartment choices, and late-game route locks.

<h2 id="faq">
  Thank You For Your Application guide FAQ
</h2>

### Which guide should I read first?

Start with the Beginner Guide if you are new. Use the Resume Rules & Thanks Letter Guide once you start losing money from wrong approvals, wrong rejections, or missing Thanks Letter reasons.

### How many endings are in Thank You For Your Application?

The game advertises **six final endings**. The Endings Guide also tracks failure and punishment states that can end or lock a run before a clean final ending.

### How many achievements are in Thank You For Your Application?

There are **15 Steam achievements**. Use the Achievements Guide for hidden achievements, route achievements, overtime, A02, and cleanup order.

### How do Thanks Letters work?

After Thanks Letters become required, select the exact failed requirement or invalid field, generate the Thanks Letter, attach it to the resume, then send the resume to the red reject folder.

### What should I check first during work?

Open the Job Requirements tab. It shows which school, document, internship, report, and breakdown-index rules are active that day. Read it before calling any candidate.

### What is the breakdown index limit?

Candidate breakdown-index limits can change by day. Early report checks can use 40%, and later workdays can show 35. C89's own breakdown danger also uses 40 on Day 4 and 35 after Day 4.

### Does Thank You For Your Application have Subsidy Mode?

Yes. Subsidy Mode gives 30P per day and removes the interview time barrier, making it easier to learn document checks and story routes.

### Does the game have manual saves?

Treat it as an automatic-checkpoint game, not a free manual multi-save game. Check your Load / Continue point before testing risky achievements, overtime, or late ending branches.

### Does this guide cover the latest patch?

This hub covers the launch version through Patch 1.0.4. If later patches change choice wording or route behavior, follow the guide’s route meaning and recheck the updated page notes.

### Where are the job-hopping answers?

Use the Final Evaluation & Job-Hopping Answers guide. It gives the four-answer chain and explains why wrong job-hopping answers fail.
