<h2 id="thank-you-for-your-application-endings-guide">
  Thank You For Your Application endings guide
</h2>

This **Thank You For Your Application endings guide** is built for route control. The game has six advertised final endings, but a normal player can also hit several failure or punishment states before reaching a clean final route.

That difference matters. **Job for Life**, **Escape**, **The Meaning of Work**, **Refuse Permanent Employment**, **Illegal Layoff**, and **Failed Performance Review** are late-route outcomes. **Bills Ending**, **Residence Failure**, **Mental Breakdown**, **Overtime Death**, **Illegal Activity**, and **Probation Failure** are run-ending or route-locking states you still need to avoid or test carefully.

<h2 id="escape-ending">
  Escape Ending
</h2>

The Escape route starts from **work-shift mail**, not apartment mail.

On Day 27, open K88's Confidential Mail during the work shift and reply:

<strong>I agree to leave</strong>

Then, on Day 29, use the apartment exit and choose:

<strong>Leave apartment</strong>

<h2 id="day-30-final-evaluation">
  Day 30 final evaluation routes
</h2>

Day 30 is where clean performance routes split. If your final rating is A or B and you have no recorded violations, the permanent-employment choice becomes the important branch.

If your file has a recorded H11 scanner violation or shock misuse, that can override a normal clean route and push you toward **Illegal Layoff**. If your rating is C or D, the route points toward **Failed Performance Review** instead.

<h2 id="job-for-life">
  Job for Life

**Job for Life** requires a clean Day 30 final evaluation and accepting permanent employment.

Use this route if you want the stable company ending:

  <li>Avoid recorded H11 scanner violations.</li>
  <li>Avoid shock misuse.</li>

<h2 id="refuse-permanent-employment">
  Refuse Permanent Employment
</h2>

This route uses the same clean Day 30 setup as **Job for Life**, but you refuse the final offer instead of accepting it.

Use this route if:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>your final rating is A or B,</li>
  <li>you have no recorded H11 scanner violation,</li>
  <li>you have no shock misuse problem,</li>
  <li>and you want the refusal branch instead of the company-stability branch.</li>
</ul>

Keep a checkpoint before Day 30 so you can test both accept and refuse from the same clean final evaluation.

<h2 id="illegal-layoff">
  Illegal Layoff
</h2>

**Illegal Layoff** is controlled by recorded violations, not only rating. The two major red flags are:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>H11 scanner violation,</li>
  <li>shock misuse.</li>
</ul>

If you want clean final routes, avoid both. If you are testing Illegal Layoff, do it from a checkpoint you are willing to abandon.

<h2 id="failed-performance-review">
  Failed Performance Review
</h2>

**Failed Performance Review** is the route for a weak final rating. Use the rating table above: C or D points away from the clean permanent-employment routes.

This is different from Illegal Layoff. A bad rating means your work performance failed the final review. A recorded violation means your file can be punished even if your general work score would otherwise be high enough.

<h2 id="job-hopping-failure">
  Job-Hopping Failure
</h2>

Job-Hopping Failure happens when you enter the job-hopping final interview but give a wrong answer.

Because the answer chain is strict, keep a checkpoint before the final interview. Do not mix this route with **What’s the point of working?** style answers. That achievement points toward a no-purpose work answer, while job-hopping success wants loyal-worker language.

<h2 id="illegal-activity-ending">
  Illegal Activity
</h2>

The **Illegal Activity** route is tied to the Day 24 black-market / Data Cleaning Module setup.

The safe rule is simple:

<strong>If you unlock the black-market route, buy the Data Cleaning Module before ending Day 24.</strong>

If you end Day 24 without buying it, the run can be pushed into Illegal Activity.

Use a checkpoint before ending Day 24 if you are testing this branch or trying to keep a clean A02 route.

<h2 id="mental-breakdown">
  Mental Breakdown
</h2>

Mental Breakdown happens when C89 ends a day over the breakdown-index limit.

The important thresholds are:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li><strong>Day 4:</strong> 40.</li>

These are C89 survival thresholds, not candidate-screening thresholds. Candidate Psychological Evaluation Report rules can also use breakdown-index checks, such as a candidate rule requiring the value not to exceed 40%.

<h2 id="bills-and-residence-failure">
  Bills Ending and Residence Failure
</h2>

Bills and residence payments are separate dangers.

**Bills Ending** happens when normal rent or bills go overdue. **Residence Failure** is tied to residence / certificate payments and whether you have enough money for automatic payment.

The safe habit is:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>check bills before shopping,</li>
  <li>keep money reserved for residence-related payments,</li>
  <li>do not spend down to zero after a good workday,</li>
  <li>and confirm payment status before sleeping.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="overtime-death">
  Overtime Death
</h2>

Overtime Death happens when you work overtime for five consecutive days.

Patch 1.0.4 added an extra automatic save on the fourth overtime day to avoid a dead save file. That helps with the overtime trap, but it does not make the route safe for a normal ending plan.

If you only want endings, do not stack five overtime days. If you are also cleaning up **Work Life Not Balanced**, use a route you are willing to abandon.

<h2 id="faq">
  Thank You For Your Application endings FAQ
</h2>

### How many endings are in Thank You For Your Application?

The official launch note says the game has six final endings. This guide also tracks extra failure and punishment states that can end or lock a run before a clean final ending.

### How do I get The Meaning of Work ending?

Follow A02's route, reach Day 29 with breakdown index above 20, choose Link consciousness, then choose **(Do not reach out)**.

### How do I get Escape ending?

On Day 27, reply **I agree to leave** to K88's work-shift Confidential Mail. On Day 29, choose **Leave apartment** from the apartment exit.

### How do I get Job for Life?

Reach Day 30 with an A or B final rating, avoid H11 scanner violations and shock misuse, then accept permanent employment.

### What causes Illegal Layoff?

A recorded H11 scanner violation or shock misuse can push the final evaluation into Illegal Layoff.

### What rating causes Failed Performance Review?

A C or D final rating causes Failed Performance Review. The rating table in this guide shows the exact thresholds.

### How do I avoid Illegal Activity?

If you unlock the black-market route, buy the Data Cleaning Module before ending Day 24.

### How do I avoid Mental Breakdown?

Do not end the day over the breakdown-index limit. Day 4 uses 40, and after Day 4 the limit becomes 35.

### What are the job-hopping answers?

Use the four-answer chain listed in this guide: contribute to the Minato Residents' Association, feel excited to work, choose Minato Communications for better prospects, and promise cross-department teamwork.

### Can I get all endings on one route?

Not cleanly. Use checkpoints around Day 17, Day 24, Day 27, Day 29, Day 30, and the job-hopping final interview so you can branch without replaying the whole game.
