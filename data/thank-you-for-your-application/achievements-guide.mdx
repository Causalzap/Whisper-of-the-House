<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
There are **15 achievements** in **Thank You For Your Application**. Three of them are shown by Steam as **Hidden achievement** before unlock: **Brain in a Vat**, **Work Life Not Balanced**, and **I Refuse to Answer**.

The game uses automatic checkpoint saves, not a free manual multi-save system. Your “checkpoint” is the day-start or route autosave the game gives you. Before Patch 1.0.4, a five-day overtime streak could create a dead-save problem. Patch 1.0.4 added an extra automatic save on the fourth overtime day to protect that specific risk.

For 100%, keep your main route clean for **Job for Life**, **Escape**, **The Meaning of Work**, and **A New Job for Life**. Use checkpoint cleanup for **I’m the Worst**, **Approve All**, **Reject All**, **Attitude Adjustment**, **Work Life Not Balanced**, and **I Refuse to Answer**.

<h2 id="thank-you-for-your-application-achievements-guide">
  Thank You For Your Application achievements guide
</h2>

This **Thank You For Your Application achievements guide** is built around route safety. Some achievements are safe during normal play. Others can damage your rating, record a violation, fail probation, or push your run into a bad ending.

The most important thing to understand first is the save system. Treat the game as an automatic-checkpoint game. Do not assume you can freely create several manual saves before every risky choice. If the game has already advanced past a bad choice, your available checkpoint may also have advanced.

<h2 id="checkpoint-save-warning">
  Checkpoint save warning: do not test risky achievements blindly
</h2>

**Thank You For Your Application** uses automatic checkpoint saves rather than a free manual multi-save system. Your checkpoint is usually the day-start or route-state autosave the game gives you, so do not assume you can freely go back before every choice.

This matters most for risky achievements:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li><strong>I’m the Worst</strong> and <strong>I Refuse to Answer</strong> should be tested from the probation review checkpoint.</li>
  <li><strong>Approve All</strong>, <strong>Reject All</strong>, and <strong>Attitude Adjustment</strong> should be tested from a start-of-workday checkpoint.</li>
  <li><strong>Work Life Not Balanced</strong> should not be tested on your clean route, especially on builds before Patch 1.0.4.</li>
  <li><strong>Escape</strong>, <strong>The Meaning of Work</strong>, <strong>Job for Life</strong>, and <strong>A New Job for Life</strong> need late-route checkpoints because one missed mail or wrong answer can lock the route.</li>
</ul>

Patch 1.0.4 changed the most dangerous overtime case: when you choose overtime on the fourth overtime day, the game now adds an extra automatic save to avoid a dead save file. Treat that as protection for the overtime issue only. It does not mean every achievement route has free rollback protection.

If you are on an earlier version, treat <strong>Work Life Not Balanced</strong> as genuinely save-destroying, not just risky. Do not test five consecutive overtime days on your main route.

<h2 id="the-meaning-of-work">
  The Meaning of Work: continue from Brain in a Vat
</h2>

**The Meaning of Work** continues from the same A02 route used for **Brain in a Vat**. Do not repeat the early A02 setup from scratch; once A02's secret route is active, focus on the Day 29 branch.

Continue like this:

<ol className="my-6 list-decimal space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Reach Day 29 with the A02 route still active.</li>
  <li>Keep your breakdown index above 20.</li>

Do not choose **(Take A01's hand)** if you are aiming for **The Meaning of Work**. That branch points toward **Job for Life** instead.

<h2 id="whats-the-point-of-working">

This is valuable to separate from the job-hopping route:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">

Use a late-route checkpoint if you still need both achievements.

<h2 id="work-life-not-balanced">
  Work Life Not Balanced: five overtime days
</h2>

**Work Life Not Balanced** is one of the three Steam-hidden achievements. It unlocks by working overtime for five days in a row.

This is the achievement where save behavior matters most. Before Patch 1.0.4, a five-day overtime streak could create a dead-save situation. Patch 1.0.4 added an extra automatic save on the fourth overtime day to reduce that risk.

If you are on Patch 1.0.4 or later, still avoid doing this on your clean route. If you are on an earlier version, treat this achievement as genuinely dangerous and do not test it unless you are ready to restart or abandon that route.

<h2 id="faq">
  Thank You For Your Application achievements FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are in Thank You For Your Application?

There are **15 achievements**.

### How many hidden achievements are there?

There are **3 Steam-hidden achievements**: Brain in a Vat, Work Life Not Balanced, and I Refuse to Answer.

### Does Thank You For Your Application have manual saves?

The game uses automatic checkpoint saves rather than free manual multi-save routing. Check your Load / Continue point before testing risky achievements.

### How do I unlock Brain in a Vat?

Follow A02's apartment route, connect to A02's consciousness, and continue the data-route choices to discover A02's secret.

### How do I unlock The Meaning of Work?

Continue from the A02 route. On Day 29, keep breakdown index above 20, choose Link consciousness, then choose **(Do not reach out)**.

### How do I unlock A New Job for Life?

Reach the job-hopping final interview and give the correct answer chain listed in this guide.

### How do I unlock Work Life Not Balanced?

Work overtime for five days in a row. On builds before Patch 1.0.4, this can be save-destroying, so do not test it on your main route.

### Should I do Approve All, Reject All, or Attitude Adjustment on my main route?

No. Use a start-of-workday checkpoint because these achievements can hurt rating, pay, or final-route safety.
