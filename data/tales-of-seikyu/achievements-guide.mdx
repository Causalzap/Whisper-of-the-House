<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
There are **36 achievements** in **Tales of Seikyu**. The easy ones unlock naturally from your first monster, furniture, crop, dish, and early story progress. The achievements that actually need planning are **Free to Roam**, **Inari is Pleased**, **Move Over, Yohji**, **Oh Kappa, My Kappa**, **Something Fishy**, **That’s a Big One**, **Nioh’s Knockout**, **Giant Green Thumb**, **Animal Whisperer**, **Yokai-About-Town**, **Happily Ever After**, and **Best of the Best**.

Start tracking **shrines, relic waypoints, shrine offerings, locked ruins rooms, fish species, Kappa clues, crop variety, giant crop types, ranch animals, friendship, and festival dates** early. Those are the routes that become annoying if you ignore them until the end.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="achievements-guide">
  Tales of Seikyu achievements guide
</h2>

Tales of Seikyu achievements split into two groups.

The first group is natural. You will unlock them by learning the game: defeat a monster, build furniture, cook a dish, harvest your first crop, and progress the early story.

The second group is where planning matters. These achievements ask you to track travel points, offerings, ruins rooms, rare fish, hidden losses, giant crops, ranch animals, friendships, festivals, and marriage.

Use this page as a route planner, not just a checkbox list. If you are still in the first week, start with the <Link href="/tales-of-seikyu/beginner-guide">Beginner Guide</Link>. If your current blocker is shrines or ruins, use the <Link href="/tales-of-seikyu/fox-ruins-guide">Fox Ruins Guide</Link>. For relationship achievements, use the <Link href="/tales-of-seikyu/romance-gifts-marriage-guide">Romance, Gifts, and Marriage Guide</Link>.

<h2 id="simple-achievements">
  Simple achievements you do not need to overthink
</h2>

These achievements are low-risk and should unlock naturally if you play normally:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li><strong>First Blood</strong> — defeat your first monster.</li>
  <li><strong>Move Over, Sasaki</strong> — build your first furniture item.</li>
  <li><strong>Fire Up, Feast On</strong> — cook one dish.</li>
  <li><strong>It’s Not A Weed!</strong> — harvest your first crop.</li>
  <li><strong>First Boss Blood</strong> — defeat your first boss.</li>
  <li><strong>It Always Starts In A Tavern</strong> — open the tavern through town progress.</li>
</ul>

The only advice here is not to rush combat or crafting when you are unprepared. Bring food, keep materials, and let these unlock while your farm and town route develop.

Track these:

<h2 id="faq">
  Tales of Seikyu achievements FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are in Tales of Seikyu?

There are 36 achievements in the current 1.0 Steam achievement list.

### Which achievements are hidden?

The current list marks Nioh’s Knockout and Giant Green Thumb as hidden achievements. Nioh’s Knockout asks you to be defeated by a Nioh, while Giant Green Thumb asks you to harvest 30 kinds of giant crops.

### Can I miss an achievement permanently?

Most achievements look like long-term cleanup goals, but Nioh’s Knockout is the one to treat carefully because it asks you to lose to Nioh. Make a manual save before the Nioh route.

### Should I unlock Nioh’s Knockout before Good Riddance, Nioh?

Yes, that is the safest route. Save before the fight, lose once for Nioh’s Knockout, then reload and win for Good Riddance, Nioh.

### Do bought fish count for Something Fishy?

Do not rely on bought fish unless you see the achievement progress trigger. The achievement says to catch 30 kinds of fish, so the safest route is to actually catch new fish species.

### Do I need Tengu Form for Free to Roam?

Tengu Form helps outdoor cleanup, but it does not replace shrine and waypoint activation. You still need to interact with all required travel points.

### Do I need Tengu Form for Move Over, Yohji?

Tengu Form can speed up route cleanup, but 75 ruins rooms still depend on doors, offerings, puzzles, waypoints, bosses, and other form gates.

### How do I track Inari is Pleased?

Use the shrine bundle screen, keep offering materials, and write down any altar requirement you cannot complete immediately.

### What items should I keep for shrine offerings?

Keep a reserve of wood, clay, copper ore, stone, meat, seasonal crops, forage items, and any item the shrine bundle screen asks for.

### Are festival achievements missable?

They are calendar-risk achievements. If you sleep through the festival day, you may need to wait for the next seasonal cycle. Check calendar and mail near festival dates.

### Does Early Access progress sync achievements correctly?

If you started on an older save, verify important route progress in your current 1.0 save. If one achievement does not trigger, check the Steam community discussions for that specific achievement.
