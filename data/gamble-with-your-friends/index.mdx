<h2 id="what-is-gamble-with-your-friends">
  What is Gamble With Your Friends?
</h2>

**Gamble With Your Friends** is a co-op casino-crawler where your group shares money, enters randomized casino floors, plays gambling tables under a short timer, and tries to hit quota before the run falls apart.

The trick is that the game looks like pure chaos, but good teams actually play it like a route:

- one player calls the plan
- the team protects the shared bank
- items are bought for a specific table
- big bets happen only after setup
- body parts are used as emergency value, not free comedy
- achievements are hunted one target at a time

Bad teams lose because everyone sees a different shiny table and spends the same shared money.

Good teams win because they decide what the day is for before the timer eats them.

<h2 id="best-beginner-route">
  Best beginner route
</h2>

For a new group, the cleanest route is:

1. **Start on Floor 1 with small bets.**
2. **Use Roulette or Blackjack for controlled decisions.**
3. **Use Duck Race only with a hard attempt limit.**
4. **Practice Street Craps before treating it as a money table.**
5. **Spend first tickets on practical items, not cosmetics.**
6. **Use Golden Chip, Taser, Holy Statue, Drink, or Insurance only with a plan.**
7. **Stop gambling once quota is safe unless the team agrees to push.**

The goal of the first few runs is not to become rich. The goal is to stop dying to your own teammates.

<h2 id="tickets-items-body-parts">
  Tickets, items, and body parts
</h2>

Tickets are the real between-day economy. They let you buy items, recover, reroll, and turn a weak casino day into a planned push.

The strongest early item ideas are:

- **Golden Chip** for a free all-in.
- **Taser** to increase maximum bet before the all-in.
- **Holy Statue** to prevent nearby loss while active.
- **Drink** to increase profit while drunk.
- **Insurance** to decrease loss amount.
- **Quota Gun** to convert body parts into emergency quota value.
- **Angel’s Reel / Devil’s Reel** for cheap result-based risk tools.

For most beginner groups:

- **Roulette** is the cleanest fast quota game.
- **Blackjack** is good when one player leads decisions.
- **Slots** are better with item support.
- **Duck Race** is easy but easy to spam.
- **Street Craps** is strong if the team can control the table.
- **Wheel games** are emergency swing tools.
- **Crash, HiLo, Plinko, Dragon Tower, Poker, and Mine Sweeper** should not be blind max-bet games.

<h2 id="street-craps">
  Street Craps is the skill table
</h2>

Street Craps deserves its own guide because it is one of the few tables where team discipline can change everything.

The short version:

- assign one roller
- assign one caller
- read the target before touching dice
- keep other players away from the table
- use small adjustments when possible
- use Taser + Golden Chip only after the team understands the roll
- leave after the planned payout

If your group can listen, Craps can carry runs. If your group cannot listen, Craps will eat the bank.

Use the <Link href="/gamble-with-your-friends/craps-cheese-guide">Street Craps Cheese Guide</Link> for the full route.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What is Gamble With Your Friends?

Gamble With Your Friends is a co-op casino-crawler where players share a bank, play casino games under quota pressure, buy items with tickets, and try to survive increasingly risky casino floors.

### Is Gamble With Your Friends good solo?

Yes, but the game is built around team chaos. Solo play is cleaner because nobody else can burn the shared bank, but co-op is where the game’s funniest systems matter most.

### What should beginners do first?

Beginners should learn Floor 1 games, protect the shared bank, hit quota before achievement hunting, and spend early tickets on practical items.

### What is the best beginner game?

Roulette is the cleanest fast beginner game. Blackjack is good if one player understands basic decisions. Duck Race is easy but dangerous if the team spams it.

### What is the best item combo?

The cleanest item combo is **Taser + Golden Chip**. Use Taser first to increase maximum bet, then use Golden Chip for a larger free all-in.

### What should I buy first with tickets?

Good early buys include Golden Chip, Taser, Holy Statue, Drink, Insurance, and Quota Gun. Do not buy random items before the team has a plan.

### Is Street Craps worth learning?

Yes. Street Craps is one of the best tables if your team assigns one roller, one caller, and plays slowly instead of panic throwing.

### How many achievements does Gamble With Your Friends have?

Gamble With Your Friends has 55 Steam achievements.

### Are the endings solved?

Not from achievement names alone. Ending achievements should be handled separately once completed-run triggers are confirmed.

### What is the biggest beginner mistake?

The biggest mistake is forgetting that the money is shared. One player’s bad bet is everyone’s problem.
