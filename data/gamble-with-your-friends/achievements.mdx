<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**Gamble With Your Friends has 55 Steam achievements. The easiest way to clear them is not to chase the rarest ones first: start with Floor 1 game achievements, then move to Floor 2 game targets, then use item-supported runs for high rollers, Floor 3/Floor 4 goals, and money milestones.**

Do this first:

1. **Clear Floor 1 basics** — Roulette, Duck Race, Blackjack, Slots, Street Craps, and Wheel.
2. **Move to Floor 2 once items make sense** — Penguin Cross, Crash, HiLo, Keno, Plinko, and Money Wheel.
3. **Use specialist routes for hard games** — Craps, Dragon Tower, Poker, Mine Sweeper, and Floor 4 games.
4. **Run deliberate loss achievements separately** — do not mix them with serious quota or money runs.
5. **Save money milestones and ending achievements for late runs.**

The old-player rule is simple:

**Pick one achievement target per casino day. A team that chases five achievements at once usually gets none of them and loses the bank.**
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="lucky-7-craps">
  Lucky 7 and Street Craps achievements
</h2>

**Lucky 7** asks you to get 7 on your first roll on Street Craps on Floor 1. Later, **Natural Roller** asks for a Street Craps point-phase win on Floor 3.

Craps achievements are much easier if you use the same old-player setup:

1. one roller
2. one caller
3. everyone else backs up
4. read the target before touching dice
5. use small adjustments when possible

<h2 id="floor-three-achievements">
  Floor 3 achievements
</h2>

Floor 3 is where you stop doing random achievement hunting. Pick one target and build the day around it.

  caption="Floor 3 achievements and best routes."

Good Floor 3 targets with dedicated preparation:

- **Natural Roller** if your team understands Craps
- **No Safe Rolls** if someone understands HiLo
- **Poker Face** if someone understands 1P Poker
- **Perfect Sweep** if someone understands Mine Sweeper

Bad Floor 3 plan:

**“Let’s just walk around and see what happens.”**

That is how you spend a lot of money and get no achievement.

<h2 id="achievements-to-avoid-early">
  Achievements to avoid early
</h2>

Avoid these on your first serious runs:

- **Clean Fall** — 24X Plinko is high variance.
- **Step Into Fire** — deliberate Dragon Tower loss chain.
- **Straight Down** — deliberate Plinko loss chain on Floor 4.
- **High Risk Wedge** — deliberate Money Wheel loss chain on Floor 4.
- **WORK IS NEVER OVER!** — $100B is not a beginner goal.
- **Floor 4 streak achievements** — expensive if you do not know the tables.

These are not bad achievements. They are bad **early** achievements.

<h2 id="old-player-tips">
  Old-player achievement tips
</h2>

Use these rules:

1. **One target per day.** Do not chase five achievements in one 5-minute casino day.
2. **Quota first.** If quota is unsafe, stop achievement hunting.
3. **Deliberate loss achievements get their own run.** Do not mix them with a money push.
4. **Use minimum bets to learn tables.** Achievements do not require you to learn with max stakes.
5. **Use item routes for high rollers.** Taser, Golden Chip, Holy Statue, Drink, Insurance, and profit tools matter.
6. **Use dedicated guides for specialist tables.** Craps, Dragon Tower, Poker, Mine Sweeper, and HiLo should not be improvised.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are in Gamble With Your Friends?

Gamble With Your Friends has **55 Steam achievements**.

### What achievements should I do first?

Start with Floor 1 achievements: High Roller, Big Spender, Lucky Streak, All on Red, YARLL!!!, Lucky 7, Risky Move, No Bust Run, and Jackpot Hunter.

### Do High Roller and Big Spender have exact dollar amounts?

The Steam requirement text uses wording like “very big bet” and “a lot of money.” This guide keeps the official wording instead of inventing hidden dollar thresholds.

### What is the easiest achievement route?

Clear Floor 1 game achievements first, then move to Floor 2 game achievements, then save Floor 3/Floor 4 and money milestones for later.

### Is Lucky 7 hard?

Lucky 7 depends on your first Street Craps roll on Floor 1. It is easier if one player controls the dice and another player calls the target.

### How do you get No Bust Run?

Win 4 games in a row on Blackjack on Floor 1. Let one player lead decisions and use small bets while learning.

### How do you get Greed Test?

Win at least 10X on Crash on Floor 2. Pick a cash-out target before greed takes over.

### How do you get Natural Roller?

Win on Street Craps during the point phase on Floor 3. Use the Craps guide instead of improvising.

### How do you get Poker Face?

Win on 1P Poker on Floor 3 without locking cards, with a hand equal to or better than Two of a Kind.

### How do you get Perfect Sweep?

Win at least 20X on Mine Sweeper on Floor 3. Treat it as a specialist achievement, not a panic table.

### How do you get Flameproof?

Win at least 3X three times in a row on Dragon Tower on Floor 4.

### Which achievements are deliberate loss achievements?

The captured Steam list confirms at least these deliberate loss-chain achievements: Step Into Fire, Straight Down, and High Risk Wedge. Do these on dedicated attempts, not serious money runs.

### Should I chase deliberate loss achievements early?

No. Achievements like Step Into Fire, Straight Down, and High Risk Wedge should be done on dedicated attempts, not during serious quota or money runs.

### Are ending achievements solved?

Not from the public achievement descriptions alone. Let It Ride!, End of the Line, and ...And They Lived Happily After are hidden in the captured Steam list and should be handled in the separate endings guide once exact triggers are confirmed.
