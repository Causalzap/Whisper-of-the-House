  How to get all three endings from one late-run save
</h2>

This is the practical route most players want.

You need one strong late-run state where you can reach the final debt choice, pay off the debt, or choose the risky double-or-nothing option. From there, the goal is to branch into all three ending achievements without replaying the entire game from scratch.

<h2 id="final-coin-flip">
  How to change the final coin flip result
</h2>

The risky double-or-nothing result should not be treated like a fresh 50/50 every time you reload.

If you reload from the same final-day state and choose the same option, the same result may repeat. That means the coin flip may already be tied to the current lobby state, route state, or RNG sequence.

<GuideTable
  caption="How to test and change the final coin flip result."
  columns={coinFlipColumns}
  rows={coinFlipRows}
/>

The cleanest sequence change is usually a shop interaction: buy an item, reroll the shop if available, or change the item route before choosing the risky option again.

<h2 id="ending-achievements-and-bugs">
  Are ending achievements bugged?
</h2>

Not automatically.

Ending achievements are hidden, route-dependent, and easy to misread. A failed guessed route does not prove the achievement is broken.

Before calling an ending achievement bugged, check:

- did you get the achievement pop-up and quit immediately?
- did you reload from the correct late-run state?
- did the same double-or-nothing result repeat?
- did you try a shop interaction or item-route change?
- did you accidentally overwrite the useful save?
- did the game patch after the route was posted?

For confirmed not-unlocking issues, use <Link href="/gamble-with-your-friends/bugged-achievements">Bugged Achievements Guide</Link>.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many endings are in Gamble With Your Friends?

There are three ending-style achievements: Let It Ride!, End of the Line, and ...And They Lived Happily After.

### Can I get all endings from one save?

Yes, if you preserve a late-run save before the final debt choice and quit immediately after each ending achievement pops.

### What is the best order to get all endings?

Get ...And They Lived Happily After first by paying off the debt, then use double-or-nothing for one coin-flip ending, then change the RNG sequence and do double-or-nothing again for the final ending.

### When should I press Esc?

Press Esc and quit immediately after the ending achievement pops. This helps avoid overwriting the late-run save you need for the other endings.

### Does the final coin flip reroll when I reload?

Not always. If you reload from the same final-day state and make the same choice, the same result may repeat.

### How do I change the final coin flip result?

Reload earlier, then use a shop action, buy an item, reroll the shop if available, or change your item route before choosing double-or-nothing again.

### What achievement is tied to paying off the debt?

The safe payoff-style route is tied to ...And They Lived Happily After.

### What achievements are tied to double-or-nothing?

The risky double-or-nothing route is tied to Let It Ride! and End of the Line, depending on whether the final gamble succeeds or fails.

### Are ending achievements bugged?

Not automatically. Hidden ending achievements need clean route confirmation. A repeated coin-flip result usually means the route state did not change.

### Should I use save scum for endings?

Yes. Ending routing is one of the best uses for save scum, but you need an early enough late-run save and clean route changes.

### Should I chase endings before other achievements?

No. Finish normal achievements first, skip currently bugged achievements, then route endings as late-run targets.
