<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**The first early dungeon route you should learn is Mine Estrone. Enter on Basic difficulty, clear special foes, collect the 5 Secret Orbs: Mine Estrone, then defeat Reblochonk. Do not treat this as only a boss hallway.**

Use this first-clear route:

1. Find the early dungeon entrance and choose **Basic** difficulty.
2. Follow the **Mine Estrone** objective tracker once inside.
3. Clear inventory space before entering.
4. Upgrade active weapon skills before the run.
5. Bring healing or potions.
6. Clear special foes while moving through the route.
7. Watch the **Secret Orbs: Mine Estrone** counter.
8. Keep searching until the secret orb counter reaches **5/5** if you want completion.
9. Fight **Reblochonk** after the route is ready.
10. Move during **Rampage** instead of greed-attacking.
11. Check rewards, skill points, inventory, and skill upgrades after the run.

This guide covers **Mine Estrone and Reblochonk**. **Crabgantua** belongs to **Crabgantua's Gorge** and should be treated as a separate boss route.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="reblochonk-boss">
  Reblochonk boss fight
</h2>

**Reblochonk** is the Mine Estrone boss. Before the fight, your objective list marks him as **0/1 Reblochonk slain**; after the kill, make sure that objective updates before you leave.

The key beginner rule is simple: do not greed damage during named pressure. Reblochonk can use **Rampage**, and new players often lose control when they keep attacking instead of moving.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What is the first dungeon boss in Farever?

For the Mine Estrone early dungeon route, the boss is **Reblochonk**.

### Is Reblochonk the same as Crabgantua?

No. Reblochonk is tied to **Mine Estrone**. Crabgantua is the boss of **Crabgantua's Gorge** and should be treated as a separate boss route.

### Where is the first dungeon in Farever?

Use the early level 5 dungeon entry and follow the objective tracker into **Mine Estrone**. Once inside, look for Mine Estrone objectives such as Secret Orbs, special foes, and Reblochonk.

### What level should I be for the first dungeon?

The early dungeon entry can show **Recommended level: 5**. If you are below that, finish nearby Codeex entries, map objectives, and mini bosses first.

### How many secret orbs are in Mine Estrone?

Mine Estrone has **5 secret orbs**. Use the **Secret Orbs: Mine Estrone** counter to track your progress.

### Where is the missing secret orb in Mine Estrone?

Check side paths, ledges, upper routes, wooden structures, and the route before Reblochonk. Do not only search the ground-level boss path.

### Do special foes matter in Mine Estrone?

Yes. Special foes are separate from Secret Orbs and Reblochonk, so clear them before assuming the route is finished.

### Should I do Basic difficulty first?

Yes. Use Basic difficulty for your first clear so you can learn the route, secret orbs, special foes, and Reblochonk safely.

### How do I beat Reblochonk?

Move during **Rampage**, keep the camera clear, avoid greed-attacking during pressure, and make sure the Reblochonk objective updates after the kill.

### Why did I miss rewards after the dungeon?

You may have killed the boss but skipped secret orbs, special foes, or post-run checks. Always check objectives, inventory, and the skill screen before leaving.

### Can friends help with Mine Estrone?

Yes. Party play helps, but coordinate the start, secret orb search, special foe clear, and Reblochonk pull so the group does not split.

### Which Farever guide should I read next?

Read <Link href="/farever/weapon-skills-guide">Farever Weapon Skills Guide</Link> for skill and build upgrades, or <Link href="/farever/codeex-leveling-guide">Farever Codeex and Leveling Guide</Link> if you want better pre-dungeon leveling.
