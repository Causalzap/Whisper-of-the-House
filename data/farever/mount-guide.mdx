<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**To get a mount in Farever, claim the free Sparkling Horsean from the in-game shop if it is available, then press Y to open Collections and equip it from the mount tab. Claiming a mount is not enough; it must be equipped before your mount key works.**

Commonly reported default controls:

- **Keyboard:** Shift
- **Controller:** L3
- **Collections:** Y

Fast fix if your mount does not work:

1. Press **Y** to open Collections.
2. Select the **mount tab**.
3. Equip **Sparkling Horsean** or another owned mount.
4. Press **Shift** on keyboard or **L3** on controller.
5. If it still fails, test in a safe open-world area outside combat or dungeon restrictions.

Do not buy a gold mount from a stable or vendor until you have checked whether your free Sparkling Horsean is already claimed and equipped.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="dungeon-mount-restrictions">
  Can you use mounts in dungeons?
</h2>

Do not expect the mount to solve dungeon movement. Mounts are mainly for open-world travel. Dungeons and instance routes often rely more on jumping, gliding, platforms, route reading, and special traversal points.

<GuideTable
  caption="Where mounts work and where to use other movement tools."
  columns={dungeonColumns}
  rows={dungeonRows}

The practical dungeon rule is:

**Mount in the open world. Read the route inside dungeons.**

Before your first dungeon, practice jumping into glider movement and moving through vertical paths without depending on your mount key. That habit prevents a lot of confusion when the game shifts from open terrain to platforming or instance navigation.

<h2 id="mounts-companions-pet-net">
  Mounts, companions, and the pet net are different
</h2>

Collections can include mounts, gliders, companions, appearance items, and other cosmetic rewards. They are not interchangeable.

The simple difference is:

| Item type | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Mount** | Carries you and uses the mount / dismount key |
| **Glider** | Helps with airborne movement after jumping or dropping |
| **Companion** | Follows you, but does not replace a rideable mount |
| **Pet net** | Catches pets and uses an active weapon slot |

Do not equip the pet net before combat. Use it only while catching pets, then swap back to a real weapon.

<h2 id="obelisk-fast-travel">
  Obelisks and fast travel
</h2>

Mounts are for local travel. Obelisks are for bigger route management.

When you find an obelisk, interact with it. Obelisks can:

- reveal the local zone
- show points of interest
- act as respawn points
- set teleportation points
- enable automatic travel to another unlocked obelisk
- reduce backtracking between objectives

The clean movement rule is:

- use **mount** for short open-world travel
- use **glider** for drops, gaps, and vertical movement
- use **obelisk travel** for longer backtracking
- unlock every obelisk before leaving a new area

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What is the free mount called in Farever?

The free starter mount is **Sparkling Horsean**. Claim it from the in-game shop if the free claim is available, then equip it from Collections.

### How do you get a mount in Farever?

Press **Esc**, open the shop, claim Sparkling Horsean or the available free items, then press **Y** to open Collections. Select the mount tab and equip the mount.

### Why does my mount key not work?

You probably claimed or bought the mount but did not equip it. Open Collections with **Y**, select the mount tab, equip Sparkling Horsean or another owned mount, then test **Shift** on keyboard or **L3** on controller.

### What is the default mount key on keyboard?

The commonly reported default keyboard mount key is **Shift**. Check **Options → Hotkeys** if it does not work.

### What is the default mount button on controller?

The commonly reported controller mount input is **L3**. If it does not work, check your controller layout, in-game settings, or Steam Input profile.

### Where is the mount after I claim it?

The mount is in **Collections**, not your normal inventory.

### Can I buy mounts from stables?

Yes. Stables or mount vendors in towns can sell mounts for gold. Check your current vendor stock because prices and availability can change.

### Do I need to buy a 1000 gold mount?

No, not if you already claimed and equipped Sparkling Horsean. Treat gold mounts as optional collection or appearance purchases.

### How do I get Wolf mounts?

Some Wolf variants are sold by mount vendors, and other wolf-related sources may exist. Check current vendor stock and a mount database before farming.

### Can Apix Harvester Bees drop a mount?

Yes, Apix Harvester-style bee enemies can drop a Ladybug-family mount such as **Zerzurean Leggybug**, not a bee-named mount. Check the current drop source in a mount database before committing to a farm route.

### Can I use mounts in dungeons?

Do not rely on mounts inside dungeons or restricted instance routes. Use jumping, gliding, platforms, route planning, and grapple points where available.

### Is a companion the same as a mount?

No. A companion follows you; a mount is used for riding. Check the correct Collections tab.

### Is the pet net a mount item?

No. The pet net is for catching pets and uses an active weapon slot. Do not equip it before combat.

### How do obelisks work with mounts?

Mounts help with local travel, while obelisks help with longer travel. Unlock obelisks to reveal zones, set respawn points, and use automatic travel routes.
