<h3>Phase 2: First League counters</h3>

Use the regular season to combine:

- 30 and 45 hits;
- 15 and 30 home runs;
- 15 and 25 strikeouts;
- 35 Heat pitches;
- 35 Right Hook pitches;
- 35 Left Hook pitches;
- 35 Slowballs;
- 25 fly-ball catches;
- 15 safe slides;
- 25 successful steals;
- one League double play;
- and the first season win.

### Rotate the four normal pitches early

Do not finish the postseason with one pitch counter still at zero.

A simple rotation is:

1. Heat near one edge.
2. Right Hook or Left Hook moving away from the hitter.
3. Slowball after the faster pitch.
4. Repeat from the opposite side for the next batter.

These achievements track pitch use, so every pitch does not need to produce a
strikeout.

### How to get strikeouts more consistently

Throwing normal strikes down the middle gives the CPU too many hittable balls.

The launch-build pattern that worked more reliably for me was:

1. Use Heat or a hook pitch near the edge for the first strike.
2. Move to the opposite edge for the second strike.
3. Aim the finishing pitch slightly below, outside, or beyond the corner.
4. Use a Slowball or breaking pitch so the batter must adjust both location and
   timing.
5. Accept a called ball when the batter does not chase.

A ball is safer than a hittable pitch. Do not move the target back toward the
middle merely because the count reaches two strikes.

In one game, the first clean strikeout came after repeatedly pushing the target
below the normal zone and forcing the batter to chase the final pitch.

Patch changes can affect pitching timing, but the decision rule remains useful:
**get ahead near the edges, then finish outside the zone**.

### Build the double play in League Play

`Double Trouble` specifically requires League Play.

The cleanest setup is a runner on first with fewer than two outs:

1. Force a ground ball.
2. Throw to second for the lead force.
3. Throw immediately to first.
4. Stay upright unless a dive is required to stop the ball.

A Pickup or Quick Play double play may look identical, but it does not satisfy
a League-specific requirement.

### Slide only near the destination

For `Slide to Victory`, sprint through most of the path and slide during the
last section.

An early slide can end before the runner reaches the base.

The runner must be safe. An `OUT` does not advance the safe-slide or successful
steal counters.

5. Swing only after the target reaches a hittable pitch.

<h2 id="hidden-achievements">
  Hidden achievement solutions and status
</h2>

Hidden descriptions often indicate the category without explaining the exact
scope.

I separate a useful working route from a fully isolated trigger instead of
turning an internal label into a false guarantee.

<h3>Prodigy in the Making and Mark of a Legend</h3>

The current working triggers are:

- **Prodigy in the Making:** hit one out-of-the-park home run;
- **Mark of a Legend:** hit 10 out-of-the-park home runs.

A ball that remains playable inside the stadium may count toward normal home-run
achievements without advancing these two trackers.

Use strong hitters and a field where clean power contact can leave the playable
area.

<h3>No Place Like Home</h3>

The internal tracker points to three runs.

The remaining uncertainty is whether it means:

- three team runs;
- three runs in one game;
- or one player crossing home three times.

The safest route is to use the same fast leadoff player throughout a
high-scoring game and let that player score three times.

That satisfies the strictest interpretation without creating a separate grind.

<h3>Error 404</h3>

The hidden description says `Flawless execution`, and the tracker points toward
error-free progress.

Until the exact scope is isolated:

- leave Errors enabled;
- use strong fielders;
- avoid unnecessary dives;
- secure the ball before throwing;
- and complete multiple clean defensive innings or games.

Do not turn Errors off solely to force this achievement unless a clean-profile
test confirms that the setting is accepted.

<h3>Power Siblings</h3>

Use a known sibling pair on the same roster.

The easiest useful options are:

- Achmed and Amir Khan;
- Ashley and Sidney Webber;
- Tony and Angela Delvecchio;
- Kenny and Vicki Kawaguchi;
- Sally and Ronnie Dobbs.

I prefer the Khans because both already have practical roles in a balanced
League team.

<h3>In a Pickle</h3>

Create a rundown between two bases.

The easiest offensive setup is:

1. Advance a runner beyond the safe base.
2. Retreat when the defense throws ahead.
3. Reverse again after the next throw.
4. Remain trapped long enough for the rundown to register.

Do this with a safe lead rather than sacrificing a scoring opportunity in a
close tournament game.

<h2 id="common-problems">
  Why an achievement is not unlocking
</h2>

### My strikeouts are not increasing

Make sure the batter actually reaches strike three.

Foul balls, fielded outs, and runners thrown out on the bases do not count as a
pitcher strikeout.

Work near the edges, then use a Slowball or breaking pitch just outside the
zone after reaching two strikes.

### Double Trouble did not unlock

The double play must occur in League Play.

A visually identical double play in Pickup or Quick Play does not meet the
mode-specific description.

### A stolen base did not count

The runner must be called safe.

An attempted steal ending in `OUT` does not advance the counter.

### A slide did not count

The runner must finish safely without being tagged.

Trigger the slide near the base rather than immediately after beginning the
run.

### Grand Slammer did not unlock

All three bases must be occupied at the moment the home run is hit.

A normal multi-run hit or home run with only one or two runners aboard is not a
grand slam.

### A Power-Up counter did not increase

Use the actual Power-Up during the play.

Simply earning, storing, or selecting an option without completing its use may
not advance the counter.

### A rarity achievement did not unlock

The requirement tracks cards currently owned, not how many cards of that rarity
have appeared across all pack openings.

Return to the collection screen and check the owned total.

### Master of Modes is still locked

Finish each mode rather than opening and quitting it.

Include the Tutorial and League Play, then return to the Clubhouse after each
completed mode.

### Popular Coach is still locked

Check every category:

- special Clubhouse characters;
- challenge-based Backyard Kids;
- late League characters;
- and all seven MLB Pros.

Seeing a player in Records does not always mean that the player is selectable.

### The correct play happened but nothing unlocked

Finish the inning or game, return to the Clubhouse, and check Records before
repeating the attempt.

Then:

1. confirm the game is updated;
2. retry in the correct mode;
3. avoid quitting during the result animation;
4. and reproduce the play in a fresh game.

For a tag, rundown, double play, or fly-ball achievement, the animation may look
correct while the game's underlying play classification records something
different.

<h2 id="faq">
  Backyard Baseball achievements FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are in Backyard Baseball 2026?

There are 51 Steam achievements. All 51 were added on July 9, 2026.

### What is the best 100% achievement route?

Complete the Tutorial and Clubhouse cleanup first, use one League for normal
gameplay counters and the first championship, complete a second championship,
then finish the roster and card collection.

### What is the rarest achievement?

Gotta Collect 'Em All was the rarest in the July 13 snapshot at 0.1%. It
requires every Backyard Kids baseball card.

### What is the easiest way to get strikeouts?

Reach two strikes with pitches near the edges, then use a Slowball or breaking
pitch aimed just outside the strike zone. Do not repeatedly feed the CPU
hittable strikes down the middle.

### Do I need more than one League?

Yes. Victory Lap tracks a second Ultra Grand Championship, and the second
League can also carry late player unlocks and remaining counters.

### Does Pickup Game count for Double Trouble?

No. Double Trouble specifically requires a double play in League Play.

### How do I unlock Grand Slammer?

Load all three bases, then hit a home run. Saving Aluminum Power Bat for a
reliable hitter makes the setup easier.

### What counts for Slide to Victory?

The runner must slide into the base and finish the play safely without being
tagged.

### How do I unlock In a Pickle?

Get a runner trapped in a rundown between two bases while the defense moves the
ball ahead of and behind the runner.

### What are the pitching Power-Ups called?

The official launch names are Fire Balls, Crazy Balls, and Freezeballs.

### How do I unlock Hello, World?

Find Mr. Clanky lying in the Clubhouse, hold Shift, and click directly on him.

### How do I unlock Popular Coach?

Unlock the entire playable roster, including special characters, hidden
Backyard Kids, late League rewards, and MLB Pros.

### Should I save all Tokens until the end?

No. Opening packs during League progression advances pack counts, rarity
ownership, the full card collection, and some player unlocks together.

### Are every hidden achievement's exact conditions confirmed?

No. Prodigy in the Making, Mark of a Legend, Power Siblings, In a Pickle,
Master of Modes, Oops, I Pitched it Again, Triple Threat, Popular Coach, and
Victory Lap have strong tracker or gameplay evidence. The exact scopes of No
Place Like Home and Error 404 still need cleaner isolation.
