<h2 id="alt-fire-walk-with-me">
  Alt-Fire Walk with Me: easiest level to use
</h2>

For **Alt-Fire Walk with Me**, I use a very short tool-only mission instead of a big mold-heavy level. The easiest timing is around the first leaf blower-style job or another simple early mission where the objective is small and controlled.

The rule is strict: start the level in secondary cleaning mode and do not use normal cleaning mode at all. If the level has only a small object or a tiny dirt objective, this achievement is much safer than trying it in a large regrowth-heavy mission.

<GuideCallout title="Alt-Fire tip">
Do not test normal spray at the start. If you want the achievement, commit to secondary mode from the first input until the level clears.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="sushi-set">
  Sushi Set: best level and method
</h2>

**Sushi Set** requires two things:

1. Buy every tool.
2. Use every tool to clean dirt in one level.

I recommend doing it in **Level 1 or another simple floor-dirt level** after **IDKFA** unlocks. Pick a level with a plain dirt patch that does not disappear instantly, then touch the dirt with every tool before you fully clear the objective.

<h2 id="highgrow">
  HighGrow: why it may not unlock
</h2>

**HighGrow** unlocks when every flower in the pot has been watered and opened.

The important detail is that the mission can look finished before the achievement triggers. If the **CLEARED** banner appears, do not press Space immediately. Keep spraying the pot and the flowers until the remaining buds visually open into full blooms and the achievement pops.

<figure className="my-8">

This is the main reason players can think HighGrow is bugged: they finish the visible objective, leave too fast, and never wait for the final flowers to fully bloom.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are in Moldwasher?

Moldwasher has 27 achievements.

### Are the Steam achievement rates stable?

Not fully. The rates on this page are based on Steam achievement stats copied on June 24, 2026, shortly after launch. Early percentages can change quickly.

### What is the best FreshCash farm?

Garage toy batches are the best farm I found. In my run, they paid $200 FreshCash per batch and also helped with coins, toys, and cleanup progress.

### How do I unlock Alt-Fire Walk with Me?

Use only secondary cleaning mode for an entire short level. Do not use normal spray even once.

### What is the best level for Sushi Set?

Use Level 1 or another simple floor-dirt level after buying every tool. Touch dirt with every tool before the level clears.

### How do I unlock HighGrow?

Water every flower in the pot. If the level says CLEARED, do not press Space immediately; keep watering until every flower visually opens into a full bloom.

### How do I unlock Waterboarding?

Spray the fly in the fridge mission until the achievement pops. The fly may still move afterward, so use the achievement pop-up as the success check.

### How do I unlock Yakimeshi?

Use the flamethrower on a grain of rice after the flamethrower is unlocked.

### How do I unlock Toasty!?

Use the flamethrower on the cockroach when it appears.

### What is the hardest Moldwasher achievement?

**Gamer Brain®** is usually the hardest because it requires every collectible. All stickers, all toys, Sushi Set, and Finally, Inner Peace are also common final blockers.

### How do I unlock Gamer Brain®?

Find every collectible: hidden drops, stickers, toys, cassettes, CDs, and gachapon rewards. Use the collectibles guide for the full cleanup route.
