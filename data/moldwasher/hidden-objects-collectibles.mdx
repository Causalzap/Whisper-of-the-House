<h2 id="gamer-brain-cleanup">
  How I finish Gamer Brain®
</h2>

For **Gamer Brain®**, I use this order:

1. Check every mission result for hidden drops.
2. Replay only missions that still show a missing hidden drop.
3. Return to the garage when new toy batches are ready.
4. Farm coins through short repeatable jobs.
5. Buy gachapon rewards until sticker / toy / cassette progress stops moving.
6. Place, play, or inspect new rewards when needed.
7. Stop only when **Find every collectible in the game** unlocks.

<GuideCallout title="Do not waste the final cleanup">
If Gamer Brain® is still missing, do not replay the longest mission just because it feels important. I get better results by checking hidden drop counters first, then using garage and gachapon cleanup for the final reward pool.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How do I find all collectibles in Moldwasher?

Use the mission clear screen first. Replay missions with missing hidden drops, then finish garage toy batches, music rewards, Decoration Room rewards, and gachapon rewards.

### What counts as a collectible in Moldwasher?

Collectibles include hidden drops, stickers, toys, cassettes, CDs, and reward-pool items. Use the collectible types table above to identify which category is incomplete.

### How many hidden drops are in Moldwasher?

I would not publish a fixed total until every map node is checked in the same build. The reliable method is to use each mission's clear screen and replay any mission that shows 0/1 Hidden Drops.

### What should I do if a mission says 0/1 hidden drops?

Replay that mission and look for optional dirt, hidden objects behind stickers, cleaned reward items, bowl routes, floor / egg areas, and areas blocked by props.

### Where is the sticker-removal hidden drop?

In the unauthorized sticker fridge mission, clean the stickers with secondary / high-pressure mode, then check the area that was covered by the stickers.

### Where is the coffee / espresso collectible?

In the espresso mission, clean the beans / coffee-side object before leaving. The collectible comes from optional coffee cleanup, not only the main mold objective.

### Where is the fly fridge hidden drop?

Control the fly first, then sweep the floor / egg route before pressing Space. The fly distracts you and can make you leave before checking the pickup.

### Where is the dog bowl hidden drop?

Use the can as a ramp and check the bowl route, especially the upper or inside bowl angle. Do not only clean the floor mat.

### How do I fix Gamer Brain® not unlocking?

Check every mission's hidden drop result first. If hidden drops are complete, diagnose the missing category: stickers, toys, cassettes, CDs, or gachapon rewards.

### How do I diagnose all stickers, toys, or cassettes?

Use the missing collectible table above. Stickers usually point to sticker checks and gachapon, toys point to garage batches, and cassettes / CDs point to music rewards and the media center.

### What is the best coin farm for gachapon cleanup?

Garage toy batches are the best loop in my run because they are short, pay about $200 FreshCash, and also support coins, toys, and final cleanup.
