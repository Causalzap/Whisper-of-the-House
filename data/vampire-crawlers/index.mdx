<h2 id="what-this-hub-covers">
  What this hub covers
</h2>

The Vampire Crawlers guide cluster is organized around two problems:

1. **How do I make runs stronger?**
2. **What should I unlock next?**

The first problem is handled by upgrades, combos, and evolutions. These pages explain the systems that make every run better.

The second problem is handled by character and unlock pages. Those pages help once you already understand the basics of Power-Ups, combo order, and payoff cards.

This separation matters because many new players chase characters too early. A new Crawler helps, but it does not fix weak upgrades, bad card order, or unfinished evolution plans.

<GuideCallout title="Best beginner principle">
Do not start with hidden characters.

Start with the systems that make every run stronger: upgrades, combos, and evolutions.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="best-reading-order-for-new-players">
  Best reading order for new players
</h2>

### 1. Best Early Upgrades

Start with permanent progression.

This tells you whether your first Gold should go into Reroll, Recovery, Might, Greed, Cooldown, or another upgrade. It also explains early traps like Blacksmith spending, Bing machines, and wasting Floor Chicken.

### 2. Combo Guide

After that, learn the main combat rule.

Vampire Crawlers is built around mana order. If you do not understand 0 -> 1 -> 2 turns, Wildcards, skipped numbers, and payoff slots, your deck will feel weaker than it should.

### 3. Early Evolutions Guide

Once your turns make sense, learn which weapons are worth building toward.

Evolved cards are usually your best payoff cards, but forcing the wrong recipe can make a deck worse. This guide explains what to chase first and when to delay an evolution trigger.

### 4. Best Characters for Beginners

Now character advice becomes more useful.

At this point, you can judge whether a Crawler helps your actual deck engine instead of only asking which character is strongest in isolation.

### 5. All Characters Unlock Guide

Use the full roster guide as a reference.

This is where you check exact unlock conditions, gold costs, Crawler card effects, and color bonuses.

### 6. Secret Characters

Save secret characters for later.

Hidden unlocks usually ask for challenge logic, puzzle conditions, or advanced systems. They are more rewarding once normal progression is stable.

<h2 id="if-you-are-stuck">
  If you are stuck
</h2>

### I keep dying

Your problem is probably not the roster yet. Focus on early Power-Ups, Recovery, Max Health, Armor, Floor Chicken timing, and safer reward choices.

### My turns feel weak

Your problem is probably combo structure. Check whether your deck has starters, bridges, payoffs, and enough mana to finish the sequence.

### I keep missing evolutions

Your problem is probably recipe timing. Check whether the base weapon has an empty socket, whether the item card was consumed or destroyed, and whether you opened the chest too early.

### I do not know who to unlock

Use the beginner character page first, then the full roster reference after you have a target.

### I want secrets

Finish the normal progression loop first. Secret characters are better treated as late or challenge goals.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What is the best page to start with?

Start with Best Early Upgrades if you are brand new. It improves every future run and helps you avoid wasting early Gold.

### What should I read after the upgrades guide?

Read the Combo Guide next. It explains why card order matters and why some turns hit much harder than others.

### When should I read the evolution guide?

Read it after you understand combo basics. Evolved weapons are strongest when you know how to place payoff cards late in a chain.

### When should I focus on characters?

Focus on characters after you understand upgrades and combos. Character effects make more sense once you know what your deck is trying to do.

### Should I chase secret characters early?

No. Secret characters are better saved for later, after normal runs, Power-Ups, combos, and evolutions are stable.

### What is the best overall reading order?

**Best Early Upgrades → Combo Guide → Early Evolutions Guide → Best Characters for Beginners → All Characters Unlock Guide → Secret Characters How to Unlock**.
