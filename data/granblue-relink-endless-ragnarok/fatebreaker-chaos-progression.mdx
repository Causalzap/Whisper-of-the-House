<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
In **Granblue Fantasy: Relink Endless Ragnarok**, I treat **Fatebreaker** as a progression route, not just a title unlock. The clean route is: unlock **Conflux** from the Chapter 6 side quest in **Seedhollow**, clear **The Tale of Bahamut's Rage (Proud)** to open the Endless Ragnarok chapter, beat the **Seofon and Tweyen trial**, receive **Fate Breaker** skyfarer grade, then begin **Chaos-level quests**.

The most important rule is: **being able to enter Chaos does not mean Chaos is worth farming yet**. If the clear needs constant revives, AI companions keep dying, or the boss takes too long, I go back to Conflux, fix the weak slot, then return.

<h2 id="when-to-farm-chaos">
  When I farm Chaos and when I return to Conflux
</h2>

I farm the current Chaos tier only when the clear is repeatable. If the fight exposes a weapon wall, AI survival problem, cap conversion gap, material bottleneck, or new character build gap, I go back to Conflux. If the failure is one repeated boss pattern, I practice the fight instead of farming random materials.

<h2 id="new-characters-and-chaos">
  Should I bring new characters into Chaos immediately?
</h2>

I unlock new characters early, but I do not spend Chaos materials on them until I know their role. New access is not the same as a finished build.

If a character needs manual timing to shine, I test them as my main before forcing them into an AI slot. If they are underbuilt, I use Conflux first.

For unlock order and first-pick logic, use the <Link href="/granblue-relink-endless-ragnarok/new-characters-unlock-guide/">New Characters Unlock Guide</Link>.

  The mistake I avoid
</h2>

The mistake I avoid is turning every unlock into a farm. If the trial or Chaos quest clears once but costs too many revives, exposes weak AI, or only works because of one lucky manual run, I take the progress and rebuild before repeating it.

<h2 id="faq">
  Granblue Relink Fatebreaker and Chaos FAQ
</h2>

### How do I unlock Fatebreaker?

Progress Endless Ragnarok until the **Seofon and Tweyen trial** opens. Clearing that trial grants the **Fate Breaker** skyfarer grade and opens the Chaos-level progression path.

### What are Chaos-level quests?

Chaos-level quests are Fatebreaker-grade endgame quests that open after the Fatebreaker promotion. I treat them as repeat-farm checks, not just unlock checks.

### Are Fatebreaker sigils worth using?

Yes, when they solve the current Chaos problem. I check the in-game sigil text first, then test whether the trait improves damage, survival, cap conversion, or repeat-clear stability.

### Should I use Conflux before Chaos?

Yes, if the party has a build wall. Conflux is where I fix weapons, AI survival, cap conversion, materials, and new character gaps before repeating Chaos.

### Should I push Chaos+ as soon as it opens?

Only if normal Chaos is already stable. If Chaos still needs constant revives, lucky AI behavior, or one perfect manual run, I stay in the current tier or return to Conflux first.
