<h2 id="conflux-guide">
  Granblue Fantasy Relink Endless Ragnarok Conflux Guide: portals, auras, rewards, and when to leave
</h2>

**Conflux** is the solo route I use when Endless Ragnarok starts asking for stronger weapons, better AI companions, and cleaner Chaos prep.

It is not just a random arena chain. Each run is a sequence of small rooms, portal choices, aura decisions, boss checks, reward chests, permanent upgrade pressure, and exit decisions.

The important part is simple:

<strong>Conflux is a route, not a grind button.</strong>

If I know why I entered the run, I know which portal to take, which aura to buy, which chest to choose, and when to leave.

<h2 id="conflux-routing-rule">
  My Conflux routing rule
</h2>

Every Conflux decision follows the same three checks:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>
    <strong>Room:</strong> does this portal move the run faster or give the points I need?
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Aura:</strong> does this aura fix the current bottleneck, or is it just a bigger-looking number?
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Reward:</strong> does this chest fix the next failed clear?
  </li>
</ul>

That is why I route each run around the wall I am trying to break: portal speed, aura power, reward target, resonance progress, or gray-exit safety.

<h2 id="how-to-unlock-conflux">
  How to unlock Conflux in Seedhollow
</h2>

Conflux starts from the Endless Ragnarok route in **Seedhollow**. After the expansion opening sends me back into the city, I follow the new prompt around the main path and the Quest Counter side of town instead of farming old quests.

The practical route is:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>start the Endless Ragnarok route and return to Seedhollow,</li>
  <li>follow the new expansion prompt around the Quest Counter path,</li>
  <li>investigate the mysterious gate or back alley gate when the prompt appears,</li>
  <li>talk to the representative by the Quest Counter when the game points me there,</li>
  <li>then enter the first available Conflux cycle.</li>
</ul>

If the Seedhollow gate is not there yet, I continue the Endless Ragnarok story and side-objective route until the Conflux prompt appears. I do not farm random old quests to unlock Conflux.

For the full starting route, use the <Link href="/granblue-relink-endless-ragnarok/how-to-start/">How to Start Endless Ragnarok Guide</Link>.

<h2 id="how-conflux-works">
  How Conflux works
</h2>

A Conflux run sends me through a chain of rooms. Each room gives me progress, points, rewards, or a chance to shape the build with auras.

The loop is:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>choose a portal,</li>
  <li>clear the room objective,</li>
  <li>earn points or rewards,</li>
  <li>spend points on auras when the route allows it,</li>
  <li>prepare for the boss room,</li>
  <li>then decide whether to continue or exit after a boss.</li>
</ul>

The first Conflux run can be deceptive. In my run, **Cycle 1 Inundation showed 1,500 recommended power** while my party was around **21,000 power**, so the opening cycle was a route check, not a real difficulty test.

That is why I clear Cycle 1 quickly and wait for later cycles before judging the mode's real pressure.

<h2 id="when-to-farm-before-chaos">

I go back to Conflux before pushing Fatebreaker or Chaos when one of these things happens:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">

  <li>my main character is hitting damage cap too early and needs cap conversion,</li>
  <li>the party clears but takes too long,</li>

  <li>or I unlock a new character but cannot gear them properly yet.</li>

That is the point of Conflux. It lets me pause the story push, fix the party, and return with real upgrades instead of hoping for a perfect attempt.

### How do I unlock Conflux?

Conflux starts from the Endless Ragnarok route in Seedhollow. Follow the expansion prompt around the Quest Counter path until the mysterious gate / back alley gate and Conflux representative route appears, then enter the first available cycle.

### Is Conflux solo only?

Yes. I treat Conflux as a solo progression route for strengthening my own party and AI companions before harder Fatebreaker or Chaos checks.

### Do I lose rewards if I leave through the gray exit portal?

The gray exit portal appears after a boss. I use it when the run has paid out enough and the next section is more risk than value.

### Should I farm Cycle 1 for a long time?

No. Cycle 1 is mostly for opening the system and learning the room flow. Once it is stable and the rewards stop solving upgrades, I move to the next layer.

### Should I farm Conflux before Chaos quests?

Yes, when the party is unstable. Use the <Link href="#when-to-farm-before-chaos">Conflux before Chaos checklist</Link> above if AI companions are dying, damage feels capped too low, weapons lag behind, or the next quest expects stronger prep.
