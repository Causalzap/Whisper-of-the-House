<h2 id="conflux">
  Conflux: your first catch-up route
</h2>

Conflux is the first system I use when the party starts falling behind. It gives solo material farming, reward routing, cycle progression, and a safer place to catch up weapons or AI companions.

I return to Conflux when the next fight feels slow, AI companions are dying, weapon progress is behind, or Chaos clears are not repeatable yet.

For portal choices, auras, reward chests, cycles, and early exits, use the <Link href="/granblue-relink-endless-ragnarok/conflux-guide/">Conflux Guide</Link>.

<h2 id="fatebreaker-chaos">
  Fatebreaker and Chaos: stable clears first
</h2>

Fatebreaker and Chaos are where I stop judging progress by access alone. Unlocking a quest does not mean I should farm it.

My Chaos rule is:

<strong>Stable clears first, perfect drops later.</strong>

If the clear costs too many revives, AI companions keep dying, or the boss takes too long, I take the unlock and rebuild before farming. Use the <Link href="/granblue-relink-endless-ragnarok/fatebreaker-chaos-progression/">Fatebreaker and Chaos Progression Guide</Link> for Chaos, Chaos+, Chaos++, Fatebreaker sigils, and when to return to Conflux.

<h2 id="ai-team">
  AI team: build one reliable party first
</h2>

Endless Ragnarok exposes weak AI companions quickly. I do not build six different teams before I have one reliable core.

My first AI goal is simple: one party that can survive, keep uptime, and support my main character through Conflux and early Chaos. After that, I start testing deeper characters and riskier setups.

Use the <Link href="/granblue-relink-endless-ragnarok/best-ai-team-builds/">Best AI Team Builds Guide</Link> if AI companions are dying, losing uptime, or turning solo clears into revive loops.

<h2 id="new-characters">
  New characters: test before investing
</h2>

Endless Ragnarok adds **Beatrix**, **Eustace**, **Fraux**, **Fediel**, **Gallanza**, and **Maglielle**. I unlock and test them early, but I do not spend serious materials until I know whether the character will be my manual main, AI slot, or later project.

The practical rule is:

<strong>unlock quickly, test safely, invest slowly.</strong>

Use the <Link href="/granblue-relink-endless-ragnarok/new-characters-unlock-guide/">New Characters Unlock Guide</Link> for Crewmate Card costs, first-pick order, manual vs AI testing, story / exclusive card unlocks, and when to use Conflux for catch-up.

<h2 id="faq">
  Granblue Relink Endless Ragnarok FAQ
</h2>

### What should I do first in Endless Ragnarok?

Check your save state first. Returning endgame saves should confirm the full post-game requirement and start from the Seedhollow route. Fresh or mid-story saves should keep progressing and check Seedhollow side quests as systems unlock gradually.

### Why is Endless Ragnarok not showing up?

The usual causes are install status, game version, save progress, or checking the wrong Seedhollow route. Use the <Link href="/granblue-relink-endless-ragnarok/how-to-start/">How to Start Guide</Link> before assuming the DLC is broken.

### When does Conflux unlock?

Conflux can begin through a Chapter 6 Seedhollow side quest route, and it also appears along the returning-save Endless Ragnarok route. Use it as a catch-up system before harder endgame checks.

### Should I farm Conflux before Chaos?

Yes, if the party has a build wall. I use Conflux when weapons, sigils, AI survival, or materials are behind before repeating Chaos-level quests.

### Which guide should I read first?

If the route is not open, read **How to Start**. If Conflux is open, read **Conflux Guide**. If Chaos is messy, read **Fatebreaker and Chaos**. If AI is dying, read **Best AI Team Builds**. If you are choosing characters, read **New Characters Unlock Guide**.

### Should I build new characters immediately?

No. I unlock and test new characters first, then spend serious materials only after I know whether the character is my manual main, AI slot, or later project.

### When should I push Chaos?

Push Chaos when the current clear is stable enough to repeat. If the first clear is revive-heavy, slow, or dependent on lucky AI behavior, take the unlock and rebuild before farming.
