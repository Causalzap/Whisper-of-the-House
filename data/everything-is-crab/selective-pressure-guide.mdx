<h2 id="what-is-selective-pressure">
  What is Selective Pressure?
</h2>

**Selective Pressure** is the scaling difficulty system in **Everything Is Crab**.

After you clear runs, you unlock harder Pressure levels. Each level changes what your build must prove. Early Pressure tests basic consistency. Pressure 6 tests biome adaptation. Pressure 7 tests boss survival. Pressure 10 opens the path to Endless. Pressure 11 makes monster scaling feel much faster. Pressure 12 changes animal boss damage and food bonus.

<h2 id="pressure-20-core-build">
  Pressure 20 and Endless-ready core build
</h2>

Pressure 20 is a late progression target. You are not only building to clear the final boss. You are building to survive what happens after the clear: Extinction scaling, more bosses, point-of-interest choices, defend objectives, and infinite stat scaling.

A practical Pressure 20 / Endless-ready core is:

**tested genetic → food tempo tool → sustained main attack → movement → HP / defense → final boss-safe damage → Endless scaling plan → limited Carcinisation.**

Best candidates:

- **Body Slam Tank** if you have Max HP, Shell, Plating, Revenge, movement, and ideally Hemp-style Max HP synergy.
- **Trunk Hybrid** if you can balance Physical and Ability while adding size, cooldown, and defense.
- **Spines / Reflect with sustain** if you have HP, Plating, regeneration, Poisonous / Detachable, and enough movement.
- **Big Physical with defense** if you need a simple route and can avoid becoming too slow.
- **Social Pack with backup attack** only if you have personal boss damage, because Pressure 12 weakens animal boss damage.

Avoid Pressure 20 / Endless attempts with:

- untested genetics,
- social-only boss plans,
- poison without sustain,
- high Carcinisation before the run is ahead,
- slow body plans with no terrain answer,
- builds that barely survive the final boss with no recovery left.

<GuideCallout title="Pressure 20 rule">
Use the build that cleared your highest stable Pressure tier, then improve it for Endless. A clear build is not automatically an Endless build.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What is Selective Pressure in Everything Is Crab?

Selective Pressure is the long-term difficulty ladder in Everything Is Crab. After clears, you unlock harder Pressure levels that add new modifiers, harsher conditions, and stricter build checks.

### How high does Pressure go?

Everything Is Crab 1.0 goes up to Pressure 20.

### What changes from Pressure 2 to Pressure 5?

Pressure 2 increases basic enemy damage. Pressure 3 increases boss damage and makes enemies more aggressive. Pressure 4 increases basic enemy damage again and makes environmental effects build up faster. Pressure 5 gives movement speed bonuses diminishing returns and increases enemy health.

### What changes at Pressure 6?

Pressure 6 increases boss damage and randomizes your starting biome. This means your opener needs to answer the biome first instead of forcing the same build every run.

### What changes at Pressure 7?

Pressure 7 gives bosses protection at the start of fights and after each enrage phase. Environmental damage also increases, so burst damage alone becomes less reliable.

### What changes at Pressure 8?

Pressure 8 reduces healing from health food and gives the final boss an extra attack modifier. You should not rely only on food healing to cover mistakes.

### What changes at Pressure 9?

Pressure 9 makes environmental events happen more frequently and makes enemies advance faster. Positioning, movement, terrain control, and wave clear become more important.

### What changes at Pressure 10?

Pressure 10 slows overall progression and increases enemy health. Clearing Pressure 10 also unlocks Endless Mode access, so your build should be able to scale beyond a normal clear.

### When does Endless Mode unlock?

Endless Mode access unlocks after defeating Pressure 10, but you still need to defeat the game in the run before entering Endless.

### What changes at Pressure 11?

Pressure 11 punishes slow build assembly because monsters scale faster. Use known build patterns, take flexible early value, and commit once a real build-defining piece appears.

### What changes at Pressure 12?

Pressure 12 makes animals deal less damage to bosses and decreases food bonus. Social and animal-based routes need backup personal boss damage, and food tempo becomes more important.

### What happens at Pressure 13-19?

Pressure 13-19 should be treated as late Pressure consistency tests. Keep your build or genetic stable, read the modifier before each run, watch what actually kills you, and adjust one failure point at a time.

### How do I prepare for Pressure 20?

Use your highest proven build and genetic. The safest Pressure 20 candidates are Body Slam Tank, Trunk Hybrid with defense, Spines / Reflect with sustain, or Big Physical with movement.

### What happens in Endless Mode?

Endless Mode continues after the clear with Extinction scaling, more bosses, point-of-interest choices, defend objectives, and infinite scaling for stats such as Max HP, Ability, and Physical.

### What build is best for Endless Mode?

Body Slam Tank, Trunk Hybrid, Spines / Reflect with sustain, and Big Physical with movement are the safest early Endless candidates. Endless wants long-term scaling, not just final boss burst.

### Is Social Pack good for Pressure?

Social Pack can help with wave pressure, but it needs backup personal boss damage. Pressure 12 reduces animal damage to bosses, so social-only boss plans are risky.

### Should I take Carcinisation in Pressure runs?

Take fewer crab points than you would in normal runs. Pressure already adds danger, so Carcinisation should be limited unless your build is clearly ahead.

### How do I survive Cataclysm under Pressure?

Bring movement, HP or defense, recovery, and damage that does not require standing still. During meteorites, do not chase unsafe damage windows just because the boss is low.
