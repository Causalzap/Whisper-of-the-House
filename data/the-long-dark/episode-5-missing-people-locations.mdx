<h2 id="where-to-find-all-missing-people-in-the-long-dark-episode-5">
  Where to find all missing people in The Long Dark Episode 5
</h2>

The Missing People section is one of the first places where players get properly stuck in Episode 5.

The reason is simple: the objective sounds straightforward, but the three targets are spread across the area in ways that are easy to misread:

- one is hidden in a basement
- one is tucked behind a waterfall
- one looks blocked off by a broken bridge even though you actually can reach him

There are **three people total** to find here, including the **Constable**.

If you only want the exact locations, use the sections below in order.

The first missing person is in the **lake area** on the **lower-left side** of the search region.

### How to get there

Head toward the lake and follow the river down until you find a small wooden cabin with a **trap door**.

That trap door leads into the basement where the first missing person is located.

### Do you need a key?

You can enter directly if you have access, but there is also a useful extra detail here:

there is a **key for the basement** you can find in a nearby **fishing hut** by the frozen lake.

That makes this one easier if you want to play it cleanly.

### What to watch out for

The open ice around this area is dangerous because **wolves / timberwolves** can roam nearby.

If you are on a higher difficulty, do not sprint blindly across the lake without checking the area first.

<GuideCallout title="Basement tip">

</GuideCallout>

The second missing person is at the **top of the map**, near the **right-side lake area**, but the important landmark is actually the **far north end of the western river**.

### How to get there

Go to the western river and keep following it north.

Eventually you will reach the **waterfall**. The person you are looking for is **behind the waterfall**, not out in the open.

### The route most players miss

You do **not** reach this one by walking straight at the waterfall from the front.

Instead, use the **small path on the left side of the river** to get around to the back.

That is the key detail.

### Extra clue nearby

There is also a **note in a nearby fishing hut** that points you in the right direction.

If you found the note but not the body, that means you are close. Keep checking the waterfall approach from the **left-side path**.

<GuideCallout title="Waterfall tip">
If you are standing in front of the waterfall and see nothing, you are on the wrong angle. Move to the **left-side path** and approach the area from there.
</GuideCallout>

The third missing person is the **Constable**, and this is the one most players struggle with the most.

He is on the **southeast side** of the map.

### Why this one is confusing

The obvious path appears to be blocked by the **broken bridge** on the eastern side.

That makes many players assume the area is inaccessible.

It is accessible.

### How to cross the broken bridge area

This is the trick:

- go a little farther **down along the river**
- look for the **logs**
- use the **plank on the right side of the bridge**
- cross carefully using the one-way terrain route

This is basically a small **parkour-style crossing**. It is awkward the first time, but it is the intended path.

### Where the Constable actually is

Once you get across, keep going south.

You will find the **Constable** lying near a **small fishing pier**.

### Important pickup

Do **not** forget to take the **note from the Constable’s body**.

This is easy to miss if you are rushing, and it is one of the important objective pickups tied to this section.

<h2 id="best-order-to-find-all-three-missing-people">
  Best order to find all three missing people
</h2>

If you just want the cleanest route, this is the simplest order:

1. **Basement / lake area**
2. **Waterfall**
3. **Constable / broken bridge**

This order works well because:

- the basement one is straightforward once you know about the trap door
- the waterfall one is easier once you are already thinking in terms of hidden paths
- the Constable is the most annoying, so it makes sense to leave him for last once you understand the area better

If you are specifically chasing the achievement, you may want to optimize more aggressively, but for most players this is the most readable order.

<h2 id="search-party-achievement-how-to-get-it">
  Search Party achievement — how to get it
</h2>

This section is also tied to the **Search Party** achievement.

The achievement requires you to find **all three missing people quickly enough** during this mission.

The exact thing that matters here is not deep strategy. It is avoiding wasted movement.

### Best simple rules for Search Party

- do not stop to loot every building
- go in with a route already in mind
- remember that one person is in a **basement**
- remember that one is **behind the waterfall**
- remember that the **Constable is reachable even though the bridge is broken**

If you lose time here, it is usually because of one of these three mistakes:

- you keep circling the lake without noticing the trap door
- you approach the waterfall from the wrong side
- you assume the broken bridge means the southeast target is impossible
