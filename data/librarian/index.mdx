<h2 id="book-sorting">
  Book sorting basics
</h2>

The sorting system has three layers:

1. **Category section**: the book must be in the right area, such as 1A, 1C, 1E, 1K, 1L, 2J, or 2Q.
2. **Series grouping**: books with the same title must stay together.
3. **Volume order**: volumes go from left to right in number order.

Color helps, but it is not enough. Use this table as a fast opening clue, not as a final database.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What should I do first in Librarian: Tidy Up the Arcane Library?

Check the stair maps, complete easy rows, and collect the four hidden keys. Early rows unlock magic, while chests unlock Jump, Sprint, and carry upgrades.

### What is the best ability to unlock first?

**Assemble** is the best first major ability for efficiency. It gathers books from the same series and reduces pile searching.

### Should I use Shelf Guide?

Use Shelf Guide if you are playing blind. If you already have a map open, spend points on Assemble, Insight, Sorting, or Auto-Shelving instead.

### Can I sort books by color?

Not reliably. Color helps, but white books can be Philosophy, Healing, Holy, or Priest-style books, so always confirm the title and section.

### What does it mean when the shelf does not turn blue?

Usually one book is missing, out of order, in the wrong section, or placed in a duplicate row. Check volume order and the shelf indicator before reloading.

### I placed all the books but the shelf still will not turn blue — what am I missing?
The most common cause is a volume order mistake or a split series. The game requires books in the same series to be grouped together AND placed in correct volume order from left to right. A single book out of sequence or one volume sitting in a neighboring row will keep the indicator dark even if every book is technically on the correct shelf.

### What does a dark shelf indicator mean?

A dark indicator means the row or shelf group is unfinished. Look for a missing book, wrong volume order, or a series split across shelves.

### Why does Assemble not grab a book?

Assemble may not pull books already placed on shelves. Keep temporary storage books on the floor if you plan to collect them later with Assemble.

### How many books are in the game?

The full library contains **3,072 books**.

### How many rows do I need to complete?

A full completion requires **400 completed rows**.

### How many keys are there?

There are **four hidden keys**: Crimson Octagon, Golden Diamond, Azure Star, and Emerald Club.

### Which key should I get first?

Get the **Crimson Octagon Key** first because it unlocks Jump, which helps you reach other key locations.

### Where is the Golden Diamond Key?

The Golden Diamond Key is hidden **inside** a decorative vase on the stair railing near the crimson chest. Jump up and look inside the vase.

### How do I unlock Sprint?

Sprint is unlocked from the chest tied to the **Emerald Club Key**. The key is buried under books near the scale table bench.

### How do I get Efficiency Librarian?

Finish the entire game in under 3 hours. Learn the layout first, collect chest upgrades early, and rush Assemble.

### Do chest upgrades count as magic for Anti-Magic Master?

Chest upgrades such as Jump, Sprint, and Carry Capacity are passive minor upgrades, not active major magic. For Anti-Magic Master, avoid active major spells such as Assemble, Insight, Shelf Guide, Sorting, and Auto-Shelving.

### How do I clear the Special Stage?

After finishing the main library, enter the Special Stage and use the ultimate skill. That is the route for Overtime Avoider.

### Is You Are Fired part of a normal completion route?

No. You Are Fired is a joke route where the library is filled incorrectly without completing a single row. Do it separately.

The most common cause is a volume order mistake or a split series. The game requires books in the same series to be grouped together AND placed in correct volume order from left to right. A single book out of sequence or one volume sitting in a neighboring row will keep the indicator dark even if every book is technically on the correct shelf.
