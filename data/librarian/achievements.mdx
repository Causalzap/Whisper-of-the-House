The cleanest 100% route is:

<h2 id="normal-magic-run">
  Run 1: normal magic completion
</h2>

Your first serious run should be a normal magic completion.

This run covers:

- The First Step of Organizing
- Intermediate Librarian
- Veteran Librarian
- The Grand Librarian
- Novice Mage
- Sage of the Library
- Life Hack Wizard
- The Archmage
- Overtime Avoider

Progress achievements unlock naturally at **row 1, row 50, row 200, and row 400**. Magic achievements unlock as you buy your first major spell, unlock all major spells, open all chest upgrades, and eventually max every major spell level.

The goal is simple: learn the library, collect all keys, use magic freely, complete all 400 rows, then clear the Special Stage.

<h2 id="efficiency-librarian">
  Efficiency Librarian under 3 hours
</h2>

**Efficiency Librarian** requires completing the game in under 3 hours.

Do not attempt this blind. A normal first completion can take much longer while you learn sections, keys, and spell routing.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/librarian/librarian-clear-time-results.webp"
    alt="Librarian Tidy Up the Arcane Library clear time and magic usage results screen."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    A learning run can be far slower than the 3-hour requirement, so Efficiency Librarian needs a planned route.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

Recommended Efficiency route:

1. Start with easy rows only if they are directly in front of you.
2. Get the **Crimson Octagon Key** and unlock Jump.
3. Collect the remaining key upgrades before serious sorting.
4. Prioritize **Carry Capacity +3** and **Carry Capacity +2** because they improve Assemble routes.
5. Rush **Assemble**.
6. Add **Sorting** once you can regularly carry 7+ books.
7. Use **Insight** only when searching still costs time.
8. Use **Auto-Shelving** when the target shelf is already obvious.
9. Avoid wandering between random sections.

With both carry upgrades, a 10-book Assemble route takes fewer trips. Across hundreds of rows, this is one of the biggest time saves in the game.

<h2 id="anti-magic-master">
  Anti-Magic Master no-magic route
</h2>

**Anti-Magic Master** is the manual challenge route. Do not use active major magic.

<GuideTable
  caption="What counts as magic for Anti-Magic Master."
  columns={magicColumns}
  rows={magicRows}
/>

For Anti-Magic Master, avoid:

- Assemble
- Insight
- Shelf Guide
- Sorting
- Auto-Shelving

Based on community testing, chest upgrades such as **Jump**, **Sprint**, and **Carry Capacity** are passive minor upgrades rather than active major magic. The safest interpretation is still: do all actual sorting manually and avoid active spell assistance.

Without active sorting magic, expect this run to take much longer than a normal clear. Community reports suggest **8 to 12 hours or more**, depending on how well you know the shelf sections and book series.

Recommended Anti-Magic route:

1. Open maps or screenshots before starting.
2. Collect passive chest upgrades early.
3. Sort by section, not by random piles.
4. Keep each title series together.
5. Check volume order from left to right.
6. Use shelf indicators to catch incomplete rows.
7. Expect this to take much longer than a magic run.

<GuideCallout title="Anti-Magic rule">
Anti-Magic Master is not a speed route. It is a patience route. Your main tools are maps, memory, section knowledge, and careful volume order.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="you-are-fired">
  You Are Fired route
</h2>

**You Are Fired!** is the joke / bad-ending achievement.

The requirement is to arrange the library incorrectly without completing a single row, then leave.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/librarian/librarian-you-are-fired-setup.webp"
    alt="Librarian Tidy Up the Arcane Library You Are Fired achievement setup with intentionally incorrect book arrangement."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    You Are Fired is the opposite of a normal run: the goal is to avoid completing rows.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

Recommended approach:

1. Do this on a separate route or save-copy route.
2. Fill shelves with books placed in the wrong rows or wrong series groups.
3. Make sure **no shelf row turns blue** and no row is completed.
4. If a row completes by accident, break it before leaving.
5. Exit through the library exit / barrier after the library is filled incorrectly.
6. Do not just close the game or quit to menu. Use the in-game exit route.

<GuideCallout title="You Are Fired trigger check">
If You Are Fired does not unlock, confirm two things: **no row is completed** and you are leaving through the library exit, not just closing the game or quitting to the menu.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are in Librarian: Tidy Up the Arcane Library?

There are **12 achievements**.

### Can I get every achievement in one run?

No practical route should try that. Efficiency Librarian, Anti-Magic Master, and You Are Fired have conflicting requirements.

### What is the best 100% achievement route?

Do a normal magic completion first, then a separate Efficiency run, a separate Anti-Magic run, and a separate You Are Fired route.

### How do I unlock The Grand Librarian?

Complete all **400 rows** and finish the main library.

### How do I unlock Overtime Avoider?

Finish the main library, enter the Special Stage, and use the ultimate skill.

### How do I unlock Efficiency Librarian?

Complete the game in under **3 hours**. Learn the library first, collect all key upgrades early, rush Assemble, and add Sorting after carry upgrades.

### Should I try Efficiency Librarian on my first run?

No. Learn the key route, shelf sections, and Assemble loop first. A blind run wastes too much time.

### How do I unlock Anti-Magic Master?

Complete the game without active major magic. Avoid Assemble, Insight, Shelf Guide, Sorting, and Auto-Shelving.

### Do chest upgrades count against Anti-Magic Master?

Based on community testing, chest upgrades such as Jump, Sprint, and Carry Capacity are passive minor upgrades, not active major magic.

### Can I use Assemble for Anti-Magic Master?

No. Treat Assemble as active major magic and avoid it during Anti-Magic Master.

### Can I use Sorting for Anti-Magic Master?

No. Sorting is an active major magic tool, so avoid it for Anti-Magic Master.

### How do I unlock You Are Fired?

Fill the library incorrectly without completing any row, then leave through the library exit / barrier. Do not just quit to menu or close the game. If any row turns blue, break that row before leaving.

### Should I do You Are Fired on my main save?

No. Do it on a separate route or save-copy route because it intentionally ruins the library arrangement.

### What achievement should I do last?

Do **Anti-Magic Master** last if you dislike long manual sorting. It is the most grind-heavy route because you cannot use active sorting magic.

### How long does Anti-Magic Master take?

Expect Anti-Magic Master to take much longer than a normal magic run. Community reports commonly put no-magic routes in the **8 to 12+ hour** range depending on section knowledge, routing, and how often you need to check maps.

### Are achievements missable?

Progress and magic achievements are easy to recover during normal play. Challenge achievements are route-specific, so plan separate runs for Efficiency, Anti-Magic, and You Are Fired.
