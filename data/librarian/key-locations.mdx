<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
There are **four hidden keys** in Librarian: Tidy Up the Arcane Library:

1. **Crimson Octagon Key** — under the starting crest — unlocks **Jump**.
2. **Golden Diamond Key** — inside the stair railing vase — unlocks **Carry Capacity +3**.
3. **Azure Star Key** — on the second-floor right-side bookshelf — unlocks **Carry Capacity +2**.
4. **Emerald Club Key** — under books on the bench near the scale — unlocks **Sprint / Run**.

Get the **Crimson Octagon Key first**, because Jump makes the other key routes possible or much easier.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

Open the matching chest to unlock **Jump**.

<h2 id="golden-diamond-key">
  Golden Diamond Key location
</h2>

The **Golden Diamond Key** is near the crimson chest area, but it is easy to miss because it is hidden **inside** a decorative vase on the stair railing.

Do not only check the top of the vase. Jump up, look down into it, and grab the key from inside.

<h2 id="chest-rewards">
  What each chest unlocks
</h2>

The key rewards are simple, but they change the pace of the whole game.

<GuideTable
  caption="Chest rewards and why they matter."
  columns={chestColumns}
  rows={chestRows}
/>

Do not over-optimize the order after Jump. For most players, the important rule is simple:

**Unlock Jump first, then get every other chest before serious sorting.**

For efficiency routes, the carry upgrades are often more valuable than Sprint because they reduce the number of trips needed after Assemble. Sprint helps movement, but carrying more books changes how many actions each series takes.

If you are planning an **Efficiency Librarian** run, collect all four upgrades before committing to long sorting routes.

<h2 id="when-to-get-keys">
  Should you get all keys before sorting books?
</h2>

Yes, in most runs.

You can sort a few obvious early rows first if they are directly in front of you, but do not delay the key route for long. The chest rewards affect the entire run:

- more carry slots mean fewer trips
- Sprint means faster movement
- Jump means better routing
- early upgrades make Assemble and manual sorting more efficient

For a casual first run, get the keys after your first few easy rows. For a fast run, get them as soon as possible.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many keys are in Librarian: Tidy Up the Arcane Library?

There are **four hidden keys**: Crimson Octagon, Golden Diamond, Azure Star, and Emerald Club.

### Which key should I get first?

Get the **Crimson Octagon Key** first. Its chest unlocks Jump, which makes the other key locations easier or possible to reach.

### Where is the Crimson Octagon Key?

It is hanging under the crest / emblem directly ahead when you load into the library.

### What does the Crimson Octagon Key unlock?

It unlocks **Jump**.

### Where is the Golden Diamond Key?

The Golden Diamond Key is hidden **inside** a decorative vase on the stair railing near the crimson chest area.

### Why can I not find the Golden Diamond Key?

You are probably looking on top of the vase instead of inside it. Jump onto the railing decoration and angle the camera down into the vase.

### What does the Golden Diamond Key unlock?

It unlocks **Carry Capacity +3**.

### Where is the Azure Star Key?

The Azure Star Key is on the second floor, on top of the right-side bookshelf near the far end of the room.

### What does the Azure Star Key unlock?

It unlocks **Carry Capacity +2**.

### Where is the Emerald Club Key?

The Emerald Club Key is on the bench to the right of the scale table, buried under a pile of books.

### What does the Emerald Club Key unlock?

It unlocks **Sprint / Run**.

### Can I get the other keys without unlocking Jump first?

Some key spots may be reachable with awkward movement, but Jump makes the route straightforward. The Crimson Octagon Key does not require Jump because it is at ground level near the starting view, so get it first and avoid fighting the movement.

### Are key upgrades required for completion?

They are not the same as sorting rows, but they make the game much faster. Sprint and carry capacity upgrades are especially valuable.

### Do chest upgrades count as magic for Anti-Magic Master?

Based on community testing, chest upgrades such as Jump, Sprint, and Carry Capacity are passive minor upgrades, not active major magic. Avoid active major spells such as Assemble, Insight, Shelf Guide, Sorting, and Auto-Shelving for Anti-Magic Master.

### Should I get the keys before an Efficiency Librarian run?

Yes. Get all four key upgrades before serious sorting. Jump unlocks the route, Sprint reduces travel time, and the carry upgrades matter most once you use Assemble. With +5 total carry capacity, a 10-book series can take about two trips instead of several smaller trips, which adds up across hundreds of rows.
