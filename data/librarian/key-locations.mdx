<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
The fastest key route in **Librarian: Tidy Up the Arcane Library** is:

**Crimson Octagon Key → Golden Diamond Key → Emerald Club Key → Azure Star Key**.

The **Crimson Octagon Key** is under the starting crest and unlocks **Jump**. After that, use Jump to get the **Golden Diamond Key** inside the stair railing vase, clear the bench near the scale for the **Emerald Club Key**, then grab the **Azure Star Key** from the second-floor bookshelf. Once every chest is opened, you can **jump, run, and carry 15 books**.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

If you only need the route, get the **Crimson Octagon Key first**, open the Jump chest, then collect the Golden, Emerald, and Azure keys before doing serious book sorting.

<h2 id="golden-diamond-key">
  Golden Diamond Key location
</h2>

After you unlock Jump, go toward the stair railing / crimson chest area. The **Golden Diamond Key** is hidden **inside a decorative vase** on the railing decoration.

Do not only check the top of the vase. Jump up, angle the camera down, and look inside the vase.

<h2 id="when-to-get-keys">
  Should you get all keys before sorting books?
</h2>

Yes, in most runs.

You can sort a few obvious nearby books first, but do not delay the key route for long. The chest upgrades affect the whole library:

- **Jump** helps you reach elevated spots.
- **Sprint / Run** reduces travel time.
- **Carry Capacity +3** and **Carry Capacity +2** reduce the number of book trips.
- The full route gives you **15 total book capacity**.

For a casual first run, collect the keys after you understand the main room. For a faster run, get the four key upgrades as early as possible.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many keys are in Librarian: Tidy Up the Arcane Library?

There are **four hidden keys**: Crimson Octagon, Golden Diamond, Emerald Club, and Azure Star.

### What is the best key order?

The best order is **Crimson Octagon → Golden Diamond → Emerald Club → Azure Star**.

### Which key should I get first?

Get the **Crimson Octagon Key** first because its chest unlocks **Jump**.

### Where is the Crimson Octagon Key?

The Crimson Octagon Key is under the starting crest / wall emblem directly ahead when you load into the library.

### What does the Crimson Octagon Key unlock?

It unlocks **Jump**.

### Where is the Golden Diamond Key?

The Golden Diamond Key is hidden **inside the stair railing vase** near the crimson chest / stair area.

### Why can I not find the Golden Diamond Key?

You are probably looking on top of the vase instead of inside it. Jump up and angle the camera down into the vase.

### What does the Golden Diamond Key unlock?

It unlocks **Carry Capacity +3**.

### Where is the Emerald Club Key?

The Emerald Club Key is on the bench near the scale table, buried under a pile of books.

### What does the Emerald Club Key unlock?

It unlocks **Sprint / Run**.

### Where is the Azure Star Key?

The Azure Star Key is on the second floor, on top of the right-side bookshelf near the far wall.

### What does the Azure Star Key unlock?

It unlocks **Carry Capacity +2**.

### How do you carry 15 books?

Open both carrying capacity chests. The Golden Diamond chest gives one carry upgrade, and the Azure Star chest gives the other. Together, they let you carry **15 books**.

### Are key upgrades required to finish the game?

They are not the same as sorting books, but they make the game much faster. Jump, Sprint / Run, and extra carrying capacity all save time across the full library.
