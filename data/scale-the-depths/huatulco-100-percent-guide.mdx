<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
To get **100% in Huatulco**, solve the pressure plate door puzzles, collect every **message, artifact, bait, cosmetic, boat item, switch and chest**, then catch and prep **Hoga**.

The safest route is:

**opening messages → boat blueprint → southwest plate → southeast plate → first cosmetic → south plate → Quetzalcoatl Statue → Red Switch → west plate → Rubber Ball → Aztec Sun Stone → Shark Tooth → Yellow Switch → second cosmetic → Blue Switches → Coffee Cup → Pouch → boat decal → cloth chest → Hoga**.

Do not rush straight to Hoga. The easiest Huatulco misses are **Shark Tooth**, **Pouch**, **boat decal**, **Blue Switch 2/2's nearby message**, and the pressure plate route around **Quetzalcoatl Statue / Rubber Ball / Aztec Sun Stone**.

<h2 id="hoga">
  How to catch Hoga
</h2>

Hoga is the Huatulco legendary fish, so treat it differently from a normal money fish. It sits at the end of the final deep route and moves in wider arcs than standard fish. The key is to stay on Hoga's path instead of chasing every small turn after it already changes direction.

Do not start the attempt with a nearly full bag. Hoga works best as a clean, dedicated run after the puzzle route, switches, Pouch, boat decal and cloth chest are done.

</ol>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What should I do if the Huatulco door does not open?

First check whether the block is fully centered on the pressure plate. If it is only touching the edge, the door may not trigger. If it still does not open, check whether the room wants a different plate direction: southwest, southeast, straight south or west.

### Where is the Quetzalcoatl Statue?

Quetzalcoatl Statue is after the early pressure plate route, in the first major artifact pocket after the straight-south door step.

### Where is Shark Tooth bait?

Shark Tooth is in the dense middle route near the Aztec Sun Stone. If you find the Aztec Sun Stone, slow down and check the nearby path before moving to the Yellow Switch.

### Where is Pouch bait?
Pouch is in the late route after the Coffee Cup artifact, before the boat decal and cloth chest. If you went straight to Hoga without collecting it, retrace the late collection route.

### Where is Cosmetic 2 in Huatulco?

Cosmetic 2 is after the Yellow Switch and before Blue Switch 1/2. Check the side pocket off the shortcut route before following the main path toward the blue switch chain.

### Where is Hoga?

Hoga is in the final deep Huatulco route after the pressure plate puzzle chain and late collection path. Clear Pouch, the boat decal and the cloth chest before treating Hoga as your final target.

### How do I catch Hoga?

Hoga moves in wider arcs than normal fish and is less forgiving if your hook or line is weak. Stay ahead of its movement path instead of chasing every turn, and upgrade hook or line if the fight takes too long.
