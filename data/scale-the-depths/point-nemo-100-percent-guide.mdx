<h2 id="base-door-puzzle">
  Point Nemo door puzzle solution
</h2>

The Point Nemo door puzzle is solved by reading **three visual hints** scattered around the sea, then pressing the three switches at the door in the matching order.

Do not look for a movable block. Do not look for pressure plates. The only thing that matters is the white-dot position on each hint sign.

<GuideTable
caption="How to solve the Point Nemo door puzzle."
columns={puzzleColumns}
rows={puzzleRows}
/>

A simple way to track it:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>If the first hint shows the white dot on the left, your first door press is left.</li>
  <li>If the second hint shows the white dot in the middle, your second door press is middle.</li>
  <li>If the third hint shows the white dot on the right, your third door press is right.</li>
</ul>

The door itself has three switches arranged as left, middle and right. Match each sea hint to the door switch in that same position, then press them in the order you found the hints.

Your exact order depends on the hints you find in your own run. If the door does not open, leave the area, come back to reset, then re-enter the sequence after checking all three hints again.

### How do I get 100% in Point Nemo?

Solve the three-hint door puzzle, activate all blue, red and yellow switches, collect 5 messages, artifacts, Magnet, Sewing Needle, the boat blueprint and cosmetic, then catch and prep Apophis 99942.

### Is the Point Nemo door puzzle the same for everyone?

No. Find the three visual hints in your own Point Nemo route, record the white-dot positions, then press the door switches in that order.

### How do I reset the Point Nemo door puzzle?

If you press the wrong switch sequence, leave the area and come back. Then recheck the three hints and enter the sequence again.

### How many messages are in Point Nemo?

Point Nemo has **5 messages in bottles**. If your message count is incomplete, recheck the opening route, blue-switch route and late boat-blueprint route.

### Where is the Sextant in Point Nemo?

Sextant is in a dim magenta-lit side room near the downward shortcut, between Loaded Support Sphere and Toy Submarine. If debris blocks the pickup, move your line away and approach from a cleaner angle.

### What do the red switches do?

The red switches unlock the middle container shortcut. You need both Red Switch 1/2 and Red Switch 2/2 for the shortcut route.

### What does the Yellow Switch do?

The Yellow Switch unlocks a shortcut to the top exit of the legendary fish nest, which helps with Apophis 99942 attempts and final cleanup.

### What is the legendary fish in Point Nemo?

The Point Nemo legendary fish is Apophis 99942.

### Why does my line snap while fighting Apophis 99942?

Apophis is deeper than normal fish and can strain the line during the chase. Back off when the line is close to snapping, reposition, and upgrade hook damage or line length if the fight keeps dragging on.

### Should I catch Apophis 99942 before collecting everything else?

No. Open shortcuts and bank nearby messages, artifacts, baits, the boat blueprint and cosmetic first. Save Apophis for the final focused route.
