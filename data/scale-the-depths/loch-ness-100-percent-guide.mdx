<h2 id="treasures-secrets">
  Treasures, messages and artifacts
</h2>

Loch Ness tracks two types of secrets on the GPS: **treasures** (7/7) and **customers** (23/23). Messages and artifacts are tracked separately in the CARP Manual data list.

If your GPS shows 7/7 treasures but the CARP Manual still has missing entries, you are not done.

Things to look for on exploration runs:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>messages in bottles — often in deep side pockets or off switch-opened branches,</li>
  <li>research artifacts — can sit behind grass, in corners, or off routes that look unimportant,</li>
  <li>money chests,</li>
  <li>boat cosmetics and decoration pickups,</li>

<h2 id="beithir">
  Where to find Beithir and how to catch it
</h2>

**Beithir** is the Loch Ness legendary fish. It is a large serpent from Scottish folklore and the highest-value catch in the area. Nessie will not fully fill on anything else.

Beithir spawns only in deep water. You will not see it until your rod has enough distance to reach the lower depth layers. If you have been fishing Loch Ness for a while and have never encountered it, upgrade your rod and go deeper.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What should I do if the Loch Ness door or switch does not work?

Hit the switch and then sweep the nearby branches before assuming nothing happened. Some switches open shortcuts or side paths in a different part of the area rather than immediately nearby.

### Where is the Beithir in Loch Ness?

Beithir spawns in deep water. You need maximum rod distance to reach its depth layer. If you have never seen it, upgrade your rod first — the fish is not accessible at shallow depths.

### How do I catch Beithir?

Upgrade hook damage before the attempt, equip repellent to keep your bag clear, reach the deep layer and stay ahead of Beithir's movement arc instead of chasing every turn. The fight takes longer than any normal fish, so do not force it with weak gear.

### How do I feed Nessie?

Nessie appears after enough Loch Ness progress. She needs legendary-tier fish — specifically Beithir — to fill meaningfully. Do not waste Beithir on other customers.

### Why does the next location unlock before Loch Ness is complete?

The next location unlocks after enough main progress, not after 100% cleanup. You can still be missing customers, treasures, fish, messages or artifacts. Return and finish before moving on if you want clean completionist progress.

### How many customers are in Loch Ness?

The GPS shows 23/23 when the customer cleanup is complete.

### How many treasures are in Loch Ness?

The GPS shows 7/7 when the treasure cleanup is complete.

### What upgrades carry over to the next location?

Most upgrades reset when you move to Outer Banks. The game frames this as different waters needing different tools. Spend any remaining Loch Ness money before leaving — you cannot take currency with you either.

### What is the best upgrade to buy first in Loch Ness?

Rod distance. It gates hidden routes, deeper fish, the Beithir spawn layer and most of the secret content. Every other upgrade compounds on top of the reach you unlock.

### Why can I not find the last treasure?

Stop money fishing and make exploration-only runs. Clear every bush, check every switch branch and use line reels to reach any depth pockets you have not fully swept. The last treasure is almost always behind a switch route or hidden grass.

### Should I 100% Loch Ness before going to Outer Banks?

Yes, especially if you want clean achievement progress. Returning later is possible but awkward — and upgrades reset, so you will need to rebuild gear from scratch when you do.
