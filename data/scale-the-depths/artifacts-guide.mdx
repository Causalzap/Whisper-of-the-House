  Scale the Depths artifacts guide
</h2>

Artifacts in **Scale the Depths** are easy to mix up with other secrets. A chest, bait pickup, message bottle, decal, boat blueprint, and artifact can all look like “something collectible” underwater, but only artifact pickups fill the **Artifacts** tab.

That is the main rule for this page:

<strong>If you are hunting artifacts, check artifact slots by region. Do not use treasure, message, bait, or cosmetic progress as a substitute.</strong>

For every collectible type together, use the <Link href="/scale-the-depths/all-collectibles-secrets">All Collectibles and Secrets Guide</Link>. This page is only for artifacts.

<h2 id="artifact-vs-treasure">
  Artifact vs treasure, message, bait, and chest
</h2>

Artifacts are research collectibles. When you collect one, the game can show an **Artifact Discovered** popup, and the item appears in the **Artifacts** tab of the almanac.

Do not confuse artifacts with:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li><strong>Messages:</strong> bottles that open text entries in the Messages tab.</li>
  <li><strong>Bait:</strong> items that unlock new bait effects in the bait bucket.</li>
  <li><strong>Chests:</strong> money, cosmetics, boats, decals, or other route rewards.</li>
  <li><strong>Knives:</strong> scaling tools tracked in a separate tab.</li>
  <li><strong>Legendary fish:</strong> boss fish that can open routes or rewards, but are not automatically artifacts by themselves.</li>
</ul>

If a region still feels incomplete, check the tab with the missing silhouette. That tells you whether you need an artifact, message, fish, customer, or knife.

<h2 id="faq">
  Scale the Depths artifacts FAQ
</h2>

### How many artifacts are in Scale the Depths?

Track artifacts by region in the almanac. This guide covers 3 Loch Ness slots, 4 Huatulco artifacts, 7 Outer Banks slots, and 5 Point Nemo slots.

### What is the difference between artifacts and treasures?

Artifacts fill the Artifacts tab and can trigger an Artifact Discovered popup. Treasures, messages, bait, cosmetics, chests, boats, decals, and knives can all count elsewhere, so always check the exact almanac tab.

### How many artifacts are in Loch Ness?

Loch Ness has 3 artifacts: Nessie Plush, Sputnik 1, and English Shield. Confirm all three before leaving the region.

### How many artifacts are in Huatulco?

Huatulco has 4 named artifacts to track: Quetzalcoatl Statue, Rubber Ball, Aztec Sun Stone, and Coffee Cup.

### Where is the Huatulco Aztec Sun Stone artifact?

The Aztec Sun Stone is behind the Huatulco pressure-plate / box route. Push the box onto the plate, wait for the door to open, then enter the opened room before retracting.

### How many artifacts are in Outer Banks?

Outer Banks has 7 artifact slots. Start with Rat King Plush, Bio-Specimen Containment Unit, Sonar Receiver, Jar of Dirt, Canned Bread, Spanish Doubloon, then check the boss-route artifact slot.

### Is Soda Can an Outer Banks artifact?

Do not use Soda Can as the artifact name unless your own Artifacts tab shows that exact name. If you only saw an Artifact Discovered popup on the lower route, treat it as a lower-route artifact and confirm the actual item name in the almanac.

### What is the most common Outer Banks artifact miss?

Sonar Receiver is one of the easiest misses because it sits in a hidden bush pocket. If your Outer Banks artifact count is short, clear the full bush pocket instead of only passing through it.

### How many artifacts are in Point Nemo?

Point Nemo has 5 artifact slots: Asteroid Fragment, Loaded Support Sphere, Sextant, Toy Submarine, and a final boss-route artifact.

### Why am I missing one Point Nemo artifact?

You probably missed the red lever secret, Toy Submarine route, or the final boss-route Artifact Discovered popup. Point Nemo mixes artifacts with messages, baits, switches, and legendary fish cleanup, so check the artifact tab directly.

### Is Toy Submarine the final Point Nemo artifact?

Toy Submarine is one of the key Point Nemo artifacts and a common miss, but your almanac may still show another artifact slot afterward. Confirm the final boss-route popup before leaving.

### Can I return later for missed artifacts?

Yes. You can return later, but finishing artifact slots before moving on is cleaner because it is easy to forget whether the missing route was a lever, pressure plate, switch, hidden wall, or boss-route cleanup.

### Do artifacts count toward 100% completion?

Yes. Region completion depends on more than fish. You also need customers, messages, artifacts, knives, and other collectibles depending on the area.
