<h2 id="what-is-the-game">
  What is Happy's Humble Burger Cult?
</h2>

Happy's Humble Burger Cult combines restaurant order management with a mandatory
horror escape phase. I complete themed food orders until quota, clock out at the
shift lever, clear the active Closeout Duties, and return to the elevator only
after the Escape objective appears.

Most failed runs happen at one of three stages: learning the restaurant, building
and delivering the correct order, or finishing the shift after quota. This hub
routes each problem to a focused guide instead of repeating every mechanic on one
oversized page.

<h2 id="faq">
  Happy's Humble Burger Cult Guide FAQ
</h2>

### Which guide should I read first?

Start with the First Shift Guide unless you have already completed a full route
from the first order to the elevator. It establishes the station layout and
terminology used by the two problem-specific guides.

### Is this a complete Happy's Humble Burger Cult Wiki?

No. This is a focused guide cluster for first-clear, kitchen-order, and Closeout
problems. New pages are added only when they solve a distinct search problem with
tested routes, values, or screenshots.

### Is Solo easier after the July 18 patch?

A fresh completion streak now begins with one Closeout Duty, and remaining
Stability delays Joy after clock-out. That makes a new Solo clear more
reasonable, but Toe still does not replace another player during Closeout.

### Does Pacifist Mode affect progression or achievements?

I have not confirmed that progression, rewards, and achievements behave
identically in Pacifist Mode. I use it for learning routes without presenting
those outcomes as verified.

### Are the recipe and Closeout details version-safe?

They reflect launch-week July 2026 testing and the July 18 balance patch. The
individual guides mark observed values and unverified boundaries rather than
presenting every number as permanent.
