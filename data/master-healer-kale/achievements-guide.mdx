<h2 id="master-healer-kale-achievements-guide">
  Master Healer Kale achievements guide
</h2>

The achievement list in **Master Healer Kale with Useless Party** is small enough to finish, but not small enough to brute force blindly.

There are **38 achievements**, and the completion rates show where players actually get stuck. The first few are common: **That Goes Fast**, for reaching exactly 0 mana, is at **81.1%**; **First Step**, for defeating Goblin Leader, is **78.0%**; and **Decent Mining**, for collecting 6 Ruby, is **67.2%**.

The endgame drops sharply. **Part Of The Journey Is The End**, the normal Demon King clear, is **21.1%**. **Why So Serious?**, the Nightmare Demon King clear, is only **5.5%**.

That gap tells me the achievement route is not just “beat the story.” It is a full cleanup path: build stability, solve dungeon effects, prepare spell chains, scale through facilities, then finish Nightmare and 100% Tavern.

<GuideCallout title="Achievement rate note">
The completion rates on this page are useful as a rarity and routing signal, not as permanent difficulty labels. </GuideCallout>

<h2 id="early-mistake-achievements">
  Early mistake achievements: get them, then stop playing badly
</h2>

A few achievements reward bad habits once. I get them early, then go back to playing properly.

**Too Close** asks me to heal Grandpa Bagel when he is at **10% health or less**. I do this in a safe early fight where I can afford the risk. I do not make it a normal healing habit.

**I Did Try** asks me to cast **Party Heal** when the entire party is at full health. Once Party Heal is unlocked, I cast it once in an easy situation, then stop wasting it.

**Peace In Our Time** asks me to cast **Meditate 2 times in a battle**. I prefer a longer safe fight, not a speed clear, because short fights can end before the second Meditate matters.

**I've Come To Bargain** asks me to cast **Heal 200 times**. I do not farm this directly. It comes naturally if I play through story, dungeon cleanup, and endgame farming.

**Another Path** asks me to use **Reset Skill Tree**. I like doing this after I understand my build direction, not before. Reset Point refunds gold, skill points, and Ruby used, and it also helps if the early tree went in the wrong direction.

<h2 id="faq">
  Master Healer Kale achievements FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are in Master Healer Kale?

There are **38 achievements** in the full list.

### What is the rarest achievement?

**Why So Serious?**, which requires defeating the Demon King in Nightmare Mode, is the rarest achievement at **5.5%**.

### What is the best order for 100% achievements?

I clear story and resource achievements first, then level and attack unlocks, then dungeon-condition achievements, then spell and facility achievements, then Nightmare bosses and 100% Tavern cleanup.

### Should I chase dungeon achievements as soon as they appear?

No. I wait until my build solves the dungeon effect. Underwater Abyss needs speed, Magma Core needs Shield timing, Gravity Arena needs Esuna, and Winterfall Peak needs mana recovery.

### How do I get Gotta Go Fast?

Clear **Underwater Abyss** in **50 seconds or less**. The dungeon gives 100 seconds before the party runs out of breath, but the achievement requires a much faster clear.

### How do I get It's Fire Burning?

In **Magma Core**, use Shield on all of Kale's party while holding Fire Dragon's ability. I prepare Protection, mana, and party survival before trying it.

### How do I get We Don't Do That Here?

Bring **Esuna** into **Gravity Arena** and remove the minus speed debuff **3 times**.

### How do I get Machine Gun Punch?

Cast **10 spells in a row** while **Angel Form** is active. I prepare mana, cooldowns, and spell order before starting the chain.

### How do I get I Can Do This All Day?

Cast **Raise 2 times in a battle**. I do it in a long controlled fight because Raise has a **60-second cooldown** in the shown tooltip.

### When should I use Reset Skill Tree?

Use it when your build is clearly solving the wrong problem or when you are reorganizing for achievements and 100% Tavern cleanup. It also gives the **Another Path** achievement.

### What should I do before Nightmare Demon King?

Farm Nightmare Ruby, upgrade Training Facility and Research Facility, secure Grandpa Bagel survival, commit to a damage route, and bring emergency tools like Raise, Protection, Party Heal, Meditate, and Angel Form.

### Should I finish 100% Tavern before Nightmare Demon King?

I usually do not. I prefer clearing Nightmare progression first, then finishing 100% Tavern nodes as final cleanup.
