<h2 id="beginnings-are-always-hard">
  Beginnings Are Always Hard: Sebastian's first job
</h2>

Sebastian is the first customer. His Solonez needs simple bodywork: remove rust, paint the vehicle green and polish the fresh paint.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/cheap-car-repair/cheap-car-repair-first-job-objectives.webp"
    alt="Cheap Car Repair Sebastian's Solonez first job objectives showing rust removal, green paint and polishing."
    width={300}
    height={400}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    Sebastian's job teaches the basic loop: remove rust, paint, polish and call the customer.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

Do not overthink this job. It teaches you that the checklist matters more than perfection.

Use this order:

<ol className="my-6 list-decimal space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Take the job from Sebastian.</li>
  <li>Enter the garage and turn on the light.</li>
  <li>Pick up the grinder.</li>
  <li>Remove enough rust for the objective.</li>
  <li>Paint the car green.</li>
  <li>Switch to polishing and polish the fresh paint.</li>
  <li>Call Sebastian when the job accepts the work.</li>
</ol>

If the job will not complete, check whether you missed a rust patch, forgot to polish or did not call the customer.

<h2 id="high-voltage-electrician">
  High Voltage Electrician: electrical box order
</h2>

After the first job, the power goes out. The game sends you to the neighbor's electrical box. This is one of the first non-car story tasks, and it can feel confusing because you are suddenly solving a wire puzzle instead of repairing a vehicle.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/cheap-car-repair/cheap-car-repair-electrical-box-wire-order.webp"
    alt="Cheap Car Repair electrical box wiring diagram sequence during the High Voltage Electrician mission."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    Open the electrical box and use the diagram on the panel before clicking the wires.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

The early electrical box puzzle uses **four colored wires**. Your job is to read the diagram on the box and click the wires in that shown order. Do not guess from memory if your current build shows a different layout after a patch.

<GuideTable
caption="High Voltage Electrician electrical box steps."
columns={electricalColumns}
rows={electricalRows}
/>

If you click the wrong wire, do not treat it as a permanent failure. Step back, look at the diagram again and retry the sequence. The important part is the order, not your exact standing position.

<h2 id="apple-of-my-eye">
  Apple of My Eye: fix your own car
</h2>

After power is restored, you work on your own car. This is where the game teaches the opposite of cheap customer work: do not cut corners on your own stuff unless the story only needs the car to be usable.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/cheap-car-repair/cheap-car-repair-own-car-rust-repair-annotated.webp"
    alt="Cheap Car Repair own car rust repair process showing rust, grinding, repainting and a finished hood."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    Your own car teaches the full bodywork flow: remove rust, grind it down, repaint the panel and finish the surface.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

Remove rust, paint the car and polish it. You do not need to turn it into a perfect restoration, but this is a good time to learn how spray, polishing and grinder disc wear behave before harder jobs start.

Beginner tips:

<ul className="my-6 list-disc space-y-2 pl-6">
  <li>Check small rust spots before painting.</li>
  <li>Change the grinder disc when it wears out.</li>
  <li>Do not waste every spray can on tiny cosmetic differences.</li>
  <li>Use this mission to practice before real customers become more demanding.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="applied-signology">
  Applied Signology: build the shop sign
</h2>

Applied Signology opens the garage up as a real business. You need to find the broken sign parts, place them outside the shop, name the garage and paint the sign.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/cheap-car-repair/cheap-car-repair-applied-signology-shop-sign.webp"

  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    Applied Signology is not just decoration. The sign pushes the garage into regular customer work.
  </figcaption>

The sign pieces are outside near the gate and shop entrance area. Pick them up, carry them to the marked positions and place them when the prompt appears. If a part will not place, you are probably holding the wrong object or aiming at the wrong marker.

After the pieces are placed, the game asks you to name the shop. Use the naming prompt, then paint the sign when the objective changes. This step does not require the same precision as a customer car; follow the objective rather than trying to make the sign perfect.

<h2 id="shocked-driver">
  Shocked Driver
</h2>

Shocked Driver is a larger early job and should be treated as a mission-level repair, not a quick parts swap. It asks for cleaning, dents, paint, missing parts, tires, rims and pulley work.

Before you start Shocked Driver, make sure you understand where to find parts. At this point in the story, missing parts may come from your garage, yard, stored junk, the scrapyard or the part store, so do not assume every missing pulley, rim or tire has to be bought immediately.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### Is this a complete Cheap Car Repair missions list?

Not yet. This is a work-in-progress walkthrough for confirmed early story missions and major early jobs. More missions should be added as they are verified.

### What is the first mission in Cheap Car Repair?

The first major job is Sebastian's Solonez. You remove rust, paint the vehicle green, polish the fresh paint and call the customer.

### How do I restore power?

Go to the neighbor's electrical box, open it, read the four-wire diagram and click the wires in the shown order. If it does not work, step back and retry the sequence.

### What do I do if the electrical box wire sequence does not work? 

Step back from the box, open the panel again and read the four-wire diagram before clicking anything. Click the wires in the exact order shown by the diagram in your current build. If you clicked too early or missed a wire, reset your position and retry the sequence.

### What is Apple of My Eye?

Apple of My Eye is the early mission where you fix your own car. It teaches you to care more about your own car than cheap customer cars.

### What is Applied Signology?

Applied Signology is the shop sign mission. Find the sign parts near the gate, place them in the marked spots, name your shop and paint the sign.

### How do I build the shop sign?

Pick up the broken sign pieces near the shop entrance or gate, place them in the marked positions, use the shop name prompt and spray-paint the sign when the objective changes.

### How do I unlock more customers?

Build and paint the shop sign during Applied Signology, then continue the early story until regular driveway clients start appearing. Once the garage loop opens, clients can come to your driveway for repeat cash jobs.

### What is the first hard mission?

Daddy's Little Girl is the first big stuck point because it combines fluids, battery work, rear-left tire replacement and rear engine air intake work.

### Why is Daddy's Little Girl stuck?

The usual problem is the air intake pipe. Reinstall the pipe first, then use pliers to secure the clips before calling Angelica.

### What does RL mean?

RL means rear left. For Daddy's Little Girl, replace the rear-left tire.

### Who is Christopher Kingman?

Christopher Kingman is a dangerous early story customer. His job introduces washing, putty, oil, fuel filter and alternator work, and sloppy work can cause trouble.

### What happens after Kingman gets angry?

Kingman can trigger a story consequence if his car fails after leaving your garage. Follow the new objective list; it is a forced story sequence, not a permanent softlock.

### When do regular clients start coming?

After the early story opens the garage loop, clients begin coming to your driveway. You can serve them for cash or let them wait while you do other tasks.

### Is the scrapyard important?

Yes. The scrapyard and nearby part sources become important once jobs ask for more replacement parts.

### What is Shocked Driver?

Shocked Driver is a larger early repair job with cleaning, dents, paint, tires, rims, pulleys and missing parts. Use the dedicated walkthrough if you need the full step order.

### What should I do if a mission will not finish?

Open the quest log and look for unchecked lines. If that does not solve it, make sure the quest is tracked, the customer has been called and no story prompt is still waiting.
