<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**The safest Fracture Field 100% route is to progress normally, but clean up capacity and setup-sensitive achievements before late-game quarry expansion and overpowered drones make them harder.**

The roadmap is:

**Early mining → first World Fractures → capacity check → Drone Hub achievements → bomb setup cleanup → prestige milestones → late-game layers → idle playtime**

The biggest warning is **Golden Touch / Gold capacity**. If you buy **Quarry Expansion Mk. II** or similar maximum-rock upgrades too early, capacity achievements can become much more expensive because the max capacity requirement increases.

This guide includes a working index of publicly listed achievement names. Public trackers currently show **98 Steam achievements**, but some entries have incomplete public descriptions, so use your in-game achievement panel as the final checklist.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="achievement-roadmap">
  Fracture Field 100% achievement roadmap
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Fracture Field 100% achievement roadmap."
  columns={roadmapColumns}
  rows={roadmapRows}
/>

Most achievements fall into two groups:

1. achievements that unlock naturally as you progress
2. achievements that become easier or harder depending on when you attempt them

Early mining, damage, resource, and first reset achievements mostly happen naturally. The achievements that need planning are capacity achievements, bomb setup achievements, high drone requirements, repeated prestige goals, and long playtime achievements.

For the main game route, start with the <Link href="/fracture-field">Fracture Field Complete Walkthrough & Guide</Link>. For reset timing, use the <Link href="/fracture-field/prestige-guide">Fracture Field Prestige Guide</Link>.

<GuideCallout title="100% route rule">
If an achievement needs a small quarry, controlled rocks, or careful drone behavior, do it before the game becomes too automated.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="idle-vs-active-achievements">
  Idle vs active achievements
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Which Fracture Field achievements can be idled and which need active setup."
  columns={idleActiveColumns}
  rows={idleActiveRows}
/>

This is the most important planning split.

Mining totals, long playtime, and many drone-hit achievements can be handled by stable automation. Active precision achievements such as **Never Miss** need attention, but not a special field setup. Capacity, bomb, and field-control achievements need more planning because late-game automation can make the field harder to control.

If you are planning 100%, do not leave every achievement until the end. Save idle achievements for the background, but handle setup-sensitive achievements while the quarry is still manageable.

<h2 id="gold-capacity-achievement">
  Golden Touch / Gold capacity achievement warning
</h2>

**Golden Touch** is the achievement to watch most carefully because it asks you to max the Gold capacity upgrade.

If your quarry is still small, maxing Gold capacity may require fewer upgrades. After buying major quarry expansion upgrades, the maximum capacity ceiling increases, so the same achievement can take much more investment.

A safer plan is:

1. reach Gold
2. stabilize Gold income
3. check whether **Golden Touch** is still unfinished
4. finish Gold capacity while the quarry is still manageable
5. then buy major quarry expansion upgrades such as Quarry Expansion Mk. II

<GuideCallout title="Golden Touch warning">
Before buying Quarry Expansion Mk. II or similar maximum-rock upgrades, check whether you still need Golden Touch or other layer capacity achievements.
</GuideCallout>

Drone achievements are much easier after Drone Hub has scaled.

</h2>

<GuideTable

Bomb achievements are mostly about field control.

"Reduce drone pressure" means you should stop drones from clearing the exact field you are trying to build. Bombs also help with some progression walls, but achievement setups are different from progression usage. For progression, use bombs to start damage on a tough wall. For achievements, use bombs when the field shape is right.

<h2 id="time-based-achievements">
  Time-based achievements
</h2>

Time-based achievements are not skill checks.

If the game asks for long playtime milestones such as 10 hours, 100 hours, or 1000 hours, treat them as idle goals. You will not solve these through better routing; you solve them by leaving the game running safely over time.

A few tips:

- use stable automation before long idle sessions
- avoid leaving the game in a state where the field clogs immediately
- check performance if late-game visual clutter lowers FPS
- return occasionally to spend upgrades or reset
- do not build your active 100% route around time achievements

Some idle-style achievements may only award after you interact again. If an idle achievement does not pop during the idle period, click or interact once after returning.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are in Fracture Field?

Fracture Field currently has **98 Steam achievements** listed by public achievement trackers and Steam profile data. SteamDB confirms Steam Achievements support for the app, but the SteamDB achievement list itself may require JavaScript to load, so use your in-game achievement panel as the final checklist.

### Are there missable achievements in Fracture Field?

Most achievements are better described as **make-harder** rather than truly missable. Capacity achievements can become much harder after quarry expansion upgrades increase the maximum capacity requirement.

### Should I finish Golden Touch before Quarry Expansion Mk. II?

Yes. If you care about 100% completion, finish **Golden Touch** before buying major quarry expansion upgrades such as Quarry Expansion Mk. II.

### How do I complete bomb achievements?

Move drone targets away from the setup layer, lower high-damage drone priority if needed, let rocks cluster, and place bombs on dense groups.

### When should I do drone achievements?

Do basic drone achievements after Drone Hub unlock, but save high-count and expensive drone-type achievements for later when you have more slots, drone cores, and upgrade levels.

### Do Reality Shatter resets ruin drone achievements?

Reality Shatter keeps Drone Hub unlocked in v1.1.0 and preserves drone rules, but drone upgrade levels still reset. Do high drone achievements during a loop where your drone upgrades are already strong.

### When should I start Reality Shatter achievements?

Start serious Reality Shatter achievement progress when your shard reward buys meaningful upgrades. Weak shatters can slow your overall 100% route.

### How do I get long playtime achievements?

Leave the game running with stable automation. Treat 10-hour, 100-hour, and 1000-hour achievements as idle-time goals, not routing challenges.

### What is the hardest achievement type?

The hardest types are usually long playtime, Golden Touch after expansion, multi-rock bomb setups, high drone-count goals, repeated Reality Shatters, and late-layer progression achievements.
