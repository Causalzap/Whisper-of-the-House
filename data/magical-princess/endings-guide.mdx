<h2 id="how-many-endings">
  How Many Endings Are in Magical Princess?
</h2>

Magical Princess has **50+ endings** when you count career endings, partner endings, secret endings, and special late-route endings.

The main ending families are:

- **Career endings:** Brave Hero, Archmage, Princess, Magnate, Scholar, Thief, Evil Cultist, and other graduation outcomes.
- **Partner endings:** Crois, Fran, Chocolat, Shail, Hasis, Noah, and other companion romance outcomes.
- **Secret endings:** Two Queens, Heir of the Stargazer, Golden Ending, and related Labyrinth routes.

This page lists the major known endings and points you to the right focused guide instead of trying to cram every route detail into one article.

<h2 id="forbidden-research-and-golden-ending">
  Forbidden Research and Golden Ending
</h2>

Forbidden Research is the biggest late-route bottleneck.

If the Royal Library still shows **Decipher Literature**, you are not on the hidden research route yet. You need the Cornet / Blackburn necklace chain before the Library changes in NG+.

Golden Ending is even more specific. It is not a normal graduation option and can skip the usual career, marriage, partner, and no-partner ending flow.

<h2 id="which-ending-did-you-get">
  Which Ending Did You Get?
</h2>

A lot of players search by outcome instead of ending name.

If Alice becomes a shut-in, starts a strange career, becomes a dark-route professional, or lands in a high-status political outcome, you are probably dealing with a career ending rather than a secret ending.

Common examples:

- **Dark / crime endings:** Assassin, Robber, Dealer, Thief, Mafia Boss.
- **Dark belief endings:** Heretic, Evil Cultist, Sect Leader.
- **High-status endings:** Princess, Chancellor, Oligarch, Magnate.
- **Combat endings:** Brave Hero, General, Queen's Knight, Hero, Sword Master, Dark Knight, Mercenary Leader.
- **Magic / intelligence endings:** Archmage, Sage / Hermit, Clergy, Scholar, Mad Scientist.
- **Work / odd career endings:** Merchant, Traveling Merchant, Maid, Apologist, Underground Doctor, Outlaw Baker, Paparazzo.
- **Lifestyle endings:** Recluse.

If the ending involves Cornelia, Red Moon Eve, the Inheritance Fragment, Forbidden Research, or Infinite Mirror Labyrinth, you are probably dealing with the secret route chain.

<h2 id="why-you-got-the-wrong-ending">
  Why You Got the Wrong Ending
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Common ending problems and where to go next."
  columns={diagnosisColumns}
  rows={diagnosisRows}
/>

When debugging an ending, do not start by changing everything. Check one layer at a time:

1. Did you reach the correct loop?
2. Did the right route character take priority?
3. Did the key item carry into the next loop?
4. Did the Library or Labyrinth gate open?
5. Did another partner or career ending override your target?

<h2 id="career-endings">
  Career Endings
</h2>

Career endings are the normal graduation outcomes. These are usually shaped by Alice's stats, classes, jobs, morality, money, reputation, activities, and final ending priority.

Examples include Brave Hero, Archmage, Princess, Magnate, Scholar, Thief, Evil Cultist, Mad Scientist, Chancellor, Oligarch, Assassin, Apologist, Traveling Merchant, Recluse, and other job or lifestyle outcomes.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many endings are in Magical Princess?

Magical Princess has 50+ endings across career, partner, secret, and special late-route endings.

### What is the best ending order in Magical Princess?

A clean order is: finish any first ending, do Two Queens in Loop 2 / first NG+, do Heir of the Stargazer in Loop 3 / second NG+ if the Inheritance Fragment is confirmed, then do Golden Ending and career / partner cleanup on separate saves.

### Is Two Queens required for the true ending?

Yes. Two Queens gives the Inheritance Fragment, which is needed for the Heir of the Stargazer route.

### Is Golden Ending the same as Heir of the Stargazer?

No. Heir of the Stargazer is the true ending route. Golden Ending is a separate special route tied to Cornet's necklace, Forbidden Research, and the Labyrinth.

### What are the partner endings?

Known partner routes include Crois, Fran, Chocolat, Shail, Hasis, and Noah. Cornelia's Two Queens route is a secret Queen-route ending, not a normal partner ending.

### Why does the Library only show Decipher Literature?

You have not unlocked Forbidden Research yet. The hidden Library route requires Cornet's necklace carried into NG+.

### Should I do romance endings before Two Queens?

Not on the same serious route attempt. Partner endings can take priority and block Cornelia's final-day invitation.

### Do career endings need Forbidden Research?

No. Forbidden Research is for the hidden Library / Labyrinth route, not normal career endings.

### Why did I get the wrong career ending?

Your highest stats, morality, reputation, activity focus, or final route priority likely pointed to a different result. Use the career endings guide for stat-specific cleanup.

### Should I make backup saves?

Yes. Make backup saves before final-day route choices, before committing to Labyrinth routes, and before Golden Ending.
