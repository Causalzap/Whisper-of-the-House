<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**Career endings in Magical Princess are decided by your stat route, Renown / Reputation, Morality, and special conditions.**

Do not plan only around “high STA” or “high INT.” Many career endings require exact stat thresholds and an extra gate. For example, **Brave Hero** needs STA 5000 plus high supporting stats, reputation, morality, and a Demon / Illusion Dragon clear. **General** needs STA 4000 and participation in every Red Moon battle. **Magnate** is not an “income stat” route; it requires **50,000 Gold**.

If you got the wrong career ending, check in this order: **main stat → Renown / Reputation → Morality → special condition → partner or secret-route override**.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="how-career-endings-work">
  How Career Endings Work
</h2>

Career endings are normal graduation outcomes. They are separate from partner endings, Two Queens, Heir of the Stargazer, and Golden Ending.

<GuideTable
  caption="Systems that decide career endings in Magical Princess."
  columns={statSystemColumns}
  rows={statSystemRows}
/>

The most important rule is that career endings are not decided by one number alone. A strong route usually needs:

1. The correct main stat.
2. Enough Renown / Reputation if the ending is honorable or high-status.
3. The correct Morality / alignment.
4. The required special condition.
5. No partner or secret route overriding your graduation result.

<h2 id="combat-endings">
  Combat and Military Endings
</h2>

Combat endings usually start from high STA, but each ending has a different extra gate.

Key examples:

- **Brave Hero:** the broadest combat check, with very high stats and Demon / Illusion Dragon clear.
- **General:** requires every Red Moon battle, so missing one can break the route.
- **Queen's Knight:** requires the School Sword Tournament.
- **Sword Master:** requires Knight's Gym Advanced Training MAX.
- **Dark Knight:** needs low morality and 100 enemy defeats.
- **Mercenary Leader:** uses STA + CHA and the Belzer street fight route.

If you only raise STA and ignore the special condition, the game may send you to a lower or different combat ending.

---

<h2 id="magic-and-faith-endings">
  Magic and Faith Endings
</h2>

Magic endings split into honorable, academic, faith-based, and dark routes.

Key examples:

- **Archmage:** INT 5000, Magic 1500, reputation, morality, and School Magic Tournament victory.
- **Sage / Hermit:** INT 4000 plus SEN 2000.
- **Clergy:** Faith, Morality, Church prayers, and high good alignment.
- **Evil Cultist:** lower morality plus Magic Bread.
- **Sect Leader:** Faith and Spindle Bread route.

Do not treat all INT endings as the same. The difference between Archmage, Clergy, Sage / Hermit, and Evil Cultist is usually reputation, morality, faith, and special actions.

---

<h2 id="social-and-arts-endings">
  Social, Arts, and Royal Endings
</h2>

Social endings use CHA, SEN, reputation, clothing, events, and performance routes.

Key examples:

- **Princess:** CHA 5000, reputation, and the Childhood Promise Event in second playthrough.
- **Idol:** Charm / Social, Music, and Bar Theme Costume Command MAX.
- **Fashion Designer:** SEN 2000, reputation, and clothing purchases.
- **Legendary Teacher:** STA + INT, reputation, and 50 completed courses.
- **Maid:** balanced stats, good alignment, Childhood Promise, and Maid Outfit.

If you are trying for Princess or Maid, do not ignore event requirements. These endings are not just raw CHA checks.

---

<h2 id="business-and-underworld-endings">
  Business and Underworld Endings
</h2>

Business and underworld endings are where players often make the worst assumptions.

<GuideCallout title="Gold is not the same as an Income stat">
For Magnate, the confirmed requirement is **50,000 Gold**. Do not describe this as “high income” unless your current build shows a separate income stat.
</GuideCallout>

Key examples:

- **Magnate:** accumulate 50,000 Gold.
- **Merchant:** STA + CHA and Sophie's Red Moon item route.
- **Mafia Boss:** INT + CHA, low morality, street fights, and gambling.
- **Gambler:** INT + CHA + SEN and 3 consecutive School Fest wins.
- **Thief:** low morality and theft.

If your route includes gambling, street fights, theft, or low morality, you may be drifting away from honorable careers and into underworld endings.

</h2>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What decides career endings in Magical Princess?

Career endings are decided by main stats, Renown / Reputation, Morality, and special conditions. For some endings, the special condition matters as much as the stat threshold.

### What are the four main career stats?

The four main stats are **STA**, **INT**, **CHA**, and **SEN**. STA supports combat routes, INT supports magic and scholar routes, CHA supports social and royal routes, and SEN supports arts, luck-adjacent, and festival-related routes.

### Why did I get General wrong even with high STA?

General requires participation in every Red Moon battle. High STA alone is not enough if you missed the Red Moon battle condition.

### How do I get Brave Hero?

Brave Hero requires STA 5000, INT 2000, CHA 2000, SEN 2000, Reputation 400, Good / Evil 60↑, and Demon / Illusion Dragon clear.

### How do I get Archmage?

Archmage requires INT 5000, Magic 1500, Reputation 350, Good / Evil 30↑, and School Magic Tournament victory.

### How do I get Magnate?

Magnate requires accumulating **50,000 Gold**. Do not confuse this with a separate income stat.

### How do I avoid dark endings?

Avoid pushing morality too low, and avoid dark-route actions such as theft, gambling-heavy routes, street-fight routes, forbidden food routes, or evil-state routing unless you are aiming for a dark ending.

### Can partner endings block career endings?

Yes. If a partner route takes final priority, it can override normal career cleanup. Use separate saves for romance endings.

### Do career endings need Forbidden Research?

No. Forbidden Research is part of the hidden Library / Labyrinth route, not normal career endings.

### What should I check before graduation?

Check your target career in the in-game Gallery / Professions screen, confirm the main stat, Renown / Reputation, Morality, and special condition, then save before the final result.
