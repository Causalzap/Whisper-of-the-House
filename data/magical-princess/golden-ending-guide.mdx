<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**To get the Golden Ending in Magical Princess, follow the Red Moon / Crimson Moon secret route, defeat Blackburn, complete the Cornet route sequence, receive Cornet's necklace, then carry that necklace into NG+.**

In the next loop, go to the **Royal Library**. Cornet's necklace changes the Library route so **Decipher Literature** is replaced by **Forbidden Research**. Raise Forbidden Research to **Level 5**, follow **Anna's Infinite Mirror Labyrinth** cue, then push the Labyrinth special adventure route.

Golden Ending is **not** a normal graduation ending. Once you commit to it, you do not get the usual career, marriage, partner, or no-partner ending flow from that loop.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

---

<GuideCallout title="Before you enter the Labyrinth">
Make a separate save, confirm that Forbidden Research is Level 5, and make sure you are ready to give up the normal graduation reward flow. Golden Ending is a route commitment, not a normal ending choice.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="golden-ending-route-overview">
  Golden Ending Route Overview
</h2>

The Golden Ending route is: **Red Moon / Crimson Moon route → Blackburn → Cornet → Cornet's necklace → NG+ → Forbidden Research → Anna's Labyrinth cue → Infinite Mirror Labyrinth special route → Golden Ending.**

<GuideTable
  caption="Golden Ending route steps in Magical Princess."
  columns={routeColumns}
  rows={routeRows}
/>

The key difference from the true ending route is the item chain. **Two Queens gives the Inheritance Fragment through Cornelia. Golden Ending uses Cornet's necklace to change the Library route in NG+.**

---

<h2 id="earliest-golden-ending-loop">
  Earliest Loop for Golden Ending
</h2>

Golden Ending cannot be completed on a fresh first playthrough, and it cannot be completed in the same loop where you first receive Cornet's necklace.

<GuideTable
  caption="Earliest possible Golden Ending timing."
  columns={earliestLoopColumns}
  rows={earliestLoopRows}
/>

The clean rule is: **Golden Ending starts for real in the NG+ loop after Cornet's necklace is obtained.**

On a fast route, that can mean **Loop 3 / NG+2**, assuming the required true-ending setup happened in Loop 2. If you get the necklace later, your Golden Ending attempt also shifts later.

---

<h2 id="cornet-vs-cornelia">
  Cornet vs Cornelia: Which One Matters for Golden Ending?
</h2>

This is the biggest naming trap in the endgame routes.

<GuideTable
  caption="Cornet, Cornelia, and Corone route meanings."
  columns={cornetColumns}
  rows={cornetRows}
/>

For Golden Ending, focus on **Cornet**, not Cornelia.

Cornelia is important for **Two Queens** and **Heir of the Stargazer**. Cornet is important for **Blackburn**, **the necklace**, **Forbidden Research**, and **Golden Ending**.

If you already completed Two Queens but the Library still shows Decipher Literature, you are probably missing the Cornet side of the route.

---

<h2 id="blackburn-and-cornet-necklace">
  Step 1: Defeat Blackburn and Get Cornet's Necklace
</h2>

Blackburn is the boss gate for the Golden Ending route.

After you progress the Red Moon / Crimson Moon secret story far enough, you need to defeat Blackburn and continue into the Cornet route sequence. This is where you get **Cornet's necklace**.

That necklace is not just story flavor. It is the key item that changes the next loop.

If you have not fought Blackburn yet, you are too early. If you have fought Blackburn but did not receive Cornet's necklace, the Golden Ending route is not ready.

---

<h2 id="ng-plus-necklace-trigger">
  Step 2: Carry Cornet's Necklace into NG+
</h2>

After getting Cornet's necklace, start the next NG+ loop.

Do not treat this as a normal cleanup run. Your first job is to confirm that the Library changed. If the necklace carried correctly, the Royal Library should no longer behave like the normal Decipher Literature route.

The practical check is simple:

- If the Library still shows **Decipher Literature**, you are missing the Golden Ending route trigger.
- If the Library shows **Forbidden Research**, you are on the correct hidden research path.

<h2 id="forbidden-research-step">
  Step 3: Unlock and Max Forbidden Research
</h2>

With Cornet's necklace carried into NG+, return to the **Royal Library**.

The normal **Decipher Literature** option should be replaced by **Forbidden Research**. Push Forbidden Research to **Level 5**. At Level 5, Anna tells you about the **Infinite Mirror Labyrinth**. That scene is your next route cue.

<h2 id="labyrinth-route-branch">
  Does Golden Ending Use the Same Labyrinth as Heir of the Stargazer?
</h2>

Both late routes point through the same broad gateway: **Forbidden Research → Anna's cue → Infinite Mirror Labyrinth**.

The difference is the route state you bring into that Labyrinth step.

<GuideTable
  caption="How Infinite Mirror Labyrinth connects to Heir of the Stargazer and Golden Ending."
  columns={labyrinthColumns}
  rows={labyrinthRows}
/>

The safe assumption is this: **the Labyrinth is the shared endgame gateway, but the ending payoff depends on your active route flags.**

If your file has both true-ending and Golden Ending conditions available, make a separate save before entering or committing to the Labyrinth special route. Do not overwrite your only save until you know which payoff your current route state is going to trigger.

---

<h2 id="can-heir-and-golden-coexist">
  Can Heir of the Stargazer and Golden Ending Coexist on One Save?
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="How to think about Heir of the Stargazer and Golden Ending on the same file."
  columns={coexistColumns}
  rows={coexistRows}
/>

The safest route order is:

1. Finish **Heir of the Stargazer** first.
2. Save before experimenting with Golden Ending.
3. Use Golden Ending as a separate completion / secret-route branch.

This avoids losing a clean true-ending clear to a special route payoff you did not mean to trigger.

<h2 id="infinite-mirror-labyrinth">
  Step 4: Enter the Infinite Mirror Labyrinth Route
</h2>

Once Anna points you toward the Infinite Mirror Labyrinth, stop thinking like you are preparing for a normal ending.

The Labyrinth route is the actual Golden Ending path. Prepare Alice like you would for an endgame route.

<GuideTable
  caption="What to prepare before entering the Labyrinth route."
  columns={labyrinthPrepColumns}
  rows={labyrinthPrepRows}
/>

Player reports suggest the fight may include help from allies or alternate-time lovers, and that past relationship completion may make the hidden boss route easier. Treat that as useful extra support, not a reason to enter underbuilt.

If you clear the Labyrinth special route and commit to Golden Ending, the loop no longer resolves like a normal graduation route.

<h2 id="golden-ending-vs-heir-of-the-stargazer">
  Golden Ending vs Heir of the Stargazer
</h2>

Do not confuse **Golden Ending** with **Heir of the Stargazer**.

**Heir of the Stargazer** is the true ending route tied to Cornelia, Two Queens, the Inheritance Fragment, Forbidden Research, and the Infinite Mirror Labyrinth.

**Golden Ending** is the Cornet / necklace route that uses Forbidden Research and the Labyrinth special route, but it does not resolve through normal graduation.

If your goal is story completion, do **Heir of the Stargazer** first. If your goal is completion cleanup or secret-route experimentation, do Golden Ending later with a separate save.

<h2 id="why-golden-ending-is-not-unlocking">
  Why Golden Ending Is Not Unlocking
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Common problems that block Golden Ending."
  columns={troubleshootingColumns}
  rows={troubleshootingRows}
/>

The fastest way to troubleshoot is to ask these questions in order:

1. Have you reached and defeated Blackburn?
2. Did you complete the Cornet sequence and get the necklace?
3. Did you start NG+ with Cornet's necklace?
4. Did the Library change from Decipher Literature to Forbidden Research?
5. Did you raise Forbidden Research to Level 5?
6. Did Anna point you toward the Labyrinth?
7. Are you following the Labyrinth special route instead of normal graduation?

If the answer to any of those is no, that is where your route is broken.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### Is Golden Ending a normal graduation ending?

No. Golden Ending does not appear as a normal career, partner, or marriage ending on the standard graduation screen.

### What starts the Golden Ending route?

The route starts from the Red Moon / Crimson Moon secret story chain, then moves through Blackburn, Cornet, Cornet's necklace, NG+, Forbidden Research, and the Labyrinth route.

### What is the earliest loop for Golden Ending?

The earliest Golden Ending attempt is usually **Loop 3 / NG+2** on a very fast route, assuming you complete the required true-ending / key-item setup in Loop 2. If you get Cornet's necklace later, Golden Ending shifts later too.

### Who gives the necklace for Golden Ending?

Cornet gives the necklace. Do not confuse this with Cornelia, who is tied to Two Queens and the Inheritance Fragment.

### Is Cornelia required for Golden Ending?

Cornelia is mainly tied to Two Queens and Heir of the Stargazer. Golden Ending is tied to Cornet's necklace, Forbidden Research, and the Labyrinth special route.

### Does Golden Ending use the same Labyrinth as Heir of the Stargazer?

Both routes use the Forbidden Research → Anna → Infinite Mirror Labyrinth gateway, but the payoff depends on your active route state. Make a save before entering if your file has multiple secret-route flags.

### Does the Labyrinth fight have allies?

Player reports suggest allies or alternate-time lovers can appear during the hidden boss route. Treat allies as helpful support, but still prepare Alice properly before entering.

### Does Golden Ending block career endings?

Yes. Once you commit to Golden Ending, you do not get the normal career ending flow from that loop.

### Does Golden Ending block marriage or partner endings?

Yes. Treat Golden Ending as a separate special route. Do partner and marriage endings on another save or cleanup loop.

### Should I do Golden Ending before Heir of the Stargazer?

No. I recommend doing Heir of the Stargazer first, then Golden Ending later with a separate save.

### Should I make a backup save before Golden Ending?

Yes. Make a separate save before the Labyrinth special route so you can return to normal ending cleanup if needed.
