<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**To get the True Ending in Magical Princess, unlock Heir of the Stargazer.**

The route has two halves. First, get **Two Queens** in NG+2 or later by making **Queen Cornelia** your main route target, avoiding committed partner endings, accepting Cornelia's final-day invitation, and winning the **Red Moon Eve** battle. That gives you the **Inheritance Fragment**.

Then start NG+3 or later with the Fragment, complete **Forbidden Research** at the Royal Library, follow Anna's cue toward the **Infinite Mirror Labyrinth**, and clear the Labyrinth to trigger **Heir of the Stargazer**.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

---

<GuideCallout title="The route has two halves">
Do not treat Heir of the Stargazer as one long stat check.

The first half is **Cornelia → Two Queens → Inheritance Fragment**. The second half is **Forbidden Research → Anna's Labyrinth cue → Infinite Mirror Labyrinth**. If either half is missing, the true ending route stalls.
</GuideCallout>

  True Ending Route Overview
</h2>

The clean way to think about the true ending is this:

**Two Queens gives you the Inheritance Fragment. Forbidden Research points you toward the Labyrinth. The Labyrinth triggers Heir of the Stargazer.**

If you have the Fragment but no Labyrinth cue, your Library side is broken. If you have Forbidden Research but no Fragment, your Cornelia side is broken.

<h2 id="red-moon-eve-battle">
  Red Moon Eve Battle: When It Happens and How to Prepare
</h2>

The **Red Moon Eve** battle happens after Cornelia's final-day route invitation. It is not the same thing as every recurring Blood Moon / Crimson Moon city event during normal play.

Think of it as the combat gate at the end of the Two Queens route:

- If Cornelia's final invitation appears, you are on the route.
- If you accept it, you move into the Red Moon Eve sequence.
- If you win the battle, you complete Two Queens and receive the Inheritance Fragment.
- If you lose, the route fails even if your Cornelia setup was correct.

For preparation, do not run a pure social build. Cornelia needs route priority, but Alice still needs enough combat power to win the final battle.

<GuideTable
  caption="Combat preparation for Red Moon Eve and Infinite Mirror Labyrinth."
  columns={combatColumns}
  rows={combatRows}
/>

I would treat Red Moon Eve as the readiness check. If that fight feels shaky, do not rush straight into the later Labyrinth route without improving Alice first.

<h2 id="inheritance-fragment">
  Step 2: Carry the Inheritance Fragment into NG+3
</h2>

After you complete Two Queens, you should receive the **Inheritance Fragment**.

That Fragment is the reason the next loop matters. Start NG+3 or later and confirm that the Fragment carried over before you spend the whole run preparing for Heir of the Stargazer.

If you do not have the Fragment, stop here and go back to Two Queens. Forbidden Research and Labyrinth prep will not fix a missing Fragment.

<h2 id="forbidden-research-step">
  Step 3: Complete Forbidden Research
</h2>

Once you are in the correct later loop, your next major gate is **Forbidden Research** at the Royal Library.

This is where many players get stuck. If the Library still shows **Decipher Literature**, you are not on the correct hidden research step yet. Do not keep grinding Decipher Literature and hope it turns into the true ending route.

For Heir of the Stargazer, you need to push the hidden Library research chain until the game points you toward the Infinite Mirror Labyrinth.

<h2 id="infinite-mirror-labyrinth">
  Step 4: Clear Infinite Mirror Labyrinth
</h2>

The **Infinite Mirror Labyrinth** is the final route gate for Heir of the Stargazer.

Do not enter it underprepared. Treat it like the endgame check for the whole secret route. If you barely survived Red Moon Eve, delay the Labyrinth attempt and strengthen Alice first.

Before committing, make sure Alice has:

- A reliable main damage plan.
- Enough survivability to handle more than one bad turn.
- Your best weapon and defensive gear equipped.
- A recovery or support plan.
- A separate save before entering.

Clearing the Labyrinth is what turns the route from “I have the true-ending pieces” into **Heir of the Stargazer**.

<h2 id="why-heir-of-the-stargazer-is-not-unlocking">
  Why Heir of the Stargazer Is Not Unlocking
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Common problems that block Heir of the Stargazer."
  columns={mistakeColumns}
  rows={mistakeRows}
/>

Most failed true-ending attempts come from one of three mistakes:

1. Missing Two Queens and therefore missing the Inheritance Fragment.
2. Accidentally taking a companion partner ending instead of Cornelia's final route.
3. Reaching NG+3 but not completing the Forbidden Research / Labyrinth half of the route.

If you are not sure which half is broken, check the route in this order: **Two Queens → Inheritance Fragment → Forbidden Research → Labyrinth**.

---

<h2 id="heir-vs-golden-ending">
  Heir of the Stargazer vs Golden Ending
</h2>

Heir of the Stargazer and Golden Ending are both late-route endings, but they are not the same target.

**Heir of the Stargazer** is the true ending route tied to Two Queens, the Inheritance Fragment, Forbidden Research, and Infinite Mirror Labyrinth.

**Golden Ending** is a separate special ending path. It is more punishing because it does not behave like a normal graduation reward.

If your target is story completion, do **Heir of the Stargazer** first. If your target is total completion, make a separate save before experimenting with Golden Ending.

Full guide: <Link href="/magical-princess/golden-ending-guide/">Golden Ending Guide</Link>

---

<GuideCallout title="Make a safety save">
Make a separate save before Cornelia's final-day invitation, before entering Infinite Mirror Labyrinth, and before experimenting with Golden Ending. This route has too many multi-loop checks to risk overwriting your only file.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### Is Heir of the Stargazer the true ending?

Yes. Heir of the Stargazer is the true ending route for Magical Princess.

### Can I get Heir of the Stargazer on the first playthrough?

No. You need at least a later NG+ loop because the route depends on Two Queens and the Inheritance Fragment.

### What ending do I need before Heir of the Stargazer?

You need **Two Queens** first. Two Queens gives you the **Inheritance Fragment**, which carries into the later true ending route.

### How do I get the Inheritance Fragment?

Complete the Two Queens route by raising Queen Cornelia's route state, accepting her final-day invitation, and winning the Red Moon Eve battle.

### Why did Cornelia's invitation not appear?

You probably did not reach the required Cornelia route state, or you committed to another companion partner ending before the final day.

### How do I know if I accidentally locked a partner route?

If you see a confession-style scene, special date, romance activation, or final partner commitment prompt, assume the partner route may take priority. Reload if your target is Two Queens.

### Can I date companions during the Two Queens run?

You can interact with companions, but do not lock into a partner ending. Keep the route clean until Cornelia's final-day event is secured.

### When does the Red Moon Eve battle happen?

It happens after you accept Cornelia's final-day invitation on the Two Queens route. Do not confuse it with normal Blood Moon / Crimson Moon city events.

### How strong should Alice be for Red Moon Eve and the Labyrinth?

As a practical baseline, aim for at least **B / B+ Battle Rank** before Red Moon Eve, one **A-rank main combat path**, updated gear, and a support or recovery plan. For Infinite Mirror Labyrinth, push closer to **A- or A Battle Rank** if you want a safer clear. Treat these as route-planning baselines, not confirmed hard requirements.

### Is Forbidden Research required for Heir of the Stargazer?

Yes. Forbidden Research is the Royal Library gate that leads toward Infinite Mirror Labyrinth.

### What should I do after Forbidden Research Level 5?

Follow Anna's cue toward Infinite Mirror Labyrinth, then prepare for the final dungeon route.

### Is Infinite Mirror Labyrinth required?

Yes. Clearing Infinite Mirror Labyrinth is the final step before Heir of the Stargazer.

### Should I do Golden Ending before Heir of the Stargazer?

No. Do Heir of the Stargazer first unless you already understand the Golden Ending consequences and have a separate save.
