<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**To unlock Forbidden Research in Magical Princess, follow the Red Moon / Crimson Moon secret story route, defeat Blackburn, receive Cornet's necklace, then carry that necklace into NG+.**

In the next loop, return to the **Royal Library**. The normal **Decipher Literature** option is replaced by **Forbidden Research**. It is not unlocked by maxing Decipher Literature, and it does not appear as a separate third Library option.

Once Forbidden Research appears, raise it to **Level 5**. At Level 5, **Anna tells you about the Infinite Mirror Labyrinth**, which is your next cue for the true / golden ending route.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

---

<GuideCallout title="Do not start at the Library">
The Library is where Forbidden Research appears, but it is not where the route starts.

If the Library still says **Decipher Literature**, stop grinding it. You are missing the Red Moon / Crimson Moon route step that leads to Blackburn, Cornet's necklace, and the NG+ Library change.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="how-to-unlock-forbidden-research">
  How to Unlock Forbidden Research
</h2>

The full unlock chain is: **Red Moon / Crimson Moon route → Blackburn → Cornet's necklace → NG+ → Royal Library → Forbidden Research Level 5 → Anna's Labyrinth cue.**

<GuideTable
  caption="Forbidden Research unlock route in Magical Princess."
  columns={unlockColumns}
  rows={unlockRows}
/>

The important part is that this is a **route-flag unlock**, not a stat check. You can have high Intelligence, strong Magia progress, and multiple Library visits, but without Cornet's necklace carried into NG+, Forbidden Research will not appear.

---

<h2 id="where-the-route-starts">
  Where the Forbidden Research Route Starts
</h2>

Forbidden Research does not start as a normal school activity.

The route starts from the **Red Moon / Crimson Moon secret story chain**. This is the hidden story path tied to the monster crisis, Blackburn, the weakening seal, and the larger secret ending route. If you are only doing classes, jobs, normal Library work, or partner dates, you are not on the Forbidden Research route yet.

Think of it this way:

- **Normal Library path:** classes, Library work, and Decipher Literature.
- **Forbidden Research path:** Red Moon / Crimson Moon story, Blackburn, Cornet's necklace, NG+, then the Library changes to Forbidden Research.

So if you are asking “Where do I start?”, the answer is: **push the Red Moon / Crimson Moon story until you reach Blackburn.** The Library comes after that.

---

<h2 id="who-is-blackburn">
  Who Is Blackburn?
</h2>

**Blackburn is the boss gate for the Forbidden Research route.**

He is not a normal Library enemy, not a random monster hunt target, and not a standard graduation boss. You reach him by progressing the Red Moon / Crimson Moon secret story route far enough.

That is why the usual Library logic fails. You cannot unlock Forbidden Research by studying harder, raising Intelligence, or maxing Decipher Literature. You need to clear the story gate first:

- Fight Blackburn.
- Get Cornet's necklace.
- Start NG+.
- Check the Library again.

If you have never reached the Blackburn fight, you are not ready for Forbidden Research yet.

---

<h2 id="cornet-vs-cornelia">
  Cornet vs Cornelia
</h2>

Do not mix up **Cornet** and **Cornelia**.

**Cornelia** is the Queen route character. She is tied to outings, affinity, Two Queens, and the Inheritance Fragment.

**Cornet** is the late-route character tied to Blackburn, the necklace, NG+, Forbidden Research, and the Golden Ending route.

For this guide, the key item is **Cornet's necklace**, not Cornelia's necklace. If you are working on Two Queens, focus on Cornelia. If you are trying to unlock Forbidden Research, you need the Cornet / Blackburn necklace chain.

You may see **Corone** in community posts, translations, or auto-transcripts. For route planning, treat Corone as a naming variant for Cornet unless your current build clearly uses a different official name.

<h2 id="forbidden-research-vs-decipher-literature">
  Forbidden Research vs Decipher Literature
</h2>

**Forbidden Research is not a third Library option. It replaces Decipher Literature entirely.**

If you open the Royal Library and still see **Decipher Literature**, the hidden research route has not unlocked. Maxing Decipher Literature will not unlock Forbidden Research, and it will not give you the Forbidden Literary Explorer achievement.

Use this rule:

- **Library shows Decipher Literature:** you are missing Cornet's necklace / NG+ trigger.
- **Library shows Forbidden Research:** you are on the correct hidden research route.

  Why Forbidden Research Is Not Showing Up
</h2>

For most players, the problem is one of three things:

1. You have not progressed the Red Moon / Crimson Moon secret story route far enough.
2. You have not defeated Blackburn and received Cornet's necklace.
3. You are checking the Library before starting the correct NG+ loop.

Once you are in the correct NG+ state, the Library change is obvious: **Decipher Literature is gone, and Forbidden Research is there instead.**

<h2 id="forbidden-research-level-5">
  What Happens at Forbidden Research Level 5
</h2>

After Forbidden Research appears, push it to **Level 5**.

Level 5 matters for two reasons. First, it unlocks **Forbidden Literary Explorer**, one of the completion checks. Second, once you hit Level 5, **Anna speaks to you about the Infinite Mirror Labyrinth**.

That Anna scene is your next route cue. If you reach Level 5 and are not sure what to do, stop looking for another normal Library upgrade. The next step is to prepare for the Labyrinth route.

Do this as soon as Forbidden Research appears in the correct NG+ loop. You do not need to max Decipher Literature first, and you should not leave Forbidden Research until the final month if your target is Heir of the Stargazer or Golden Ending.

<h2 id="how-it-connects-to-heir-of-the-stargazer">
  How Forbidden Research Connects to Heir of the Stargazer
</h2>

**Heir of the Stargazer** is the true ending route. Forbidden Research is one of the gates between the earlier Two Queens route and the Infinite Mirror Labyrinth.

The clean route looks like this:

1. Get the **Two Queens Ending**.
2. Carry the **Inheritance Fragment** into a later NG+ loop.
3. Progress the **Blackburn / Cornet's necklace** route.
4. Unlock **Forbidden Research** at the Royal Library.
5. Raise Forbidden Research to **Level 5**.
6. Follow **Anna's Labyrinth cue**.
7. Clear the **Infinite Mirror Labyrinth**.
8. Reach **Heir of the Stargazer**.

The important distinction is:

- **Two Queens gives you the Inheritance Fragment through Cornelia.**
- **Cornet's necklace changes the Library route in NG+.**
- **Forbidden Research Level 5 points you toward the Labyrinth.**

Full guide: <Link href="/magical-princess/true-ending-heir-of-the-stargazer">True Ending / Heir of the Stargazer Guide</Link>

<h2 id="how-it-connects-to-golden-ending">
  How Forbidden Research Connects to Golden Ending
</h2>

Golden Ending is the dangerous one.

It is not a normal graduation ending, and you should not treat it like a career or partner route. Once you commit to Golden Ending, you do **not** get the usual graduation flow:

- No normal graduation ending choice.
- No career ending.
- No marriage / partner ending.
- No no-partner achievement such as Flying Solo.
- No normal “pick an ending and collect the reward” moment.

That is why I recommend making a separate save before pushing the Golden Ending route. It is a special Labyrinth-related ending, and it can feel like you gave up the rest of the loop for one final route flag.

Forbidden Research matters here because it is part of the Labyrinth unlock chain. If you are aiming for Golden Ending and still only have Decipher Literature in the Library, you are not ready.

Full guide: <Link href="/magical-princess/golden-ending-guide">Golden Ending Guide</Link>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### Where does the Forbidden Research route start?

It starts from the **Red Moon / Crimson Moon secret story route**, not from the Library. Push that story route until you reach Blackburn, then carry Cornet's necklace into NG+.

### Who is Blackburn?

Blackburn is the boss gate for the Forbidden Research route. He appears through the Red Moon / Crimson Moon secret story chain, not through normal Library work.

### What is the difference between Cornelia and Cornet?

Cornelia is the Queen route character tied to outings, affinity, Two Queens, and the Inheritance Fragment. Cornet is the late-route character tied to Blackburn, the necklace, NG+, Forbidden Research, and the Golden Ending route.

### Why do I only see Decipher Literature?

You only see Decipher Literature because Forbidden Research has not unlocked yet. You need the Blackburn → Cornet's necklace → NG+ trigger before the Library changes.

### Do I need to max Decipher Literature first?

No. Maxing Decipher Literature does not unlock Forbidden Research and does not count for Forbidden Literary Explorer.

### Is Forbidden Research a third Library option?

No. Forbidden Research replaces Decipher Literature. If Decipher Literature is still there, the hidden research route has not unlocked.

### What unlocks Forbidden Research?

The unlock chain is **Red Moon / Crimson Moon route → Blackburn → Cornet's necklace → NG+ → Royal Library**. After you bring the necklace into the next loop, Decipher Literature is replaced by Forbidden Research.

### What happens after Forbidden Research Level 5?

At Level 5, Anna tells you about the Infinite Mirror Labyrinth. That is your next cue for the Labyrinth route.

### How do I get Forbidden Literary Explorer?

Reach **Forbidden Research Level 5** at the Royal Library.

### Is Forbidden Research required for Heir of the Stargazer?

Yes. Heir of the Stargazer uses the Inheritance Fragment, Forbidden Research, and Infinite Mirror Labyrinth route chain.

### Is Forbidden Research required for Golden Ending?

Yes. Golden Ending is tied to the Labyrinth special route, and Forbidden Research is part of that unlock path.

### Does Golden Ending count as a normal graduation ending?

No. Golden Ending does not give the normal graduation ending flow. You do not get a career ending, marriage ending, or standard partner / no-partner completion result from that loop.

### Should I do Forbidden Research before partner endings?

If your goal is Heir of the Stargazer or Golden Ending, yes. Keep the loop focused on the secret route first. Do partner endings in a separate cleanup loop unless you already know they will not interfere.
