<h2 id="faq">
  DragonSword Awakening guide hub FAQ
</h2>

### Which DragonSword Awakening guide should I read first?

Read the Beginner Guide when your first team and resource plan are still undecided.
Open the Walkthrough first when a specific marker, cave, road, altar, or puzzle is
blocking story progress.

### Does the walkthrough cover all eight chapters?

Yes. It follows the complete Chapter 1–8 main route and includes the major physical,
objective, puzzle, and scripted-story blockers.

### Which guide contains the most story spoilers?

The Ursula Final Boss & Ending guide contains full Chapter 8 and ending spoilers. The
Walkthrough also names late-story objectives but lets you stop at your current
chapter.

### Are these guides based on the English PC build?

Yes. The current cluster uses the English PC launch interface and the complete
Chapter 1–8 route shown in the supporting material. Major updates may change balance
values or recommendations.

### Where should I start when both damage and story progression are failing?

Open the Walkthrough first to confirm that the route or combat state is correct. If
the objective is valid but the fight remains too difficult, move to Team Comps and
then Gear & Karma.
