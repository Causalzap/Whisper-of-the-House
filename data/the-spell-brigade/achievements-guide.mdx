<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**The fastest way to complete achievements in The Spell Brigade is to route one achievement at a time instead of trying to do everything in one run.**

The game currently has **114 Steam achievements**, and a realistic 100% cleanup can easily take **35+ hours** if you are starting from normal progression. If you are serious about full completion, join the Discord or bring three friends and run a full 4-player squad. Revive setups, objective farms, scroll routes, and wizard-specific grinds become dramatically faster when everyone is helping the same route.

Start with normal clears, then unlock the right wizard, then build around the exact trigger. Star Mage needs **Falling Stars** volume. Hatti needs **Plasma routing**. Knelly needs **Bell detonation control**. Bryony needs **pickup and potion discipline**. Hardcore achievements need movement and survival decisions more than raw damage.

The rule is simple:

**Pick the target, pick the wizard, build around the trigger, and reset when the run breaks the condition.**
</GuideQuickAnswer>

For normal progression, leveling fast is good. For specific achievements, it can be smarter to keep the build compact.

This matters when you need:

- one specific spell,
- one specific infusion,
- +1 Projectile,
- split effects,
- a self-freeze setup,
- a no-potion route,
- a long cast-count farm.

Do not take side spells just because they look fun. Every side option can make the next targeted offer harder to find. This is especially important for **Starry Rain**, **π**, **Hells Bells**, **Watch the Knives**, and other achievements that depend on one spell doing one job repeatedly.

<h2 id="pyrestorm-pit-hardcore">
  Pyrestorm Pit Hardcore route
</h2>

Pyrestorm Pit Hardcore deserves its own section because it is not just “Hardcore but harder.” The map itself attacks your route. Lava, tight movement lanes, enemy density, and late-wave pressure make greedy objectives much more dangerous.

For Pyrestorm Pit Hardcore, use this rule:

**Never stop moving unless your build is already deleting the screen.**

Best priorities:

- **Sun Mage** is one of the safest picks because Solar Pulse gives constant AoE while you focus on pathing.
- **Movement speed** matters more than one more damage roll if you are getting boxed in.
- **Dodge + sustain** keeps the run alive when lava, enemies, and projectiles overlap.
- **Armor helps**, but do not rely on Armor alone unless your damage is already stable.
- **Thunder or Plasma** are strong late-game damage routes because they help clear dense waves.
- **Crit-heal / Vital Strike-style sustain** can save runs where unavoidable chip damage piles up.
- After the first key augments, skip objectives that would force you through bad terrain.

The biggest mistake is trying to full-clear every objective like this is Verdant Meadows. In Pyrestorm Pit Hardcore, survival is the achievement route.

<h2 id="recommended-cleanup-order">
  Recommended cleanup order
</h2>

If you want a clean 100% route, use this order:

1. **Finish normal progression achievements.**  
   Learn the game before optimizing.

2. **Unlock the key wizards.**  
   Star Mage, Bryony, Campanelli, Hatti, Knelly, Aldric, Ludwig, Maggie, and Pipwick open many targeted routes.

3. **Complete spell and infusion achievements.**  
   These teach you how to force builds and understand element routing.

4. **Complete objective achievements.**  
   Do these once you understand when objectives are safe.

5. **Complete wizard-specific achievements.**  
   Group them by wizard so you do not keep switching routes.

6. **Complete co-op achievements.**  
   Revives, teammate interactions, and coordinated objectives are much easier with planned teammates.

7. **Finish Hardcore and restriction achievements.**  
   Save these until your account and decision-making are stronger.

8. **Clean up long grinds.**  
   Use one-spell and high-frequency builds for cast counts, bell detonations, scrolls, sacrifices, and long-run requirements.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are in The Spell Brigade?

The current Steam achievement route should be planned around **114 achievements**. Some older or fan-made resources may use smaller milestone counts, but 114 is the safer number for Steam achievement hunting.

### How long does 100% completion take?

A realistic 100% completion push can easily take **35+ hours**, especially if you are doing wizard-specific cleanup solo. A coordinated 4-player squad can reduce revive, objective, scroll, and farming time dramatically.

### Do I need co-op for 100% achievements?

Most achievements can be attempted solo, but co-op makes several routes much faster: Campanelli revives, Aldric fungi routing, Balthazar ghost revive setups, Hatti hitting Ludwig, and team objective cleanup.

### What achievements should I do first?

Start with normal clears, wizard unlocks, spell discovery, and basic objective achievements. Save Hardcore, co-op interactions, boss precision, and long grinds for later.

### What are the hardest achievements?

The main time sinks are **Heckin' Hardcore**, **π**, **Starry Rain**, **Hells Bells**, **Out with a Bang**, **A Worthy Sacrifice**, and **The Fungi Amongi**. See the <a href="#hardest-achievements">Hardest achievements</a> section above.

### Should I chase multiple achievements in one run?

Only if they naturally share the same route. Star Mage cast farming can pair with Falling Stars scaling, but a no-potion Bryony route should not be mixed with risky objective or Hardcore attempts.

### Why do objectives matter for achievements?

Objectives unlock augments, and many achievements need specific spell behavior: projectiles, split effects, extra directions, special interactions, or spell uptime. See the <a href="#objectives-and-augments">Objectives and augments</a> section.

### Why does RNG pool dilution matter?

Targeted runs fail when you add too many side spells and make the upgrade pool harder to control. Keep the build compact when farming Starry Rain, π, Hells Bells, Watch the Knives, or other one-condition achievements.

### How do I handle Pyrestorm Pit Hardcore?

Use a mobile AoE build, keep moving, prioritize dodge / sustain / movement, and skip unsafe objectives. See the <a href="#pyrestorm-pit-hardcore">Pyrestorm Pit Hardcore</a> section above.
