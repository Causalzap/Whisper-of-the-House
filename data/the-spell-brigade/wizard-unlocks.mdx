<h2 id="quests-and-unlocks">

</h2>

Wizard progression is not just getting characters onto the roster. Quests, feats, skins, signature spell unlocks, and ascension systems all feed into the long-term grind.

Before starting a run with a specific wizard, check whether that wizard has:

- a spell unlock feat,
- a skin or outfit challenge,
- an achievement route,
- a rank or ascension goal,
- a contract or bonus gold route,
- an objective-related quest.

<h2 id="recommended-unlock-path">
  Recommended unlock path for new players
</h2>

If you are still learning, use this path:

1. **Stabilize your runs first.**  
   Learn movement, objectives, infusions, and basic survival before chasing specialist routes.

2. **Unlock one safe all-purpose route.**  
   Sun Mage is the easiest choice here because Solar Pulse covers solo and farming well.

3. **Unlock one boss or ceiling route.**  
   Pick Star Mage for Falling Stars ceiling, or Reginald / Moon Mage for multi-hit routes.

4. **Unlock one co-op support route.**  
   Maggie, Aldric, Campanelli, or Knelly gives your account better team options.

5. **Unlock one specialist route.**  
   Pipwick, Ludwig, Balthazar, Kavin, or Wizard King is better once you understand the basics.

6. **Start planning wizard-specific progression.**  
   Once rank, outfits, and quests matter, check whether the wizard opens future spell or build routes.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What is the best wizard to unlock first?

Sun Mage is the safest early unlock for most players because Solar Pulse works well for solo, farming, and beginner-friendly AoE scaling.

### How do you unlock wizards in The Spell Brigade 1.0?

Wizard unlocks can involve quests, milestones, survival goals, trying new spells, level-up goals, roster requirements, map progression, wizard rank, outfits, and ascension systems. Use the in-game wizard screen for the exact requirement.

### Is gold used to unlock wizards?

Gold is mainly used for Enchantments, Bounty, permanent upgrades, and meta-progression. If a specific roster entry shows a gold or purchase prompt, follow that in-game requirement; otherwise, track wizard unlocks through the quest and progression systems.

### How many wizards are in The Spell Brigade?

There are 15 wizards in 1.0.

### Which wizard is best for solo or farming?

Sun Mage is the easiest recommendation for both solo and farming because Solar Pulse gives safe AoE, strong scaling, and a simple build route. Star Mage can have a higher damage ceiling, but Sun Mage is easier to reproduce.

### What is the best wizard for bosses?

Star Mage is best for high-ceiling Falling Stars damage. Reginald and Moon Mage are strong alternatives if their multi-hit spells can use Acid, Flux, or Corrosion.

### What is the best co-op wizard?

Maggie, Aldric, Campanelli, and Knelly are strong co-op or utility picks because they bring healing, support, revive value, objective awareness, or zone utility.

### What does Ascension do?

Ascension Skin routes can change a wizard's starting spell, so Ascension can affect gameplay and not just appearance.

### What is Reginald's Ascension Skin starting spell?

Reginald's known Ascension Skin example starts with Bell March.

### What is Sun Mage's Ascension Skin starting spell?

Sun Mage's known Ascension Skin example starts with Chthonic Charge.

### Is Wizard King just a Magic Missile wizard?

Wizard King's visible card shows Increased Critical Chance and Magic Missile, making him a crit-focused projectile wizard. Treat any extra mechanics as advanced only if confirmed by your current game UI.

### Is Knelly the bell wizard?

Yes. Your in-game screenshot shows **Knelly** as the bell wizard, and her signature spell is **Bell March**.

### Is Pipwick worth unlocking?

Yes, if you want a summoner build. Pipwick's Impish Havoc gives the clearest minion route, while his passive adds random elemental infusion and a critical damage tradeoff.
