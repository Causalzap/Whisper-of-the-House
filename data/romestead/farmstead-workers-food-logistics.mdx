<h2 id="unlock-farmstead">
  How to unlock the Farmstead
</h2>

Farmstead is not a starter building you should expect on minute one. It comes through altar progression, and the key early requirement is **40 wheat**.

That changes how you should treat wheat. Do not spend all of it just because your town needs food right now. If you are close to Honoring the Soil, keep a dedicated wheat stack for the offering.

Once you finish the objective, return to your building options and confirm that Farmstead is available before redesigning your farming area.

<h2 id="crops">
  What should you plant first?
</h2>

Do not think of every crop as equal. Early farming has two jobs: keep people fed and unlock the systems that make food easier later.

The safe early answer is: **protect wheat progression first**, then use other food sources to stabilize the town. Wheat leads into Farmstead. Other food keeps citizens and your character from falling behind while the farm loop is still rough.

If you are preparing for a boss or dungeon, do not put every strong food item into town storage. Some food is more useful in your inventory when you need healing, buffs, or recovery during a risky trip.

<h2 id="watermill-trade-routes">
  Watermill and Trade Routes for Farmstead
</h2>

Farmstead only handles raw crop collection. Processing requires Watermill and correctly configured trade routes.

<ul className="my-6 list-disc pl-6">
  <li><strong>Watermill</strong>: Processes wheat and other crops into flour. If your bakery or food processing buildings are empty while crops are ready, the bottleneck is often Watermill placement or stock management.</li>
  <li><strong>Trade Routes</strong>: Make sure Food Storage, Warehouse, and Farmstead are connected. Unlinked warehouses or missed trade route connections can cause items to stall in the system.</li>
  <li><strong>Workflow Tip</strong>: Always assign workers to both Farmstead and processing buildings. Monitor storage and processing queues to ensure smooth food-chain flow.</li>
  <li><strong>Early Automation</strong>: For optimal early setup, place Watermill and storage close to Farmstead cluster. Delayed processing may cause Farmstead output to backlog.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="farmstead-upgrades">
  When should you upgrade the Farmstead?
</h2>

Do not upgrade the Farmstead just because the button exists. Upgrade when the building tier is the thing holding the farm back.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How do I unlock Farmstead in Romestead?

In the current route, Farmstead comes from the Honoring the Soil objective and requires 40 wheat. Save wheat once you know you are approaching this unlock.

### How close does farmland need to be to the Farmstead?

Use the in-game highlighted area as the final answer. In the current build shown here, the Farmstead working area appears to be around 3 tiles, but Early Access tuning can change exact range.

### Why is my Farmstead not watering crops?

Farmstead and watering are separate steps. If crops are not being watered, check the water support requirement and follow The Moonstring route if you still need Well.

### Does Farmstead need a worker?

Yes. Farmstead is a workplace, so check worker assignment at the Town Core if nothing is happening.

### How do I level up a farmer?

Keep the Farmstead working. Citizens gain job XP from completing work orders, and higher-level citizens can work faster. If the worker hits the building’s level cap, upgrade the building.

### When should I upgrade the Farmstead?

Upgrade when the farmer is capped, new recipes or orders require a higher level, or the farm loop already works but production speed is now the bottleneck. Do not upgrade before fixing water, storage, and inputs.

### What should I plant first?

Wheat is the safest priority because 40 wheat unlocks Farmstead. Use other food sources to keep the town stable while saving enough wheat for progression.

### Does Warehouse automate all food movement?

No. Warehouse helps with storage, but it does not automatically solve every finished order, Farmstead output, or processing step. Check where the item actually is.

### How does the mill fit into Farmstead?

Farmstead handles farming. The mill handles processing, such as turning wheat into flour. If crops exist but bread or processed food is missing, the problem may be the processing chain, not the Farmstead itself.

### Are food buffs important?

Yes, but do not overbuild the page around unstable numbers yet. Save stronger food for boss fights, dungeon runs, and recovery once you know which meals give the best current-version bonuses.

### How do I set up trade routes for Farmstead?

Ensure your warehouses and Food Storage are linked. Check that Farmstead output reaches processing buildings, and that all transport chains are connected.

### How does Watermill interact with Farmstead output?

Watermill handles processing crops like wheat into flour. If crops are harvested but not processed, check Watermill placement, worker assignment, and connection to storage.
