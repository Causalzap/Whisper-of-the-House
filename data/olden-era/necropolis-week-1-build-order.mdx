<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**The safest Necropolis Week 1 build order in Heroes of Might and Magic: Olden Era is to stabilize Skeleton Archers first, build economy only when it supports your next timing, and delay Vampires or Liches unless your resources already support them.**

Use this opening rule:

1. **Day 1–2:** build toward Skeleton Archers and take only low-loss fights.
2. **Day 3–4:** add Marketplace, Mage Guild, or a creature/economy building based on your bottleneck.
3. **Day 4–6:** choose your first major engine: Skeleton Archer mass, Vampires, Liches, or Undead Transformer setup.
4. **Day 7:** recruit, consolidate, and prepare your Week 2 route.
5. **Never force lethal fights** just because they are nearby.

The goal is not a perfect town screen. The goal is a Week 2 army that can keep clearing without bleeding your core stacks.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="week-one-goal">
  Core Week 1 goal
</h2>

Necropolis wins the first week by building an army engine, not by rushing the most expensive unit as fast as possible.

Your engine can be:

- Skeleton Archer ranged clearing
- Necromancy value from repeated low-loss fights
- Marketplace resource fixing
- Mage Guild support
- Vampire or Lich timing
- Undead Transformer conversion
- second hero pickups and delivery

The safest default is:

**Skeleton Archers first, then build whatever makes the next two fights safer.**

<GuideCallout title="Week 1 rule">
Build for the next fight, not for a perfect town screen. If a building does not help your next fights, your next resource bottleneck, or your Week 2 army, delay it.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="skeleton-archer-upgrade">
  Skeleton Archer upgrade timing
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Skeleton to Skeleton Archer upgrade cost examples."
  columns={upgradeColumns}
  rows={upgradeRows}
/>

Skeletons upgrade into Skeleton Archers for **10 gold per Skeleton** in the current Early Access data. That makes the upgrade easy to calculate: 100 Skeletons means 1000 gold.

Upgrade early when:

- the ranged attack prevents losses
- you can take several fights safely afterward
- your Skeleton stack is already large enough to matter
- your next building does not need the same gold immediately

Delay when:

- the upgrade blocks a key building
- the stack is too small
- you need Marketplace or a resource fix first
- nearby fights do not require ranged damage yet

<h2 id="marketplace-timing">
  When to build Marketplace
</h2>

Marketplace is a fix, not a habit.

Build it when trading resources immediately unlocks something important:

- Skeleton Archer upgrade
- Mage Guild
- Vampire path
- Lich path
- creature building
- resource bottleneck
- Undead Transformer or conversion setup

Do not build it just because it is cheap.

<h2 id="hero-adjustments">
  Adjust the build order by hero
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="How Necropolis heroes change the Week 1 build order."
  columns={heroColumns}
  rows={heroRows}
/>

The build order should change with your hero.

Onkos wants the cleanest Skeleton Archer snowball. Marl can take safer fights with control and tempo. Funerella wants repeated clean fights to benefit from Necromancy scaling. King-of-Kings changes the route completely because Diplomacy and Undead Transformer value make creature huts and second heroes more important.

<GuideCallout title="Hero rule">
Do not copy the same Day 1–7 route for every Necropolis hero. Your hero decides whether Week 1 is Skeleton tempo, spell control, Necromancy scaling, or conversion economy.
</GuideCallout>

</h2>

<GuideTable

<GuideCallout title="Second hero rule">
Your main hero should win fights. Your second hero should save movement, collect value, and feed the Week 2 engine.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What is the best Necropolis Week 1 build order?

The safest plan is Skeleton Archer access first, then Marketplace, Mage Guild, creature buildings, Vampires, Liches, or Undead Transformer setup depending on your resource bottleneck and hero.

### Should I upgrade Skeletons into Skeleton Archers on Day 1?

Upgrade early if the gold cost lets you clear several fights with fewer losses. Delay only if the stack is too small or the gold blocks a key building.

### How much does the Skeleton Archer upgrade cost?

The current Early Access upgrade cost is 10 gold per Skeleton. For example, 100 Skeletons cost 1000 gold to upgrade.

### Should I rush Vampires?

Only rush Vampires if your Skeleton Archer, economy, and resource situation are already stable. Do not use Vampires as a panic rush after bad early losses.

### Should I rush Liches?

Only if the resource path supports it. If Arcane Dust or another resource blocks the plan, stabilize first.

### When should Necropolis build Marketplace?

Build Marketplace when it immediately solves a resource bottleneck. Do not build it just because it is cheap.

### Do I need a second hero as Necropolis?

Usually yes. A second hero helps with resource pickups, creature huts, army delivery, scouting, and Undead Transformer setup.

### What if I take too many losses in Week 1?

Stop forcing fights, recruit, delay greedy buildings, and rebuild your Skeleton Archer engine before pushing into harder objectives.
