<h2 id="what-are-law-and-astrology">
  What are Law Points and Astrology Points?
</h2>

Law Points and Astrology Points are not normal resources like gold, wood, ore, or rare materials. They are progression resources that shape your faction and magic plan.

**Law Points** are used for faction laws. These are faction-specific bonuses that can improve growth, economy, combat, unit scaling, or special mechanics.

**Astrology Points** are tied to the Magic Observatory and spell constellation progression. They matter most when your route uses spells, map magic, or mobility tools.

<GuideCallout title="Core difference">
Law Points usually answer “how does my faction get stronger?” Astrology Points usually answer “how does my magic and map utility get stronger?”
</GuideCallout>

</h2>

<GuideTable

Gold is the safest default because it turns into buildings, units, and upgrades immediately. But safe does not always mean best.

Law Points are better when a faction law will create a clear timing. Astrology Points are better when a magic route, map spell, or Magic Observatory unlock will change what your hero can do.

<GuideCallout title="Beginner decision order">

</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="how-to-spend-astrology-points">
  How to spend Astrology Points
</h2>

Astrology Points are not spent like gold. You do not use them to buy units, recruit creatures, or upgrade a stack directly. In the current Early Access footage, Astrology belongs to the **Magic Observatory** layer: you check the Observatory, look at the available spell constellation or magic progression node, then decide whether Astrology is worth choosing over gold or Law Points.

That means the real question is not “are Astrology Points good?” The real question is:

**does the next Observatory unlock change my route soon enough to matter?**

<GuideTable
  caption="How to turn Astrology Points into an actual early-game decision."
  columns={astrologySpendColumns}
  rows={astrologySpendRows}
/>

Astrology is strongest when the next unlock gives you something practical:

- a spell tier you can actually use
- a map tool that changes your route
- mobility that saves turns
- a combat spell that prevents losses
- a buff that makes your next fights safer
- a magic plan that matches your hero

Astrology is weakest when the payoff is vague. If you have not checked the Magic Observatory, have not built the required Mage Guild path, or cannot name the unlock you want, gold or Law Points are usually safer.

<GuideCallout title="Astrology spending rule">
Before choosing Astrology income, open the Magic Observatory and identify the next useful unlock. If you cannot name the payoff, do not treat Astrology as your default economy choice.
</GuideCallout>

Law Points and Astrology Points can come from town choices, map objectives, and your hero’s activity. That means you do not always need to force them through your first building.

If your hero is clearing well, map activity may already feed your progression. If your route needs a specific law or magic unlock quickly, daily income becomes more attractive.

Use this simple split:

- **city income** is better when you want compounding value across many turns
- **map rewards** are better when one pickup solves the next timing
- **gold** is better when you need to buy something immediately
- **Law Points** are better when a faction law is the next power spike
- **Astrology Points** are better when a spell or map-magic route is the next power spike

<GuideCallout title="Income timing rule">
Daily Law or Astrology income is strongest when you take it early and have enough army to keep clearing while it compounds.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="law-seals">
  Should you spend or save Law Seals?
</h2>

Spending your first Law Seal can feel good, but it is not always correct.

Spend early when the law:

- improves the next few fights
- unlocks unit growth before weekly refresh
- fixes a resource or economy bottleneck
- supports your hero’s main route
- helps you clear safely right now

Save when:

- the early law is only mildly useful
- the next-tier law is much stronger
- your current army cannot use the bonus yet
- gold, units, or a building would help more
- you do not know your final route

<GuideCallout title="Law Seal rule">
Spend a Law Seal when the law creates tempo. Save it when the current law is only a small bonus and the next tier is your real power spike.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What do Law Points do in Heroes Olden Era?

Law Points let you activate faction laws. These laws can improve unit growth, combat, economy, resource access, or faction-specific scaling depending on your faction and route.

### What do Astrology Points do in Heroes Olden Era?

Astrology Points are tied to Magic Observatory and spell constellation progression. They are most useful when your plan involves spells, map magic, mobility tools, or magic-heavy hero routing.

### Where do you spend Astrology Points?

Astrology Points are used through the Magic Observatory layer. Check the Observatory, look at the next spell constellation or magic progression node, and decide whether that unlock is worth choosing Astrology over gold or Law Points.

### Should I choose gold, Law Points, or Astrology Points first?

Choose gold if you need immediate units, upgrades, or buildings. Choose Law Points if you know the faction law you are chasing. Choose Astrology Points if Magic Observatory progress or map magic will matter soon.

### Are Law Points better than gold?

Not always. Law Points are better when a law directly improves your next turns. Gold is better when you still need recruitment, upgrades, or core buildings.

### Are Astrology Points better than Law Points?

Only for magic-oriented routes. Law Points usually support faction laws, while Astrology Points support magic and map progression. The better choice depends on your hero, faction, and next timing.

### Should beginners pick gold by default?

Usually yes. Gold is the safest beginner choice because it is flexible and immediate. Switch to Law Points or Astrology Points when you understand which law, spell, or map tool you are trying to unlock.

### When should I save Law Seals?

Save Law Seals when the current law is only a small bonus and a higher-tier law is much stronger for your route. Spend early when the law immediately improves fights, growth, or economy.

### Does every faction use Law Points the same way?

No. Temple, Necropolis, Schism, Grove, Dungeon, and Hive all care about different laws and timings. Use Law Points based on the faction route you are actually playing.

### When should I prioritize Astrology Points?

Prioritize Astrology Points when Magic Observatory progress, spell constellations, Town Portal-style mobility, or map magic will change your route soon. Delay them if your army still needs basic power.
