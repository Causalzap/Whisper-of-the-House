  Esports Manager 2026 achievements guide
</h2>

The achievement list in **Esports Manager 2026** is small, but it is not a single route.

The common achievements are first-week checks. **First Contract** is at **81.6%**, **First Victory** is **77.7%**, **First Deal** is **72.2%**, and **Staff Debut** is **57.4%**. If those are missing, I do not need an endgame plan yet. I need to finish the first organization loop.

The rare achievements are different. **Dream Team** is only **2.2%**, **Financial Cushion** is **0.7%**, **Academy Star** is **0.4%**, and **Scout of the Year**, **Normal Major**, and **Hard Major** are all at **0.1%**. Those are not first-week goals. They need a stable organization.

I start Academy Star when:

This is a long project, not a first-week unlock.

<h2 id="faq">
  Esports Manager 2026 achievements FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are in Esports Manager 2026?

There are 21 achievements in the current list, from early unlocks like First Contract to rare long routes like Financial Cushion, Academy Star, Hard Major, and Scout of the Year.

### What achievement should I unlock first?

First Contract should usually come first because signing a player fixes the early roster gate. After that, First Victory, First Deal, and Staff Debut follow naturally.

### What are the hardest achievements?

The hardest group is Scout of the Year, Hard Major, Normal Major, Academy Star, Financial Cushion, and Dream Team because they need long-term scouting, Major success, academy development, money control, or a full 4-star roster.

### How do I get Rising Star?

Scout and sign a player with 4+ star potential. I wait until the roster is stable enough to sign for potential without ignoring role, wage, and contract fit.

### How do I get Negotiation King?

Secure a sponsorship deal with a tournament win bonus. I wait until the team can realistically win events before chasing this.

### Should I do Risky Investor early?

No. Risky Investor asks you to spend 80% of the budget on a single player, so I treat it as planned cleanup rather than a normal first-season transfer.

### How do I prepare for Financial Cushion?

Control wages, avoid panic transfers, complete realistic sponsor objectives, and stop spending every reward immediately. It requires $20,000,000 in the organization account.

### Which achievements should I save for last?

Save Financial Cushion, Academy Star, Scout of the Year, Normal Major, and Hard Major for late cleanup because they need a stable organization and long-term planning.
