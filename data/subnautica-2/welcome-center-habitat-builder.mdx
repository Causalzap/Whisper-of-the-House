<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**To get the Habitat Builder in Subnautica 2, craft a Scanner, swim southeast / south-southeast from the Lifepod to the Welcome Center about 80-90m away, scan one Habitat Builder in the open crate by the entrance sign, then clear the floor hole inside the Welcome Center and scan the second Habitat Builder in the lower crate.**

After that, return to the **Lifepod Fabricator** and craft the Habitat Builder with **2 Titanium, 1 Glass, 1 Basic Battery, and 1 Copper Wire**. Bring another Battery back to the Welcome Center to power the site, unlock the early Bio Lab / Biomod step, then unlock the **Room blueprint** separately so your first base is more than a hallway.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="before-you-leave">Before you leave the Lifepod</h2>

Before swimming to the Welcome Center, make sure you have a working **Scanner** and enough oxygen confidence to make one short POI trip. The Welcome Center is close, but the second fragment asks you to drop below the floor, so do not enter with an almost-empty tank.

For a smooth run, bring:

1. **Scanner** for both Habitat Builder fragments.
2. **One spare Basic Battery** for the Welcome Center power outlet.
3. **Food and water** if you are also detouring for Digestion, Quartz, or Copper.
4. **Air Bladder or Fins** if this is your first time leaving the Lifepod area.

<h2 id="habitat-builder-fragment-locations">Habitat Builder fragment locations</h2>

The current best route is the **Welcome Center**, about **80-90m southeast / south-southeast of the Lifepod**. You need two scans.

The first Habitat Builder fragment is outside the Welcome Center, in an **open crate to the right of the entrance near the Welcome Center sign**. Scan it before going inside so you immediately know whether your Scanner has enough battery.

The second fragment is inside the Welcome Center. Look for the **metal blocking the hole in the floor**, clear it with your Survival Multitool, then swim down through the opening and check the crate at the bottom. Scan the second Habitat Builder there to unlock the tool blueprint.

<GuideCallout title="If your route looks different">
There are several broken Habitat Builders in the early map. A scan near the Digestion / Gene Donor route can still count toward the same 2/2 progress, but the Welcome Center pair is the simplest route because both scans are tied to one landmark.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="room-blueprint">Why the Room blueprint still matters</h2>

Crafting the Habitat Builder does not always mean you can build the base you actually want. In the current Early Access route, many players unlock the tool first and then wonder why their build menu still feels limited.

If you only see corridors, get the **Room blueprint** next. The reliable route for this guide is the **Old Habitat** north / north-northwest of the Lifepod, around **350-420m** depending on your marker and approach. Inside the ruin, scan the **Processor**. That Processor scan is the practical unlock target for this route.

There is also a **Room recipe data card** in a deeper colony ruin route, and it can unlock the same basic Room blueprint, but do not treat that as the required step here. For a first base route, go to Old Habitat, scan the Processor, then come back and build.

<h2 id="build-first-base">Build your first functional base</h2>

Do not make the first base pretty. Make it useful.

A good first base has:

- a **Room or starter corridor** you can safely enter,
- a **Hatch**,
- enough **power** for oxygen,
- a **Fabricator** or storage plan,
- clear space around it for expansion.

A good rule of thumb is to build near an early resource / storage route because it shortens your next three chores. Place your first base where it helps you craft, store, breathe, and return safely, not where it looks cinematic.

<h2 id="where-to-build">Where should your first base go?</h2>

The safest first base is close enough to the Lifepod that you can retreat, but far enough from the Lifepod that it actually improves your route.

Use these rules:

- **Near resources:** Titanium, Quartz, Copper, Water Slugs, and Fibrous Pulp should be a short swim away.
- **Near progression:** Welcome Center, Old Habitat, or the early black box route are better anchors than random pretty terrain.
- **Flat but not buried:** leave room for hatches, solar panels, and later expansion.
- **Think ahead to power:** natural currents matter later once Hydroelectric Turbines unlock.

<h2 id="common-mistakes">Common mistakes</h2>

### Scanning only one Habitat Builder fragment

The tool needs **two** fragment scans. If you only have 1/2, return to the Welcome Center and check both the entrance crate and the lower crate below the floor hole.

### Forgetting the spare Battery

A Scanner battery is not the same thing as a spare Battery for the Welcome Center. If you want the Welcome Center power step and Biomod access in the same loop, craft an extra Basic Battery before going back.

### Expecting the Habitat Builder to unlock every room

The Habitat Builder is the tool. Some base pieces, especially the **Room**, are separate blueprints. If the menu only gives you corridors, scan the Processor at Old Habitat instead of assuming the base system is broken.

### Building without power

A base with no power is not a base; it is a wet box. Build power first so the structure supplies oxygen.

<h2 id="faq">FAQ</h2>

### Where is the Welcome Center in Subnautica 2?

The Welcome Center is roughly **80-90m southeast / south-southeast of the Lifepod**. It is close enough to reach early, but bring a Scanner and enough oxygen to check the lower floor section safely.

### Where are the two Habitat Builder fragments?

The fastest pair is at the Welcome Center. One is in an open crate near the entrance sign, and the other is in a lower crate reached through the floor hole inside the building.

### What is the Habitat Builder recipe?

The Habitat Builder recipe is **2 Titanium, 1 Glass, 1 Basic Battery, and 1 Copper Wire**. Craft it at the **Lifepod Fabricator** after scanning two fragments.

### Do I need a base Fabricator to craft the Habitat Builder?

No. The Habitat Builder itself is crafted at the Lifepod Fabricator. You build a base Fabricator later after you have a habitat.

### Why can I only build corridors?

You probably have the Habitat Builder but not the Room blueprint yet. For this route, unlock it by scanning the Processor at the Old Habitat north / north-northwest of the Lifepod. A Room recipe data card is another valid unlock source elsewhere, but the Old Habitat Processor is the cleaner first-base route.

### Should I build my first base at the Welcome Center?

You can, but I usually treat the Welcome Center as a progression checkpoint rather than the only base location. Build nearby if the terrain has space, resources, and safe power options; otherwise build closer to your main early resource route.
