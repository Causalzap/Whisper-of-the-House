<h2 id="faq">
  ZeroSpace Guide FAQ
</h2>

### Which ZeroSpace guide should I read first?

Start with the Beginner Guide when Supply, Flux, production, hero control, or
expansion is still unclear. Move directly to the Campaign, Arcadia, or Galactic
War page when the problem belongs to one specific mission or mode.

### Is the Campaign Walkthrough the same as the Mission 3 guide?

No. The Campaign Walkthrough owns the Act 1 mission order, major choices, and
confirmed consequences. The Mission 3 page owns the complete Arcadia defense,
including Old Bessie, the southern source, final swarm, and evacuation panel.

### Can Galactic War be played without another player?

Yes. Select Play with AI from the mission panel. It is useful for learning a
mission's objective order before choosing Play Co-op or increasing the
difficulty.

### Does choosing a Galactic War alliance lock my faction?

No. Alliance membership controls which strategic side receives the Capture
Point contribution. The commander and combat faction selected inside missions
remain separate choices.

### Are all Campaign choice outcomes confirmed?

No. The guides identify visible consequences from the completed route and do
not claim hidden relationship scores, alternate rewards, or ending changes
that have not been verified.

### Is this a complete ZeroSpace Wiki?

No. This is a focused problem-solving guide cluster for beginner systems, the
Act 1 Campaign, Mission 3 Arcadia, and Galactic War. New pages are added only
when they solve a distinct player problem with enough verified information to
avoid duplicating the existing guides.
