<h2 id="how-many-altars-are-there-in-the-occultist">
  How many Altars are there in The Occultist?
</h2>

There are **7 Altars** in **The Occultist**.

This is a much smaller collectible line than the Medallions, which is exactly why it matters. Missing one altar is not a small mistake. It means you have lost **one-seventh** of the entire route.

The cleanest way to think about the altar route is simple:

**track it like a dedicated collectible line, not like optional room loot.**

<GuideCallout title="Are Altars missable?">
No. All 7 Altars can be cleaned up later through **Chapter Select**, so there is no permanent miss risk if your count is wrong during a first run.
</GuideCallout>

---

<h2 id="how-altars-work-in-the-occultist">
  How Altars work in The Occultist
</h2>

Altars are not passive pickups.

They are ritual objects that have to be **activated**.

### How to activate an Altar

- walk up to the altar
- interact with it
- **light the candles**

That is the full activation rule.

### Why players still miss them

Players usually miss Altars for one of three reasons:

- they are focused on the next story trigger
- they assume ritual objects on side routes are just scenery
- they stop tracking their altar count after the midgame

<GuideCallout title="The biggest Altar mistake">
The biggest altar mistake is thinking, “If it mattered, the game would force me into it.”

That is not how this route works.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="altar-1-parkers-basement">
  Altar 1/7 — Parker’s basement
</h2>

This is the altar that starts the entire collectible line.

### Where it is
Inside **Parker’s basement** in the Farm chapter.

### How to get it
1. Find the **ring** in the house
2. Use the name **TELSA** to open the basement door
3. Go downstairs
4. Turn **right immediately**
5. Light the altar candles

<h2 id="altar-2-godstone-road-cottage">
  Altar 2/7 — cottage near the Godstone road stables
</h2>

This is the first altar that players often miss because it sits in a side house rather than a dramatic story room.

### Where it is
In the **left bedroom** of the small cottage near the abandoned stables on the Godstone road route.

### How to get it
1. Leave the barn area
2. Follow the road toward the abandoned stables
3. Enter the nearby house by the courtyard
4. Go into the **left bedroom**
5. Light the altar candles beside the bed

<h2 id="altar-4-circus-shooting-range-path">
  Altar 4/7 — Circus shooting range side path
</h2>

This is the altar that proves the Circus is not just about tickets and mini-games.

### Where it is
On the side path behind the **BORNE** wagon after the shooting range sequence.

### How to get it
1. Complete the shooting range mini-game
2. Move **left** instead of pushing straight on
3. Follow the small path toward the wagon marked **BORNE**
4. Continue forward, then turn **right**
5. Light the altar candles

**Gospel Fragment 4/5** is also nearby, which makes this one of the best “clear the whole corner before leaving” checkpoints in the game.

<h2 id="altar-5-lighthouse-bedroom">
  Altar 5/7 — Lighthouse bedroom
</h2>

This is one of the cleanest altar pickups in the game because it shares a room with another important collectible.

### Where it is
Inside the **right-side bedroom** of the Lighthouse.

### How to get it
1. Finish the cemetery stone lion sequence
2. Trigger the Gabriel cutscene
3. Once you are in the Lighthouse, enter the **small bedroom on the right**
4. Light the altar candles
5. Check the room fully, because **Medallion 10/15** is also here

<h2 id="altar-6-mausoleum-right-path">
  Altar 6/7 — Mausoleum right path
</h2>

This is the altar that tells you the collectible route is almost finished.

### Where it is
On the **right-hand path** in the Mausoleum after the slide into the pipe-water section.

### How to get it
1. Solve the cemetery gate and enter the Mausoleum
2. Progress through the underground route
3. Slide down into the area with the pipe water
4. Go **right first**, not left
5. Continue to the end and light the altar candles
6. Only then return and continue along the left route

<h2 id="altar-7-redler-manor-music-room">
  Altar 7/7 — Redler Manor Music Room side room
</h2>

This is the final altar and the one that closes the whole route.

### Where it is
Inside the **Music Room side room** in **Redler Manor**.

### How to get it
1. Reach the Music Room / concert hall in Redler Manor
2. Go up onto the stage
3. Take the **Treble Clef Key**
4. Use it to open the **side door** beside the stage
5. Enter the small room
6. Light the final altar candles on the table

Lighting this altar unlocks **A Lasting Prayer / Still Praying** depending on your platform naming.

This room also contains the piano sheet clue used for the nearby **FADGE** password door, so it is another strong double-value stop.

<h2 id="how-to-find-the-last-missing-altar">
  How to find the last missing Altar
</h2>

If your altar count is short late in the game, do **not** sweep the entire game blindly.

Use this order instead:

1. **Check the Orphanage**
2. **Then check the Circus side path**
3. **Then check the Lighthouse room**
4. **Then check the Mausoleum right path**
5. **Finally check Redler Manor**

Why this works:

The altar line is short enough that the missing one is usually in the chapter where you stopped tracking the count, not in some impossible secret zone.

<GuideCallout title="Best last-altar rule">
The last missing altar is usually in the chapter where your priorities changed, not the chapter with the hardest combat or puzzles.
</GuideCallout>
