<h2 id="best-guide-order">Best guide order for The Occultist</h2>

If you are trying to complete **The Occultist** efficiently, do not treat collectibles, altars, Codex pages, and puzzles as separate tasks. In the second half of the game, they overlap heavily.

The safest route is:

### 1. Clear Medallions first

Start with the <Link href="/the-occultist/all-medallion-locations">All 15 Medallion Locations</Link> guide if your goal is a full completion run. Medallions are spread across the Farm, Hospital, Orphanage, Circus, Cemetery, Redler Manor, and Temple, so tracking them from the beginning is easier than backtracking later.

### 2. Check Altars before moving on

Use the <Link href="/the-occultist/all-altar-locations">All 7 Altar Locations</Link> guide before leaving each major area. Several altar locations are placed near side rooms or optional paths, which makes them easy to miss if you only follow the main objective marker.

### 3. Finish Codex Lumina fragments in Redler Manor

The <Link href="/the-occultist/codex-lumina-fragments">Codex Lumina Fragments</Link> route becomes most useful once you reach Redler Manor. The missing pages are connected to mansion puzzles, so you should solve the relevant rooms before assuming the area is complete.

<GuideCallout title="Completion tip">
For a clean playthrough, use this order: **Medallions → Altars → Codex Lumina fragments → final puzzle cleanup**.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="redler-manor-warning">Why Redler Manor causes missed progress</h2>

Redler Manor is the point where The Occultist stops being a simple exploration route and becomes a layered puzzle area. A solved room does not always mean the room is fully cleared.

Before leaving the manor, check whether you have:

- collected the related Codex Lumina fragment
- checked for nearby Medallion progress
- completed any altar interaction in the area
- solved the puzzle that unlocks the next route

<GuideCallout title="Best manor rule">
If a puzzle opens a new path, check the old room again before moving on. Several late-game objectives are easy to miss because story progress and collectible progress overlap.
</GuideCallout>

### What is this The Occultist guide hub for?

If you are in Redler Manor, start with the room that is currently blocking you: Planetarium, Clock Room, Greenhouse, or Mausoleum. Each puzzle page gives the exact solution without forcing you through a full walkthrough.
