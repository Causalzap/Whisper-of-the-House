To solve the **Greenhouse Statue Puzzle** in **The Occultist**, place the four animal statues on the matching pedestals:

- **Raven → Death**
- **Rat → Disease**
- **Pig → Hunger**

<h2 id="where-is-the-greenhouse-statue-puzzle">
  Where is the Greenhouse Statue Puzzle in The Occultist?
</h2>

The **Greenhouse Statue Puzzle** is in **Redler Manor**, in the **lower central Greenhouse area**.

This is a manor puzzle, not an earlier hospital or orphanage puzzle. If you have not reached the Greenhouse through the manor progression yet, you are simply too early.

### What you need before this puzzle
- progress far enough in **Redler Manor**
- trigger **Emilia’s Codex task**
- reach the **Greenhouse**
- have **Caecus Corvus** ready if you also want the nearby Medallion

<GuideCallout title="Important requirement">
You can solve the statue puzzle without collecting the nearby Medallion immediately, but if you want to clear the room fully, you will need **Caecus Corvus** for **Medallion 14/15**.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="greenhouse-statue-puzzle-solution">
  Greenhouse Statue Puzzle solution
</h2>

The correct statue placements are:

- **Raven → Death**
- **Rat → Disease**
- **Pig → Hunger**
- **Wolf → War**

That is the full solution.

This puzzle is not about entering a code or rotating a symbol lock. It is a **matching puzzle**, where each animal statue has to be moved onto the correct themed pedestal.

### How to solve it step by step
1. Enter the **lower Greenhouse puzzle area**
2. Identify the four animal statues
3. Match each statue to the correct sacrifice theme pedestal
4. Move the statues carefully so they do not block one another
5. Confirm all four are in the correct positions
6. Wait for the **iron gate** to open
7. Check the room ceiling and use **Caecus Corvus** to collect **Medallion 14/15**

<h2 id="does-the-greenhouse-statue-puzzle-have-an-order">
  Does the Greenhouse Statue Puzzle have an order?
</h2>

No. There is **no fixed solution order**.

What matters is the final placement of all four statues.

That said, movement space is still important. If you push the larger or more awkward statues first, you can make the room harder to navigate.

### The safest movement approach
- move **Raven** first
- move **Rat** second
- then place **Pig**
- finish with **Wolf**

That is not a mandatory order, but it usually keeps the area cleaner and reduces blocking problems.

<GuideCallout title="Common mistake">
The puzzle does not fail because you moved the “wrong statue first.”

The real problem is usually **pathing**. Players block themselves by pushing one statue into a bad lane too early.
</GuideCallout>

  What do you get from the Greenhouse Statue Puzzle?

Solving the Greenhouse Statue Puzzle opens the **iron gate** so you can continue through the area.

It also sets up one of the room’s best collectible payoffs:

- **Medallion 14/15** is on the **ceiling**
- you need **Caecus Corvus** to bring it down

So even if you only came here for progression, this room is also a strong collectible checkpoint.

<h2 id="why-players-get-stuck-on-the-greenhouse-puzzle">
  Why players get stuck on the Greenhouse puzzle
</h2>

This puzzle is easier to understand once you know the symbolism, but it still causes problems for a few reasons:

- the game does not present the animal-theme matches as a simple code
- players mix up **Disease** and **Death**
- some players remember **symbolism**, while others remember **room placement**
- statue movement can become awkward if the path is blocked

### The short logic
- this is a **matching puzzle**
- the correct pairings are fixed
- the movement order is flexible, but the final placement is not

<h2 id="what-to-do-if-the-greenhouse-puzzle-is-not-working">
  What to do if the Greenhouse puzzle is not working
</h2>

If the Greenhouse puzzle is not resolving, check these first:

1. **Make sure all four statues are on the correct pedestals**
2. **Re-check Disease vs Death**
3. **Make sure Wolf is on War and not another combat-themed pedestal**
4. **Look for movement blockage that left one statue slightly out of position**
5. **Check whether the iron gate has already opened**
6. **Use Caecus Corvus to collect the ceiling Medallion once the room is clear**

If the room still feels wrong, the problem is usually not a hidden extra mechanic. It is almost always a **misplaced statue** or a statue that was not pushed fully into place.

<GuideCallout title="Best troubleshooting rule">
If you think the Greenhouse puzzle is bugged, do not start guessing new combinations.

Go back to the confirmed matching set:

**Raven → Death, Rat → Disease, Pig → Hunger, Wolf → War.**
</GuideCallout>
