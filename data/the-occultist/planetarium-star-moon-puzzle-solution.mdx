<h2 id="where-is-the-planetarium-star-and-moon-puzzle">
  Where is the Planetarium Star and Moon Puzzle in The Occultist?
</h2>

The **Star and Moon Puzzle** is in the **Planetarium on the second floor of Redler Manor**.

This is a late-game manor puzzle, not an early hospital, orphanage, or cemetery puzzle. If you have not reached the upper manor route yet, you are simply too early.

### What you need before this puzzle
- progress into **Redler Manor**
- open the **Lion Key** route that leads deeper into the manor
- already have **Caecus Corvus** unlocked

<GuideCallout title="Important requirement">
If you cannot interact with the celestial symbols correctly, the most likely reason is that you have not fully unlocked or used **Caecus Corvus** for this section yet.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="planetarium-star-and-moon-puzzle-solution">
  Planetarium Star and Moon Puzzle solution
</h2>

The solution is:

- activate **2 Star symbols**
- activate **1 Moon symbol**

That is the entire answer.

You do **not** need a strict left-to-right sequence or a complex timing trick. The important part is that the correct total ends up being **two Stars and one Moon**.

### How to solve it step by step
1. Enter the **Planetarium**
2. Face the celestial puzzle setup
3. Use **Caecus Corvus**
4. Hit the correct symbols until **2 Stars** and **1 Moon** are activated
5. Confirm the mechanism has accepted the input
6. Watch the **top of the sphere open**
7. Use the raven interaction to retrieve **Codex Lumina Page 4**

<h2 id="what-to-do-if-the-puzzle-is-not-working">
  What to do if the puzzle is not working
</h2>

If the Planetarium puzzle is not resolving, check these first:

1. **Make sure you are in the correct manor room**
2. **Make sure you already opened the Lion Key route**
3. **Make sure Caecus Corvus is available**
4. **Make sure you activated exactly 2 Stars and 1 Moon**
5. **Check whether the sphere top has already opened**

If all of that is correct, you are usually not missing a hidden extra step. You are most likely just activating the wrong symbol count.

<GuideCallout title="Best troubleshooting rule">
If the Planetarium puzzle seems unclear, stop looking for a complicated sequence.

Re-check the combination first:
**2 Stars, 1 Moon.**
</GuideCallout>
