<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What is the correct Area 41 symbol order?

The correct order is **Far West → Desert → Canyon → Woodlands**.

### What does the Area 41 clue mean?

The clue words are map themes. **Frontier** means Far West, **sand** means Desert, **cliffs** means Canyon, and **trees** means Woodlands.

### Where do the four Area 41 symbols come from?

The four input symbols come from **Far West, Desert, Canyon, and Woodlands**, in that order. Area 41 itself is the clue and input location, not one of the four clue maps.

### How do I enter the Area 41 symbols?

Rotate the four symbol pillars until they match Far West, Desert, Canyon, and Woodlands in order, then press the button.

### Why did the Area 41 puzzle not complete?

You may have entered the symbols in the wrong order, used the Area 41 symbol as one of the inputs, or forgot to press the button.

### What reward do you get from Area 41?

You unlock the **Anti-Gravity Falls Joker**.

### What does Anti-Gravity Falls do?

Based on current footage, Anti-Gravity Falls can spawn blue anti-gravity balls after an enemy dies. These balls lift you upward.

### Is Anti-Gravity Falls worth using?

It is fun, but not a high-priority combat Joker. It is mostly worth unlocking for completion or movement experiments.

### Should I do Area 41 before the boss?

Yes. Like other secret missions, Area 41 is easier to finish before killing the boss or starting extraction.
