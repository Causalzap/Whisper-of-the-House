<h2 id="woodlands-map-legend">
  How to read the Woodlands mushroom map
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Woodlands mushroom map markers and what they mean."
  columns={mapLegendColumns}
  rows={mapLegendRows}
/>

The map is the main solution for this secret. The important markers are:

- **Green star** = start at the Large Tree
- **Orange marker** = Sequence Start
- **Mushrooms 1-7** = required mushrooms
- **Red marker** = Return to Stone
- **Question marks** = unknown or optional locations

For the main Shroom Grave route, ignore the question marks and follow only the numbered mushroom route.

  Woodlands mushroom sequence
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Correct Woodlands mushroom sequence."
  columns={sequenceColumns}
  rows={sequenceRows}
/>

The route moves around the map edge in a wide loop. Start from the Large Tree, begin at the Sequence Start marker, then follow the mushroom numbers in order.

Do not skip from Mushroom 3 to Mushroom 6 or chase question marks during the route. The sequence is meant to be followed as a clean 1-7 path.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many mushrooms are required for the Woodlands secret?

The Woodlands secret requires **7 mushrooms**, touched in the numbered order shown on the map.

### What is the correct Woodlands mushroom order?

The correct order is **Mushroom 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5 → 6 → 7**, then return to the stone marker.

### Where do I start the Woodlands mushroom sequence?

Start at the **Large Tree**, then go to the orange **Sequence Start** marker before touching Mushroom 1.

### What do I do after Mushroom 7?

Return to the red **Return to Stone** marker. The secret does not end at Mushroom 7.

### Are the question mark markers required?

No. Treat the question marks as unknown or optional. They are not required for the main Shroom Grave route.

### Why did the Woodlands secret not complete?

You probably skipped the Sequence Start, touched a mushroom out of order, or forgot to return to the red stone marker after Mushroom 7.

### What reward do you get from the Woodlands mushroom secret?

You unlock the **Shroom Grave Joker**.

### What does Shroom Grave do?

Shroom Grave is associated with healing mushrooms, making it a sustain-focused reward.

### Is Shroom Grave worth unlocking?

Yes, especially if you want more sustain. It is less direct than Pigpick for damage, but useful for staying alive.

### Should I do the Woodlands secret before the boss?

Yes. Complete the mushroom route before killing the boss or starting extraction so you can follow the full map route without late-run pressure.
