<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**To complete the Canyon secret in Far Far West, collect all four medallions: the Sheriff Medallion near the secret door, the Prospector Medallion under the bridge, Bobby's Medallion inside the dynamite mine, and Johnny Hawkeye's Medallion from the 10-target safe puzzle.**

After you have all four medallions, return to the four-slot door and insert them into the sockets. **The door opens automatically after all four medallions are placed.** The main rewards are **Pigpick / Pick Pick Joker**, the **Elder Pickaxe**, and extra gold.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

---

<GuideCallout title="Do this before the boss">
Complete the Canyon secret before killing the boss or starting extraction. The route is long, and the Hawkeye 10-target puzzle is much harder to finish while enemies are piling up.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="how-to-read-the-canyon-map">
  How to read the Canyon secret map
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Canyon secret map markers and what each marker means."
  columns={canyonMarkerColumns}
  rows={canyonMarkerRows}
/>

The Canyon secret is easier if you treat the four-slot door as your anchor. Start at marker **1**, collect the other medallions, then return to marker **1** to open the cave.

Do not rely only on direction words like "west" or "under the bridge." Canyon has vertical paths, cliffs, ziplines, and similar-looking rock structures, so the numbered map is the safest way to follow the route.

The Prospector Medallion is under the bridge. You need to pick up a shovel in the same area, then dig the buried mounds.

**Steps:**

1. Go to marker **3**, the bridge area.
2. Drop down under the bridge.
3. Pick up one of the shovels near the prospector area.
4. Look for buried mounds with small rocks on top.
5. Dig the southern mound first.
6. If you get bones, keep digging the other mounds until the medallion appears.

The shovel is in the same under-bridge search area as the buried mounds, so do not leave the bridge area before picking it up. Some mounds give bones instead of the medallion. That is normal.

<GuideCallout title="Prospector tip">
Check the southern dig area first, but do not assume the first mound will always be correct. If you dig up bones, keep checking the nearby mounds.
</GuideCallout>

  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">

</figure>

Bobby's Medallion is inside the mine area blocked by glowing red / gray rocks. The visual clue is the glowing rock wall near the mine shaft.

**Steps:**

1. Go to marker **4**, the mine shaft under one of the broken bridges.
2. Look for the glowing red / gray rocks blocking the mine path.
3. Pick up dynamite from the left side of the area.
4. Light the dynamite before throwing it.
5. Throw it directly onto the blocked rocks.
6. Repeat the dynamite throws until the path opens.
7. Move deeper into the mine and pick up Bobby's Medallion.

You usually need to blow up more than one rock section, so do not leave after the first explosion unless the path is fully open.

<GuideCallout title="Dynamite mistake">
If nothing happens, you probably threw unlit dynamite or placed it too far from the rock wall. Light it first, then throw it directly at the glowing rocks.
</GuideCallout>

The Johnny Hawkeye Medallion is the hardest part of the Canyon secret. You need to shoot **10 bullseye targets** around the safe area.

The safe has a counter that starts at **10**. Each target you shoot lowers the counter. When the counter reaches **0**, the safe opens and you can loot Johnny Hawkeye's Medallion.

Match each number below to the numbered panel in the collage above. The table is meant to be used with the image, not as a replacement for it.

<GuideTable
  caption="Johnny Hawkeye 10 target checklist."
  columns={hawkeyeColumns}
  rows={hawkeyeRows}

If the safe counter is stuck at **1**, recheck **target 10** first. In your collage, target 10 is the tall hanging pipe / wooden support shot on the right side. After that, recheck targets **3**, **5**, and **9**, because they are also easy to miss against the dark blue background.

<GuideCallout title="Hawkeye target rule">
Do not leave the Hawkeye area until the safe opens. If the safe is still locked, one numbered bullseye has not been shot yet.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="pigpick-joker-reward">
  Pigpick / Pick Pick Joker reward explained
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Canyon secret rewards and why they matter."
  columns={rewardColumns}
  rows={rewardRows}
/>

The main reward is the **Pigpick / Pick Pick Joker**. It can spawn damaging pickaxes on hit, which makes it a damage-over-time style reward.

This is especially good if you use weapons that hit often. Faster weapons get more chances to trigger the effect, so Pigpick is more interesting for automatic or rapid-fire setups than for slow single-shot weapons.

The Elder Pickaxe is inside the opened Canyon secret cave, not outside near the medallion door.

After placing all four medallions:

1. Walk through the opened door.
2. Enter the reward cave.
3. Look around the reward area before leaving.
4. Pick up the Elder Pickaxe.
5. Grab the nearby gold before continuing the run.

Do not leave the secret cave immediately after the door opens. If you only see the door open and then run back to the main objective, you can miss the Elder Pickaxe reward.

<h2 id="canyon-secret-troubleshooting">
  Canyon secret troubleshooting
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Common Canyon secret problems and fixes."
  columns={troubleshootingColumns}
  rows={troubleshootingRows}
/>

Most Canyon secret problems come from one missing medallion or one unshot Hawkeye target.

If the door will not open, do not assume the game bugged. Recheck the Hawkeye safe first, then the prospector dig area, then the dynamite mine.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many medallions do I need for the Canyon secret?

You need four medallions: Sheriff, Prospector, Bobby / Dynamiter, and Johnny Hawkeye.

### Where is the first Canyon medallion?

The first medallion is on the sheriff body near the four-slot Canyon secret door.

### Where are the Prospector Medallion and shovel?

The Prospector Medallion and shovel are both in the under-bridge prospector area. Drop under the bridge, pick up a shovel near the buried mounds, then dig the mounds with small rocks on top until the medallion appears.

### How do I get Bobby's Medallion?

Use lit dynamite to blow through the glowing red / gray rocks in the mine area. Bobby's Medallion is inside after you open the path.

### How do I open the Hawkeye safe?

Shoot all 10 bullseye targets around the Hawkeye safe area. The safe opens when the counter reaches zero.

### What should I check if the Hawkeye safe is stuck at 1?

Check target 10 first. In the numbered collage, target 10 is near the tall hanging pipe / wooden support. Then recheck targets 3, 5, and 9.

### How do I open the Canyon secret door?

Return to the four-slot door after collecting all four medallions, then insert them into the sockets. The door opens automatically after all four medallions are placed.

### What reward do you get from the Canyon secret?

You get the Pigpick / Pick Pick Joker, access to the Elder Pickaxe, and extra gold inside the secret cave.

### Where is the Elder Pickaxe?

The Elder Pickaxe is inside the opened Canyon secret cave. After the door opens, enter the reward area and search the cave before leaving.

### Is Pigpick / Pick Pick worth using?

Yes. It is especially useful with fast-hit weapons because more hits mean more chances to trigger damaging pickaxes.

### Can I do the Canyon secret on any difficulty?

Yes. The Canyon secret can be completed on any difficulty.

### Should I do the Canyon secret before the boss?

Yes. Finish the Canyon secret before killing the boss or starting extraction. The route is long, and the Hawkeye target section is harder under pressure.
