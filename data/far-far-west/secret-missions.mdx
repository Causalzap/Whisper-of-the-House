<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**Far Far West has six secret missions: Ghost Bells, Canyon Medallions, Cactus Day, Mushroom Sequence, Snowman Parts, and Area 41 Symbols.**

Start with **Ghost Bells** if you want the fastest useful unlock, because Bellshot gives strong early healing value. Do **Canyon Medallions** next if you want damage, gold, and the Elder Pickaxe.

If you only have time for one secret, do **Ghost Bells**. If you have time for one high-value but longer secret, do **Canyon Medallions**. Finish secrets **before killing the boss**, because the run becomes much harder to explore once the boss and extraction phase begin.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="all-secret-missions-checklist">
  All secret missions checklist
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Every secret mission in Far Far West, with estimated time and priority."
  columns={secretMissionColumns}
  rows={secretMissionRows}
/>

Estimated times assume you already know the route and complete the secret **before** pushing the main objective to the boss or extraction phase. If you are exploring blind, **Canyon Medallions** and **Cactus Day** can take much longer because both require several map stops.

The most efficient unlock path is:

**Ghost Bells → Canyon Medallions → Cactus Day → Mushroom Sequence → Area 41 Symbols → Snowman Parts**

Do not save secrets for after the boss. Treat them like pre-boss objectives: clear the secret route first, then finish the main mission and extract.

<h2 id="when-to-do-secret-missions">
  When to do secret missions
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Best timing for completing secret missions in Far Far West."
  columns={timingColumns}
  rows={timingRows}
/>

The safest rule is simple: **secret first, boss second, extraction last**.

You can sometimes finish a final turn-in after the boss, but do not plan your route around that. The longer the extraction phase goes on, the easier it is to run out of ammo, get surrounded, or miss your exit while trying to solve a puzzle.

<h2 id="secret-joker-rewards">
  Secret Joker rewards compared
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Secret rewards in Far Far West and whether they are worth using."
  columns={rewardColumns}
  rows={rewardRows}
/>

The secret rewards fall into three groups.

**Bellshot and Pigpick / Pick Pick** are the main combat rewards. Bellshot gives survival value, while Pigpick gives damage value.

**Cactus Day and Shroom Grave** are useful but more situational. Cactus Day helps with group clearing. Shroom Grave gives healing, but the visual effect can be annoying.

**Anti-Gravity Falls and the Far Far North skin** are mostly for fun or completion.

<GuideCallout title="Bellshot tooltip note">
Some sources describe Bellshot as triggering from headshots, while others describe the requirement as weak spot hits. Treat it as a precision-hit reward and confirm the exact wording on your current in-game tooltip.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="far-west-ghost-bells-bellshot">
  Far West Ghost Bells: Bellshot
</h2>

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/far-far-west/bellshot-great-bell.webp"
    alt="Far Far West Bellshot secret with the church, three bell shard locations, and great bell return point marked."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    Follow the numbered markers: start at the church, clear the three ghost bell mini-games, then return to the great bell.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

**Estimated time:** 5-8 minutes  
**Reward:** Bellshot weapon Joker  
**Best for:** Solo survival, weak spot builds, co-op support  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Trigger note:** Bellshot is tied to precision hits, so it is stronger if you can aim consistently.

Use the annotated screenshot first. Marker **1** should be the church, markers **2-4** should be the three ghost bell mini-games, and marker **5** should be the great bell return point.

**Route:**

1. Start a mission on the **Far West** map.
2. Go to marker **1**, the church.
3. Visit markers **2-4** and complete each bell order mini-game.
4. Interact with the floor bell at each location.
5. Watch the order that the bells appear.
6. Shoot the bells in that same order.
7. Collect all three bell shards.
8. Return to marker **5**, the great bell.
9. Finish the run to unlock Bellshot for weapon customization.

**Common mistakes:** Leaving after the third small bell puzzle, or trying to finish the bell route after the boss phase has already started. Complete the three bell mini-games first, return to the great bell, then move on to the boss and extraction.

<h2 id="canyon-medallions-pigpick">
  Canyon Medallions: Pigpick / Pick Pick and Elder Pickaxe
</h2>

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/far-far-west/canyon-secret-medallion-door.webp"
    alt="Far Far West Canyon secret medallion route with the door, sheriff, prospector dig area, dynamite mine, and Hawkeye safe marked."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    Follow the marked Canyon route: door, sheriff, prospector dig spot, dynamite mine, Hawkeye safe, then return to the door.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

**Estimated time:** 10-15 minutes  
**Reward:** Pigpick / Pick Pick Joker, Elder Pickaxe, gold  
**Best for:** Damage builds, fast weapons, boss DPS, gold farming  
**Difficulty:** Hardest secret in this Hub

Use the annotated screenshot first. Marker **1** should be the medallion door. Marker **2** should be the sheriff body. Marker **3** should be the prospector dig area. Marker **4** should be the dynamite mine. Marker **5** should be the Hawkeye safe and target area.

**Route:**

1. Go to marker **1**, the four-slot medallion door.
2. Pick up the sheriff medallion at marker **2**.
3. Go to marker **3** and dig under the bridge for the prospector medallion.
4. Go to marker **4** and use dynamite to break the red / gray rocks for Bobby's medallion.
5. Go to marker **5** and shoot all 10 Hawkeye bullseye targets.
6. Loot the safe for the final medallion.
7. Return to marker **1** and insert all four medallions.
8. Enter the cave and collect the reward.

**Common mistake:** Missing one Hawkeye target. Check high spots, the safe area, wooden structures, rocks, zipline, and minecart area.

<h2 id="desert-cactus-day">
  Desert Cactus Day: Cactus Day Joker
</h2>

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/far-far-west/cactus-day-locations-map.webp"
    alt="Far Far West Cactus Day map with each child cactus and matching parent cactus marked by color."

  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    Use matching colors or labels to pair every child cactus with the correct parent cactus.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

**Estimated time:** 10-14 minutes  
**Reward:** Cactus Day Joker  
**Best for:** AoE clear, enemy packs, kill-chain builds  
**Difficulty:** Medium, mostly because of travel time

Cactus Day should be solved from the annotated map, not from vague directions. Each child cactus should share a color or label with its correct parent cactus.

**Route:**

1. Start a mission on the **Desert** map.
2. Find a child cactus on the marked map.
3. Pick it up.
4. Carry it to the matching parent cactus with the same color or label.
5. Repeat until all six child cactuses are returned.
6. Complete the secret to unlock Cactus Day.

**Common mistake:** Trying to solve every pair only from dialogue hints. Use the marked cactus-pair map instead.

**Estimated time:** 5-7 minutes  
**Reward:** Shroom Grave Joker  
**Best for:** Backup healing, completion  
**Difficulty:** Easy once the order is marked

The Woodlands secret is all about order. The screenshot should number every mushroom from **1** to **7**, then mark the cave stone as the return point.

**Route:**

4. Do not skip or reverse the sequence.
5. Return to the cave stone.
6. Interact with it to finish the secret.

**Common mistake:** Touching the mushrooms out of order or forgetting to return to the cave stone after the seventh mushroom.

<h2 id="far-far-north-snowman-parts">
  Far Far North Snowman Parts: skin reward
</h2>

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/far-far-west/far-far-north-snowman-parts-map.webp"
    alt="Far Far West Far Far North snowman parts map with the snowman cave, head, chest, body, and return route marked."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    Mark the snowman cave and the safest return route for the head, chest, and body parts.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

**Estimated time:** 8-12 minutes  
**Reward:** Caribou / Carriable skin  
**Best for:** Cosmetic collection, completion  
**Difficulty:** Medium because the large snowman part can get stuck

The Far Far North secret gives a skin instead of a weapon Joker. The most important part of the screenshot is the return route, especially for the large bottom snowball.

**Route:**

1. Go to the marked snowman cave.
2. Find the snowman body, chest, and head.
3. Roll each part back to the snowman cave.
4. Use the marked return route instead of forcing the part through trees.
5. Rebuild the snowman.
6. Claim the skin reward.

**Common mistake:** Pushing the large bottom snowball through trees or rough terrain. A cleaner route is better than a shorter route.

<h2 id="which-secret-joker-is-best">
  Which secret Joker is best for your goal?
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Best Far Far West secret reward by goal."
  columns={buildUseColumns}
  rows={buildUseRows}
/>

For new players, **Bellshot** is the best first unlock because healing is useful in almost every mission.

<h2 id="secret-mission-troubleshooting">
  Secret mission troubleshooting
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Common Far Far West secret mission problems and fixes."
  columns={troubleshootingColumns}
  rows={troubleshootingRows}
/>

Most secret mission problems come from missing one interaction, missing one target, or not returning to the final turn-in point.

If a secret does not complete, first check whether it has a final interaction. Ghost Bells ends at the great bell, Canyon ends at the medallion door, Woodlands ends at the cave stone, and Area 41 ends at the button after symbol entry.

<GuideCallout title="Random side objectives are different">
Far Far West also has side objectives, graves, question-mark events, disc fragments, wishing wells, cow herding, skull puzzles, and battery puzzles. Those are not the same as the six secret missions in this guide unless they unlock one of the secret rewards listed above.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What is the fastest useful secret mission to do first?

Do Ghost Bells first. It is one of the easiest secrets once the bell locations are marked, and it unlocks Bellshot, which gives useful healing value in normal missions.

### What should I do if I want the highest-value secret in one run?

Do Canyon Medallions if you have enough time. It takes longer than Ghost Bells, but it gives Pigpick / Pick Pick, the Elder Pickaxe, and extra cave rewards.

### Should I finish secret missions before killing the boss?

Yes. Finish secret missions before killing the boss or triggering the final extraction phase. After the boss, the run pushes you toward leaving, enemies keep spawning, and exploration becomes much riskier.

### Which secret mission takes the longest?

Cactus Day and Canyon Medallions usually take the longest. Cactus Day requires matching all cactus children with their parents, while Canyon requires several mini-puzzles.

### Which secret mission is hardest?

Canyon Medallions is the hardest because the Hawkeye section requires shooting 10 targets, and missing one target keeps the safe locked.

### Which secret reward should I choose for survival, damage, or AoE?

Choose Bellshot for survival, Pigpick / Pick Pick for damage, and Cactus Day for AoE clearing. Shroom Grave is backup healing, while Anti-Gravity Falls is mostly for fun.

### Which secret reward is only cosmetic?

The Far Far North snowman secret gives a skin instead of a weapon Joker.

### Should I do Area 41 early?

No. Area 41 is worth doing for completion, but Anti-Gravity Falls is not a high-priority combat Joker.

### Are the secret mission rewards weapon Jokers or character Jokers?

Most secret mission rewards are weapon Jokers, not character Jokers. Far Far North is the exception because it gives a skin.

### Why did my secret mission not complete?

Most secrets need a final interaction. Ghost Bells requires returning to the great bell, Canyon requires inserting all four medallions, Woodlands requires returning to the cave stone, and Area 41 requires pressing the button after entering the symbols. Also make sure you are not trying to finish the secret after the boss phase has already started.
