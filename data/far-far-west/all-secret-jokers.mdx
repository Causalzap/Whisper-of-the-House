<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**The best Far Far West secret Jokers to unlock first are Bellshot, Pick Pick / Pigpick, and Cactus Day.**

**Bellshot** is the best survival reward: each Joker has a **5% chance** to spawn a healing bell, and shooting the bell heals **20 HP**. **Pick Pick / Pigpick** is the best damage reward: it uses **2 Joker slots**, has a **5% chance on hit** to spawn damaging pickaxes, and can later be upgraded with **300 Souls** for a higher trigger chance. **Cactus Day** is the best AoE clear reward, with a **3% chance on kill** to spawn cactus mines.

The main tradeoff is cost: **Canyon gives the best value, but it is also the hardest secret to finish**. Do Canyon early in the run because it requires medallions, shovel digging, dynamite, and the Johnny Hawkeye 10-target puzzle.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many secret rewards are in Far Far West?

There are six secret mission rewards: five weapon Jokers and one Far Far North skin reward.

### What is the best secret Joker in Far Far West?

Bellshot is the best first secret Joker for most players because it can spawn a healing bell, and shooting that bell heals 20 HP.

### What secret Joker should I unlock first?

Unlock **Bellshot** first, then **Pick Pick / Pigpick**, then **Cactus Day** if you want the most practical early rewards.

### Which secret Joker is best for survival?

**Bellshot** is the best survival reward because it gives cleaner healing value than Shroom Grave. Bellshot heals 20 HP when you shoot the spawned bell.

### Which secret Joker is best for bosses?

**Pick Pick / Pigpick** is usually the best boss-focused secret Joker because its on-hit pickaxe effect works well with fast-hit weapons and long fights.

### Which secret Joker is best for AoE clear?

**Cactus Day** is the best AoE-focused secret Joker because cactus mines can help clear grouped enemies after kills.

### Is Shroom Grave better than Bellshot?

Usually no. Shroom Grave has a higher trigger chance at 10%, but it heals only 10 HP and adds a vision / buzz drawback. Bellshot has a lower 5% trigger chance, but it heals 20 HP and is more practical.

### Does Canyon give rewards besides Pick Pick / Pigpick?

Yes. The Canyon secret cave also contains multiple Elder Pickaxes and multiple gold veins, making it one of the best secrets to repeat when you need gold.

### Does Far Far North give a Joker?

No. Far Far North gives a skin reward instead of a weapon Joker.

### Is Anti-Gravity Falls worth unlocking?

Yes for completion and fun, but it is not a high-priority combat Joker.

### Do secret missions need to be completed before extraction?

Plan to finish each secret mission before killing the boss or starting extraction. Some secrets require multiple steps in the same map, and exploration becomes much harder once the run moves toward extraction.

### Are secret Joker effects final?

Far Far West is still in Early Access, so reward effects, trigger rates, slot costs, and upgrade costs can change. Always check the current in-game tooltip before building around exact numbers.
