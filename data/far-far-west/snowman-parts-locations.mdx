<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**To complete the Snowman Parts secret in Far Far West, go to Far Far North, find the Snowman Cave on the west side, then return the Head, Chest, and Body to that cave.**

The route usually takes about **8-12 minutes** with a marked map. The **Head** is in the upper-right / northern area, the **Chest** is on the right side below it, and the **Body** is in the lower-middle area near the Start / Anchor marker. Completing the secret unlocks the **Caribou / Carriable skin**, which is a cosmetic reward.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

---

<GuideCallout title="Completion reward, not a combat Joker">
The Snowman Parts secret rewards a skin, not a weapon Joker. Do it for completion or cosmetics. If you want combat power first, prioritize Bellshot, Pigpick / Pick Pick, Cactus Day, Shroom Grave, or Anti-Gravity Falls.
</GuideCallout>

The Head and Chest use the same basic delivery loop, so the important part is not the mechanics — it is knowing where they are on the map.

**Steps:**

1. Go to the Head or Chest marker.
2. Clear enemies around the part.
3. Face back toward the west-side Snowman Cave.
4. Move the part directly back to the cave.
5. Confirm the part is placed before going for the next one.

Do the Head first if you are learning the route, then the Chest.

The **Body** is the main challenge of this secret. It can get stuck if you push it through rocks, trees, slopes, tight corners, or uneven terrain.

**Steps:**

1. Go to the Body marker in the lower-middle area.
2. Clear enemies before touching the Body.
3. Choose the widest route back toward the west-side Snowman Cave.
4. Avoid rocks, trees, tight corners, and rough slopes.
5. If it starts drifting into an obstacle, stop and reposition before pushing again.
6. Return the Body to the cave and finish the rebuild.

<GuideCallout title="Body route tip">
Take the clean route, not the shortest route. A wide path that works is faster than a shortcut that gets the Body stuck.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="snowman-troubleshooting">
  Snowman Parts troubleshooting
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Common Snowman Parts problems and fixes."
  columns={troubleshootingColumns}
  rows={troubleshootingRows}
/>

Most problems come from one of three things:

1. confusing the orange cave icon with the red boss marker  
2. getting the Body stuck  
3. visiting question mark locations instead of the required parts

For the core route, ignore question marks and focus on:

**Cave → Head → Chest → Body → Cave**

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### What map is the Snowman Parts secret on?

The Snowman Parts secret is on the **Far Far North** map.

### Where is the Snowman Cave?

The Snowman Cave is the **orange cave icon on the west side** of the map, near but separate from the red boss marker.

### Where is the Head?

The Head is the **upper-right marker** in the northern area of the map.

### Where is the Chest?

The Chest is the **right-side marker below the Head**, east of the Start / Anchor marker.

### Where is the Body?

The Body is the **lower-middle marker southeast of the Start / Anchor marker**.

### Are the question mark markers required?

No. The question mark markers are unknown or optional points. They are not required for the core Snowman Parts route.

### How long does the Snowman Parts secret take?

With a marked map, expect about **8-12 minutes**. It can take longer if the Body gets stuck.

### Which snowman part is hardest to move?

The **Body** is the hardest because it can get stuck on rocks, trees, slopes, and rough terrain.

### What should I do if the Body gets stuck?

Stop pushing it into the obstacle. Reposition and nudge it from the open side. If it is fully wedged and will not move, a fresh run may be the only reliable fix.

### What reward do you get from Snowman Parts?

You unlock the **Caribou / Carriable skin**.

### Is Snowman Parts a weapon Joker secret?

No. Snowman Parts gives a cosmetic skin, not a weapon Joker.

### Is the Snowman Parts secret worth doing?

Yes for completion and cosmetics. No if you only care about combat upgrades.

### Should I do Snowman Parts before the boss?

Yes. Finish it before killing the boss or starting extraction because moving the parts is much harder under pressure.
