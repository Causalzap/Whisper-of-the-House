<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**To unlock Bellshot in Far Far West, collect all three Ghost Bell Shards on the Far West map, then return to the Great Bell on top of the church.**

Use the church as your anchor point. Complete the **church-side floor bell**, the **northwest zipline cliff bell**, and the **northern beach bell**, then return to the church tower and ring the Great Bell. The full route usually takes about **5-8 minutes** once your screenshots are marked.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<GuideCallout title="Do this before the boss">
Complete the Ghost Bell route before killing the boss or starting extraction. The route is short, but it still sends you from the church to the northwest cliff and then to the northern beach, so it is much easier before the map becomes chaotic.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="how-the-bell-mini-game-works">
  How the bell mini-game works
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="How to complete each Ghost Bell mini-game."
  columns={miniGameColumns}
  rows={miniGameRows}
/>

Each Ghost Bell location uses the same mini-game: interact with the floor bell, watch the bells appear, then shoot them in the order they spawned.

The safest way to play it is to wait for the full sequence, count the bells in your current attempt, then shoot. If you make a mistake, interact with the floor bell again and replay the sequence.

<GuideCallout title="Mini-game tip">
Do not memorize a universal order from screenshots. The important thing is the order shown by the floor bell in your current attempt.
</GuideCallout>

The first shard comes from the **church-side floor bell** marked as **Marker 1** on your route map.

The key is to separate two things:

1. **Floor bell** — starts the mini-game and gives Bell Shard 1.
2. **Great Bell** — the final return point after all three shards.

**Steps:**

1. Go to Marker **1** on your annotated route map.
2. Interact with the small floor bell.
3. Watch the spawned bell sequence.
4. Shoot the bells in the same order.
5. Pick up Bell Shard 1.

<h2 id="ghost-bell-troubleshooting">
  Ghost Bell troubleshooting
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Common Ghost Bell problems and fixes."
  columns={troubleshootingColumns}
  rows={troubleshootingRows}
/>

Most Ghost Bell problems happen because players either shoot the bell order wrong, confuse the first floor bell with the Great Bell, or forget to return to the Great Bell after collecting the third shard.

If you are stuck, first check whether you actually have all three Bell Shards. Then return to the church and interact with the Great Bell on top.


<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many Ghost Bell Shards do I need?

You need three Ghost Bell Shards to complete the Far West Ghost Bell secret and unlock Bellshot.

### How long does the Ghost Bell secret take?

Once your route map is marked, the Ghost Bell route usually takes about 5-8 minutes.

### Where is the first Ghost Bell Shard?

The first Ghost Bell Shard comes from the church-side floor bell. Do not confuse it with the Great Bell on the church tower.

### Where is the second Ghost Bell Shard?

The second Ghost Bell Shard is at the northwest zipline cliff. From the church anchor, follow the marked northwest route and take the zipline path up the cliff face.

### Where is the third Ghost Bell Shard?

The third Ghost Bell Shard is on the northern beach near the top edge of the Far West map. From the church, ride north toward Marker 3.

### How many bells appear in the mini-game?

The exact order is shown by the floor bell during your current attempt. Watch the full sequence, count the spawned bells, then shoot them in that same order.

### Can I retry the bell mini-game?

Yes. If you fail the bell order, interact with the floor bell again to replay the sequence.

### Why did Bellshot not unlock after I collected three shards?

You probably forgot to return to the Great Bell. After collecting all three shards, go back to the church and ring the Great Bell on top of the tower.

### What does Bellshot do?

Based on current testing, Bellshot has a chance on weak spot hits to summon a healing ghost bell. Shoot the ghost bell to recover health.

### Is Bellshot worth unlocking?

Yes. Bellshot is one of the best early secret rewards because healing is useful in solo runs, co-op support, and general progression.

### Should I do Ghost Bells before the boss?

Yes. Complete the Ghost Bell route before killing the boss or starting extraction. It is much easier to travel between the church, zipline cliff, and northern beach before the run becomes chaotic.

### Can I repeat the Ghost Bell secret?

Yes. After unlocking Bellshot, you can still complete the Ghost Bell route again during Far West runs.
