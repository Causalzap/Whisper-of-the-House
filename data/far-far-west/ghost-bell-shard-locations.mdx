<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**To unlock Bellshot in Far Far West, complete all 3 bell locations on the Far West map, solve each bell order puzzle, then return to the Great Bell on top of the church.**

The three bell locations are the **church-side floor bell**, the **northwest zipline cliff bell**, and the **northern beach bell**. Each one gives a Ghost Bell Shard after you shoot the bells in the correct order. Once you have all three shards, go back to the church tower and ring the Great Bell to unlock **Bellshot / Bell Shot Joker**.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="how-the-bell-mini-game-works">
  How the Bell Order Puzzle Works
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="How to complete each Ghost Bell mini-game."
  columns={miniGameColumns}
  rows={miniGameRows}
/>

Each Ghost Bell location uses the same mini-game: interact with the floor bell, watch the bells appear, then shoot them in the order they spawned.

The safest way to play it is to wait for the full sequence, count the bells in your current attempt, then shoot. If you make a mistake, interact with the floor bell again and replay the sequence.

<GuideCallout title="Mini-game tip">
Do not memorize a universal order from screenshots. The important thing is the order shown by the floor bell in your current attempt.
</GuideCallout>

The first shard comes from the **church-side floor bell** marked as **Marker 1** on your route map.

The key is to separate two things:

1. **Floor bell** — starts the mini-game and gives Bell Shard 1.
2. **Great Bell** — the final return point after all three shards.

**Steps:**

1. Go to Marker **1** on your annotated route map.
2. Interact with the small floor bell.
3. Watch the spawned bell sequence.
4. Shoot the bells in the same order.
5. Pick up Bell Shard 1.

<h2 id="ghost-bell-troubleshooting">
  Bell Order Troubleshooting
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Common Ghost Bell problems and fixes."
  columns={troubleshootingColumns}
  rows={troubleshootingRows}
/>

Most Ghost Bell problems happen because players either shoot the bell order wrong, confuse the first floor bell with the Great Bell, or forget to return to the Great Bell after collecting the third shard.

If you are stuck, first check whether you actually have all three Bell Shards. Then return to the church and interact with the Great Bell on top.


<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### Where are all bell locations in Far Far West?

There are three bell locations on the Far West map: the church-side floor bell, the northwest zipline cliff bell, and the northern beach bell. Complete the bell order puzzle at each one, then return to the Great Bell.

### What is the bell order in Far Far West?

The bell order is shown during each puzzle attempt. Watch the full sequence first, then shoot the bells in the same order they appeared.

### How do you unlock Bellshot?

To unlock Bellshot, complete all three bell order puzzles, collect the three Ghost Bell Shards, then return to the Great Bell on the church tower.

### Why did Bellshot not unlock after I collected three shards?

You probably skipped the final Great Bell step. After collecting all three Ghost Bell Shards, go back to the church tower and ring the Great Bell.

### Can I retry the bell order puzzle?

Yes. If you fail the bell order puzzle, interact with the floor bell again, watch the sequence, and shoot the bells in the same order.

### Where is the first bell location?

The first bell location is the church-side floor bell. This is the small floor bell near the church area, not the Great Bell on top of the tower.

### Where is the second bell location?

The second bell location is at the northwest zipline cliff. From the church anchor, follow the northwest route and take the zipline path up the cliff face.

### Where is the third bell location?

The third bell location is on the northern beach near the top edge of the Far West map. From the church, ride north toward Marker 3.

### Is Bellshot the same as Bell Shot Joker?

Players may search for it as Bellshot, Bell Shot, or Bell Shot Joker. This guide covers the secret bell route that unlocks that reward.
