<h2 id="cactus-day-map-markers">
  How to read the Cactus Day map
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Cactus Day map markers and what they mean."
  columns={mapMarkerColumns}
  rows={mapMarkerRows}
/>

Cactus Day is a child-parent matching puzzle. There are **12 cactuses** involved: six children and six parents.

The fastest way to solve it is to use both **colors and letters** on the map. Colors make the pairs easy to scan quickly, while letters help if two colors look similar or if the screenshot is compressed.

For example:

- **Child A → Parent A**
- **Child B → Parent B**
- **Child C → Parent C**
- **Child D → Parent D**
- **Child E → Parent E**
- **Child F → Parent F**

Do not pick up a child cactus until you know where its matching parent is. Since you cannot ride your horse while carrying a child cactus, every wrong delivery wastes a lot of time.

  How the Cactus Day secret works
</h2>

The secret works the same way for all six pairs:

1. Find a child cactus on an **X** marker.
2. Check the matching parent circle on the map.
3. Clear enemies along the delivery path.
4. Pick up the child cactus.
5. Walk it to the matching parent.
6. Confirm the parent accepts the child.
7. Repeat until all six children are returned.

The most common mistake is trying to solve the route by guessing from dialogue. Dialogue can help, but the marked map is faster and safer.

<h2 id="cactus-day-route-planning">
  Best way to plan the Cactus Day route
</h2>

Because you cannot ride while carrying a child cactus, Cactus Day is mostly a route-planning task.

Before each delivery, ask three questions:

1. **Where is the matching parent?**
2. **Is the path clear?**
3. **Is there another nearby pair I should do next?**

Do not carry a child cactus across the Desert just because you found it first. It is usually better to complete nearby pairs together, then move to the next cluster.

If your map has route arrows, follow them. If your map only has pair labels, start with the pairs closest to your current position and avoid long cross-map deliveries until the nearby area is finished.

<h2 id="cactus-day-troubleshooting">
  Cactus Day troubleshooting
</h2>

<GuideTable
  caption="Common Cactus Day problems and fixes."
  columns={troubleshootingColumns}
  rows={troubleshootingRows}
/>

The most common Cactus Day mistake is carrying a child cactus to the wrong parent. The parent rejection is not a bug; it means the child-parent pair is wrong.

If this happens, go back to the annotated map and match the child marker to the parent marker with the same color or label.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many cactuses are in Cactus Day?

There are 12 cactuses involved: six child cactuses and six parent cactuses.

### What do the X markers mean on the Cactus Day map?

X markers show child cactus locations. Pick up the child cactus from the X marker and carry it to the matching parent.

### What do the circle markers mean on the Cactus Day map?

Circle markers show parent cactus locations. Each parent should match one child with the same color or label.

### How do I know which child cactus belongs to which parent?

Use the matching colors or letters on the annotated map. Child A goes to Parent A, Child B goes to Parent B, and so on.

### Why does the parent cactus say this is not their child?

You brought the wrong child cactus. Check the map again and match the child to the parent with the same color or letter.

### Can I solve Cactus Day from dialogue hints?

Yes, but it is slower. The marked map is much faster and less confusing.

### How long does Cactus Day take?

With a marked map, expect about 10-14 minutes. It can take much longer if you solve it only from dialogue hints or deliver children to the wrong parents.

### Can I use my horse while carrying a child cactus?

No. While carrying a child cactus, you cannot use your horse normally, so plan the route before picking it up.

### What reward do you get from Cactus Day?

You unlock the Cactus Day Joker.

### What does the Cactus Day Joker do?

Based on current testing, Cactus Day has a chance on kill to spawn cactus mines near where the enemy died. It is useful for AoE clear.

### Is Cactus Day worth doing?

Yes, especially if you want more area control. It is not as universally useful as Bellshot, but it is a good secret Joker for clearing enemy packs.

### Should I do Cactus Day before the boss?

Yes. Finish Cactus Day before killing the boss or starting extraction, because the route requires carrying multiple child cactuses across the Desert map.
