**Carmine Falcone is the Iceberg Lounge mission in LEGO Batman Legacy. To finish it, follow the club route into the VIP Lounge, question Flass, head to the basement, complete the three-item food order, clear Falcone’s House of Fun, and open the Red Brick safe with code 596.**

For completion, this mission has **5 Disco Balls, 5 WayneTech Caches, and 1 Red Brick**. Finding all 5 Disco Balls rewards the **Disco Balls Batcave Trophy**, and the Red Brick unlocks the **Filthy Rich** modifier.

<h2 id="carmine-falcone-mission-overview">
  Carmine Falcone Mission Overview
</h2>

The Carmine Falcone mission sends Batman and Gordon into the Iceberg Lounge to find Falcone and gather hard evidence.

This is not just a straight combat level. The mission moves through several different spaces:

- the fish-market entrance,
- the shield guard and first lighting rig,
- the security camera rooms,
- the VIP Lounge,
- Flass and the basement clue,
- the kitchen food order,
- Falcone’s House of Fun,
- the final chase,
- and the Red Brick safe.

If you only want to clear the story, follow the route table. If you want completion, collect the Disco Balls, WayneTech Caches, and Red Brick as you move through the same route.

<h2 id="shield-guard-and-disco-ball-1">
  Shield Guard and Disco Ball 1
</h2>

The first real blocker is the shield guard.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/lego-batman/lego-batman-iceberg-lounge-shield-guard.webp"
    alt="Shield guard blocking Batman in the Iceberg Lounge."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    Use the room route instead of attacking the shield from the front, then check the lighting rig for Disco Ball 1.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

When your attacks bounce off the shield, stop forcing basic combos. The solution is to move through the room route and use the lighting rig instead of fighting the shield directly.

After you grapple onto the lighting rig, look to the **right side** before leaving. **Disco Ball 1** hangs there. Hit it with a Batarang.

<h2 id="first-security-camera-and-waynetech-cache-1">
  First Security Camera and WayneTech Cache 1
</h2>

The first security camera room is also where you can grab the first WayneTech Cache.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/lego-batman/lego-batman-iceberg-lounge-security-camera.webp"
    alt="Security camera room in the Iceberg Lounge mission."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    After entering the first camera room, turn around and check behind you for WayneTech Cache 1.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

Disable the security camera, then look behind you before you run forward. **WayneTech Cache 1** is easy to miss because the room naturally pulls you deeper into the club.

After that, keep following the route into the vent and second lighting rig section.

<h2 id="second-lighting-rig-and-disco-ball-2">
  Second Lighting Rig and Disco Ball 2
</h2>

Disco Ball 2 is on the second lighting rig route.

After the vent section, break the machinery and use Gordon’s Foam Gun to seal the hazard. Then climb over the rig and **look up before dropping down**. The Disco Ball is above the route, so it is easy to miss if you only follow the floor path.

Hit **Disco Ball 2** with a Batarang, then continue toward the VIP Lounge.

<h2 id="vip-lounge-and-disco-ball-3">
  VIP Lounge and Disco Ball 3
</h2>

The VIP Lounge is the main route toward Falcone’s office.

Keep moving through the VIP route until you meet Flass again. After Selena opens the next door, check the **right corner of the room** before continuing. That is where **Disco Ball 3** is hidden.

<h2 id="where-is-falcones-office">
  Where Is Falcone’s Office?
</h2>

Falcone’s office is in the basement.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/lego-batman/lego-batman-falcone-office-basement.webp"
    alt="Flass telling Batman that Falcone's office is in the basement."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    Flass gives the key route clue: Falcone’s office is in the basement.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

After Flass gives up the clue, do not backtrack through the club. Go straight, then head down a couple of floors.

You will eventually reach the office door, but the mission continues through the kitchen before you actually get to Falcone.

<h2 id="how-to-complete-the-food-order">
  How To Complete the Food Order
</h2>

The food order is the kitchen puzzle that opens the elevator route.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/lego-batman/lego-batman-iceberg-lounge-food-order-elevator.webp"
    alt="Food order objective used to open the elevator route to Falcone's office."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    You need three food items on the tray before the elevator route opens.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

Place **three food items** on the tray. After the first item, the game confirms that two more are still needed. For the second item, use **Gordon** while Batman holds the setup in place, then carry the food back to the tray. Find the final nearby food item and place it on the tray to finish the order.

Once all three food items are on the tray, the elevator route opens.

<h2 id="waynetech-cache-2-after-the-elevator">
  WayneTech Cache 2 After the Elevator
</h2>

Do not rush forward after the food order elevator.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/lego-batman/lego-batman-waynetech-cache-2-gate.webp"
    alt="Gate route to WayneTech Cache 2 near the Ska-Kham Asylum door after the Iceberg Lounge food order."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    WayneTech Cache 2 is behind the side gate near the Ska-Kham Asylum door after the food order elevator.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

After the elevator ride, look left near the **Ska-Kham Asylum** door and the nearby vent. This is the route to **WayneTech Cache 2**, and it has several small steps:

1. Use the vent to get into the side route.
2. Use Gordon’s Foam Gun to seal the hazard.
3. Use the cable socket to power the gate setup.
4. Use Batman’s Batarang to finish opening the gate.
5. Grab the WayneTech Cache before continuing.

This is one of the easiest caches to miss because the story route wants you to keep moving forward.

<h2 id="falcones-house-of-fun">
  Falcone’s House of Fun
</h2>

After the kitchen and elevator route, the mission moves into Falcone’s House of Fun.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/lego-batman/lego-batman-falcone-house-of-fun.webp"
    alt="Falcone's House of Fun area in the Iceberg Lounge mission."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    House of Fun has the densest collectible cluster in the mission, so slow down before leaving the play area.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

This is the section where most completion mistakes happen. The path has ballpits, rollers, shooters, speakers, a DJ booth, and a bouncy castle. Several collectibles sit just off the main route.

<h2 id="house-of-fun-waynetech-cache-3">
  House of Fun: WayneTech Cache 3
</h2>

After the first ballpit, climb up to the upper route. Before you keep going forward, turn back toward the **blue rollers**. Go through the rollers to reach **WayneTech Cache 3**.

This cache is easy to miss because the main route naturally pushes you away from the rollers after you climb up.

<h2 id="house-of-fun-disco-ball-4">
  House of Fun: Disco Ball 4
</h2>

In the ballpit room, look across the area before you cross the thin bridge. **Disco Ball 4** is above the shooters on the opposite side of the ballpit.

If you only watch the bridge path, you will probably miss it. Stop, look across the room, and hit it with a ranged attack before leaving the area.

<h2 id="house-of-fun-waynetech-cache-4">
  House of Fun: WayneTech Cache 4
</h2>

In the larger upside-down L-shaped ballpit area, clear the enemies first. Then check near the start of the area for a grate hidden under the balls.

That hidden grate route leads to **WayneTech Cache 4**. The key is to inspect the ballpit itself instead of only following the upper platforms.

<h2 id="house-of-fun-waynetech-cache-5">
  House of Fun: WayneTech Cache 5
</h2>

Near the end of House of Fun, you reach the DJ booth, loud speakers, and bouncy castle section.

Before dealing with the speakers, look above the bouncy castle for a lock. Break the lock, bounce up, and grab **WayneTech Cache 5**. If you solve the speaker section too quickly and leave, this cache is easy to skip.

<h2 id="disco-ball-5-near-the-elevator">
  Disco Ball 5 Near the Elevator
</h2>

After leaving House of Fun, keep chasing Falcone toward the elevator.

Before you enter the elevator route, walk up to it, then immediately look right above the speakers. **Disco Ball 5** is there. Hit it before moving on.

This completes the set of 5 Disco Balls and gives you the **Disco Balls Batcave Trophy**.

<h2 id="iceberg-lounge-red-brick-safe-code">
  Iceberg Lounge Red Brick Safe Code
</h2>

The Iceberg Lounge Red Brick safe code is **596**.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/lego-batman/lego-batman-iceberg-lounge-red-brick-safe.webp"
    alt="Red Brick safe in the Iceberg Lounge mission with the code 596."
    width={1600}
    height={900}
    className="rounded-xl"
  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    Enter 596 into the safe to get the Iceberg Lounge Red Brick.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

You find the safe near the end of the Falcone chase, after returning through the Iceberg Lounge area where you met Selena and Flass earlier. Look for the newly opened room with the safe.

Enter **596** into the safe. This gives you the mission’s Red Brick and unlocks the **Filthy Rich** modifier.

<h3 id="where-is-falcones-office-faq">
  Where is Falcone’s office in Iceberg Lounge?
</h3>

Falcone’s office is in the basement. After Flass gives you the clue, go straight and down a couple of floors.

<h3 id="what-is-the-iceberg-lounge-red-brick-safe-code">
  What is the Iceberg Lounge Red Brick safe code?
</h3>

The Red Brick safe code is **596**.

<h3 id="what-does-the-iceberg-lounge-red-brick-unlock">
  What does the Iceberg Lounge Red Brick unlock?
</h3>

The Red Brick unlocks the **Filthy Rich** modifier.

<h3 id="how-many-collectibles-are-in-carmine-falcone">
  How many collectibles are in Carmine Falcone?
</h3>

There are **11 collectibles**: 5 Disco Balls, 5 WayneTech Caches, and 1 Red Brick.

<h3 id="where-is-disco-ball-1">
  Where is Disco Ball 1?
</h3>

After the shield guard section, grapple onto the lighting rig and look to the right. Hit the Disco Ball with a Batarang.

<h3 id="where-is-waynetech-cache-1">
  Where is WayneTech Cache 1?
</h3>

It is in the first security camera room. After entering, check behind you before moving forward.

<h3 id="where-is-waynetech-cache-2">
  Where is WayneTech Cache 2?
</h3>

WayneTech Cache 2 is after the food order elevator, near the Ska-Kham Asylum door. Use the vent, Gordon’s Foam Gun, the cable socket, and Batman’s Batarang to open the gate.

<h3 id="how-do-i-finish-the-food-order">
  How do I finish the food order?
</h3>

Place three food items on the tray. For the second item, use Gordon while Batman holds the setup in place. Once all three are placed, the elevator route opens.

<h3 id="what-do-the-disco-balls-unlock">
  What do the Disco Balls unlock?
</h3>

Finding all 5 Disco Balls in this mission rewards the **Disco Balls Batcave Trophy**.

<h3 id="should-i-collect-everything-on-the-first-run">
  Should I collect everything on the first run?
</h3>

If you want 100% completion, yes, this mission is worth cleaning as you go. If you only want story progress, follow the route first and replay later for collectibles.

<h3 id="can-i-replay-carmine-falcone-later">
  Can I replay Carmine Falcone later?
</h3>

Yes. If you miss a Disco Ball, WayneTech Cache, or the Red Brick, replay the mission later and use the collectible table above.
