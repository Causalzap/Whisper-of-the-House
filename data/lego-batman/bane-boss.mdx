<h2 id="bane-boss-fight-overview">
  Bane Boss Fight Overview
</h2>

Bane is not a boss you beat by attacking every time you get close.

The fight is built around three readable states:

- **Bane is active**: he is moving, attacking, or pressuring the arena.
- **Bane is supported**: League enemies are in the arena and can interrupt your movement.
- **Bane is exposed**: the game clearly shows that you can punish him.

Most deaths happen because players confuse the first state with the third. If Bane is still active, focus on spacing. If League enemies are surrounding you, clean up the nearby ones. If Bane drops or the game calls out the opening, move in and punish.

<h2 id="opening-pressure-and-league-reinforcements">
  Opening Pressure and League Reinforcements
</h2>

The fight starts with Bane trying to control your space. Stay away from his front and avoid getting pinned near the edge of the arena.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image
    src="/images/lego-batman/lego-batman-bane-reinforcements.webp"

  />
  <figcaption className="mt-3 text-sm text-slate-400">
    The reinforcement wave comes before the booster phase, so clear space before Bane powers up.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

Bane calls in League reinforcements before the booster phase starts. This is the first major rhythm change in the fight.

Treat this part as arena control. Clear the enemies closest to you, especially anything blocking your escape path. Do not chase Bane through the crowd, because the adds are there to break your spacing and make the next phase harder to read.

Once the wave is under control, get ready for Bane’s booster line. That is the next transition, not the same phase.

<h2 id="booster-phase">
  Booster Phase
</h2>

The booster phase starts when Bane says he needs a little booster.

This phase is best handled defensively. Keep moving, avoid standing directly in front of Bane, and do not waste your strongest attacks while he is still active.

The useful sign is not the start of the booster. The useful sign is what happens after it: Bane eventually overextends or drops into a state where the game lets you attack safely.

<h2 id="health-bar-and-difficulty-notes">
  Health Bar and Difficulty Notes
</h2>

Do not write this fight around exact health percentages unless you verify them yourself in-game.

The boss bar is shown as a broad continuous bar rather than a clean segmented phase tracker. Because of that, the safer guide strategy is to explain the fight through visible and audible cues: reinforcements, booster, Bane being exposed, and the post-fight bomb handoff.

For harder difficulty, the strategy stays the same, but mistakes matter more:

- clear nearby adds earlier,
- respect Bane’s active attacks,
- do not force damage during the booster section,
- and save your aggression for confirmed openings.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

<h3 id="how-do-you-beat-bane">
  How do you beat Bane in LEGO Batman Legacy?
</h3>

Clear the nearby League enemies, survive the booster phase, and attack when Bane is actually exposed.

<h3 id="when-can-you-damage-bane">
  When can you damage Bane?
</h3>

Damage Bane when he drops, becomes clearly open, or the game calls out that he is vulnerable.

<h3 id="what-does-the-booster-phase-do">
  What does the booster phase do?
</h3>

The booster phase is a pressure transition. Play safely through it and wait for Bane to expose himself afterward.

<h3 id="what-should-you-do-when-bane-calls-reinforcements">
  What should you do when Bane calls reinforcements?
</h3>

Clear the closest League enemies first. They are there to interrupt your spacing and make Bane harder to read.

<h3 id="is-there-a-specific-character-you-should-use">
  Is there a specific character you should use?
</h3>

Use Batman or the nearest active character for the boss punish. Use your partner to keep the arena clear.

<h3 id="do-gadgets-skip-the-bane-fight">
  Do gadgets skip the Bane fight?
</h3>

No confirmed gadget skip is shown in this route. Use tools for add control, but beat Bane through the exposed-state openings.

<h3 id="does-bane-have-exact-health-phases">
  Does Bane have exact health phases?
</h3>

The fight is easier to read through cues than percentages. The boss bar is continuous, so watch for reinforcements, booster, and exposed-state moments instead of relying on guessed health thresholds.

<h3 id="is-bane-harder-on-dark-knight-difficulty">
  Is Bane harder on Dark Knight difficulty?
</h3>

The strategy is the same, but mistakes are less forgiving. Clear adds sooner and do not attack while Bane is active.

<h3 id="what-happens-after-bane">
  What happens after Bane is defeated?
</h3>

The story moves straight into the bomb objective. Batgirl needs time to shut it down while the team keeps the threat contained.

<h3 id="should-you-farm-before-bane">
  Should you farm before fighting Bane?
</h3>

No. Bane is mainly a timing and arena-control fight, not a farming check.
