<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
The **Docks** section of **New Beginnings** has **5 Surfboards** and **1 Nautical Red Brick** in **LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight**.

You can collect all of them in your first **Story Mode** run. You do **not** need Free Play, because Batman and Jim Gordon already have the required abilities.

The two easiest things to miss are **Surfboard #4**, which is behind the crawlspace near the ice cream truck, and the **Nautical Red Brick**, which requires you to grapple onto the small cabin roof and drop through the open roof hatch.

If you are looking for the **WayneTech Cache** in New Beginnings, it is not in the Docks section. It appears earlier in the Wayne Industries / Wayne Tower portion of the mission.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="new-beginnings-docks-collectibles">
  New Beginnings Docks Collectibles Checklist
</h2>

The collectible part of **New Beginnings** starts once Batman and Jim Gordon reach the Docks and begin chasing **Detective Flass**.

For this section, do not overthink the collectible count. The Docks route has **5 Surfboards** and **1 Nautical Red Brick**. There is no WayneTech Cache you need to route in this specific mission section.

The clean route is simple: clear each room, check the obvious side space, and never solve the battering ram puzzle until you have both **Surfboard #5** and the **Nautical Red Brick**.

<h2 id="story-mode-or-free-play">
  Can You Get Everything In Story Mode?
</h2>

Yes. You can 100% the Docks collectibles during **Story Mode**.

This is not one of those LEGO missions where you need to come back later with a character who can use a special panel. Batman and Jim Gordon cover the entire Docks route between them.

  caption="Docks collectible requirements."

If you miss something, it is almost always because you moved too quickly through a side room, not because you lacked an ability.

<h2 id="surfboard-1">
  Surfboard #1 Location
</h2>

Surfboard #1 is just after the first proper Gordon machinery puzzle.

When the moving gears block the route, switch to **Jim Gordon** and use his goop gun to gum up the machinery. Once the path is safe, switch back to Batman, pass through, and check the left side near the movable container.

<h2 id="surfboard-2">
  Surfboard #2 Location
</h2>

Surfboard #2 is near the **spinning blue container** puzzle.

After you push the large trailer or cart out of the way, Flass sabotages a crane and the blue container starts spinning. Before you leave this area, look near the base of the crane and the container machinery. The Surfboard is in the open, but the moving container can pull your attention away from it.

After you deal with the spinning container section and move forward, the mission pushes you into another combat space. Before following the objective marker, check the lower area around the ladder and nearby machinery. The Surfboard is leaning there and can be grabbed before you leave the room.

<h2 id="surfboard-4">
  Surfboard #4 Location
</h2>

Surfboard #4 is the most missable Surfboard in the Docks.

After the gunfire section, you will enter a warehouse room with enemies and an **ice cream truck**. Do not leave the room as soon as the fight is over. Go behind the truck and look left for a small crawlspace or duct.

Use the crawlspace, exit outside, then turn toward the dock edge. Surfboard #4 is leaning near the wall by the water.

Before solving the ram puzzle, check the right side of the area. The Surfboard is near the **yellow container** and explosive barrels or explosive stockpile.

<h2 id="nautical-red-brick">
  Nautical Red Brick Location
</h2>

The **Nautical Red Brick** is in the same final area as Surfboard #5, but the entrance is easy to misunderstand.

Look to the **left of the door** you need to break down with the battering ram. There is a small cabin or workshed with the front entrance blocked. Do not try to force the front door.

Instead, use Batman to **grapple up to the roof**, then drop down through the open roof hatch. The Red Brick is inside the cabin.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

<h3 id="where-is-the-waynetech-cache-in-new-beginnings">
  Where is the WayneTech Cache in New Beginnings?
</h3>

The WayneTech Cache in **New Beginnings** is not in the Docks section. It appears earlier in the mission during the **Wayne Industries / Wayne Tower** segment, around the gadget bench tutorial sequence.

For the Docks section specifically, the full collectible route is **5 Surfboards** and **1 Nautical Red Brick**.

<h3 id="what-does-the-nautical-modifier-do">
  What does the Nautical Modifier do?
</h3>

The Nautical Modifier is a cosmetic Red Brick modifier. It changes the look of suits or colors rather than acting like a classic LEGO stud multiplier.

<h3 id="why-cant-i-enter-the-red-brick-room">
  Why can’t I enter the Red Brick room?
</h3>

You are probably trying the blocked front entrance. Grapple onto the small cabin roof and drop in through the open hatch instead.

<h3 id="which-docks-collectible-is-easiest-to-miss">
  Which Docks collectible is easiest to miss?
</h3>

**Surfboard #4** is the easiest to miss because it is behind the crawlspace near the ice cream truck. The Red Brick is the second easiest because the correct entrance is on the roof.

<h3 id="who-is-detective-flass">
  Who is Detective Flass?
</h3>

Detective Flass is Gordon’s corrupt partner in the Docks section. Some searches and auto-captions may spell his name as Flash, Flask, or Fast, but the correct name is **Flass**.

<h3 id="can-you-replay-new-beginnings-if-you-miss-a-collectible">
  Can you replay New Beginnings if you miss a collectible?
</h3>

Yes. None of the Docks collectibles are permanently missable. Replay the mission and check the Surfboard counter before finishing the battering ram puzzle.
