<h2 id="when-batgirl-unlocks">
  When Batgirl Unlocks
</h2>

Batgirl unlocks in **Chapter 4**, during the **Wayne Industries masquerade ball / Firefly sequence**.

Barbara Gordon appears at the event before Firefly attacks. Once the fire triggers the security doors, Barbara steps in, hacks the system, raises the shutters, and becomes active as Batgirl.

Do not look for her in Bat-Mite’s shop. Do not search for a code. Just keep progressing the main story until the Firefly emergency begins.

This is the clean marker:

**Chapter 4 → Firefly attacks → security doors close → Barbara hacks the system → shutters open → Batgirl joins.**

<h2 id="what-batgirl-can-do">
  What Batgirl Can Do
</h2>

Batgirl is mainly a **tech and hacking character**. Her value is not only in combat. She is useful because she can interact with systems that block routes, reveal information, or unlock map progress.

  caption="Batgirl abilities and why they matter."

The important difference is this: Batman can fight through many rooms, but Batgirl is often the character who makes tech-locked routes readable.

<h2 id="radio-towers-and-map-icons">
  Radio Towers and Map Icons
</h2>

Radio Towers are the biggest practical reason to unlock Batgirl before serious cleanup.

Once Batgirl is available, use her hacking tools on Radio Towers around Gotham. Activating towers helps reveal map icons in that area, which makes open-world cleanup much easier.

This is the section most players actually need after asking “when do you unlock Batgirl?” If you are trying to clear Gotham, wait until Batgirl is unlocked, then start activating towers district by district.

Good cleanup order:

1. unlock Batgirl through the Firefly story sequence,
2. return to Gotham exploration,
3. activate Radio Towers with Batgirl,
4. use the revealed map icons to route collectibles and side activities,
5. come back to older areas once you know what is still missing.

Do not rush straight after Firefly. Batgirl’s first section teaches you to read the room:

- use the whale display for the sprinkler-style rescue,
- use the mammoth display for another rescue setup,
- follow the museum route after the civilians are safe,
- prepare the shuttle section when Firefly takes the high ground,
- and use Batgirl’s hack panel when the final setup is ready.

For the boss route itself, use the <Link href="/lego-batman/firefly-boss">Firefly Boss Guide</Link>.

  Should You Wait Before Cleanup?
</h2>

For most players, yes.

You can still do quick side activities before Batgirl unlocks, but full Gotham cleanup becomes more efficient after she joins. Radio Towers, map icon reveals, and tech-based objectives make Batgirl one of the most important unlocks for 100% routing.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

<h3 id="when-do-you-unlock-batgirl">
  When do you unlock Batgirl in LEGO Batman Legacy?
</h3>

Batgirl unlocks in Chapter 4 during the Wayne Industries masquerade ball / Firefly sequence.

<h3 id="is-batgirl-a-shop-unlock">
  Is Batgirl unlocked from Bat-Mite’s shop?
</h3>

No. Batgirl is a main story unlock, not a Bat-Mite shop purchase.

<h3 id="what-is-the-exact-batgirl-unlock-moment">
  What is the exact Batgirl unlock moment?
</h3>

The unlock happens after Firefly triggers the security doors and Barbara hacks the system to raise the shutters. Batman then calls her Batgirl.

<h3 id="what-can-batgirl-do">
  What can Batgirl do?
</h3>

Batgirl can interact with tech systems, hack route blockers, help with panels, and use her hacking tools for Radio Towers and cleanup routing.

<h3 id="why-do-radio-towers-need-batgirl">
  Why do Radio Towers need Batgirl?
</h3>

Radio Towers are tied to Batgirl’s hacking role. Activating them helps reveal map icons, which makes open-world cleanup easier.

<h3 id="should-you-wait-for-batgirl-before-cleanup">
  Should you wait for Batgirl before cleanup?
</h3>

Yes, for serious cleanup. Quick nearby activities are fine earlier, but Radio Towers and tech-based routes make cleanup much smoother after Batgirl unlocks.

<h3 id="is-batgirl-needed-for-firefly">
  Is Batgirl needed for the Firefly fight?
</h3>

Yes. During the Firefly route, Batgirl’s tech is used for the hack panel after the shuttle setup.

<h3 id="what-should-you-do-after-unlocking-batgirl">
  What should you do after unlocking Batgirl?
</h3>

Finish the Firefly story route first. After that, start using Batgirl on Radio Towers before deep Gotham cleanup.

<h3 id="is-batgirl-temporary">
  Is Batgirl temporary?
</h3>

No. After the Firefly sequence, Batgirl becomes part of the active team and her tech remains useful for later routes.
