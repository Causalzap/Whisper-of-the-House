<h2 id="firefly-boss-overview">
  Firefly Boss Overview
</h2>

Firefly is not a normal stand-and-fight boss.

The section is built around route problems:

- follow the burning trail,
- rescue trapped people,
- open blocked doors,
- survive fire-heavy rooms,
- prepare the space shuttle,
- and use Batgirl’s hack panel for the takedown.

If you are trying to attack Firefly every time he appears, you are probably solving the wrong problem. Most of this section is about reading the room and completing the next route step.

<h2 id="ship-rescue-wheel">
  Ship Rescue: Find and Turn the Wheel
</h2>

The ship rescue is the first place where the old version was too vague.

<figure className="my-8">
  <Image

The missing interaction is the **ship’s wheel**. Look on board the ship, find the wheel, place it back, and turn it. Once the wheel is working, the trapped person can get down safely.

After the rescue, pieces appear for the next locked door. Use those pieces before you leave the room.

The confirmed danger here is the burning route itself: Firefly blocks normal movement with fire and forces you through the room puzzle. Some flames are visual background, but anything that blocks your path or stops a grapple route should be treated as the actual obstacle.

Keep moving, follow the safe path, and watch for the next door or tech prompt. Firefly’s role here is mostly to keep pushing you through the burning museum route, not to stand still and trade attacks.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

<h3 id="how-do-you-beat-firefly">
  How do you beat Firefly in LEGO Batman Legacy?
</h3>

Follow the flaming wreckage, complete the ship rescue, build through the locked door, activate the space shuttle, then use Batgirl to hack the panel and knock Firefly down.

<h3 id="how-do-you-rescue-the-person-on-the-ship">
  How do you rescue the person on the ship?
</h3>

Find the ship’s wheel on board, place it back, and turn it. That lowers the trapped person safely.

<h3 id="why-will-the-locked-door-not-open">
  Why will the locked door not open?
</h3>

You probably have not finished the ship rescue. Complete the rescue first, then use the pieces that appear to build through the door.

<h3 id="why-can-not-you-reach-firefly">
  Why can’t you reach Firefly?
</h3>

Firefly takes the high ground. You need to activate the space shuttle instead of trying to attack him directly.

<h3 id="how-do-you-activate-the-space-shuttle">
  How do you activate the space shuttle?
</h3>

Complete the fuel and ignition stations, return to the controls, and start the launch sequence.

<h3 id="what-does-batgirl-do-in-the-firefly-fight">
  What does Batgirl do in the Firefly fight?
</h3>

Batgirl uses the hack panel after the shuttle setup is complete. That hack knocks Firefly down.

<h3 id="what-happens-after-firefly">
  What happens after Firefly is defeated?
</h3>

Mr. Freeze appears immediately after Firefly, and the story moves into the next major threat.
