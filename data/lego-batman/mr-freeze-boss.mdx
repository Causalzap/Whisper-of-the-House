<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
To beat **Mr. Freeze** in **LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight**, focus on shutting down his giant ice machine instead of only attacking him.

The fight starts as a normal boss encounter, then shifts after Batman is frozen and **Nightwing** joins the fight. From there, your goal is to drop the **energy shield**, hack the machine, climb up, manually crank it, and later use the **icicles** with the **cable launcher**.

After the boss is defeated, do not leave immediately. Sweep the arena for **5 Snow Globes** and the mission **Red Brick** before exiting.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

</h2>

After Mr. Freeze is defeated, take control of the arena cleanup before leaving. This is the best time to collect the **5 Snow Globes** and the **Red Brick**.

The safest route is to start near the energy-shield side of the arena, sweep the circular wall, then check the left side of the ice machine.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

<h3 id="who-fights-mr-freeze">
  Which characters fight Mr. Freeze?
</h3>

The Mr. Freeze fight involves Batman, Batwoman, and Nightwing. Batman starts the encounter, but Nightwing becomes important after the phase change.

<h3 id="is-this-fight-about-damaging-freeze">
  Is this fight mostly about damaging Mr. Freeze?
</h3>

No. The main goal is shutting down the ice machine. Damage matters, but the fight is built around shield, hack, crank, and cable launcher mechanics.

<h3 id="when-should-you-collect-snow-globes">
  When should you collect the Snow Globes?
</h3>

Collect the Snow Globes after the boss is defeated. The arena is calmer, and you can safely sweep the circular wall and ice machine area.

<h3 id="how-many-snow-globes-are-there">
  How many Snow Globes are in the Mr. Freeze fight?
</h3>

There are **5 Snow Globes** in the Mr. Freeze arena.

<h3 id="does-mr-freeze-have-a-waynetech-cache">
  Does Mr. Freeze have a WayneTech Cache?
</h3>

No. The Mr. Freeze mission does not have a WayneTech Cache. The main collectibles to route here are **5 Snow Globes** and **1 Red Brick**.

<h3 id="is-mr-freeze-harder-on-dark-knight">
  Is Mr. Freeze harder on Dark Knight difficulty?
</h3>

Yes. His tracking and AoE pressure are more punishing, so you need cleaner movement, better spacing, and faster objective interactions.
