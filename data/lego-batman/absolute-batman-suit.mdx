<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
To get the **Absolute Batman suit** in **LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight**, rescue **9 Escaped Zoo Animals** in Gotham City.

You do **not** need to rescue all 21 animals just to unlock Absolute Batman. The full set of 21 is for completion, but the suit reward only needs **9**.

The animal rescue activity starts after you reach **Chapter 2** and progress through the early **Catwoman** section. For the fastest route, unlock **Batgirl**, clear the **9 Radio Towers** to reveal map icons, then follow the Escaped Zoo Animal markers until you have rescued 9 animals.

<h2 id="nine-animals-vs-all-21-animals">
  Do You Need 9 Animals Or All 21?
</h2>

You only need **9 Escaped Zoo Animals** for **Absolute Batman**.

The game has **21 Escaped Zoo Animal** tasks overall, but the full set is for completion. Do not confuse the suit requirement with the full collectible route.

<h2 id="what-is-absolute-batman">
  What Is Absolute Batman?
</h2>

**Absolute Batman** is based on DC’s **Absolute Batman** comic version of the character.

This version of Batman comes from a different take on Bruce Wayne: a Batman without the usual Wayne fortune, mansion, and support system behind him. No Alfred, no Wayne Manor, no inherited wealth. That is why the design feels heavier, rougher, and more physical than many classic Batman suits.

In **LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight**, the suit stands out because of its oversized shoulder pads, heavier silhouette, and longer cowl ears. It is a cosmetic Batman outfit, so the main reason to unlock it is style, collection progress, and comic fan appeal.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

<h3 id="do-you-need-all-21-or-just-9-animals">
  Do you need all 21 Escaped Zoo Animals for Absolute Batman, or just 9?
</h3>

You only need **9 Escaped Zoo Animals** for Absolute Batman. The full **21** animals are for completion, full Gotham cleanup, and 100% progress.

<h3 id="when-do-escaped-zoo-animals-unlock">
  When do Escaped Zoo Animals unlock?
</h3>

Escaped Zoo Animal rescue activities start appearing after you reach **Chapter 2** and progress through the early **Catwoman** section.

<h3 id="do-you-need-batgirl-for-absolute-batman">
  Do you need Batgirl for Absolute Batman?
</h3>

Not directly. Batgirl is useful because she can clear the **9 Radio Towers**, which reveal map icons and make animal markers easier to find.

<h3 id="where-is-absolute-batman-in-the-menu">
  Where is Absolute Batman in the suit menu?
</h3>

Open **Options**, go to **Characters** with **R1 / RB**, select **Batman**, then press **Square / X** for **Suit Options**. Absolute Batman should appear near the top of the list.

<h3 id="is-absolute-batman-a-dlc-suit">
  Is Absolute Batman a DLC suit?
</h3>

No. Absolute Batman is unlocked through the in-game Escaped Zoo Animals activity.

<h3 id="is-absolute-batman-missable">
  Is Absolute Batman missable?
</h3>

No. If you skip the animal activity early, you can return to Gotham cleanup later and rescue the animals.

<h3 id="why-is-absolute-batman-still-locked">
  Why is Absolute Batman still locked?
</h3>

You probably have not rescued 9 Escaped Zoo Animals yet, or you have not checked Batman’s suit options after earning the reward. Rescue more animals, then reopen Batman’s suit list.
