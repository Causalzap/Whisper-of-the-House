<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
There are **9 Radio Towers** in **LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight**, with **one tower in each Gotham district**.

You can start hacking Radio Towers after reaching **Chapter 4** and unlocking **Batgirl**. To activate a tower, switch to Batgirl, aim her **Hackarang / Batarang** at the tower’s flashing panel, then complete the hacking minigame by guiding the hack toward the center.

Radio Towers reveal nearby map content such as **Riddler challenges, collectibles, trophies, and side-activity icons**, but some revealed activities may still require later story progress or characters before you can complete them.

<h2 id="before-you-start">
  Before You Start Radio Towers
</h2>

Radio Towers are Gotham’s district reveal system. If your map looks empty or other players can see icons that you cannot, you probably have not hacked the tower for that district yet.

The important part is that this is not just a normal interact prompt. You need **Batgirl**, and you need to hit the tower’s flashing panel with her hacking projectile.

  How To Hack Radio Towers
</h2>

The tower hack is the part that trips up most players. Standing near the tower is not enough, and walking around the base usually will not show the answer.

Look for the lit panel or button on the tower, then use Batgirl’s hacking projectile to start the minigame.

<h2 id="should-you-do-radio-towers-early-or-late">
  Should You Do Radio Towers Early Or After The Story?
</h2>

There are two good ways to handle Radio Towers, depending on how you play.

If you want Gotham to be readable as soon as possible, activate towers right after you unlock Batgirl. This makes side activities, Riddler markers, trophies, and cleanup icons much easier to see while you are still progressing.

If you care about the most efficient 100% route, waiting until after more story progress is also valid. Some activities that appear after a tower reveal may still need later characters or abilities, so revealing icons early does not always mean you can complete everything right away.

For most players, the best route is the hybrid approach: **hack the towers once Batgirl is unlocked, but save locked or character-gated activities for later**.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

<h3 id="how-many-radio-towers-are-there">
  How many Radio Towers are there in LEGO Batman Legacy?
</h3>

There are **9 Radio Towers** in total, with **one tower in each Gotham district**.

<h3 id="when-do-radio-towers-unlock">
  When do Radio Towers unlock?
</h3>

Radio Towers become useful once you reach **Chapter 4** and unlock **Batgirl**, since she is the character needed to hack them.

<h3 id="how-do-you-activate-a-radio-tower">
  How do you activate a Radio Tower?
</h3>

Switch to **Batgirl**, aim at the tower’s flashing panel, and hit it with her **Hackarang / Batarang**. This starts a hacking minigame. Complete the puzzle by guiding the hack toward the center.

<h3 id="why-cant-i-hack-the-radio-tower">
  Why can’t I hack the Radio Tower?
</h3>

You are probably using the wrong character, standing too far from the panel, or missing the flashing target on the tower. Use Batgirl and throw her hacking projectile at the lit-up panel.

<h3 id="does-one-radio-tower-reveal-the-whole-map">
  Does one Radio Tower reveal the whole map?
</h3>

No. Each Radio Tower reveals only its own district. You need all **9 towers** for the full Gotham reveal route.

<h3 id="why-are-activities-still-locked-after-activating-a-tower">
  Why are activities still locked after activating a tower?
</h3>

Radio Towers reveal icons, but they do not unlock every activity requirement. Some revealed objectives still need later story progress, specific characters, or later abilities before you can complete them.

<h3 id="should-you-do-radio-towers-early-or-after-the-story">
  Should you do Radio Towers early or after the story?
</h3>

Do them early if you want the map to show more icons while you play. Wait until later if you want the most efficient 100% cleanup route. The best middle-ground is to activate towers after unlocking Batgirl, then come back later for any activity that still needs another character or ability.

<h3 id="do-radio-towers-reveal-riddler-puzzles">
  Do Radio Towers reveal Riddler puzzles?
</h3>

Yes. Radio Towers help reveal district icons, including Riddler-related markers and other side activities.

<h3 id="can-you-see-radio-towers-before-batgirl">
  Can you see Radio Towers before Batgirl is unlocked?
</h3>

Yes. You may see Radio Towers on the map or spot the tower structures while exploring before Batgirl joins the roster, but you cannot activate them yet.

Radio Towers require Batgirl’s hacking ability, so if you reach a tower early and nothing works, keep progressing the story until Chapter 4 and unlock Batgirl first.
