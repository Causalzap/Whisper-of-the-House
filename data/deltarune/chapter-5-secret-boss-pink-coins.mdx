<GuideQuickAnswer title="Quick Answer">
**DELTARUNE Chapter 5 has 19 Pink Coins:** 4 in Garden, 4 in Cliffs, 9 in Flower Castle, and 2 near the Top of Castle / Pink Door route.

You need **10 Pink Coins** to buy the **Mystery Key** and open the Pink Door for the Pink / Miumu secret boss. Do not stop at 10 if you care about post-Pink flower gear, because the other 9 coins can still be useful after the fight.

The best sweep is after **Petal Feather** and before committing to **Flowery**. Use the checklist below as the main route: Garden coins 1-4, Cliffs coins 5-8, Flower Castle coins 9-17, and Top of Castle / Pink Door coins 18-19.
</GuideQuickAnswer>

<h2 id="quick-10-route">
  Fast Route to 10 Pink Coins for the Mystery Key
</h2>

If your only goal is unlocking Pink quickly, you do not need every coin before buying the key.

The easiest 10 usually come from mixing:

* the four Garden coins after Petal Feather;
* the Cliffs shortcut vine and dash-gate coins;
* the five-fox door coin in Flower Castle;
* early Flower Castle statue and canvas puzzle coins;
* one late Top of Castle coin if you are already near the Pink Door.

I still prefer collecting more than 10 if I can. The only reason I stop at exactly 10 is if I am testing the boss quickly or trying to avoid a risky platforming challenge before a save.

<h2 id="pink-coin-rule">
  Pink Coin Rule: 19 Total, 10 for the Key, 9 for Gear
</h2>

The important rule is:

**Chapter 5 has 19 Pink Coins total.**  
**10 Pink Coins buy the Mystery Key.**  
**The remaining 9 Pink Coins are still useful after Pink.**

The old way to think about this route was “get 10 and stop.” I do not recommend that anymore. Ten is the hard gate for the secret boss, but extra coins can be spent after beating Pink on flower weapons and armor. That means the real route has two goals:

1. **Minimum route:** collect 10 coins, buy the Mystery Key, fight Pink.
2. **Better cleanup route:** collect as many of the 19 coins as possible before the point of no return, then use the leftover coins after Pink.

<GuideCallout title="Do not stop at 10 if you care about gear">
Ten Pink Coins are enough to unlock Pink, but they are not the whole reward route. If you want the best Chapter 5 cleanup file, collect extra Pink Coins before moving into the final route.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="petal-feather">
  Get the Petal Feather Before Serious Cleanup
</h2>

Do not start a full Pink Coin sweep too early.

Many Pink Coins sit behind platforming-mode routes, bells, dash gates, vines, leaf climbs, side rooms, and shortcuts that only make sense after the Chapter 5 movement kit opens up. The key progression point is the **Petal Feather** route after Aqua. Once you have that movement tool and more magic-door shortcuts, the coin hunt becomes much less painful.

My route timing is:

1. Play normally until Aqua and the Petal Feather route are done.
2. Pick up obvious coins if they are on your path.
3. Continue until the important Garden, Cliffs, Castle, and Top of Castle routes are open.
4. Before Flowery, stop and sweep Pink Coins.
5. Buy the Mystery Key only when the party is ready for Pink.

If you are still before Aqua and cannot reach a bell, chest, vine, dash gate, or platforming path, the answer may not be “you missed a jump.” You may simply be too early.

<h2 id="when-to-clean-up">
  Best Time to Collect Pink Coins
</h2>

The best time for serious Pink Coin cleanup is **late Chapter 5, before committing to Flowery**.

By that point, you should have:

* Petal Feather movement;
* magic-door shortcuts;
* access to Garden, Cliffs, Flower Castle, and Top of Castle routes;
* enough HP and items to survive platforming mistakes;
* a save file you are not using for Weird Route / Side B testing.

I do not wait until after the final route starts. Once the game pushes you into Flowery and the ending sequence, backtracking becomes risky or impossible depending on the route state. Treat the pre-Flowery window as your cleanup checkpoint.

<h2 id="coins-after-10">
  What to Do With Coins 11-19: Flower Gear After Pink
</h2>

Coins 11-19 are not just “extra.”

After beating Pink, the remaining Pink Coins can be spent on flower gear. This is why I do not describe 10 coins as the real finish line anymore. Ten opens the boss. More coins improve the post-boss reward route.

The practical choice is:

* **Only want to see Pink:** collect 10, buy the key, fight the boss.
* **Want a strong cleanup file:** collect as many of the 19 as possible before Flowery.
* **Want post-Pink flower gear:** do not ignore coins 11-19.
* **Hate a specific platforming coin:** skip it for now, but remember that missed coins affect post-Pink buying power.

If you beat Pink and later realize coins are missing, there is a late helper option: if Ferroll has been found in the Cliffs, Ferroll can appear in the Pink fight room after Pink and offer help finding missed Pink Coins for Flowery Dollars. That help points you toward the area count, not exact room-by-room solutions, so a real checklist is still useful.

<h2 id="point-of-no-return">
  Point of No Return: Collect Pink Coins Before Flowery
</h2>

Do Pink Coin cleanup before committing to Flowery.

The safest mental cutoff is: once you are heading into the final Flowery route, stop assuming you can freely backtrack. If you still care about Pink Coins, flower gear, the five-fox room, or the Secret Egg, do that cleanup first.

My late-route order is:

1. Save before the final route.
2. Sweep Pink Coins.
3. Decide whether 10 is enough or whether I want more.
4. Buy the Mystery Key.
5. Beat Pink if I am ready.
6. Spend post-Pink coins if I am doing gear cleanup.
7. Return to the normal ending route.
8. Keep Weird Route on a separate file.

This is not about being slow. It is about protecting the file.

<h2 id="common-mistakes">
  Common Pink Coin Mistakes
</h2>

* **Thinking 10 coins are the whole route:** 10 buys the Mystery Key, but Chapter 5 has 19 Pink Coins total.
* **Ignoring coins 11-19:** extra coins can matter for post-Pink flower gear.
* **Trying full cleanup before Petal Feather:** many coins are easier or possible only after movement and shortcuts open.
* **Entering Flowery before cleanup:** clean up Pink Coins before committing to the final route.
* **Searching the whole chapter for the five-fox coin:** that coin is local to the Second Diner fox area.
* **Confusing sun-style checks with the five-fox route:** sun / color-change rooms belong to total Pink Coin cleanup.
* **Buying the key without preparing for Pink:** the key opens a hard secret boss route.
* **Overwriting the wrong save:** keep normal, cleanup, and Weird Route files separate.
* **Assuming Ferroll gives exact locations:** Ferroll can help point to missing area counts, but you still need to find the rooms.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many Pink Coins are in DELTARUNE Chapter 5?

There are **19 Pink Coins** in Chapter 5.

### Where are all Pink Coins in DELTARUNE Chapter 5?

There are 4 Pink Coins in Garden, 4 in Cliffs, 9 in Flower Castle, and 2 near the Top of Castle / Pink Door route.

### How many Pink Coins do I need for the Mystery Key?

You need **10 Pink Coins** to buy the Mystery Key.

### Should I stop collecting after 10 Pink Coins?

Only if you just want to unlock Pink quickly. If you care about post-Pink flower gear, collect more than 10.

### What are the extra 9 Pink Coins for?

The remaining Pink Coins can be used after beating Pink to buy flower gear. That is why they are not useless cleanup currency.

### When should I start serious Pink Coin cleanup?

Start serious cleanup after Aqua / Petal Feather and after more shortcuts are open. The best sweep is late Chapter 5 before Flowery.

### Do I need Petal Feather for Pink Coins?

You need Petal Feather-style movement and platforming access for many coin routes. If a coin seems unreachable early, continue the story and come back later.

### Where do I buy the Mystery Key?

Buy the Mystery Key from Pink’s shop in the Cliffs after collecting at least 10 Pink Coins.

### What if the Mystery Key does not show up?

If you have enough coins but the shop state looks wrong, save, reload, and check again before re-hunting the entire chapter.

### Where is the Pink Door?

The Pink Door is on the late Top of Castle / secret boss route near the final stretch before Flowery.

### Is the five-fox room required for a Pink Coin?

Yes. Finding all five local foxes near the Second Diner opens a secret room where a fox becomes a chest and gives a Pink Coin.

### Can Ferroll help with missed Pink Coins?

After Pink, if Ferroll has been found in the Cliffs, Ferroll can appear in the Pink fight room and help point out missing Pink Coin counts by area for Flowery Dollars.

### Should I do Pink before Flowery?

If you want secret boss and flower gear cleanup, yes, do it before committing to Flowery. If you only want the normal ending, you can save Pink for a cleanup file.

### Should I do this on Weird Route?

I would not. Keep Pink Coin cleanup, Pink / Miumu, and post-Pink gear on a normal or cleanup file, and keep Weird Route / Side B separate.
