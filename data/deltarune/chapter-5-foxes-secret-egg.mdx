<h2 id="what-this-guide-covers">
  What this foxes and Secret Egg guide covers
</h2>

This guide covers two Chapter 5 secrets:

* the **five foxes** near the Second Diner in Flower Castle;
* the **Secret Egg** route that starts from the wheat field.

The fox section is a location checklist. The egg section is a route puzzle. I handle them separately because they ask for different things: foxes are about checking the right rooms in one small area, while the egg is about repeating the wheat route and following the dog’s flower pattern.

If you are trying to unlock Pink / Miumu, the fox reward matters because the five-fox secret room gives a Pink Coin. For the full Secret Boss route, use:

<h2 id="fox-rule">
  Fox rule: 3 for Aqua, 5 for the Pink Coin room
</h2>

The correct fox rule is:

**3 foxes = enough to pass Aqua’s story check.**
**5 foxes = opens the nearby locked secret room for a Pink Coin.**

This is where I changed my route notes. I do not treat the fox hunt as a chapter-wide backtracking job anymore. Once I reach the Second Diner area inside Flower Castle, I take the door to the left and search the connected fox-statue / Terracotta rooms. All five foxes are there or directly connected to that spot.

The practical route is:

1. Reach the **Second Diner** in Flower Castle.
2. Take the door to the **left** of the diner area.
3. Find the room fronted by fox statues.
4. Collect at least **3 foxes** if you only need story progress.
5. Collect all **5 foxes** if you want the locked secret room.
6. Enter the secret room and get the Pink Coin reward.

<GuideCallout title="Important correction">
If you are missing foxes for the Second Diner secret room, do not run back through Garden, Cliffs, sun rooms, or later castle routes. The five foxes for this check are in the same local fox area.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="three-or-five">
  Should you stop at 3 foxes or find all 5?
</h2>

If you only want to continue the story, **3 foxes** are enough for Aqua.

If you want the Secret Boss route, do not stop at three. Find all **5 foxes**, open the locked secret room nearby, and collect the Pink Coin from the chest inside. That Pink Coin is part of the larger Pink Coin route that leads to the Mystery Key and Pink Door.

My decision is simple:

* first clear with no Secret Boss plan: 3 foxes is fine;
* Secret Boss cleanup: find all 5 before leaving the area;
* missing one fox: stay in the local fox rooms, not the whole chapter;
* low HP or messy file: pass Aqua first, then come back before pushing too far.

The five-fox reward is optional for story progress, but it is not optional if you are trying to collect enough Pink Coins cleanly.

<h2 id="pink-coin-reward">
  What the 5-fox room gives you
</h2>

After all five foxes are found, the locked secret room opens. Inside, another fox appears and turns into a chest. Open it for the Pink Coin.

This is why the fox hunt belongs in the same cleanup path as Pink Coins and the Mystery Key. The foxes themselves are not the Secret Boss unlock, but the Pink Coin reward helps you reach the 10 Pink Coin target for the Mystery Key route.

After collecting this Pink Coin, I do not keep searching the fox room. I mark the room done and return to the main Pink Coin route.

The route I follow is:

1. Find the wheat field between two gates.
2. Run through the wheat repeatedly until almost all of it is harvested.
3. Keep using the remaining wheat segment until a balloon appears.
4. Follow the balloon left with the wind.
5. Enter the flower room with the dog.
6. Watch which flowers the dog visits.
7. Interact with the flowers in the same order.
8. Take the watering can after the flower pattern works.
9. Use the watering can to make the path left.
10. Continue until you reach the man behind the tree and receive the Egg.

The important thing is patience. I do not count a fixed number of wheat passes because the safe player-facing instruction is to keep going until the route visibly changes.

<h2 id="dog-flower-puzzle">
  Dog flower puzzle: copy the order
</h2>

The flower room is the part I would not describe as “just follow the guide” anymore. The dog is showing the order.

The dog hovers over flowers one by one. I watch the route, then interact with each flower in the same sequence. If I rush and click the wrong flower, I reset my attention and watch the pattern again instead of guessing.

My practical method:

1. Stand still when the dog starts moving.
2. Watch the first flower it visits.
3. Follow the dog’s full order.
4. Touch the flowers in that order.
5. Wait for the watering can to appear.
6. Use the watering can to open the left-side path.

This is the real puzzle step between the wheat route and the Egg. If you harvest the wheat correctly but never solve the dog flower order, you will not finish the route.

<h2 id="egg-evidence">
  Egg evidence in the Yellow / Blue trial
</h2>

The Egg can appear as an evidence option during the Yellow / Blue trial route, but I do not treat it as a hidden power boost.

For practical play, think of it as a secret-state joke or extra acknowledgement. If the trial asks you to prove the real culprit, use the correct evidence for the case instead of assuming the Egg solves the trial.

That means my recommendation is:

* get the Egg early if you want to see the extra evidence option;
* do not expect it to make the trial easier;
* do not delay your whole run if you only want the normal clear;
* use a cleanup file if you want to compare trial evidence states.

This keeps the Egg useful without overselling it as a required trial tool.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### How many foxes do I need for Aqua?

You need **3 foxes** to pass Aqua’s story gate.

### How many foxes do I need for the secret room?

You need **5 foxes** to open the nearby locked secret room and claim the Pink Coin inside.

### Are all five foxes spread across Chapter 5?

No. The five foxes for this check are in the same Second Diner / fox-statue area inside Flower Castle.

### Where do I start the fox hunt?

Reach the Second Diner, take the door to the left, and search the fox-statue / Terracotta room area.

### What does finding all five foxes unlock?

It opens a locked secret room nearby. The fox inside becomes a chest, and the chest gives a Pink Coin.

### Is the five-fox Pink Coin required for the secret boss?

It is part of the Pink Coin cleanup route. You need enough Pink Coins to buy the Mystery Key, so I collect this one if I am going for Pink / Miumu.

### Where does the Secret Egg route start?

It starts at the wheat field between two gates. Run through the wheat repeatedly until the route changes and a balloon appears.

### What do I do after the balloon appears?

Follow the balloon left, enter the dog flower room, copy the order of flowers the dog visits, then take the watering can.

### Does the Egg help in the Yellow / Blue trial?

It can appear as an evidence option, but I treat it as a secret-state joke rather than a correct trial solution. Use the real case evidence for trial progress.

### Should I do foxes and Egg before Weird Route?

Yes, if you want clean routing. I prefer doing foxes, Egg, Pink Coins, and Secret Boss cleanup on a normal or cleanup file, then keeping Weird Route / Side B separate.
