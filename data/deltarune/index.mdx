<h2 id="start-here">
  Start here: what Chapter 5 asks you to manage
</h2>

Chapter 5 is not difficult because one puzzle blocks the whole route. It is difficult because several routes overlap:

* the normal Flower Kingdom route;
* all **19 Pink Coins**;
* the **10 Pink Coin** Mystery Key gate;
* the post-Pink flower gear economy;
* the **Pink / Miumu** secret boss;
* the **3 foxes for Aqua / 5 foxes for Pink Coin room** check;
* the Secret Egg route;
* the Flowery final boss route;
* the separate Weird Route / Side B file.

My clean route plan is:

1. Play the normal route first if this is your first Chapter 5 clear.
2. Pick up obvious Pink Coins, but do not panic if some are unreachable early.
3. After Aqua and Petal Feather-style movement opens, start serious Pink Coin cleanup.
4. Before Flowery, decide whether you want only 10 coins for the Mystery Key or more coins for post-Pink gear.
5. Use a cleanup file for Pink / Miumu, foxes, Secret Egg, and flower gear.
6. Keep Weird Route / Side B on its own file.

The most important rule is simple: **do not mix normal clear, Secret Boss cleanup, and Weird Route testing on one file unless you are intentionally experimenting.**

<h2 id="first-run-route">
  Recommended first-run route
</h2>

For a first Chapter 5 clear, I do not chase every secret immediately.

My first-run route is:

1. Play the festival and Flower Kingdom route normally.
2. Collect obvious Pink Coins while moving forward.
3. Do not force every platforming coin before the movement kit and shortcuts open.
4. Skip Weird Route completely.
5. Save before the late castle / final route stretch.
6. If you are stocked and want the secret boss, collect at least **10 Pink Coins** for the Mystery Key. Collecting more than 10 is still useful for post-Pink gear; see the <Link href="#pink-coins-and-secret-boss">Pink Coins section</Link> below.
7. If you only want a clean first ending, skip Pink / Miumu for now.
8. Beat Flowery and finish the normal Chapter 5 ending.
9. Keep that normal clear file clean.
10. Use a separate cleanup file for Pink Coins, Pink / Miumu, foxes, Secret Egg, and flower gear.

<GuideCallout title="First-run rule">
If you are unsure whether your file is normal, cleanup, or Weird Route, stop and check before committing. Chapter 5 has enough route flags that guessing can make the file harder to understand later.
</GuideCallout>

<h2 id="pink-coins-and-secret-boss">
  Pink Coins, Mystery Key, and the secret boss route
</h2>

Pink Coins are not ordinary money.

Chapter 5 has **19 Pink Coins** total. You need **10 Pink Coins** to buy the Mystery Key and open the Pink Door for Pink / Miumu. The remaining **9 Pink Coins** still matter because they can be used after Pink for flower gear.

That changes the route advice:

* **10 coins** is the minimum secret boss gate.
* **More than 10 coins** is useful if you care about post-Pink equipment.
* **All 19 coins** is the best cleanup target.
* **Petal Feather and shortcuts** make cleanup much easier.
* **Before Flowery** is the safest time to finish serious coin cleanup.

<h2 id="pink-boss-prep">
  Pink / Miumu secret boss prep
</h2>

Pink / Miumu is not a damage race.

The fight is built around DOKI and TP. Phase 1 needs **15 DOKI**, Phase 2 needs **15 DOKI**, and Phase 3 needs **20 DOKI**. Flirt is small progress, MegaFlirt / MegaFlirtX are +5 pushes, and GigaFlirt is the +10 push that needs **80 TP available** after the TP cap is rebuilt. LovelyBoy and safe pink hearts help recover the Max TP loop after Pink pressures it.

* a normal or cleanup file, not my Weird Route file.

The fight also includes dating sim sections. The first date answer pattern is **Yes / Yes / No**. Later, the body / ghost split requires reading paired cues, and the final section becomes a DIE maze. Do not enter expecting a normal boss HP fight.

<h2 id="foxes-and-secret-egg">
  Foxes and Secret Egg cleanup
</h2>

Foxes and the Secret Egg are separate cleanup routes.

The fox rule is:

**3 foxes pass Aqua.**
**5 foxes open the nearby secret room for a Pink Coin.**

This is not a chapter-wide fox hunt. The five foxes for that check are in the same local Second Diner / fox-statue / Terracotta area. If you are missing one fox for the Pink Coin room, do not run back across the whole chapter. Stay local and clear that connected area.

For the Secret Egg, use the wheat field between two gates. Harvest the wheat route until it changes, follow the balloon, copy the dog’s flower order, take the watering can, open the left-side path, and reach the man behind the tree.

The Egg can appear as an evidence option later, but I do not treat it as a required power boost. It is best handled as a secret-state cleanup item.

<h2 id="normal-ending-route">
  Flowery final boss and normal ending route
</h2>

The normal ending route does not end the moment you seal the first fountain.

Flowery is the normal-route final boss route, and the fight is about Mercy progress instead of damage. Posey / PoseyZ can give strong Mercy progress with good timing, Blow Away is safer but smaller, Orange dash tests charge-and-release movement, Knife Cutting can feed TP back into the fight, and the Justice trial punishes weak resource planning.

The key target is around **50% Mercy**, where the fight begins moving toward its route break.

My normal final route checklist:

* heal before the final stretch;
* do not spend every item before Flowery;
* use Posey when the party is stable;
* use Blow Away when timing is shaky;
* save TP before Justice;
* be ready for the first fountain and the second fountain;
* keep the normal clear file separate from Side B.

<h2 id="weird-route-warning">
  Weird Route and Side B warning
</h2>

Weird Route is not something I casually start inside a normal Chapter 5 file.

Chapter 5 Weird Route requires a save file that already continued the Weird Route through Chapter 4. If the file qualifies, Chapter 5 starts differently: Kris refuses to get out of bed, and the route is already active.

The core Weird Route steps are:

1. Start from the correct Chapter 4 Weird Route continuation file.
2. Force Kris out of bed.
3. Leave with Susie.
4. Get Bread from Sans at the ice cream stand.
5. Go to the lake with Susie.
6. Enter Noelle’s lake scene.
7. Handle the early Stop / Proceed prompts.
8. Once submerged, quickly select Proceed through roughly 70 prompts before the hidden timer expires.
9. Success gives **Insert Chapter 7 Side B**.
10. Failure creates an aborted route state that can later show **Shattered Rose**.

This route has no normal SAVE point inside the sequence, and the lake is a timed input challenge, not just a dialogue choice. Use a separate file.

<h2 id="when-to-backtrack">
  Cleanup checkpoint before Flowery
</h2>

This is the one point where I stop and decide whether to backtrack.

Before committing to Flowery, I check four things:

* Do I still want more Pink Coins for post-Pink flower gear?
* Did I finish the five-fox Pink Coin room if I care about Secret Boss cleanup?
* Did I finish the Secret Egg route if I want the evidence option?
* Am I on the correct file for normal ending, cleanup, or Weird Route?

If the answer to any of those is unclear, I backtrack or switch files before pushing the final route. If I only want a clean normal ending, I continue to Flowery and leave cleanup for later.

This section is not a second route plan. It is the final safety check before the chapter stops feeling open.

<h2 id="common-mistakes">
  Common Chapter 5 mistakes
</h2>

* **Thinking 10 Pink Coins are the whole economy:** Chapter 5 has 19, and extras matter after Pink.
* **Trying full Pink Coin cleanup too early:** wait until Petal Feather movement and shortcuts make it practical.
* **Entering Flowery before cleanup:** sweep important Pink Coins and secret routes first if you care about them.
* **Buying the Mystery Key without boss prep:** 10 coins unlock the route, but they do not make Pink easy.
* **Trying to damage-race Pink:** use DOKI, TP, flirt ACTs, dating sim answers, and survival planning.
* **Stopping at 3 foxes when you want the Pink Coin room:** Aqua needs 3, but the secret room needs 5.
* **Searching the whole chapter for a local fox:** the five foxes are around the Second Diner fox area.
* **Running through the wheat once and leaving:** the Secret Egg route needs the wheat route, balloon, dog flower order, and watering can.
* **Assuming the first fountain ends Chapter 5:** Flowery and the second fountain still matter.
* **Starting Weird Route from a normal file:** Chapter 5 Weird Route needs the previous Weird Route state.
* **Skipping Sans on Weird Route:** Bread is required before the lake.
* **Reading the underwater Proceed prompts too slowly:** the lake has a hidden timer.
* **Using one file for everything:** normal, cleanup, and Weird Route files should stay separate.

<h2 id="faq">
  FAQ
</h2>

### Should I do the normal route or Weird Route first?

Do the normal route first unless you specifically want Side B. Chapter 5 Weird Route needs a previous Weird Route save state and should be kept on a separate file.

### How many Pink Coins are in Chapter 5?

There are **19 Pink Coins** in Chapter 5.

### How many Pink Coins do I need for the Mystery Key?

You need **10 Pink Coins** to buy the Mystery Key.

### Should I keep collecting Pink Coins after 10?

Yes, if you care about post-Pink flower gear. The remaining 9 coins are not useless.

### When should I do serious Pink Coin cleanup?

Do serious cleanup after Aqua / Petal Feather movement and shortcuts open, but before committing to Flowery.

### Is Pink / Miumu the final boss?

No. Pink / Miumu is the Chapter 5 secret boss. Flowery is tied to the normal-route final stretch.

### What should I know before fighting Pink?

Track DOKI, not damage. Phase targets are 15 / 15 / 20 DOKI, and GigaFlirt needs 80 TP available after the TP cap is rebuilt.

### How many foxes do I need?

You need 3 foxes to pass Aqua, but all 5 local foxes open the nearby Pink Coin room.

### Where does the Secret Egg route start?

The Secret Egg route starts at the wheat field between two gates. Harvest the wheat route, follow the balloon, solve the dog flower order, use the watering can, and reach the man behind the tree.

### Does Chapter 5 end after sealing the first fountain?

No. Be ready for Flowery, the first fountain, the second fountain, and the Chapter 6 continuation.

### What is the first Weird Route warning sign?

Kris refusing to get out of bed is the first major sign. If you expected a normal route, stop and check your save.

### Do I need Bread from Sans for Weird Route?

Yes. In Chapter 5 Weird Route, get Bread from Sans at the ice cream stand before going to the lake.

### What happens if I fail the lake Proceed sequence?

The Weird Route can abort into an altered state, and Kris can later carry the Shattered Rose title. Reload if you wanted the clean Side B result.
