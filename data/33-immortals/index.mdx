<h2 id="relics">
  Relics: build around triggers
</h2>

Relics in 1.0 are not just random bonuses. They belong to Paths, use types, appear at different rarities, can be rerolled before selection, can be upgraded, can be dismantled, and can be targeted through Wishing.

The most useful rule is:

<strong>Pick Relics your weapon can trigger often.</strong>

A Co-op Power relic is stronger when you stay near allies and actually use Co-op Powers. A Co-Strike relic is stronger when the group focuses the same target. A crit, missile, blast, or stat conversion relic is only worth deep investment when it fits what the run is already doing.

Read the <Link href="/33-immortals/relics-paths-wishing-guide">Relics, Paths and Wishing Guide</Link> for key Relic names, Wishing rules, Bones rerolls, dismantling, boss Relics, and common build mistakes.

<h2 id="faq">
  33 Immortals FAQ
</h2>

### What is 33 Immortals?

33 Immortals is a 33-player co-op action roguelike from Thunder Lotus. You join raid-style runs, fight through large groups of enemies, cooperate with other players, defeat major bosses, and grow stronger between attempts.

### When did 33 Immortals 1.0 release?

33 Immortals 1.0 launched in June 2026, with Steam, Epic Games Store, and Xbox availability rolling out around the 1.0 release window.

### What platforms is 33 Immortals on?

33 Immortals is available on Steam, Epic Games Store, and Xbox platforms. Platform rollout details can change, so check the store page you plan to use before buying or installing.

### What should I do first in 33 Immortals?

Follow safe early objectives, break jars and bones, spend level-up choices, avoid risky rooms while low, and regroup before Ascension. Start with the <Link href="/33-immortals/beginner-progression-guide">Beginner Progression Guide</Link>.

### What is the best weapon in 33 Immortals 1.0?

The strongest current signal points to Bow of Hope, Glaive of Temperance, and Crossbows of Pride, but the best weapon is the one whose loop you can execute consistently. See the <Link href="/33-immortals/weapons-unlock-upgrades-guide">Weapons Guide</Link>.

### What weapon should I unlock first?

Most new players should start with Bow of Hope or Crossbows of Pride for safer learning. Sword is better if you want frontline timing, while Staff or Scepter fits support-focused players.

### How much do weapons cost to unlock?

As of 1.0, weapons cost 50 Eternal Shards to unlock. Scepter of Charity is also unlocked for 50 Eternal Shards at its Weapon Shrine in Dark Woods.

### How does Weapon Mastery work?

Weapon upgrades have Master Gauges. Each mastered upgrade gives 1% damage, and mastering all 48 Weapon Upgrades gives 48% damage. The <Link href="/33-immortals/weapons-unlock-upgrades-guide">Weapons Guide</Link> explains how to plan around that.

### How do Relics work in 1.0?

Relics now use Paths and types. You can reroll choices with Bones before selecting, upgrade rarity, dismantle bad fits, collect Relic Tokens, and Wish for Action Relics. Read the <Link href="/33-immortals/relics-paths-wishing-guide">Relics Guide</Link>.

### How does Relic Wishing work?

Relic Wishing lets you target one Action Relic from a specific Path before a run. It costs Eternal Shards, and the same Relic can be Wished multiple times in 1.0.

### Why do random lobbies wipe at bosses?

Most random lobby boss wipes come from missed mechanics: ignored priority targets, wrong pillar order, bad stack movement, missed shields, or ignored puzzle objects. Use the <Link href="/33-immortals/bosses-guide">Bosses Guide</Link>.

### How do I finish all achievements?

Route achievements in layers: early progression, hidden goals, boss clears, party goals, weapon goals, Daily Quest Chest, Relic Wishing, cosmetics, Ordeals, and endgame cleanup. See the <Link href="/33-immortals/achievements-guide">Achievements Guide</Link>.

### Is this hub a wiki?

No. This hub is a practical route page. It points you to focused guides that explain what to do during a run, not just item names or database entries.
