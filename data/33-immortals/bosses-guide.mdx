<h2 id="bosses-guide">
  33 Immortals bosses guide

Boss fights in **33 Immortals** punish groups that play on autopilot.

A run can look strong for ten minutes and still collapse instantly if players ignore priority targets, hit Adam & Eve pillars in the wrong order, or tunnel Wrath of God while puzzle objects are active.

This guide is not a full database of every number. It is a practical wipe-fix guide: what each boss is asking you to notice, what usually kills random lobbies, and how to make the next attempt cleaner.

<h2 id="before-boss">
  Before any boss: enter with a stable run
</h2>

Most boss wipes start before the boss room.

If you enter low, scattered, under-upgraded, or broke, the boss does not need to do much to finish the run. Before the final route, check your health, bones, level-up choices, weapon role, and team position.

<h2 id="co-strike">
  Co-Strike: focus the right target together
</h2>

Co-Strike matters because boss fights are not only about personal damage. The group needs to delete the correct target at the correct time.

Sometimes that target is the boss. Sometimes it is a blue enemy, crystal tear, pillar, puzzle crystal, or another mechanic object. The mistake is treating every moment as a boss damage window.

<GuideTable
caption="How to use Co-Strike during boss fights."
columns={coStrikeColumns}
rows={coStrikeRows}
/>

A good beginner rule is: **if a mechanic object can wipe the team, Co-Strike that object first**.

### How many major bosses are in 33 Immortals?

The main boss route covers Lucifer, Adam & Eve, and Wrath of God across the current 1.0 progression.

### How do I beat Lucifer?

Learn Lucifer's three phases: Frozen Hate, Eternal Sorrow, and Last Betrayal. Handle Frozen Hate safe lanes and shackles, destroy Eternal Sorrow crystal tears, stack during Last Betrayal, and only push a phase if the group clearly has enough damage.

### What are Lucifer's phases?

Lucifer's key phases are Frozen Hate, Eternal Sorrow, and Last Betrayal. Frozen Hate uses lane and shackle pressure, Eternal Sorrow uses crystal tears, and Last Betrayal uses stack marker pressure.

### What is the Lucifer phase push trick?

If Lucifer is very close to the next health threshold and the group has enough damage, pushing the phase can cancel the current mechanic. If the damage is uncertain, solve the mechanic first.

### How do I beat Adam & Eve?

Spread for the six pillars, read the edge order indicators, hit the correct symbols, dash through waves, and clear mobs without losing the order check.

### What are the Adam & Eve pillars?

Adam & Eve can spawn six pillars in symbol pairs, such as hands, birds, and fish. The group needs to cover the arena and follow the correct order instead of hitting random pillars.

### How do I beat Wrath of God?

Respect shield timing, follow sun/moon/fire calls, learn memory patterns, and destroy puzzle crystals or required objects before the cast bar finishes.

### Why does Wrath of God wipe the whole arena?

A common cause is ignoring cast bar puzzle objects. When Wrath begins a dangerous cast, destroy the required crystals or targets before the cast completes.

### Is boss damage more important than mechanics?

No. Boss damage matters during safe windows, but mechanics, priority adds, pillars, tears, shields, and puzzle objects come first.

### How should random lobbies handle boss fights?

Follow the group during stack and movement mechanics, focus the correct priority target together, and use pings or emotes only when they help the group make a better decision.
