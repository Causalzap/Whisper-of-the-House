<h2 id="faq">
  33 Immortals achievements FAQ
</h2>

### How many achievements are in 33 Immortals?

There are 33 achievements in 33 Immortals.

### Are there hidden achievements?

Yes. The most important hidden achievements include <strong>Hidden Weapon</strong> for completing a Secret Chamber, <strong>Pulling out the Weeds</strong> for defeating Adam & Eve, <strong>Prayer Answered</strong> for Relic Wishing, <strong>My Own Way</strong> for defeating Wrath of God, <strong>Apotheosis</strong> for Beatrice progression, and <strong>Zenith of the Starbound</strong> for the third Constellation. Use the Hidden Achievements table above for the full route.

### What achievement should I do first?

Start with natural progression: The Rebellion Begins, Unified Triumph, Rising Star, Incipit, Bone Gatherer, and From King to Nothing.

### How do I get Bone Gatherer?

Use a Bone Shrine to heal, acquire a key, and acquire a Teleport Stone. Do not treat the shrine only as a healing station.

### How do I get United We Conquer?

Beat any boss while already in a party. The safest route is to invite a friend or use an LFG group, then clear the easiest boss you can reliably reach.

### How do I get Synergetic Triune?

Cast Co-op Powers from 3 different weapons. Use a safe rotation such as Bow, Sword, and Staff or Scepter, and cast each power during real combat.

### How do I get The Erudite's Favorite?

Complete a Daily Quest objective, then claim the Daily Quest Chest. The chest claim is the part players often forget.

### How do I get Style Explorer?

Equip a common cape, common halo, and common weapon skin. Open every wardrobe slot when you have enough basic cosmetic options.

### How do I get Fashionista?

Equip an epic halo, epic cape, epic weapon skin, and epic familiar. Save it for wardrobe cleanup after you have enough epic cosmetic options.

### How do I get Prayer Answered?

Wish for a relic, then find and equip that relic in the Beyond. Pick a Wish target you can recognize during the run.

### How do I get Master Craftsman?

Fully level a weapon's Co-op Power and equip two Upgrades. Commit to one main weapon instead of spreading upgrades across every weapon.

### How do I get Apotheosis?

Follow the Beatrice and Constellation route: complete Feats, claim Bright Stars, fill Constellation progress around Beatrice, and return to Beatrice progression after major star milestones.

### What are the hardest achievements?

The hardest cleanup goals are usually Apostle of Agony, Collector, My Own Way, Apotheosis, Structured Poetry, A Rebel's Journey, and Zenith of the Starbound.
